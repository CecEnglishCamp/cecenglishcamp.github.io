A structured curriculum for parents who want to teach English at home — Grade 3 through Grade 6, episode by episode, with confidence.

👩‍🏫
Parent-Led Lessons

Clear lesson plans any parent can follow — no teaching experience required. Just open and teach.

📚
Grade 3 – 6 Coverage

15 episodes per grade, 60 total — systematically covering vocabulary, grammar, and reading comprehension.

🤖
AI Support

AI tutor assists both parent and child — answering questions and providing pronunciation guidance in real time.

🏠
Home-Friendly Format

Short, focused episodes designed for a home setting — 20 to 30 minutes per session, no special equipment needed.
