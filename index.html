Tuition Plans in Detail

A closer look at our 3 program options

We understand that every child learns differently.
That’s why CEC English Camp offers three tiers of learning experiences,
each designed to support students at their own pace, with the right level of guidance and interaction.
Here’s a breakdown of what’s included in each plan—and what makes each one special:

🔹 Self-Paced Digital Learning

From $29/month

Perfect for students who are ready to learn independently, at their own pace.

  • Full access to all episode-based lessons (A1–B2 levels)
  • MP3 audio + English subtitles
  • Grammar and vocabulary explanations
  • Practice quizzes with instant feedback
  • Downloadable PDFs and flashcards
Read More

🔸 Guided Learning + Teacher Feedback

From $79/month

A step up in support—great for students who need regular check-ins and writing/speaking feedback.

  • Everything in Tier 1, plus:
  • 1–2 speaking tasks per week with AI or teacher feedback
  • Writing assignments reviewed by real teachers
  • Monthly group speaking classes (live on Zoom or app)
  • Grammar correction reports & improvement tips
Read More

🟣 Premium Live Coaching & Projects

From $149/month

The full experience—for students who thrive with live interaction, personal coaching, and project-based learning.

  • Everything in Tiers 1 & 2, plus:
  • Weekly small-group coaching with native/ESL teachers
  • Monthly project presentations or role-plays
  • Individual learning progress reports
  • Personalized curriculum suggestions
  • Completion certificate & digital portfolio
Read More

💬 How to Choose the Right Tier?

If your child loves to learn independently, Tier 1 may be enough.

If they need structure and encouragement, Tier 2 is the best starting point.

And if they’re ready to truly live the English language, Tier 3 offers the full journey.
