Camp C

Enter →

Camp C serves adult learners and parents who want practical English in their daily lives. The Everyday English track builds conversational confidence episode by episode, while the Mom Teacher program empowers parents to bring structured English learning into their own homes.

🏠
Everyday English

Practical, real-life English for adult learners — phone calls, shopping, travel, parenting conversations. No textbook abstractions, just English you actually use.

👩‍🏫
Mom Teacher Program

A dedicated curriculum for parents who want to teach English at home. Grade 3 through Grade 6 editions provide structured lesson plans parents can deliver with confidence.

🎮
Gamified Learning

XP points, mascot interactions, word games and confetti rewards keep adult learners motivated — proving that playful design works at any age.

🤖
AI Conversation Partner

Learners practice real dialogue with an AI tutor — repeating phrases, correcting mistakes in real time, and gaining confidence before real-world conversations.
