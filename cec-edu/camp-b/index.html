CAMP B · MIDDLE & HIGH SCHOOL

Camp B

Enter →

Camp B is built for students who are ready to think, argue, and write in English. Through weekly essay workshops and structured debate, students develop the critical thinking and expression skills that matter most in secondary school and beyond.

📝
Essay Writing Workshop

Students receive a prompt, write a structured essay, and get line-by-line AI feedback — covering grammar, logic, and expression at their exact grade level.

⚔️
Debate Training

Middle School Year 2 and above engage in structured debate. Year 2 argues pros & cons; Year 3 runs full three-round debates with opening, rebuttal, and closing.

💬
Discussion & Wrap-Up

Every session closes with a guided discussion and a link to ChatGPT for open-ended conversation practice — extending learning beyond the lesson.

📊
Grade-Differentiated Feedback

AI feedback is calibrated per grade. Middle school gets accessible corrections; high school receives college-level editorial critique.
