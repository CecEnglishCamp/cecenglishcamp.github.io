1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

NOUNCountable vs Uncountable Nouns

가산명사 (Countable): **셀 수 있는** 명사. 개별적인 형태로 존재.
**불가산명사 (Uncountable):** **셀 수 없는** 명사. 액체, 물질, 개념 등 집합적인 형태.
가산명사는 복수형 가능 (**-s** 부착) / 불가산명사는 **단수형**만 사용

I have **two apples**. (**가산**)

I need **some water**. (**불가산**)

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

  • **가산명사 단수:** **a/an** + 명사 (a book, an apple)
  • **가산명사 복수:** 숫자 + 명사**-s** (two books, many chairs)
  • **불가산명사:** **a/an 사용 불가** (❌ an advice)
  • **불가산명사에는 복수형 없음** (❌ informations)
  • **불가산 수량 표현:** **much**, **a little**, **some** 사용 (much rice, a little milk)
  • **가산 수량 표현:** **many**, **a few**, **some** 사용 (many books, a few apples)
  • **some, a lot of:** 가산/불가산 명사 **모두** 사용 가능
  • **의문문/부정문:** **any** 사용 (Do you have **any** rice?)

3. 긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

  • I have **a book**. (가산)
  • She has **two pens**. (가산)
  • We need **some chairs**. (가산 - 복수형)
  • I need **some water**. (불가산)
  • He wants **a little rice**. (불가산)
  • They have **much information**. (불가산)
  • She gave me **some advice**. (불가산)
  • We need **a few apples**. (가산 - 복수형)
  • I drank **a lot of milk**. (불가산)
  • He bought **three notebooks**. (가산 - 복수형)

4. 의문문 예시 (Question Examples)

  • **Do** you have **a pen**? (가산 단수)
  • **Do** they need **some chairs**? (가산 복수)
  • **Is** there **any water**? (불가산)
  • **Do** you want **some rice**? (불가산)
  • **How many** books **do** you have? (가산 수량)
  • **How much** milk **do** you drink? (불가산 수량)
  • **Do** you have **any advice**? (불가산)
  • **Are** there **any apples**? (가산 복수)
  • **Is** there **any sugar**? (불가산)
  • **Do** you need **a notebook**? (가산 단수)

5. 상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (가산)
A: **Do** you have **a pen**?
B: Yes, I have **two pens**.
A: Great!

여행 상황 (불가산)
A: **Is** there **any water** in the bottle?
B: Yes, there’s **a little water** left.
A: Thanks!

직장 상황 (가산)
A: **Do** we have **any chairs** for the meeting?
B: Yes, we have **some chairs** in the next room.
A: Perfect.

온라인 상황 (불가산)
A: **Do** you need **any information**?
B: Yes, I need **some advice** about the project.
A: I’ll send it.

가족 상황 (가산)
A: **Do** we have **any apples**?
B: Yes, we have **a few apples** in the fridge.
A: Let’s eat!

병원 상황 (불가산)
A: **Do** you need **some water**?
B: Yes, please. Just **a little**.
A: Here you go.

6. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

  • I have book. → I have **a book**. (가산 단수)
  • She needs water. → She needs **some water**. (불가산)
  • We bought apples. → We bought **a few apples**. (가산 복수)
  • He wants rice. → He wants **a little rice**. (불가산)
  • They have chairs. → They have **many chairs**. (가산 복수)
  • I need information. → I need **some information**. (불가산)
  • She gave me advice. → She gave me **some advice**. (불가산)
  • We drank milk. → We drank **a lot of milk**. (불가산)
  • You have pens. → You have **several pens**. (가산 복수)
  • He brought sugar. → He brought **some sugar**. (불가산)

7. 실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

  • **학교 상황:** I need **a notebook** and **some paper**. (가산+불가산)
  • **직장 상황:** We received **some feedback** and **many emails**. (불가산+가산)
  • **가족 상황:** She bought **a few apples** and **some milk**. (가산+불가산)
  • **온라인 상황:** I sent **some information** and **two files**. (불가산+가산)
  • **병원 상황:** He needs **some water** and **a blanket**. (불가산+가산)
  • **여행 상황:** We packed **a lot of clothes** and **some snacks**. (가산+가산)
  • **회의 상황:** They shared **some advice** and **several ideas**. (불가산+가산)

8. 자주 쓰이는 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • **a/an + 단수 가산명사:** a book, an apple
  • **숫자 + 복수 가산명사:** two pens, three chairs
  • **some + 불가산명사:** **some water**, **some advice**
  • **much/little + 불가산명사:** **much rice**, **a little sugar**
  • **many/few + 복수 가산명사:** **many books**, **a few apples**
  • **a lot of + 가산/불가산 모두 가능:** a lot of milk, a lot of students
  • **any + 의문문/부정문:** Do you have **any** sugar? / I don’t have **any** pens.
  • **불가산명사에는 복수형 없음:** ❌ informations → ✅ **information**
  • **불가산명사에는 a/an 사용 불가:** ❌ an advice → ✅ **some advice**

9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ I have books. (처음 언급) → ✅ I have **a book**. (단수 가산명사에는 a/an을 붙여야 함)
  • ❌ She needs an information. → ✅ She needs **some information**. (불가산명사 앞에 a/an 사용 불가)
  • ❌ We bought many rice. → ✅ We bought **much rice**. (rice는 불가산이므로 much 사용)
  • ❌ He gave me advices. → ✅ He gave me **some advice**. (advice는 불가산이므로 복수형 없음)
  • ❌ I drank waters. → ✅ I drank **some water**. (water는 불가산이므로 복수형 없음)

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • **수량 표현 구분:**
    • **가산:** **many** (많은), **few/a few** (적은/약간의)
    • **불가산:** **much** (많은), **little/a little** (적은/약간의)
  • **불가산명사 암기:** `Advice, Information, Furniture, Luggage, Money, News, Bread, Rice` 등 자주 틀리는 불가산 명사를 따로 암기하세요.
  • **측정 단위:** 불가산 명사를 셀 때는 단위를 사용합니다. (예: **a cup of** coffee, **a piece of** advice, **two slices of** bread)
  • **some/any의 사용:** 긍정문에서는 주로 **some**, 의문문/부정문에서는 주로 **any**를 사용합니다. (단, 권유나 요청의 의문문에서는 **some** 사용 가능)
  • **a lot of/lots of:** 일상 대화에서 가산/불가산을 크게 신경 쓰지 않고 **'많은'** 이라는 의미로 편하게 사용하기 좋습니다.

11. 연습문제 (Practice Exercises)

객관식

  1. Which is correct?
    A. I need an information. B. I need **some information**.
    A. I need an information.
    B. I need **some information**.
  2. Choose the correct noun type:
    Milk is a(n) ___ noun.
    A. countable
    B. **uncountable**
  3. Fill in: She has ___ apples.
    A. much
    B. **a few**

1. B. **Information**은 불가산명사입니다. (a/an 사용 불가)

2. B. **Milk**는 액체이므로 셀 수 없는 **uncountable** 명사입니다.

3. B. **Apples**는 가산명사의 복수형이므로 **a few**를 사용합니다. (much는 불가산에 사용)

문장 결합 및 오류 수정 (정답 예시 제공)

  1. I have book. → I have **a book**.
  2. She needs water. → She needs **some water**.
  3. ❌ We bought many rice. → ✅ We bought **much rice**.
  4. ❌ He gave me advices. → ✅ He gave me **some advice**.
  5. ❌ I have books. (처음 언급) → ✅ I have **a book**.
