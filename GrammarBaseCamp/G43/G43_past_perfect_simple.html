1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

PPPAST PERFECT

과거의 두 사건 중 먼저 일어난 일을 표현
주로 과거시제와 함께 사용되어 사건 순서를 명확히 함

사건 순서: I had finished dinner before he arrived.

배경 설명: She had studied English before moving.

원인 설명: I was tired because I had worked all day.

기본 구조

had + 과거분사 (p.p)

예시: had gone / had seen / had finished

시간 순서 이해하기

과거완료 (Earlier)

I had finished dinner

→
과거시제 (Later)

He arrived

"He arrived after I had finished dinner."

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

문법 구조

  • had + p.p → had gone / had seen / had finished
  • 주로 과거시제와 함께 사용됨 → 과거 사건 + 과거완료 사건
  • before / after / when / by the time 등의 시간 표현과 자주 사용됨
  • 과거완료는 단독 사용 가능하지만, 대부분 비교 시점이 함께 나옴
  • 과거완료는 배경 설명, 이유, 순서를 명확히 할 때 유용함

시간 표현

BEFORE

  • before he arrived
  • before dinner
  • before class
  • before the meeting

AFTER

  • after I had finished
  • after she had left
  • after we had eaten
  • after they had gone

WHEN

  • when I called
  • when he arrived
  • when she came
  • when they returned

BY THE TIME

  • by the time I arrived
  • by the time he came
  • by the time she called
  • by the time we left

사용 목적

  • 사건 순서: I had packed before the taxi came.
  • 배경 설명: They had studied English before moving to Canada.
  • 원인 설명: I was tired because I had worked all day.
  • 경험 표현: I had never tried sushi before I visited Japan.

4. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (School Situation)
A: Perfect. Let's start.
B: Ready!
가족 상황 (Family Situation)
A: Did you clean your room?
B: Yes, I had cleaned it before Mom came home.
A: Good timing.
B: I didn't want her to be upset.
병원 상황 (Hospital Situation)
A: Had you taken the medicine before breakfast?
B: Yes, I had taken it at 7.
A: That's correct.
B: I feel better now.

5. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining Practice)

문장 변환 연습

→ He arrived after I had finished dinner.
She left. I called her.
→ I called her after she had left.

→ They watched it after we had seen it.

→ He had gone home by the time I got there.
They studied English. Then they moved.
→ They had studied English before they moved.

→ I had packed my bag before the taxi came.
She cooked dinner. Then guests arrived.
→ She had cooked dinner before the guests arrived.

→ We had cleaned the room before the teacher came in.

→ He had written the email before the meeting started.

→ I had never tried sushi before I visited Japan.

7. 자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴 (Common Patterns)

표현 패턴

had + p.p had gone / had seen / had finished
before + 과거시제 I had finished before he arrived
after + 과거완료 He arrived after I had finished
when + 과거시제 I had left when he called
by the time + 과거시제 She had left by the time I arrived
already + 과거완료 I had already eaten when they arrived
never + 과거완료 I had never tried sushi before
just + 과거완료 He had just finished when the phone rang
had + p.p + before/after + 과거시제 Full structure pattern
순서, 배경, 이유 Past Perfect explains background and sequence

10. 자가진단 (Self-Check)

학습 완료 체크

레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • had + 과거분사 형태를 정확히 기억
  • 과거완료는 두 과거 사건 중 먼저 일어난 일을 표현
  • before / after / when / by the time 등의 시간 표현과 함께 사용
  • 과거완료는 배경 설명, 이유, 순서를 명확히 할 때 유용
  • 과거시제와 함께 쓰면 시간 흐름이 자연스러움
  • "I had already…" → 이미 완료된 상태 강조
  • "I had never…" → 경험 부정 강조
  • 과거완료는 단독 사용 가능하지만 비교 시점이 있으면 더 명확함
  • 동사 형태는 반드시 과거분사여야 함
