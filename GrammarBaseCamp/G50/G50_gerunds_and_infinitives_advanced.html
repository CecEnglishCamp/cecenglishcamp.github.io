1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

G&I동명사와 to부정사

Gerund (-ing): 동사에 -ing를 붙여 명사처럼 쓰는 형태
Infinitive (to + verb): to + 동사원형으로 목적, 의도, 미래 행동을 표현
두 형태 모두 동사의 목적어로 쓰일 수 있으나, 의미가 달라질 수 있음

I stopped smoking. (담배를 끊었다)

I stopped to smoke. (담배 피우려고 멈췄다)

I remember locking the door. (잠근 기억이 있다)

I remembered to lock the door. (잠가야 한다는 걸 기억했다)

동명사 vs to부정사 의미 비교

Gerund (-ing)

과거 경험, 습관, 일반 행동 강조

I remember locking the door.
(잠근 기억이 있다)

I tried opening the window.
(방법을 시도했다)

Infinitive (to + verb)

미래 행동, 목적, 의도 강조

I remembered to lock the door.
(잠가야 한다는 걸 기억했다)

I tried to open the window.
(열려고 노력했다)

의미 차이가 나는 주요 동사들

Remember
doing (과거 기억) / to do (미래 기억)
Stop
doing (행동 중단) / to do (목적으로 멈춤)
Try
doing (방법 시도) / to do (노력)
Regret
doing (과거 후회) / to do (유감 표현)
Forget
doing (기억 없음) / to do (할 일 잊음)

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

기본 규칙

  • 동명사만 받는 동사: enjoy, avoid, finish, mind, suggest, deny, consider
  • to부정사만 받는 동사: decide, want, hope, plan, agree, refuse, learn
  • 둘 다 가능하지만 의미가 달라지는 동사: remember, forget, stop, try, regret
  • 동사 + 목적어 + to부정사: I want him to help / She told me to wait
  • 동사 + 목적어 + 동명사: I caught him stealing

사용 원칙

  • to부정사: 미래 행동, 목적, 의도 강조
  • 동명사: 과거 경험, 습관, 일반 행동 강조
  • 의미 차이 동사는 반드시 예문과 함께 익히기
  • "stop / remember / try / regret / forget" → 의미 변화 주의

3. 예시 문장 (Example Sentences)

동명사만 받는 동사

  • I enjoy reading books.
  • She avoids talking about it.
  • We finished cleaning the room.
  • He denied stealing the money.
  • They suggested going together.
  • Do you mind waiting a bit?
  • I consider buying a new car.
  • She keeps forgetting his name.
  • We practiced speaking English.
  • He quit smoking last year.

to부정사만 받는 동사

  • I want to learn Spanish.
  • She decided to stay home.
  • We hope to visit Korea.
  • He refused to help.
  • They agreed to join us.
  • I plan to travel next month.
  • She offered to drive me home.
  • We promised to call you.
  • He managed to fix the problem.
  • They chose to leave early.

의미 차이 중심 동사

  • I stopped smoking. (끊음) / I stopped to smoke. (피우려고 멈춤)
  • I remember locking the door. (잠근 기억) / I remembered to lock the door. (잠가야 한다는 걸 기억)
  • I tried opening the window. (방법 시도) / I tried to open the window. (노력)
  • I regret telling her. (말한 걸 후회) / I regret to tell you this. (말해야 해서 유감)
  • I forgot meeting him. (만난 기억 없음) / I forgot to meet him. (만나는 걸 잊음)
  • He went on talking. (계속 말함) / He went on to become famous. (다음에 유명해짐)
  • I hate being late. (지각하는 것 싫음) / I hate to be late today. (오늘 지각해서 미안)
  • She likes swimming. (수영 좋아함) / She'd like to swim. (수영하고 싶음)

동명사/to부정사 의문문

5. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황
A: Did you remember to submit the homework?
B: Yes, I remembered to send it last night.
A: Good. I forgot sending mine.
B: You mean you forgot to send it?
A: No, I sent it but forgot doing it!
여행 상황
A: I stopped smoking last year.
B: Really? I stopped to smoke during the trip.
A: You mean you started again?
B: Just once!
직장 상황
A: Did you try fixing the printer?
B: Yes, I tried to fix it but failed.
A: Did you try turning it off and on?
B: That worked!
온라인 상황
A: I regret telling him my password.
B: You should never do that.
A: I regret to inform you it was leaked.
B: Oh no!
가족 상황
A: I forgot meeting Aunt Mina.
B: You met her last week!
A: I forgot to call her back.
B: Let's do it now.
병원 상황
A: Did you remember to take your medicine?
B: I remember taking it this morning.
A: Good. Don't forget tomorrow.
B: I won't.

6. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

상황 + 동명사/to부정사 표현 결합

I locked the door. I remember it.
→ I remember locking the door.
I had to lock the door. I remembered it.
→ I remembered to lock the door.
I smoked. I stopped.
→ I stopped smoking.
I paused. I smoked.
→ I stopped to smoke.
I opened the window. I tried it.
→ I tried opening the window.
I wanted to open the window. I tried.
→ I tried to open the window.
I told her. I regret it.
→ I regret telling her.
I must tell you. I regret it.
→ I regret to tell you this.
I met him. I forgot.
→ I forgot meeting him.
I had to meet him. I forgot.
→ I forgot to meet him.

7. 실생활 사용 (Real-Life Usage)

이메일 안내

I remembered to attach the file. Please check it. I also remember sending it yesterday.

자기소개

I enjoy teaching English. I hope to help more students this year.

업무 회의

We decided to postpone the launch. I regret informing you this late.

여행 후기

I tried opening the window, but it was stuck. Then I tried to call the staff.

건강 상담

I stopped drinking coffee last month. I forgot to take my medicine today.

8. 자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴 (Common Patterns)

패턴별 정리

동명사만 받는 동사 enjoy reading / avoid talking / finish cleaning
to부정사만 받는 동사 want to go / decide to stay / hope to help
remember + -ing 과거 기억 (remember doing)
remember + to 해야 할 일 기억 (remember to do)
stop + -ing 행동 중단 (stop doing)
stop + to 행동 위해 멈춤 (stop to do)
try + -ing 방법 시도 (try doing)
try + to 노력 (try to do)
regret + -ing 과거 후회 (regret doing)
regret + to 유감 표현 (regret to do)
forget + -ing 기억 없음 (forget doing)
forget + to 할 일 잊음 (forget to do)

9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

11. 자가진단 체크리스트 (Self-Check)

학습 완료 체크

레벨업 팁

  • 동명사 (-ing): 과거 경험, 일반 행동, 습관
  • to부정사: 목적, 의도, 미래 행동
  • 의미 차이 동사는 반드시 예문과 함께 익히기
  • "stop / remember / try / regret / forget" → 의미 변화 주의
  • 동사별로 어떤 형태를 받는지 정리해두면 좋음
  • "동사 + 목적어 + to부정사" 구조는 요청/지시 표현에 자주 사용
  • "동사 + 목적어 + 동명사"는 목격/행동 포착 표현에 자주 사용
  • 의미 차이를 문맥 속에서 판단하는 연습 필요
  • 실생활 대화와 이메일, 보고서 등에서 자주 등장

다음 단계 학습 가이드

  • Review verb lists that take gerunds, infinitives, or both.
  • Practice meaning-shifting verbs in writing and conversation.
  • Use gerunds and infinitives to express habits, goals, and regrets.
  • Compare with modal verbs and causative structures for fluency.
  • Record and review your own sentences for clarity and accuracy.
