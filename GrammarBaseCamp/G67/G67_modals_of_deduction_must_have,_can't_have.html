1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

MODALModals of Deduction

추측 조동사(Modals of Deduction): 과거 상황에 대한 확신 또는 부정 추측을 표현하는 구조
must have + p.p. → 분명 ~했을 거야 (강한 확신)
can't have + p.p. → 절대 ~하지 않았을 거야 (강한 부정)
논리적 단서나 정황을 바탕으로 과거를 추론함
must have + 과거분사
강한 확신 (90% 이상)
can't have + 과거분사
강한 부정 (거의 0%)

확신: He must have left early. (불이 꺼져 있어)

부정: She can't have forgotten. (메모를 했으니까)

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

  • 기본 구조: 주어 + must/can't + have + 과거분사(p.p.)
  • must have + p.p. → 과거에 분명히 일어난 일에 대한 확신
  • can't have + p.p. → 과거에 일어나지 않았을 것이라는 강한 부정
  • 시제는 항상 과거를 가리킴
  • 논리적 단서나 정황을 바탕으로 사용
  • 의문문에서는 거의 사용되지 않음 (주로 진술문)
  • 구어체와 글쓰기 모두에서 사용 가능

3. 긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

must have + p.p. (확신)

  • He must have left early. (불이 꺼져 있어)
  • She must have studied hard. (시험을 잘 봤어)
  • They must have arrived by now. (길이 막히지 않았어)
  • You must have been tired. (늦게까지 일했잖아)
  • We must have missed the bus. (정류장에 아무도 없어)
  • He must have forgotten his keys. (문 앞에 서 있어)
  • She must have worked overtime. (사무실 불이 켜져 있었어)
  • They must have enjoyed the movie. (웃으면서 나왔어)
  • You must have been hungry. (음식을 다 먹었네)
  • We must have taken the wrong turn. (여기가 어디지?)

can't have + p.p. (부정)

  • He can't have forgotten the meeting. (캘린더에 적혀 있어)
  • She can't have gone home. (가방이 여기 있어)
  • They can't have finished already. (너무 빨라)
  • You can't have seen him. (그는 해외에 있어)
  • We can't have made that mistake. (다시 확인했어)
  • He can't have left without saying goodbye. (그는 예의바른 사람이야)
  • She can't have known about this. (아무도 말하지 않았어)
  • They can't have arrived yet. (비행기가 지연됐어)
  • You can't have eaten all of that. (양이 너무 많아)
  • We can't have been wrong. (증거가 명확해)

여행 상황

B: They must have taken a taxi instead.
A: That makes perfect sense.

B: He must have missed it somehow.
A: I'll resend it with high priority.

A: She posted that at 3 a.m.
B: She must have stayed up all night.
A: That's dedication to her work.
가족 상황

B: He must have gone for his morning walk.
A: Let's wait for him to come back.
병원 상황
A: She's not in her hospital room.
B: She can't have left without permission.
A: Let's check with the nurse's station.
쇼핑 상황
A: The store is completely empty.
B: They must have had a big sale.
A: We missed all the good deals.
파티 상황
A: John isn't here yet.
B: He can't have forgotten about the party.
A: Maybe he's stuck in traffic.

    • 학교 상황: The homework is checked every morning.

    8. 자주 쓰이는 패턴 (Common Patterns)

    • 현재: is/are + p.p. → The book is read.
    • 과거: was/were + p.p. → The book was read.
    • 현재완료: has/have been + p.p. → The book has been read.
    • 과거완료: had been + p.p. → The book had been read.
    • 미래: will be + p.p. → The book will be read.
    • 진행형: is/was being + p.p. → The book is being read.
    • 조동사: can/must/should be + p.p. → The book must be read.
    • 의문문: Is the report finished?
    • 부정문: The task wasn't completed on time.
    • 행위자 표시: The letter was written by John.
    • 현재진행 수동: The problem is being solved.
    • 과거진행 수동: The room was being cleaned.
    • 미래완료 수동: The work will have been done.
    • 조건문 수동: If it is completed, we can proceed.
    • 명령문 수동: Let the door be opened.

    9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

    ❌ The room cleaned → ✅ The room was cleaned
    ❌ The report is write → ✅ The report is written
    ❌ The homework has been check → ✅ The homework has been checked
    ❌ The email will sent → ✅ The email will be sent
    ❌ The task should complete → ✅ The task should be completed
    ❌ The book is reading → ✅ The book is being read
    ❌ The work will finished → ✅ The work will be finished
    ❌ The letter was wrote → ✅ The letter was written

    10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

    • 수동태는 be + p.p. 구조가 핵심
    • 시제에 따라 be동사 형태가 달라짐
    • 행위자(by + 사람)는 생략 가능, 강조할 때만 사용
    • 진행형 수동태는 is/was being + p.p.
    • 완료형 수동태는 has/have/had been + p.p.
    • 조동사 수동태는 can/must/should be + p.p.
    • 문장 결합 연습을 통해 시제별 수동태에 익숙해지기
    • 과거분사 형태 정확히 기억하기 (regular vs irregular verbs)
    • 수동태는 객관적이고 정중한 표현에 유용
    • 공식 문서나 학술 글에서 자주 사용됨
    • 행위자를 모르거나 결과에 집중할 때 효과적
    • 비즈니스 영어에서 책임을 돌리지 않으려 할 때 사용
    • 뉴스나 보고서에서 객관성을 유지하려 할 때 활용
    • 수동태로 문장의 주어를 바꿔 강조점 변경 가능
    • 과정이나 절차를 설명할 때 수동태가 자연스러움

    11. 연습문제 (Practice Exercises)

    객관식 문제

    1. Which is correct?
    A. The report was write.
    B. The report was written.
    정답: B. "The report was written." - 수동태에서는 과거분사(written)를 사용해야 합니다.
    2. Choose the correct form: The homework ___ every day.
    A. is checked
    B. checks
    정답: A. "is checked" - 수동태 구조 "be + 과거분사"를 사용합니다.
    3. Fill in: The email ___ before noon.
    A. had been sent
    B. has sent
    정답: A. "had been sent" - 과거완료 수동태로 "had been + 과거분사"를 사용합니다.
    4. Which sentence shows correct passive voice?
    A. The room is cleaning now.
    B. The room is being cleaned now.
    정답: B. "The room is being cleaned now." - 현재진행 수동태는 "is/are being + 과거분사"입니다.
    5. Complete the sentence: The task ___ by tomorrow.
    A. will complete
    B. will be completed
    정답: B. "will be completed" - 미래 수동태는 "will be + 과거분사"입니다.
