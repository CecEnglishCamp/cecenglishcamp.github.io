1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

PAST-INGPast Continuous Tense

**과거 진행형:** 과거의 **특정 시점**에 **진행 중**이었던 동작을 나타냅니다.
**형태:** **was/were + 동사원형-ing**
**용도:** 1) 과거의 한 시점에 진행 중이던 일. 2) 한 동작이 진행 중일 때 다른 동작이 끼어든 상황. 3) 과거의 두 동작이 동시에 진행된 상황.

**I was reading** a book at 8 PM. (나는 8시에 책을 읽고 있었다.)

**She was cooking** dinner **when** I arrived. (내가 도착했을 때 그녀는 저녁을 요리하고 있었다.)

**They were playing** soccer all afternoon. (그들은 오후 내내 축구를 하고 있었다.)

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

  • **기본 구조:** **주어 + was/were + V-ing**
  • **Was 사용:** 주어가 **I, He, She, It** (단수)일 때.
    (예: **He was studying**.)
  • **Were 사용:** 주어가 **You, We, They** (복수)일 때.
    (예: **We were watching**.)
  • **부정문:** **was/were + not + V-ing** (축약: wasn't, weren't)
    (예: I **wasn't sleeping**.)
  • **의문문:** **Was/Were + 주어 + V-ing?**
    (예: **Were you waiting**?)
  • **When / While:** 과거 진행형은 종종 **When** (단순 과거 시점과 연결) 또는 **While** (동시에 진행된 동작과 연결)과 함께 사용됩니다.
  • **동사 -ing 형태 주의:** 동사 끝이 -e로 끝나면 e를 빼고 -ing (live $\rightarrow$ living), 단모음+단자음으로 끝나면 마지막 자음을 하나 더 쓰고 -ing (run $\rightarrow$ running)

3. 긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

  • I **was reading** a book at 8 PM.
  • She **was cooking** dinner when I arrived.
  • They **were playing** soccer all afternoon.
  • We **were studying** English last night.
  • He **was watching** TV when you called.

  • It **was raining** heavily that morning.
  • The students **were writing** in their notebooks.
  • My parents **were talking** while I was listening.
  • The dog **was sleeping** on the sofa.

5. 상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

과거 시점 묘사
A: What **were** you **doing** at 7 PM?
B: I **was eating** dinner.
A: Me too!

끼어든 동작
A: What **were** you **doing** when the phone rang?
B: I **was taking** a shower.
A: Oh, I see.

동시에 진행
A: **Was** he **working** while she **was sleeping**?
B: **Yes, he was.** They **were both** busy.
A: That's rough.

부정문 활용
A: **Were** you **listening** to music?
B: **No, I wasn't.** I **was studying** quietly.
A: My bad.

여행 상황
A: **Were** they **driving** when the storm started?
B: **Yes, they were.** They **were driving** home.
A: They must be scared.

  • They played soccer. (It was afternoon.) $\rightarrow$ They **were playing** soccer all afternoon.

  • The baby cried. (All morning.) $\rightarrow$ The baby **was crying** all morning.
  • I worked. (While you slept.) $\rightarrow$ I **was working** while you were sleeping.
  • She talked on the phone. (I came in.) $\rightarrow$ She **was talking** on the phone when I came in.

7. 실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

(과거 진행형은 지난 일이나 사건을 이야기할 때 당시의 생생한 상황을 묘사하는 데 필수적입니다.)

  • **사건 설명:** I **was driving** home when I saw the accident. (집으로 운전하고 있는데 사고를 봤어요.)
  • **특정 시점:** At 10 PM last night, we **were watching** a movie. (어젯밤 10시에 우리는 영화를 보고 있었어요.)
  • **배경 묘사:** The sun **was shining** and the birds **were singing** when we arrived. (우리가 도착했을 때 해가 빛나고 새들이 노래하고 있었어요.)
  • **동시 동작:** While I **was cleaning**, my brother **was studying**. (내가 청소하는 동안, 동생은 공부하고 있었어요.)
  • **계속된 불만:** He **was always complaining** about the food. (그는 항상 음식에 대해 불평했었지.)

8. 자주 쓰이는 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • **Was/Were + V-ing + Time:** I **was working** at 3 o'clock. (과거 특정 시점)
  • **V-ing + When + Simple Past:** We **were sleeping** **when** the fire alarm rang. (진행 중인 동작에 단발적인 동작이 끼어듦)
  • **While + V-ing + V-ing:** **While** I **was cooking**, he **was reading**. (두 동작의 동시 진행)
  • **Time Phrases:** yesterday, last week, all day, at that moment 등 과거 시점을 나타내는 표현과 함께 자주 쓰입니다.
  • **질문/부정 패턴:** **Wasn't** he **waiting** for you? / No, he **wasn't talking**.

9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ I was read a book. (동사원형 사용) → ✅ I **was reading** a book. (V-ing 형태 사용)
  • ❌ They were play soccer. (V-ing 형태 누락) → ✅ They **were playing** soccer. (V-ing 형태 사용)
  • ❌ Was you watching TV? (Be동사 was/were 오류) → ✅ **Were you watching** TV? (주어 you에는 **were** 사용)
  • ❌ She wasn’t cook dinner. (V-ing 형태 누락) → ✅ She **wasn't cooking** dinner. (V-ing 형태 사용)
  • ❌ He was study when I arrived. (V-ing 형태 누락) → ✅ He **was studying** when I arrived. (V-ing 형태 사용)

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • **Simple Past (단순 과거)와 비교:** **I read a book** (책을 읽었다 - 완료된 동작) vs. **I was reading a book** (책을 읽고 있었다 - 진행 중인 동작)의 뉘앙스 차이를 이해하고 활용하세요.
  • **감정/인지 동사 제외:** **like, know, want, believe**와 같은 상태나 인지 동사는 진행형으로 잘 쓰이지 않습니다. (❌ I was knowing $\rightarrow$ ✅ I knew)
  • **When vs. While:**
    - **When**은 보통 **단순 과거**와 함께 쓰여 진행 중인 동작을 **방해**합니다. (I was sleeping **when** the phone rang.)
    - **While**은 보통 **진행형**과 함께 쓰여 **동시 동작**을 나타냅니다. (**While** I was driving, I was listening to music.)
  • **스토리텔링 활용:** 과거의 경험을 이야기할 때 배경 상황(**was/were + -ing**)을 먼저 설명하고, 중요한 사건(**Simple Past**)을 사용하여 이야기를 더 풍부하게 만드세요.

11. 연습문제 (Practice Exercises)

객관식

  1. Which is correct?
    A. I was read a book.
    B. **I was reading a book.**
  2. Choose the correct form:
    They ___ soccer yesterday afternoon.
    A. **were playing**
    B. was playing
  3. Fill in: She ___ dinner when I arrived.
    A. **was cooking**
    B. were cook

1. B. 과거 진행형은 **was/were + V-ing** 형태입니다.

2. A. 주어 **They**는 복수이므로 **were**를 사용하고, 진행형이므로 **-ing**를 붙입니다.

3. A. 주어 **She**는 단수이므로 **was**를 사용하고, 진행형이므로 **-ing**를 붙입니다.

오류 수정 및 번역 (정답 예시 제공)

  1. ❌ I was read a book. $\rightarrow$ ✅ **I was reading a book.** (V-ing 형태 사용)
  2. ❌ They were play soccer. $\rightarrow$ ✅ **They were playing soccer.** (V-ing 형태 사용)
  3. Translate: 나는 8시에 책을 읽고 있었어. $\rightarrow$ ✅ **I was reading a book at 8 PM.**
  4. Translate: 그녀는 내가 도착했을 때 저녁을 만들고 있었어. $\rightarrow$ ✅ **She was cooking dinner when I arrived.**
  5. Translate: 너는 그때 TV를 보고 있었니? $\rightarrow$ ✅ **Were you watching TV then?**
