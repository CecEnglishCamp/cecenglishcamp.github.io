1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

HEDGEHedging Language (완곡어법)

Hedging: 확신을 줄이고, 말투를 부드럽게 하며, 일반화나 추측을 표현하는 **전략적 언어**
직접 단정하지 않고, **가능성이나 경향**을 표현함
학술적 글쓰기, 토론, 시험 응답, 일상 대화에서 유용함

단정: He is late.

완화: He **tends to be** late. / He **seems** to be late.

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

  • seem / appear + to + 동사원형: It **seems to work**. / She **appears to know**.
  • tend to + 동사원형: He **tends to forget** things. (경향)
  • may / might + 동사원형: It **may help**. / She **might come**. (가능성/추측)
  • 빈도 부사 (일반화): **often / usually / generally** → They **often arrive** late.
  • Hedging 표현은 문장 전체를 부드럽게 만들며, **논리적/객관적 느낌**을 줌

3. 긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

seems / appears (관찰 기반 추측)

  • She **seems to understand** the topic.
  • It **appears to be working**.
  • He **seems tired** today.
  • The results **appear unclear**.
  • This method **seems effective**.

tend to (일반적인 경향)

  • He **tends to be late**.
  • They **tend to forget** instructions.
  • Students **tend to struggle** with grammar.
  • We **tend to rely** on examples.
  • She **tends to speak** quickly.

may / might / often (가능성 및 빈도)

  • It **may help** to review again.
  • She **might join** later.
  • They **often miss** the deadline.
  • We **usually check** twice.
  • He **generally agrees** with the plan.

6. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

  • He is late. → He **tends to be** late.
  • She knows the answer. → She **seems to know** the answer.
  • It works well. → It **appears to work** well.
  • They forget instructions. → They **tend to forget** instructions.
  • She will come. → She **might come**.
  • He is tired. → He **seems tired**.
  • We rely on examples. → We **tend to rely** on examples.
  • She agrees. → She **generally agrees**.
  • He is focused. → He **appears focused**.
  • They miss deadlines. → They **often miss** deadlines.

7. 실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

  • 학교 상황: Students tend to struggle with grammar.
  • 직장 상황: She might join the meeting later.
  • 가족 상황: Dad often stays late on Fridays.
  • 온라인 상황: The app appears to be down right now.
  • 병원 상황: She seems to need more rest.
  • 여행 상황: Trains tend to be delayed on weekends.
  • 회의 상황: He generally agrees with the proposal.

8. 자주 쓰이는 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • seem(s) to + 동사원형: She **seems to understand**.
  • appear(s) to + 동사원형: It **appears to work**.
  • tend(s) to + 동사원형: He **tends to forget**.
  • may / might + 동사원형: She **might come**.
  • 빈도 부사 + 동사: They **often miss** deadlines.
  • generally / usually + 동사: We **generally agree**.
  • seem + 형용사: He **seems tired**.
  • appear + 형용사: She **appears confident**.
  • might / may + be + 형용사: He **might be busy**.
  • tend to + be + 형용사: They **tend to be late**.

9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ He seems like tired. → ✅ He **seems tired**. (Like 불필요)
  • ❌ She tend to forget. → ✅ She **tends to forget**. (주어-동사 수 일치)
  • ❌ It appears working. → ✅ It **appears to be working**. (Appear 뒤 to be + V-ing)
  • ❌ He might comes. → ✅ He **might come**. (조동사 뒤 동사원형)
  • ❌ They often to be late. → ✅ They **often are** late / **tend to be** late. (구조 오류)

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • Hedging은 확신을 줄이고 부드럽게 표현할 때 사용
  • seems / appears는 외부 관찰 기반 표현
  • tend to는 일반적인 경향을 나타냄
  • may / might는 가능성을 표현하며 정중함을 더함
  • often / usually / generally는 빈도와 일반화를 표현
  • 문장 흐름을 부드럽게 만들고, 논리적이고 객관적인 느낌을 줌
  • 문장 결합 연습을 통해 자연스러운 완화 표현 향상

11. 연습문제 (Practice Exercises)

객관식

  1. Which is correct?
    A. She tend to forget.
    B. She tends to forget.
  2. Choose the correct form:
    He ___ to be late.
    A. tend
    B. tends
  3. Fill in: It ___ to be working.
    A. appears
    B. appear

1. B. 주어가 3인칭 단수(She)이므로 동사도 3인칭 단수형('tends')을 사용해야 합니다.

2. B. 주어가 3인칭 단수(He)이므로 동사 'tend'에 -s를 붙여 'tends'를 사용합니다.

3. A. 주어가 3인칭 단수(It)이므로 동사 'appear'에 -s를 붙여 'appears'를 사용합니다. ('to be working'은 현재 진행형을 완화하는 형태입니다.)
