기본 개념
⭐ TODAY'S KEY GRAMMAR
Future Tense: Will & Going To
미래 시제 — will과 be going to
Future Tense – will / going to

미래 시제: 앞으로 일어날 일, 계획, 예측, 약속 등을 표현
will: 즉흥적인 결정, 예측, 약속, 의지
be going to: 이미 계획된 일, 확실한 미래 행동, 현재의 증거를 기반으로 한 예측

I will call you later. (즉흥적 결정/약속)

She is going to visit her grandma. (계획된 일)

Will they come tomorrow? (단순한 질문/예측)

2
문법 규칙
  • will 형태: 주어 + will + 동사원형 (모든 주어 동일)
  • be going to 형태: 주어 + be동사(am/is/are) + going to + 동사원형 (be동사는 주어에 따라 변화)
  • 긍정문: 주어 + will / be going to + 동사원형
  • 부정문 (will): 주어 + will not (won't) + 동사원형
  • 부정문 (going to): 주어 + be not going to + 동사원형
  • 의문문 (will): Will + 주어 + 동사원형?
  • 의문문 (going to): Be + 주어 + going to + 동사원형?
  • 축약형 (will): I’ll / You’ll / He’ll / She’ll / We’ll / They’ll
  • 시간 표현: tomorrow, next week, soon, later, in 2026 (미래 시점을 명확히 함)
3
긍정문 예시
  • I will call you later.
  • You will get a message soon.
  • He will help us tomorrow.
  • She will join the meeting.
  • We will travel next month.
  • They will arrive at 5 PM.
  • I am going to study tonight. (I + am)
  • She is going to visit her grandma. (She + is)
  • We are going to watch a movie. (We + are)
  • They are going to play soccer. (They + are)

실생활 사용 예시
  • 학교 상황 (계획): I am going to study for the test tonight. (이미 결정된 계획)
  • 직장 상황 (약속/예측): He will send the report tomorrow. (업무 약속)
  • 가족 상황 (계획): We are going to eat out this weekend. (주말 계획)
  • 온라인 상황 (즉흥적 결정): I think I will upload the file soon. (즉흥적인 결정)
  • 병원 상황 (정보): The doctor will arrive in 10 minutes. (정보 전달)
  • 여행 상황 (계획): They are going to visit Jeju Island next month. (확정된 여행 계획)
  • 회의 상황 (약속): I will present my idea next Friday. (발표 약속)
8
자주 쓰이는 패턴
  • will + 동사원형: I will go, She will help (즉흥, 약속, 예측)
  • be going to + 동사원형: We are going to eat (계획, 확실한 미래)
  • Will + 주어 + 동사원형? (의문): Will you call me?
  • Be + 주어 + going to + 동사원형? (의문): Are you going to study?
  • will not (won't) + 동사원형 (부정): He won't join the meeting.
  • be not going to + 동사원형 (부정): I am not going to eat dinner.
  • 축약형 (I’ll, won't, she’s going to): 구어체에서 필수적으로 사용됩니다.
  • I think I will...: 즉흥적인 결정을 나타낼 때 자주 사용됩니다.
9
흔한 실수
  • ❌ I going to study. → ✅ I am going to study. (be동사 누락)
  • ❌ She will goes to school. → ✅ She will go to school. (will 뒤에는 동사원형)
  • ❌ We are going watch a movie. → ✅ We are going to watch a movie. (going to 사이의 'to' 누락)
  • ❌ He will helps us. → ✅ He will help us. (will 뒤에는 동사원형, S를 붙이지 않음)
  • ❌ They going to play soccer. → ✅ They are going to play soccer. (be동사 누락)
10
레벨업 팁
  • will vs. going to 구분:
    • will: 의사결정의 순간 (예: "I'm hungry." "I will order pizza.")
    • going to: 미리 계획했을 때 (예: "I am going to study for the exam tomorrow.")
  • 현재 진행형의 미래: 가까운 미래의 확실한 계획은 현재 진행형으로도 표현 가능합니다. (예: I am meeting him tomorrow.)
  • won't 사용: 부정문은 `will not` 대신 won't를 사용하여 유창성을 높이세요. (예: He won't be late.)
  • I think / I don't think: 예측을 말할 때 이 표현들과 will을 함께 사용하면 자연스럽습니다. (예: I think it will rain.)
  • going to의 발음: 구어체에서는 gonna로 발음하는 경우가 많지만, 글쓰기나 공식적인 자리에서는 사용하지 않습니다.
11
연습문제
객관식
  1. Choose the correct negative sentence using 'will':
    He will join the party. (Negative)
    A. He is not join the party.
    B. He won't join the party.
    C. He will not to join the party.
  2. Choose the correct question form using 'going to':
    They are going to travel next month. (Question)
    A. Will they going to travel next month?
    B. Do they are going to travel next month?
    C. Are they going to travel next month?
  3. Fill in the blank (instant decision):
    The phone is ringing. I ___ answer it.
    A. will
    B. am going to
    C. am

1. B. will not의 축약형인 won't가 가장 자연스럽습니다. (will not join도 가능)

2. C. be going to의 의문문은 Be동사 + 주어 + going to + 동사원형 형태를 사용합니다.

3. A. 전화가 울리는 소리를 듣고 내린 즉흥적인 결정이므로 will을 사용합니다.
