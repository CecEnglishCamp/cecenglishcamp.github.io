Both, Either, Neither
둘 다 / 둘 중 하나 / 둘 다 아닌
기본 개념문법 규칙긍정문의문문대화 흐름문장 결합실생활패턴흔한 실수레벨업 팁연습문제자가진단
1
기본 개념BASIC CONCEPT
⭐ TODAY'S KEY GRAMMAR
Both, Either, Neither
두 가지에 대해 말할 때 사용하는 표현들

Both → 두 개 모두 (긍정). I like both books.

Either → 둘 중 하나 (긍정/부정). You can choose either option.

Neither → 둘 다 아님 (부정). I like neither movie.

  • Both students passed the test.두 학생 모두 시험에 합격했어요
  • Either answer is fine.둘 중 어떤 답이든 괜찮아요
  • Neither answer is correct.둘 다 정답이 아니에요
2
문법 규칙GRAMMAR RULES
BOTH · 둘 다
Both + 복수 명사 + 복수 동사
Both students are here.
EITHER · 둘 중 하나
Either + 단수 명사 + 단수 동사
Either answer is fine.
NEITHER · 둘 다 아닌
Neither + 단수 명사 + 단수 동사
Neither option is good.
접속사 패턴 · CONNECTORS
both...and / either...or / neither...nor
  • Both + and: I like both tea and coffee.
  • Either + or: You can choose either tea or coffee.
  • Neither + nor: I like neither tea nor coffee.
  • Both of / Either of / Neither of + the/my/these + 복수 명사
  • 동사 일치: Both → 복수 동사 / Either, Neither → 단수 동사
  • 대명사로도 사용: Both are correct. / Neither is true.

상황별 대화 흐름SITUATIONAL DIALOGUES
🏫 학교 상황 · SCHOOL
A: Do both students have their books?
B: Yes, both of them are prepared.
A: Can either student present today?
B: Yes, either one is ready.
✈️ 여행 상황 · TRAVEL
A: Are both hotels available?
B: No, neither is open this weekend.
A: So we can't choose either?
B: Unfortunately, no.
💼 직장 상황 · WORK
A: Did both teams submit their reports?
B: Yes, both did it on time.
A: Is either report missing data?
B: No, both are complete.
💻 온라인 상황 · ONLINE
A: Can I use either browser?
B: Yes, either works fine.
A: Are both browsers secure?
B: Definitely.
🏠 가족 상황 · FAMILY
A: Do both kids want pizza?
B: No, neither likes cheese.
A: So we'll choose something else.
B: Good idea.

→ I like both books.
You can choose option A. You can choose option B.
→ You can choose either option.
I don't like movie A. I don't like movie B.
→ I like neither movie.
Student A is ready. Student B is ready.
→ Both students are ready.
Shirt A doesn't fit. Shirt B doesn't fit.
→ Neither shirt fits me.
Team A won. Team B won.
→ Both teams won.
Time A works. Time B works.
→ Either time is fine.
Option A is bad. Option B is bad.
→ Neither option is good.
Answer A is correct. Answer B is correct.
→ Both answers are correct.

10
레벨업 팁LEVEL-UP TIPS
TIP 1 · 접속사 짝
both...and / either...or / neither...nor 짝을 기억하세요!
TIP 2 · 동사 일치
Both → 복수 동사, Either/Neither → 단수 동사가 원칙입니다.
TIP 3 · Neither = 부정
Neither는 이미 부정의 의미를 포함하므로 not과 함께 사용하지 않습니다.
TIP 4 · 실생활 활용
선택/비교 상황에서 자연스럽게 사용해보세요. Both are good! / Either is fine!

연습문제PRACTICE EXERCISES

빈칸에 both, either, neither 중 알맞은 것을 넣으세요.

  • ___ students are ready for the test. (Both)
  • You can choose ___ option. Both are good. (either)
  • I like ___ of the movies. They're both boring. (neither)
  • I like tea. I like coffee. → I like ___ tea ___ coffee. (both...and)
  • Correct: "Either books are fine" → ___ (Either book is fine.)
  • ___ answers are correct. Well done! (Both)
  • ___ option is good. We need a new plan. (Neither)
  • ___ time works for me. Pick whichever you prefer. (Either)
