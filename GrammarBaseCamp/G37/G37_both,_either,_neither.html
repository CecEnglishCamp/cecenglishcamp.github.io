1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

BBOTH

두 개 모두 → 긍정
I like both books.
(두 권 모두 좋아함)

EEITHER

둘 중 하나 → 긍정 또는 부정
You can choose either option.
(둘 중 하나 선택 가능)

NNEITHER

둘 다 아님 → 부정
I like neither movie.
(둘 다 안 좋아함)

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

기본 문법 규칙

  • Both + 복수 명사 + 복수 동사 → Both students are here.
  • Either + 단수 명사 + 단수 동사 → Either answer is fine.
  • Neither + 단수 명사 + 단수 동사 → Neither option is good.
  • Both of / Either of / Neither of + the/my/these + 복수 명사
  • 동사 일치: Both → 복수 동사 / Either, Neither → 단수 동사
  • 대명사로도 사용 가능: Both are correct. / Neither is true.

자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴

  • Both + and → I like both tea and coffee.
  • Either + or → You can choose either tea or coffee.
  • Neither + nor → I like neither tea nor coffee.
  • Both of them / Either of them / Neither of them → 대명사 형태

4. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (School Situation)
A: Do both students have their books?
B: Yes, both of them are prepared.
A: Can either student present today?
B: Yes, either one is ready.
여행 상황 (Travel Situation)
A: Are both hotels available?
B: No, neither is open this weekend.
A: So we can't choose either?
B: Unfortunately, no.
직장 상황 (Work Situation)
A: Did both teams submit their reports?
B: Yes, both did it on time.
A: Is either report missing data?
B: No, both are complete.
온라인 상황 (Online Situation)
A: Can I use either browser for this site?
B: Yes, either works fine.
A: Are both browsers secure?
B: Definitely.
가족 상황 (Family Situation)
A: Do both kids want pizza?
B: No, neither likes cheese.
A: So we'll choose something else.
B: Good idea.
병원 상황 (Hospital Situation)
A: Did either patient arrive?
B: Yes, one of them is here.
A: Is either room ready?
B: Both rooms are clean and available.

문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining Practice)

  • I like book A. I like book B. → I like both books.
  • You can choose option A. You can choose option B. → You can choose either option.
  • I don't like movie A. I don't like movie B. → I like neither movie.
  • Student A is ready. Student B is ready. → Both students are ready.
  • Door A is open. Door B is open. → Either door is open.
  • Shirt A doesn't fit. Shirt B doesn't fit. → Neither shirt fits me.
  • Team A won. Team B won. → Both teams won.
  • Answer A is correct. Answer B is correct. → Both answers are correct.
  • Option A is bad. Option B is bad. → Neither option is good.
  • Time A works. Time B works. → Either time is fine.

7. 자가진단 (Self-Check)

학습 완료 체크

레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • Both: 두 개 모두 → 복수 명사 + 복수 동사
  • Either: 둘 중 하나 → 단수 명사 + 단수 동사
  • Neither: 둘 다 아님 → 단수 명사 + 단수 동사
  • Both of / Either of / Neither of + 복수 명사 → 동사는 단수 또는 복수 가능
  • Neither는 부정 의미이므로 not과 함께 쓰지 않음
  • Either…or / Neither…nor는 병렬 구조로 자주 사용됨
  • 문맥에 따라 선택/부정/전체 강조를 구분해야 함

  • Record and review your own sentences for clarity and fluency.
