1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

INVInversion (도치)

일반 어순(SVO)을 뒤집어 강조하거나 문어체 표현을 만드는 구조
보통은 부사구나 조건 표현이 문장 앞에 올 때 발생
도치 시 조동사 + 주어 + 동사 순서로 바뀜
시험, 글쓰기, 공식 표현에서 자주 사용됨

일반: I had known the truth.

도치: Had I known the truth...

강조: So tired was she that she slept.

핵심 포인트

  • Inversion: 일반 어순을 뒤집어 강조하는 문법 구조
  • 부사구나 조건 표현이 문장 앞에 올 때 발생
  • 도치 시 조동사 + 주어 + 동사 순서로 변경
  • 시험, 글쓰기, 공식 표현에서 자주 사용
  • 강조와 문어체 효과를 위한 고급 문법

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

부사구 도치
부정어/강조 부사구 + 조동사 + 주어 + 동사

• Never have I seen such a view.

• Only then did he understand.

• Not until she arrived did we start.

조건 도치
조동사 + 주어 + 동사 (if 생략)

• Should you need help, call me.

• Had I known, I would have acted.

• Were he here, we'd begin.

강조 도치
강조어 + be동사/조동사 + 주어

• So fast did he run that he won.

• Such was her talent that everyone noticed.

• No sooner had we arrived than it rained.

도치 규칙

  • 부정어 도치: Never, Rarely, Not until 등이 문장 앞에 올 때
  • 조건 도치: Should, Had, Were로 if 없이 조건 표현
  • 강조 도치: So, Such, No sooner 등으로 강조 표현
  • 도치 시 조동사 + 주어 + 동사 순서 유지
  • 의문문과 혼동하지 않도록 문맥과 억양 중요

어순 변화

  • 일반문: I have never seen this → 도치: Never have I seen this
  • 일반문: If I had known → 도치: Had I known
  • 일반문: He was so tired → 도치: So tired was he
  • 일반문: She rarely speaks → 도치: Rarely does she speak

3. 긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

부사구 도치 (15개)

  • Never have I been so surprised.
  • Rarely does he speak in meetings.
  • Only then did she understand.
  • Not until midnight did they arrive.
  • Hardly had I sat down when the phone rang.
  • Seldom do we see such dedication.
  • Never again will I make that mistake.
  • Not only did he pass, but he excelled.
  • Little did she know what awaited her.
  • Nowhere can you find better service.
  • Only after the exam did I relax.
  • Not once did he complain about the work.
  • Scarcely had we begun when it started raining.
  • At no time did she lose her composure.
  • Under no circumstances will I agree to this.

조건 도치 (15개)

  • Should you need help, let me know.
  • Had I known, I would have told you.
  • Were they ready, we could begin.
  • Should it rain, we'll cancel the trip.
  • Were I you, I'd take the offer.
  • Had she arrived earlier, we could have started.
  • Should anyone call, take a message.
  • Were the weather better, we'd go hiking.
  • Had we known the truth, we would have acted.
  • Should the need arise, contact me immediately.
  • Were he more careful, accidents wouldn't happen.
  • Had they listened, this could have been avoided.
  • Should you change your mind, let us know.
  • Were I in charge, things would be different.
  • Had the meeting been postponed, we could have attended.

강조 도치 (15개)

  • So beautiful was the sunset that we stopped walking.
  • Such was his anger that he couldn't speak.
  • No sooner had she entered than the lights went out.
  • So quickly did they finish that we were amazed.
  • Nor did he apologize for his mistake.
  • So tired was she that she fell asleep immediately.
  • Such was the noise that we couldn't hear anything.
  • No sooner had we arrived than the storm began.
  • So impressive was her performance that everyone applauded.
  • Neither did she call nor did she write.
  • So difficult was the test that many students failed.
  • Such was their enthusiasm that they worked all night.
  • No sooner had the meeting started than problems arose.
  • So clearly did he explain that everyone understood.
  • Such was the confusion that no one knew what to do.

5. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

8. 자주 쓰이는 도치 패턴

Never/Rarely/Not until + 조동사 + 주어 + 동사

• Never have I seen this.

• Rarely does she speak.

• Not until he arrived did we begin.

조건 도치

Should/Had/Were + 주어 + 동사 (if 생략)

• Should you need help, call me.

• Had I known, I would have acted.

• Were they ready, we'd begin.

강조 도치

So/Such/No sooner + be동사/조동사 + 주어

• So fast did he run.

• Such was her talent.

• No sooner had we arrived than it rained.

10. 레벨업 팁

💡 도치 구조는 강조와 문어체 표현에 효과적

💡 조동사 위치가 핵심 → do/does/did/had/should/were 등

💡 도치된 문장은 의문문처럼 보이지만 질문 아님

💡 부정어/조건/강조 표현이 문장 맨 앞에 올 때 도치 발생

💡 실생활에서는 글쓰기, 발표, 시험에서 자주 사용됨

11. 연습문제 (25문항)

객관식 (5문항)

1. Which is correct?
A. Never I have seen this.
B. Never have I seen this.
정답: B. Never have I seen this.
2. Choose the correct form: ___ you need help, call me.
A. Should
B. Had
정답: A. Should (현재/미래 조건)
3. Fill in: ___ had we arrived than it started raining.
A. No sooner
B. Sooner
정답: A. No sooner (No sooner...than 패턴)
