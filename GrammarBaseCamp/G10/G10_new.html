Comparative and Superlative Adjectives

비교급 (Comparative): 두 대상을 비교할 때 사용합니다. (예: taller, more beautiful)
최상급 (Superlative): 세 대상 이상 중에서 최고를 말할 때 사용합니다. (예: tallest, most beautiful)
형용사의 길이에 따라 형태를 바꾸는 규칙이 다릅니다.

My house is bigger than yours. (비교급)

This is the biggest house. (최상급)

2
문법 규칙
  • 비교급 형태: 형용사 + -er / more + 형용사
  • 최상급 형태: the + 형용사 + -est / the most + 형용사
  • 짧은 형용사 (1~2음절): tall → taller → the tallest
  • 긴 형용사 (2~3음절 이상): beautiful → more beautiful → the most beautiful
  • 불규칙 변화: good → better → the best / bad → worse → the worst
  • 비교급 문장 구조: A is taller than B. (than은 비교 대상 앞)
  • 최상급 문장 구조: A is the tallest in the group. (the는 최상급 앞에 필수)
  • 주의: than은 비교급에만, the는 최상급 앞에 반드시 사용합니다.

긍정문 예시
  • She is taller than me. (비교급, -er)
  • This book is more interesting than that one. (비교급, more)

  • This is the biggest cake I’ve ever seen. (최상급, -est)
  • She is the most talented singer in the group. (최상급, most)
  • Today is the coldest day of the year. (최상급, -est)
  • My car is faster than yours. (비교급, -er)
  • This is the best restaurant in town. (최상급, 불규칙)
  • He is more careful than his brother. (비교급, more)
  • That was the worst movie I’ve seen. (최상급, 불규칙)

상황별 대화 흐름

학교 상황 (비교급/불규칙)
A: Who is better at math?
B: I think she is.
A: I agree.

여행 상황 (최상급)
A: Is this mountain higher than the last one?
B: Yes, it’s the highest in the region.
A: Amazing!

직장 상황 (비교급)
A: Which design is more modern?
B: The second one looks more stylish.
A: Let’s choose that.

온라인 상황 (최상급)
A: Is this app faster than the old one?
B: Yes, and it’s the most efficient too.
A: Great!

가족 상황 (최상급)
A: Who is the youngest in our family?
B: My little sister.
A: She’s adorable!

병원 상황 (비교급)
A: Is this medicine stronger than the last one?
B: Yes, but it’s also the safest.
A: That’s good to know.

문장 결합 연습
  • This cake is big. That cake is bigger. → That cake is bigger than this one. (비교급)
  • She is tall. He is taller. → He is taller than her. (비교급)
  • This movie is interesting. That movie is more interesting. → That movie is more interesting than this one. (비교급)
  • Today is cold. Yesterday was colder. → Yesterday was colder than today. (비교급)
  • This is a good book. That is a better book. → That book is better than this one. (비교급/불규칙)
  • This cake is big. It’s the biggest. → This is the biggest cake. (최상급)
  • She is talented. She is the most talented. → She is the most talented singer. (최상급)
  • He is bad. He is the worst. → He is the worst player. (최상급/불규칙)
  • This app is fast. It’s the fastest. → This is the fastest app. (최상급)
  • That mountain is high. It’s the highest. → That is the highest mountain. (최상급)

실생활 사용 예시
  • 학교 상황: She is better at science than I am. (나보다 과학을 더 잘한다.)
  • 직장 상황: This design is more modern than the old one. (이 디자인이 이전 것보다 더 현대적이다.)
  • 가족 상황: My brother is the tallest in our family. (우리 가족에서 오빠가 가장 키가 크다.)
  • 온라인 상황: This app is the fastest we’ve tested. (이 앱이 우리가 테스트한 것 중 가장 빠르다.)
  • 병원 상황: This medicine is stronger than the last one. (이 약이 지난번 것보다 더 세다.)
  • 여행 상황: That hotel is more expensive than this one. (저 호텔이 이 호텔보다 더 비싸다.)
  • 회의 상황: She gave the best presentation today. (그녀가 오늘 최고의 발표를 했다.)
8
자주 쓰이는 패턴
  • 짧은 형용사 비교급: 형용사 + -er + than: taller than, bigger than, colder than
  • 긴 형용사 비교급: more + 형용사 + than: more interesting than, more careful than
  • 짧은 형용사 최상급: the + 형용사 + -est: the tallest, the biggest, the coldest
  • 긴 형용사 최상급: the most + 형용사: the most beautiful, the most talented
  • 불규칙: good → better → the best / bad → worse → the worst
  • 비교급 강조: much better, a lot faster, even more difficult (비교급을 강조할 때는 'very'를 사용하지 않습니다.)
  • 최상급 강조: by far the best, easily the most popular
9
흔한 실수
  • ❌ She is more taller than me. → ✅ She is taller than me. (이중 비교급 사용 불가: tall은 짧은 형용사)
  • ❌ This is most beautiful. → ✅ This is the most beautiful. (최상급 앞에는 the 필수)
  • ❌ That is the most tallest. → ✅ That is the tallest. (이중 최상급 사용 불가: tall은 짧은 형용사)
  • ❌ This is more best. → ✅ This is the best. (better/best는 불규칙이므로 more/most 사용 불가)
  • ❌ My car is very faster. → ✅ My car is much faster. (비교급 강조는 very 대신 much/a lot 사용)
10
레벨업 팁
  • 2음절 -y로 끝나는 형용사: -y를 -ier 또는 -iest로 바꿉니다. (예: happy → happier → happiest)
  • 비교급 강조 표현: much, far, a lot, even, still (훨씬 더)와 같은 부사를 비교급 앞에 넣어 강조할 수 있습니다. (예: This is much easier.)
  • 최상급 + of / in: 최상급은 범위를 나타내는 in (장소, 그룹)이나 of (기간, 복수 명사)와 함께 사용됩니다. (예: the fastest runner in the world / the hardest test of the year)
  • 동등 비교: A와 B가 같을 때는 as + 형용사 + as를 사용합니다. (예: My car is as fast as yours.)
  • 불규칙 변화 암기: good/better/best, bad/worse/worst, many/much/more/most는 반드시 암기하세요.
11
연습문제
객관식
  1. Which is correct?
    A. She is more taller than me. B. She is taller than me.
    A. She is more taller than me.
    B. She is taller than me.
  2. Choose the correct form:
    This book is ___ than that one.
    A. interesting
    B. more interesting
  3. Fill in: He is the ___ player in the team.
    A. better
    B. best

1. B. taller는 이미 비교급 형태이므로 more를 중복해서 사용할 수 없습니다.

2. B. interesting은 긴 형용사이므로 비교급은 more interesting입니다.

3. B. '팀에서' 가장 좋다는 의미이므로 최상급 best를 사용합니다. (the가 힌트)

문장 결합 및 오류 수정 (정답 예시 제공)
  1. This cake is big. That cake is bigger. → That cake is bigger than this one.
  2. She is tall. He is taller. → He is taller than her.
  3. ❌ This is most beautiful. → ✅ This is the most beautiful.
  4. ❌ That is the most tallest. → ✅ That is the tallest.
  5. ❌ This is more best. → ✅ This is the best.
