Speaking Goal
English: Look at the picture. Say what you see.
Korean: 그림을 보고 보이는 것을 영어로 말해 보세요.
  • 1. I see a girl.
  • 2. I see a girl and her friends.
  • 3. They are walking together.
  • 4. They look like they are going on an adventure.
