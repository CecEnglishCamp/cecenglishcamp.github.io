READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Mr. McGregor ran after Peter.
Peter lost his shoes.
He lost his jacket too.
But Peter kept running.
Mr. McGregor ran after Peter all around the garden.
Peter ran as fast as he could, but things kept going wrong.
He lost one shoe near the cabbages. He lost the other shoe near the potatoes.
Then his blue jacket got caught in a net. He had to pull free and leave it behind.
Now Peter had no shoes and no jacket. But he kept running. Mr. McGregor was still behind him.
Mr. McGregor chased Peter all over the garden. Peter ran hard, but the obstacles slowed him down.
One shoe came off near the cabbages. The other shoe came off near the potato beds. Then his jacket caught in a net and he had to wriggle free, leaving it behind.
Now he had nothing — no shoes, no jacket. But Mr. McGregor was still chasing him, and the garden felt bigger than ever.
Peter did not look back. He just ran.
맥그리거 씨가 정원 곳곳에서 피터를 쫓았어요. 피터는 열심히 달렸지만 장애물 때문에 속도가 느려졌어요.
양배추 근처에서 신발 한 짝이 벗겨졌어요. 감자 밭 근처에서 다른 신발이 벗겨졌어요. 그리고 재킷이 그물에 걸려서 빠져나와야 했고, 재킷을 뒤에 남겨두었어요.
이제 그는 아무것도 없었어요 — 신발도, 재킷도. 하지만 맥그리거 씨는 여전히 쫓아오고 있었고, 정원은 그 어느 때보다 더 크게 느껴졌어요. 피터는 뒤돌아보지 않았어요. 그냥 달렸어요.
