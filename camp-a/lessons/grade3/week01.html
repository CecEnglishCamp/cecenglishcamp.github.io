이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Mrs. Rabbit had four children.
Three sisters were good.
Peter was bad.
Mother said, "Do not go to the garden!"
Mrs. Rabbit had four children.
The three sisters were good. They listened to Mother.
But Peter did not listen.
"Do not go to Mr. McGregor's garden," Mother said.
"It is not safe for us there."
Mrs. Rabbit had four children — three good sisters and one naughty boy named Peter.
The sisters always listened to Mother. But Peter liked to do what he wanted.
One day, Mother looked at them all and said:
"Do not go into Mr. McGregor's garden. Your father went there once, and he did not come back."
옛날 옛날에 아기 토끼 네 마리가 살았어요. 이름은 플롭시, 모프시, 코튼테일, 그리고 피터였어요. 그들은 아주 큰 전나무 뿌리 아래 모래 언덕에서 엄마와 함께 살았어요.

어느 날 아침 토끼 엄마가 말했어요. "얘들아, 들판이나 오솔길에는 가도 되지만 맥그리거 씨 정원에는 절대 가면 안 된다. 너희 아빠가 거기서 사고를 당했거든. 맥그리거 부인이 아빠로 파이를 만들었단다."
