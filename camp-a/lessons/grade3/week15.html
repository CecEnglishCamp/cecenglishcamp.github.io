이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Jack climbed the beanstalk again.
He went back to the giant's castle.
He found a magic goose.
The goose laid golden eggs.
Jack took the goose home.
With the bag of gold spent, Jack climbed the beanstalk again. He crept back into the giant's castle and hid in the oven as before.
The giant came home. "Fee-fi-fo-fum!" he roared. But he did not find Jack.
After dinner, the giant called for his goose. "Lay!" he commanded -- and the goose laid a golden egg. Then another. Then another.
Jack watched through the iron door, his eyes wide. When the giant fell asleep, Jack crept out, grabbed the goose, and ran.
"Lay, goose, lay!" he whispered as he ran. The goose laid a golden egg right there in his arms. Jack brought the goose safely home.
The gold was gone. His mother had thanked him and spent it on food and rent, and now the cupboard was bare again. Jack looked at the beanstalk.
He climbed it a second time, this time knowing what to expect. The giant's wife was more reluctant to help, but her kindness won out. Jack was back in the oven when the giant arrived.
"Fee-fi-fo-fum!" But tonight the giant's treasure was not coins. It was a goose -- a small, unremarkable-looking bird that responded to a single command by laying a perfect golden egg.
"Lay, goose, lay," the giant said, three times, then fell asleep satisfied.
Jack did not hesitate. He tucked the goose under his arm and ran. The goose, to its credit, laid another golden egg while being carried.

"낳아라, 거위야, 낳아라," 거인이 세 번 말하고 만족해서 잠들었어요.
