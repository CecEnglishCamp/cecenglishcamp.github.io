이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Peter went to the garden.
He ate the vegetables.
Mr. McGregor saw him!
"Stop!" said Mr. McGregor.
Peter ran away fast.
Peter did not listen to Mother. He went to Mr. McGregor's garden.
He ate some lettuce and carrots. It was very good!
Then he saw Mr. McGregor. Mr. McGregor was very angry.
"Stop! Stop!" he shouted.
Peter was very scared. He ran away as fast as he could.
Peter did not listen to Mother. He went straight to Mr. McGregor's garden and ate the lettuce, beans, and carrots.
But then he turned a corner and stopped. Mr. McGregor was right there!
Mr. McGregor jumped up and ran after Peter, shouting loudly.
Peter's heart was beating very fast. He turned and ran as fast as his legs could carry him.
피터는 엄마 말을 듣지 않고 맥그리거 씨 정원으로 갔어요. 문 밖에서 잠깐 들여다보다가, 결국 안으로 비집고 들어가고 말았어요.

정원 안에는 맛있는 것들이 가득했어요. 피터는 상추를 먹고, 콩도 먹고, 당근도 먹었어요. 먹다 보니 배가 너무 불러졌어요. "이제 파슬리를 좀 먹어야겠어." 피터가 생각했어요.
