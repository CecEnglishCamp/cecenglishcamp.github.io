이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Jack climbed the beanstalk a third time.
He found a magic harp.
The harp called out to the giant.
The giant woke up and chased Jack.
Jack chopped down the beanstalk.
Jack climbed the beanstalk one last time. Inside the castle, he found a golden harp that could play music by itself. When he grabbed it and ran, the harp called out: "Master! Master!" and woke the giant.
The giant roared and chased Jack down the beanstalk. Jack climbed as fast as he could, the giant's heavy footsteps shaking the whole stalk.
"Chop down the beanstalk!" Jack shouted to his mother as he neared the bottom. She brought the axe. Jack jumped down, grabbed it, and swung with all his strength. The beanstalk fell with a crash -- and the giant fell with it. He was never seen again.
Jack and his mother lived happily ever after with the golden harp and the magic goose.
The third visit was the greediest and the most dangerous. Jack had gold. He had a goose. He went back for a harp -- a golden instrument that played itself, a thing of pure magic and beauty.
He got it. And the harp, loyal to its master, called out the moment Jack touched it.
The giant was awake and angry before Jack reached the door. The chase down the beanstalk was desperate -- the giant was faster than Jack had imagined, each footfall sending shudders through the stalk.
Jack hit the ground and screamed: "Chop down the beanstalk!"
Three swings. The beanstalk shook, cracked, and fell. The giant fell with it -- a distant crash, and then silence.
Jack looked at his mother. His mother looked at Jack. They went inside for tea.
세 번째 방문은 가장 탐욕스럽고 가장 위험했어요. 잭에게는 금이 있었어요. 거위가 있었어요. 하프를 가지러 돌아갔어요 -- 스스로 연주하는 황금 악기, 순수한 마법과 아름다움의 물건.
얻었어요. 그리고 하프는 주인에게 충성스럽게 잭이 손을 댄 순간 소리를 질렀어요.
거인은 잭이 문에 닿기 전에 이미 깨어나 화가 났어요. 콩나무를 내려오는 추격전은 절박했어요 -- 거인은 잭이 상상했던 것보다 빨랐고, 발걸음마다 줄기 전체가 떨렸어요.
잭이 땅에 닿으며 소리쳤어요: "콩나무를 잘라버려요!"
세 번의 도끼질. 콩나무가 흔들리고, 갈라지고, 쓰러졌어요. 거인도 함께 쓰러졌어요 -- 멀리서 쾅 소리, 그리고 침묵.
잭은 엄마를 바라봤어요. 엄마는 잭을 바라봤어요. 그들은 차를 마시러 들어갔어요.
