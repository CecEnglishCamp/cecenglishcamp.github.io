이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Peter ran and ran.
But he was lost!
The garden was too big.
He could not find the gate.

Peter saw a pond.
He found a watering can.
He hid inside it.
Mr. McGregor could not find him.
Peter ran through the garden but he was completely lost.
He could not find the gate anywhere.
Peter began to cry. He felt very hopeless.

He passed a pond where some friendly fish were swimming.
Then Peter spotted a large watering can.
He jumped inside and hid, trembling with fear.
Mr. McGregor searched everywhere but gave up at last.
Peter dashed through the garden in a blind panic.
Every path looked the same, and the gate was nowhere to be found.
Tears began to fall as Peter realised he was hopelessly lost.

He passed a pond where fish seemed to urge him to escape.
Spotting a watering can near a shed, Peter dived inside.
He crouched there, shaking with fear, while Mr. McGregor searched nearby.
After a while, the old gardener gave up and went back to his work.
피터는 정신없이 도망쳤지만 길을 잃고 말았어요. 정원이 너무 넓어서 출구를 찾을 수가 없었어요. 피터는 울음이 나올 것 같았어요.

그때 연못이 보였어요. 피터는 물뿌리개를 발견하고 그 안에 숨었어요. 덜덜 떨면서 숨어 있었어요. 맥그리거 씨는 계속 찾아다녔지만 결국 포기하고 돌아갔어요.
