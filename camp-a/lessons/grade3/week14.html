이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Jack reached the top of the beanstalk.
He saw a huge castle.
A giant lived inside.
The giant could smell Jack.
Jack hid quickly.
Jack climbed through the clouds and found himself on a huge flat land. In the distance stood an enormous castle. Jack walked up to it and knocked on the enormous door.
A giant woman opened it. She was kind and let Jack inside to eat. But then the ground began to shake.
"Fee-fi-fo-fum! I smell the blood of an Englishman!" roared the giant as he stomped in.
The woman quickly hid Jack in the oven. The giant sat down and counted his gold coins until he fell asleep. Then Jack crept out and grabbed a bag of gold. He ran as fast as he could back to the beanstalk.
The land above the clouds was vast and silent. The castle at its centre was built for creatures of enormous scale -- the door handle alone was taller than Jack.
The giant's wife was surprisingly kind. She fed Jack quietly in the kitchen, warning him her husband would be home soon. Then the ground trembled.
"Fee-fi-fo-fum! I smell the blood of an Englishman! Be he alive or be he dead, I'll grind his bones to make my bread!"
Jack was shoved into the oven. Through the small iron door he watched the giant count his gold, bellowing the rhyme over and over until his eyes closed and his snores shook the walls.
Then Jack moved. He snatched the bag of gold and ran for the beanstalk, heart hammering, not looking back.
구름 위의 나라는 광활하고 조용했어요. 중앙에 있는 성은 엄청난 규모의 생물을 위해 지어졌어요 -- 문손잡이만 해도 잭보다 컸어요.
거인의 아내는 놀랍도록 친절했어요. 조용히 부엌에서 잭에게 음식을 먹이며 남편이 곧 올 거라고 경고했어요. 그때 땅이 떨렸어요.
"피이-파이-포-팜! 영국인의 피 냄새가 난다! 살아있든 죽어있든, 뼈를 갈아 빵을 만들겠다!"
잭은 오븐 안으로 밀어 넣어졌어요. 작은 철문을 통해 거인이 금화를 세고, 눈이 감기고 코고는 소리가 벽을 흔들 때까지 그 노래를 계속 외치는 것을 지켜봤어요.
그때 잭이 움직였어요. 금 자루를 낚아채고 콩나무를 향해 달렸어요. 심장이 두근거렸지만 뒤를 돌아보지 않았어요.
