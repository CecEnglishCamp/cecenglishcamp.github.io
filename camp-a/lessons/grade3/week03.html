이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Peter went to find parsley.
He saw Mr. McGregor!
Mr. McGregor was very angry.
"Stop, thief!" he shouted.

He ran after Peter with a rake.
Peter was very scared.
He ran away fast!
Peter went to look for parsley in the corner of the garden.
Suddenly, he came face to face with Mr. McGregor!
Mr. McGregor jumped up and shouted, "Stop, thief!"

He grabbed his rake and chased after Peter.
Peter was terrified and ran as fast as he could.
He lost a shoe and his jacket caught on a gooseberry bush.
Peter turned the corner and suddenly froze.
There was Mr. McGregor on his hands and knees planting cabbages.
The old gardener looked up and immediately recognised the little rabbit.

"Stop, thief!" he roared, scrambling to his feet.
Armed with a rake, Mr. McGregor gave chase.
In his panic, Peter lost both shoes and his little jacket snagged on a gooseberry net.
