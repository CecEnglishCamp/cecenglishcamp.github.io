READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Peter was lost in the garden.
He was very scared.
He ran here and there.

Peter looked for the gate.
"I must find the gate!" he said.
He ran very fast.
At last, he saw the gate!
Peter was so happy.
Peter was lost inside Mr. McGregor's big garden. He was tired and scared. He had run so much that his heart was beating very fast.

Peter tried to find the gate, but he could not remember which way to go. He ran past the vegetables and looked behind every big flower pot.
"I must find the gate!" Peter said to himself.
Then he saw a sparrow nearby. The little bird showed him the right way. Peter ran fast — and there was the gate at last!
Peter was completely lost. The garden felt enormous — much bigger than when he had first squeezed through the gate. Every path looked the same, and the sound of Mr. McGregor's boots was never far away.

His little legs were trembling with exhaustion and fear. But giving up was not something Peter could do — home was somewhere beyond that gate, and his mother was waiting.
"I must find the gate!" he told himself, and ran on.
A sparrow landed nearby and chirped encouragingly. Peter spotted a familiar corner — and there it was. The gate. He squeezed through it without stopping and did not look back.
피터는 완전히 길을 잃었어요. 정원이 엄청나게 커 보였어요. 모든 길이 똑같아 보였고, 맥그리거 씨의 장화 소리가 항상 가까이서 들렸어요.

작은 다리가 지침과 두려움으로 떨리고 있었어요. 하지만 포기할 수 없었어요 — 집은 그 문 너머에 있고, 엄마가 기다리고 있었으니까요. "문을 찾아야 해!" 그는 스스로에게 말하고 계속 달렸어요. 참새 한 마리가 내려앉아 짹짹 울었어요. 피터가 고개를 들자 익숙한 모퉁이가 보였고 — 문이 있었어요! 그는 멈추지 않고 문을 빠져나갔어요.
