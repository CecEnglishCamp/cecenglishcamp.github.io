READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
The wolf got to Grandmother's house first.
He ate Grandmother up!
He dressed in her clothes.
Red Riding Hood came in.
She did not know it was the wolf.
The wolf ran fast through the forest and got to Grandmother's house first. He knocked on the door and tricked his way in. Then he ate Grandmother up and put on her clothes and cap. He got into her bed and waited.
When Red Riding Hood arrived, she walked in and looked at Grandmother.
"What big ears you have!" she said.
"All the better to hear you with," said the wolf.
"What big eyes you have, Grandmother!"
"All the better to see you with!"
The wolf arrived at Grandmother's cottage long before Red Riding Hood. He knocked at the door, disguised his voice, and when the old woman opened it, he swallowed her whole. Then he dressed in her nightgown and cap, climbed into her bed, and pulled the covers up.
When Red Riding Hood entered, she noticed something was wrong but could not quite name it.
"What big ears you have, Grandmother."
"All the better to hear you with, my dear."
"What big eyes you have, Grandmother!"
"All the better to see you with!" And the wolf smiled, showing his teeth.
늑대는 빨간 망토보다 훨씬 먼저 할머니 집에 도착했어요. 문을 두드리고, 목소리를 변장하고, 할머니가 문을 열자 통째로 삼켰어요. 그러고는 할머니 잠옷과 모자를 입고 침대에 들어가 이불을 덮었어요.
빨간 망토가 들어왔을 때 뭔가 이상하다고 느꼈지만 정확히 뭔지 알 수 없었어요.
"할머니, 귀가 정말 크네요."
"너를 더 잘 듣기 위해서란다, 얘야."
"할머니, 눈이 정말 크네요!"
"너를 더 잘 보기 위해서란다!" 그리고 늑대는 이를 드러내며 미소 지었어요.
