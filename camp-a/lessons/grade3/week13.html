이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Jack woke up the next morning.
He looked out of the window.
A huge beanstalk was growing.
It went up into the clouds.
Jack began to climb it.
Jack woke up and looked out the window. He could not believe his eyes. A huge green beanstalk had grown overnight. It was so tall that its top disappeared into the clouds.
"The beanstalk grew up to the sky!" Jack gasped.
He ran outside and grabbed the first branch. It was strong and solid. Jack began to climb. Higher and higher he went, through the clouds, until he reached a strange land far above the earth.
Everything up there was enormous -- the grass, the flowers, the stones. Jack felt very small indeed.
While Jack slept, something extraordinary happened in the garden. By morning, a beanstalk of impossible size had erupted from the soil -- thick as a tree trunk, spiralling upward through the clouds until it vanished from sight.
Jack did not hesitate. He seized the first branch and began to climb, hand over hand, up through cold mist and then sudden sunlight, until the ground below became invisible.
He emerged into a land of giants. The grass reached his shoulders. A single stone was the size of his mother's cottage.
"The beanstalk grew up to the sky!" he would tell people later. But standing at the top, looking at a world built for creatures ten times his size, Jack felt something new: not fear exactly, but the thrill of being somewhere he absolutely should not be.

잭은 망설이지 않았어요. 첫 번째 가지를 잡고 손을 교대로 올리며 올라가기 시작했어요. 차가운 안개를 지나 갑자기 햇빛이 나타날 때까지, 아래 땅이 보이지 않을 때까지.
거인의 나라에 도착했어요. 풀이 어깨까지 닿았어요. 돌 하나가 엄마의 오두막 크기였어요.
"콩나무가 하늘까지 자랐어요!" 나중에 사람들에게 말할 거예요. 하지만 꼭대기에 서서 자신보다 열 배 큰 생물을 위해 만들어진 세상을 바라보며, 잭은 새로운 감정을 느꼈어요: 두려움이 아니라, 절대로 있으면 안 되는 곳에 있는 짜릿함.
