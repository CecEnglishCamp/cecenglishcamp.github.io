이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Peter went into the garden.
Mr. McGregor chased him.
Peter ran away fast.
He got home at last.
He was not well that night.
Peter Rabbit had a long and difficult day. First, he went into Mr. McGregor's garden against his mother's wish. He ate vegetables and was happy. But then Mr. McGregor saw him and chased him.
Peter lost his shoes and his jacket. He hid in a watering can. At last, he found the gate and ran home.
His mother put him to bed and gave him warm tea. His sisters had a nice supper in the kitchen. Peter lay in bed and felt very tired.
It had been quite a day for Peter Rabbit. He had gone into Mr. McGregor's garden against his mother's wishes, eaten more than was wise, lost his jacket and shoes, and hidden in a cold watering can before finally running home.
His sisters had gone to the meadow and picked blackberries. They came home to a warm supper and a happy evening.
Peter went to bed with chamomile tea and felt very ill. But perhaps, just perhaps, he had learned something.
피터 래빗에게 정말 힘든 하루였어요. 엄마의 말을 어기고 정원에 들어가서, 재킷과 신발을 잃고, 차가운 물뿌리개 안에 숨었다가 마침내 집으로 달려왔어요.
세 자매는 들판에 가서 블랙베리를 따고 따뜻한 저녁을 먹었어요.
피터는 침대에 누워 캐모마일 차를 마셨어요. 기분이 매우 좋지 않았어요. 하지만 어쩌면 그날 뭔가를 배웠을지도 몰라요.
