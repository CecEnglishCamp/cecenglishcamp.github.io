READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Jim liked the cook, Long John Silver.
Silver had one leg and a parrot on his shoulder.
Silver was always kind and friendly to Jim.

One day, Jim hid in a barrel on the ship.
He heard Silver talking to the other sailors.
Silver said he was a pirate!
Jim was very scared.
Silver was not a good man after all.
Jim liked Long John Silver very much. Silver was the ship's cook, and he was always cheerful and kind. He had one leg and a friendly parrot called Captain Flint on his shoulder.

One evening, Jim climbed into a large apple barrel to hide. From inside the barrel, he heard Silver talking secretly with some of the sailors. Silver was planning a mutiny — he wanted to take over the ship and steal the treasure for himself!
"I am the captain now," Silver said with a cold smile.
Jim's heart nearly stopped. The friendly cook was really the leader of the pirates.
Jim had grown genuinely fond of Long John Silver. The ship's cook was cheerful, clever, and always had a kind word for Jim. His parrot, Captain Flint, would sit on his shoulder and squawk "Pieces of eight!" to anyone who passed.

But one dark evening, Jim slipped inside a large empty barrel near the bow to rest. Half-asleep, he heard voices. It was Silver — speaking in a low, dangerous voice to a group of sailors Jim had never fully trusted.
Silver was planning a mutiny. Once the treasure was found, he intended to kill the captain and take command of the Hispaniola for himself.
"I am the captain now," Silver said softly, with a smile that made Jim's blood run cold.
The kind, laughing cook Jim had trusted completely was in truth the most dangerous man on the ship.
짐은 존 실버를 진심으로 좋아했어요. 요리사 실버는 쾌활하고 친절했으며, 어깨 위에는 앵무새 '캡틴 플린트'가 앉아 있었어요.

그런데 어느 어두운 저녁, 짐은 배 앞쪽의 커다란 빈 통 속에 들어가 쉬고 있었어요. 반쯤 잠든 상태에서 목소리가 들렸어요 — 바로 실버였어요. 낮고 위험한 목소리로 몇몇 선원들에게 이야기하고 있었죠. 실버는 반란을 계획하고 있었어요. 보물을 찾은 후 선장을 죽이고 히스파니올라호를 차지하려 했던 거예요. "이제 내가 선장이야." 실버가 조용히, 등골이 오싹해지는 미소와 함께 말했어요. 짐이 완전히 믿었던 친절한 요리사는 사실 배에서 가장 위험한 인물이었어요.
