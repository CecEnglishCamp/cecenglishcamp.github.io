이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Fogg and Passepartout arrived in India.
They saw a woman in danger.
Her name was Aouda.
Fogg decided to save her.
They rescued Aouda together.
The steamship docked in Bombay, and Fogg and Passepartout took a train across India toward Calcutta. But the railway was not complete -- they had to hire an elephant for the remaining miles through the jungle.
During the journey, they came across a procession. A young woman named Aouda was being forced to take part in a funeral ceremony against her will.
"We must not leave her behind," said Fogg quietly.
That night, he and Passepartout crept into the ceremony and rescued Aouda. They brought her with them. Fogg did not hesitate, even though saving her cost him precious time.
India was not the smooth railway journey Fogg had planned. The train line stopped without warning, and the remaining distance could only be covered by elephant -- a slow, expensive, and uncertain substitute.
Fogg purchased an elephant without negotiating the price and set off through dense jungle. Passepartout rode atop the animal and spotted the procession first: a young woman, Aouda, being prepared for a ceremony she had clearly not chosen.
"We must not leave her behind," said Fogg. It was not a question.
The rescue required courage from Passepartout and calm from Fogg. They succeeded. Aouda joined them, bewildered and grateful. Fogg noted the delay in his book. The wager was still possible -- but only just.
인도는 포그가 계획했던 순탄한 기차 여행이 아니었어요. 기찻길이 예고 없이 끊어졌고, 남은 거리는 코끼리로만 갈 수 있었어요 -- 느리고, 비싸고, 불확실한 대안이었어요.
포그는 가격도 흥정하지 않고 코끼리를 구입해 울창한 정글을 향해 출발했어요. 파스파르투가 동물 위에 타고 먼저 행렬을 발견했어요: 자신이 선택하지 않은 의식을 준비 중인 젊은 여성 아우다였어요.
"우리는 그녀를 두고 떠날 수 없어요," 포그가 말했어요. 질문이 아니었어요.
구출에는 파스파르투의 용기와 포그의 침착함이 필요했어요. 성공했어요. 아우다가 어리둥절하고 감사해하며 합류했어요. 포그는 수첩에 지연을 기록했어요. 내기는 여전히 가능했어요 -- 하지만 아슬아슬하게.
