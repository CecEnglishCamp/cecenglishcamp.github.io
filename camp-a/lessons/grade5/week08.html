이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
They found the treasure spot.
But the hole was empty!
Ben Gunn had moved it.
He hid it in his cave.
At last they found the gold.
Captain Smollett and his men found the spot on the map. They dug and dug — but the treasure was not there. The hole was empty.
The pirates came from the trees, ready to fight. Things looked very bad.
But then Ben Gunn and the loyal men came from another direction. The pirates ran away.
They went to Ben Gunn's cave and found the gold — boxes and boxes of it. The treasure was real. They had found it at last.
The captain's party followed the map to the treasure site and dug. Nothing. Just an empty hole — and the sound of pirates closing in.
For a moment, things looked very dark. Then shots came from another direction — Ben Gunn and the loyal men arrived.
The pirates broke and ran. Later, in Ben Gunn's cave, Jim saw it at last: gold coins, bars, and jewels piled high.
The treasure was here — and then it was gone from the marked spot. But Ben Gunn had simply moved it there for safety, months before.
선장 일행은 지도를 따라 보물이 있는 곳으로 갔어요. 팠지만 — 빈 구멍뿐이었어요.
잠시 상황이 매우 어두워 보였어요. 그때 벤 건과 충직한 부하들이 다른 방향에서 왔어요.
해적들이 도망쳤어요. 나중에 벤 건의 동굴에서 짐은 마침내 보물을 봤어요: 금화, 금괴, 보석들이 높이 쌓여 있었어요.
보물이 거기 있었어요 — 표시된 곳에서는 사라졌었지만, 벤 건이 안전하게 보관하고 있었어요.
