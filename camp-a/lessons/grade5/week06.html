READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
The pirates attacked the stockade.
Captain Smollett's men were inside.
It was a big fight.

The captain said to his men:
"We shall fight to the last man."
Jim was scared but brave.
They fought hard.
The pirates ran away at last.
Long John Silver's pirates attacked the stockade where Captain Smollett and his loyal crew were hiding. The pirates came from all sides, firing their guns and shouting loudly.

Inside the log fort, Jim loaded muskets and passed them to the men. Captain Smollett stood firm and rallied his crew.
"We shall fight to the last man," the captain said calmly.
The battle was fierce and terrifying. Several men were wounded on both sides. But in the end, the pirates retreated into the jungle, leaving the stockade still in the hands of the captain's loyal crew.
The attack came at dawn. Silver's men rushed the stockade from multiple directions — a classic tactical encirclement that Captain Smollett had half-expected and prepared for as best he could with a crew of six and a boy.

Jim found a role in the chaos: loading muskets, passing them to the men at the loopholes, keeping the supply moving. It was terrifying and mechanical at once.
"We shall fight to the last man," Smollett said — not with bravado, but as a statement of fact, the way a captain speaks when he has made a decision and closed the door on every alternative.
When the smoke cleared, the pirates had retreated. The cost was high — three of Smollett's men were dead or dying. But the flag still flew above the stockade, and that, for now, was enough.
공격은 새벽에 왔어요. 실버의 부하들이 여러 방향에서 목책 요새를 습격했어요 — 선장이 예상하고 여섯 명의 선원과 한 소년으로 최선을 다해 준비했던 전술적 포위 작전이었어요.

짐은 혼란 속에서 역할을 찾았어요: 머스킷 총에 탄약을 장전하고, 총안 앞에 있는 사람들에게 전달하고, 공급을 계속 유지하는 것이었어요. 두려웠지만 기계적이기도 했어요. "우리는 마지막 한 사람까지 싸울 것이다," 스몰렛 선장이 말했어요 — 허세가 아니라, 결정을 내리고 모든 대안의 문을 닫았을 때 선장이 말하는 방식으로요. 연기가 걷혔을 때 해적들은 후퇴했어요. 대가는 컸어요 — 스몰렛의 부하 세 명이 죽거나 죽어가고 있었어요. 하지만 깃발은 여전히 요새 위에 나부꼈고, 지금으로선 그것으로 충분했어요.
