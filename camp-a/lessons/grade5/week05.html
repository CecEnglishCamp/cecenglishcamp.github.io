READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Jim was alone in the jungle.
He was scared but curious.
Suddenly, a strange man ran out!

The man had a long beard.
He looked very thin and dirty.
"I am Ben Gunn!" the man said.
"I have lived here for three years!"
Jim felt sorry for him.
Jim was alone in the jungle when a strange figure jumped out from behind a tree. The man was dressed in rags and had a long wild beard. He looked thin and frightened — but his eyes were bright and alive.

"Don't be afraid!" the man whispered. "I am Ben Gunn. I was a sailor once — but my crew left me here alone."
"I have been marooned on this island for three years," Ben said quietly.
Jim felt very sorry for him. Three whole years — alone on this island with only the trees and the sea for company.
Jim had not gone far into the trees when a figure appeared — suddenly and completely — as if he had grown out of the jungle floor. He was tanned the colour of bark, dressed in what had once been clothes, and his beard hung to his waist. His eyes, though, were quick and sharp.

"Jim Hawkins, I presume," the man said, which astonished Jim entirely, since he had told no one his name.
He was Ben Gunn, former sailor, marooned by his own crew after a failed hunt for the treasure. He had been entirely alone for three full years.
"I have been marooned on this island for three years," he said simply.
Jim tried to imagine three years of solitude and could not. Ben Gunn smiled — the smile of a man who had learned to be grateful for conversation.
짐이 나무 속으로 그다지 멀리 가지 않았을 때 인물이 나타났어요 — 갑자기 완전히 — 마치 정글 바닥에서 자란 것처럼요. 그는 나무껍질 색으로 그을렸고, 수염이 허리까지 내려왔어요. 하지만 눈은 빠르고 날카로웠어요.

"짐 호킨스인가요," 그 남자가 말했어요. 짐은 아무에게도 이름을 말한 적이 없어서 완전히 놀랐어요. 그는 벤 건이었어요 — 3년 내내 완전히 혼자였어요. "나는 이 섬에 3년 동안 홀로 버려져 있었어." 그가 단순하게 말했어요. 짐은 3년간의 고독을 상상하려 했지만 할 수 없었어요. 벤 건이 웃었어요 — 대화에 감사하는 법을 배운 사람의 미소로요.
