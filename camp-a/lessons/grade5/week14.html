이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Fix wanted to stop Fogg.
He gave Passepartout opium.
Passepartout missed the ship.
Fogg sailed without knowing.
Fogg went back to save him.
Fix the detective had a new plan. If he could delay Fogg until his arrest warrant arrived, everything would work out. He decided to use Passepartout.
He took Passepartout to an opium den in Hong Kong and made sure he smoked enough to fall unconscious. Passepartout missed the ship to Japan.
Fogg did not discover this until they were already at sea. "My servant is in danger," he said quietly to Aouda. He did not panic.
Fogg hired another boat and went back for Passepartout. He found him in Yokohama, confused but safe. The delay cost Fogg two days. But he did not leave his friend behind.
In Hong Kong, Fix finally made his move. The arrest warrant had still not arrived, but he had thought of something else: delay.
He guided Passepartout into an opium den near the harbour, ensuring the cheerful Frenchman smoked enough to lose consciousness entirely. When the ship to Yokohama sailed, Passepartout was not on it.
Fogg did not discover the absence until they were well out to sea. He told Aouda quietly: "My servant is in danger." Then he turned the problem over in his mind with characteristic precision.
He hired a small trading vessel at considerable expense and sailed back. He found Passepartout in Yokohama, bewildered and ashamed. The lost days cut dangerously into the wager's margin.
Fix, watching all this from a distance, felt something uncomfortable. The man he was pursuing had just sacrificed two precious days to retrieve a servant. It was not the behaviour of a criminal.
홍콩에서 픽스는 마침내 행동을 개시했어요. 체포 영장은 아직 도착하지 않았지만 다른 방법을 생각했어요: 지연.
그는 파스파르투를 항구 근처의 아편굴로 안내해서, 쾌활한 프랑스인이 완전히 의식을 잃을 만큼 충분히 피우도록 했어요. 요코하마로 가는 배가 출발할 때 파스파르투는 타지 못했어요.
포그는 배가 공해에 나올 때까지 그의 부재를 알지 못했어요. 아우다에게 조용히 말했어요: "제 하인이 위험에 처했어요." 그런 다음 특유의 정확성으로 문제를 생각했어요.
상당한 비용으로 작은 무역선을 고용해서 돌아갔어요. 요코하마에서 당황하고 부끄러워하는 파스파르투를 찾았어요. 잃어버린 날들이 위험하게 내기의 여유를 줄였어요.
픽스는 이 모든 것을 멀리서 지켜보며 불편한 감정을 느꼈어요. 그가 추적하는 남자가 하인을 되찾기 위해 소중한 이틀을 희생했어요. 범죄자의 행동이 아니었어요.
