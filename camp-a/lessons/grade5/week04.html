이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
The ship arrived at Treasure Island.
The island looked dark and scary.
Jim could see tall trees and black rocks.

The pirates wanted to go ashore.
Jim went with them in a small boat.
On the island, Jim ran away from the pirates.
He hid in the trees all alone.
It was very dangerous.
At last, the Hispaniola reached Treasure Island. The island looked dark and threatening, with tall jungle trees and jagged rocks along the shore. Jim felt a chill run down his spine.

The pirates were excited and restless. Silver let some of them go ashore in small boats. Jim secretly jumped into one of the boats and went with them.
On the island, Jim heard the pirates arguing. Suddenly, he ran off alone into the thick jungle to hide. He was frightened but curious.
Treasure Island lay before us, dark and threatening — but Jim knew the real danger was not the island itself. It was Silver and his men.
After long weeks at sea, the Hispaniola finally dropped anchor off the coast of Treasure Island. The crew crowded the rail, staring at the dense jungle and the grey cliffs rising behind it. Jim stared too — but where others saw adventure, he saw only threat.

Silver allowed the men to go ashore in longboats. Jim, acting on impulse, slipped into one of the boats without permission. When they landed on the beach, the pirates scattered noisily into the trees. Jim slipped away from them, pushing deeper into the jungle alone.
He had no plan. He only knew he needed to think — and that he could not think clearly surrounded by men he could no longer trust.
Behind him, he could hear Silver's voice carrying through the trees. Treasure Island lay before him, dark and threatening, its secrets still buried deep beneath the soil.
오랜 항해 끝에 히스파니올라호는 드디어 보물섬 앞바다에 닻을 내렸어요. 선원들은 난간에 몰려 울창한 정글과 그 뒤로 솟은 회색 절벽을 바라봤어요. 짐도 바라봤지만, 다른 사람들이 모험을 보는 곳에서 짐은 위협만 느꼈어요.

실버는 선원들이 작은 배를 타고 상륙하도록 허락했어요. 짐은 충동적으로 허락도 없이 배 중 하나에 슬쩍 올라탔어요. 해변에 도착하자 해적들은 시끄럽게 나무들 사이로 흩어졌어요. 짐은 그들을 빠져나와 혼자 정글 깊숙이 들어갔어요. 계획은 없었어요. 다만 생각이 필요했고, 더 이상 믿을 수 없는 사람들에 둘러싸여서는 생각이 안 됐어요. 뒤에서 실버의 목소리가 나무들 사이로 들려왔어요. 보물섬이 그의 앞에 펼쳐졌어요 — 어둡고 위협적으로, 비밀은 여전히 땅속 깊이 묻혀 있었어요.
