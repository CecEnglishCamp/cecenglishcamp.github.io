Jim liked Long John Silver very much. Silver was the ship's cook — cheerful, kind, and always friendly to Jim.
Silver had one leg and a parrot called Captain Flint on his shoulder.
One evening, Jim climbed into a big empty barrel to look for an apple. He fell asleep inside.
He woke up and heard voices. Silver was talking quietly to some of the other sailors.
Silver was planning to take over the ship and steal the treasure for himself! Jim was very shocked.
Jim had grown very fond of Long John Silver. The ship's cook was cheerful and clever, and he always had time to talk to Jim. His parrot, Captain Flint, would shout "Pieces of eight!" from his shoulder.
One evening Jim hid inside a large apple barrel and fell asleep in the dark. He woke to the sound of Silver's low, careful voice — talking to some of the sailors about a plan. They would wait until the treasure was found, then take the ship and deal with the captain and his loyal men.
Jim lay completely still, his heart beating hard. When he finally slipped away unseen, he knew exactly what he had to do.
짐은 롱 존 실버를 매우 좋아했어요. 배의 요리사는 밝고 영리했고, 항상 짐과 이야기할 시간이 있었어요. 그의 앵무새 캡틴 플린트는 어깨에서 "여덟 조각!"이라고 소리쳤어요.
어느 저녁 짐은 커다란 사과 통 안에 숨었다가 어둠 속에서 잠들었어요. 실버의 낮고 조심스러운 목소리에 깨어났어요 — 몇몇 선원들과 계획에 대해 이야기하고 있었어요. 보물이 발견될 때까지 기다렸다가 배를 빼앗고 선장과 충직한 부하들을 처리하자는 것이었어요.
짐은 완전히 가만히 누워 심장이 빠르게 뛰었어요. 마침내 들키지 않고 빠져나왔을 때, 해야 할 일이 무엇인지 정확히 알았어요.
