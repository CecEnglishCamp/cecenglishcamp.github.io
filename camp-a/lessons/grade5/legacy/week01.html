Jim Hawkins lived at a small inn by the sea with his mother.
One day, an old sailor called Billy Bones came to stay. He was rough and told scary stories about pirates.
When Billy Bones died, Jim looked inside his old wooden chest.
He found a map! It showed an island far away. Three red crosses marked where a pirate had buried his treasure.
Jim ran to tell Dr. Livesey and Squire Trelawney right away.
Jim Hawkins and his mother ran a small inn by the sea. One day, a rough old sailor called Billy Bones came to stay. He drank too much and watched the road with nervous eyes, as if he was scared of something.
When Billy Bones died suddenly, Jim searched his old sea chest and found a hand-drawn map. It showed a large island far away, with three red crosses and the words: "Treasure buried here."
Jim's hands were shaking as he looked at it. He took the map straight to Dr. Livesey and Squire Trelawney. They both looked at it for a long time. Then they looked at each other. An adventure was beginning.
짐 호킨스와 그의 어머니는 바닷가에서 작은 여관을 운영했어요. 어느 날, 빌리 본스라는 거친 늙은 선원이 묵으러 왔어요. 그는 술을 너무 많이 마시고 무언가 무서운 것이 있는 것처럼 초조한 눈으로 길을 바라봤어요.
빌리 본스가 갑자기 죽자, 짐은 그의 낡은 상자를 뒤져서 손으로 그린 지도를 찾았어요. 멀리 있는 큰 섬이 그려져 있었고, 세 개의 빨간 십자가와 "보물이 여기 묻혀 있음"이라는 글자가 있었어요.
짐은 그것을 보면서 손이 떨렸어요. 그는 바로 리브시 박사와 트렐로니 경에게 지도를 가져갔어요. 두 사람은 오랫동안 지도를 바라봤어요. 그리고 서로를 바라봤어요. 모험이 시작되고 있었어요.
