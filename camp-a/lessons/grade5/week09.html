READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
They shared the treasure.
Jim and his friends sailed home.
Long John Silver was gone.
Jim never forgot the island.
He was glad to be home.
They divided the treasure among the survivors. Captain Smollett, Dr. Livesey, and Squire Trelawney each got their share. Jim got his too.
Long John Silver had slipped away in the night with a small bag of gold. Nobody went after him.
The Hispaniola sailed home to Bristol. Jim was glad to leave the island behind.
Of Silver, they heard no more.
The gold was shared and the Hispaniola turned for home. In the night, Long John Silver vanished. He had taken a small portion of the gold and slipped overboard. No one was sorry to see him go -- and no one went after him.
Jim reached Bristol and went home to his mother. He was a different person from the boy who had found a treasure map in an old sailor's chest.
Of Silver, they heard no more. But Jim never stopped dreaming of the island.

짐은 브리스톨에 도착해서 엄마에게 돌아갔어요. 오래된 선원의 상자에서 보물지도를 발견한 소년과는 다른 사람이었어요.
실버에 대한 소식은 더 이상 듣지 못했어요. 하지만 짐은 섬을 꿈꾸는 것을 멈추지 않았어요.
