이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Fogg and his group reached America.
They took a train across the country.
The train was attacked by outlaws.
Passepartout was taken prisoner.
Fogg went back to rescue him again.
In America, Fogg and his companions boarded a transcontinental train from New York toward San Francisco. The journey was long but the schedule was clear.
Then the train was attacked. A group of outlaws stopped it and took Passepartout and several other passengers prisoner.
"We will make it in time," Fogg told Aouda calmly. Then he organised a rescue party, armed himself, and went after the outlaws on a sledge pulled across the snow.
He freed Passepartout. But the original train had gone. Fogg hired a wind-powered sledge and raced to Omaha, where he caught a different train east. He was now running dangerously close to his deadline.
The American continent had its own scale of danger. West of Chicago, the train moved through territory where the law was thin and outlaws operated openly. Fogg had calculated for the railways. He had not calculated for armed robbery.
The attack came at night. Passepartout was among those taken. Fogg listened to the situation, assessed it with characteristic precision, and announced: "We will make it in time."
He hired a sledge and a driver. They crossed the frozen landscape toward the outlaw camp and brought Passepartout back -- battered but alive.
The original train was gone. But Fogg found a wind-powered sledge that crossed the open plains faster than any locomotive. He reached Omaha, caught a train east, and settled back into his calculations.
The wager was still alive. The margin was now razor-thin.
미국 대륙에는 고유한 규모의 위험이 있었어요. 시카고 서쪽에서 기차는 법이 얇고 무법자들이 공개적으로 활동하는 지역을 통과했어요. 포그는 철도를 계산했어요. 무장 강도는 계산하지 않았어요.
공격은 밤에 왔어요. 파스파르투도 잡혀갔어요. 포그는 상황을 듣고 특유의 정확성으로 평가하고 발표했어요: "우리는 제때 도착할 거예요."
썰매와 운전사를 고용했어요. 얼어붙은 풍경을 가로질러 무법자 캠프로 가서 파스파르투를 데려왔어요 -- 두들겨 맞았지만 살아있었어요.
원래 기차는 떠났어요. 하지만 포그는 어떤 기관차보다 빠르게 넓은 평원을 가로지르는 바람 동력 썰매를 찾았어요. 오마하에 도착해서 동쪽으로 가는 기차를 잡고 다시 계산에 들어갔어요.
내기는 아직 살아있었어요. 여유는 이제 칼날처럼 얇았어요.
