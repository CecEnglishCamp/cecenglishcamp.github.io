이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Mole went into the Wild Wood.
It was dark and scary.
He heard strange sounds.
He ran but got lost.
Mole went into the Wild Wood alone. Rat had told him not to go, but Mole did not listen.
At first, it looked quiet. But soon the trees got dark and the path disappeared.
He heard strange sounds all around him. He thought he could see faces in the shadows.
"The Wild Wood is not a safe place," Mole said to himself.
He ran and ran, but he could not find the way out. He was lost and very frightened.
Mole went into the Wild Wood alone, even though Rat had warned him not to.
At first it seemed fine. But as the afternoon got darker, things changed. The trees pressed closer, and strange sounds came from the shadows. He thought he could see faces watching him from between the trees.
"The Wild Wood is not a safe place," Mole told himself — but it was too late.
He ran in every direction, but every path looked the same. His legs grew tired, but he kept running. Somewhere out there, he thought, Rat would be looking for him.
모울의 생각이었고, 거의 즉시 실수였다는 걸 깨달았어요. 쥐는 항상 와일드 우드는 피하는 게 좋다고 했었어요, 특히 겨울에 혼자서는 — 하지만 모울은 별로 믿지 않았어요.

숲은 처음에는 충분히 쾌적했어요. 하지만 오후 빛이 희미해지자 나무들이 더 가까이 다가오는 것 같았어요. 얼굴들이 시야 구석에서 나타났고, 휘파람 소리가 났고, 그 다음 발소리, 그 다음 그 어느 것보다 더 나쁜 침묵이 찾아왔어요. "와일드 우드는 안전한 곳이 아니야," 모울은 너무 늦게 스스로에게 말했어요. 그는 발이 느껴지지 않을 때까지 달렸고, 그리고도 더 달렸어요. 어딘가에서 쥐가 자신을 찾고 있다는 걸 알았어요. 그때까지 살아남기만 하면 됐어요.
