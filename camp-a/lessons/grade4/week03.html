이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Rat and Mole visited Toad.
Toad had a big new caravan.
"Let's travel!" said Toad.
He was very, very excited.
Rat took Mole to visit Mr. Toad. Toad lived in a big house called Toad Hall.
Toad had a new caravan. It was big and painted yellow and green.
"The open road!" cried Toad. "We can travel all over the country!"
He was jumping up and down with excitement.
Rat looked at Mole and shook his head. He had seen this before.
Rat took Mole to visit his friend Mr. Toad at Toad Hall — a very grand house by the river.
This time, Toad had a new hobby. He had a beautiful painted caravan, bright yellow and green, with everything a traveller could need inside.
"The open road!" Toad cried happily, spreading his arms wide. "Travel! Adventure! We leave tomorrow!"
Rat folded his arms and said nothing. He was fond of Toad, but he had seen these big ideas before — and he knew how they usually ended.
물쥐와 두더지가 친구 두꺼비(Mr. Toad)의 웅장한 저택 '두꺼비 홀(Toad Hall)'을 방문했어요. 두꺼비는 엄청난 부자인데 항상 새로운 취미에 빠져들었어요. 이번엔 자동차였어요!

두꺼비는 번쩍이는 새 자동차를 사서 시골길을 마구 달렸어요. "빵빵! 여행하는 유일한 방법은 자동차야!" 물쥐는 고개를 저었어요. "쟤는 항상 너무 심하게 간다니까."
