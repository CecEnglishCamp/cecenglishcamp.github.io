이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Mole went outside for a walk.
He found a big river.
He met a friendly rat.
"Hello!" said Rat. "Sit with me!"
Mole was cleaning his house. Then he stopped and went outside.
He walked across the fields and found a big river. It was beautiful!
He had never seen a river before. The water was sparkling and moving.
Then he saw a small brown face. It was Rat, who lived by the river.
"Hello!" said Rat with a big smile. "Come and sit with me!"
Mole had been cleaning his house all morning. Then he put down his brush and walked outside into the sunny day.
He walked across the fields until he came to a wide, sparkling river. He stopped and stared. He had never seen anything like it.
A small brown face appeared from the reeds near the bank. It was Rat, cheerful and friendly.
"Hullo!" said Rat. "Come and sit down — I was just going to have a rest. We can talk for a while!"
두더지는 아침 내내 집을 청소하고 있었어요. 그러다 솔을 내려놓고 밖으로 나갔어요.
들판을 걸어서 넓고 반짝이는 강가에 도착했어요. 멈춰 서서 바라봤어요. 이런 건 본 적이 없었어요.
강가 갈대 사이에서 작고 갈색인 얼굴이 나타났어요. 강가에 사는 쥐였어요.
"안녕!" 쥐가 말했어요. "와서 앉아 — 나도 막 쉬려던 참이야. 이야기 좀 하자!"
