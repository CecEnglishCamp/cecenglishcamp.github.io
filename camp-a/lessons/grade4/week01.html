Day 1 — Mole discovers the river

Mole was tired of digging. He looked up and saw the sun.

He wanted to explore. Mole went out and walked by the river.

He met Rat, who lived near the river.

Day 2 — Rat shows his boat

Rat was friendly and liked the river.

He showed Mole his boat.

Mole liked the boat. They decided to take a ride together.

Day 3 — Boat ride

Mole and Rat rode in the boat.

They saw trees, flowers, and animals.

Mole felt happy and excited.

He wanted to explore more of the river.

📖 오늘의 이야기 — 해석

Day 1
두더지는 땅파기가 너무 싫었어. 고개를 들어 햇빛을 봤지.
밖으로 나가고 싶었어. 두더지는 강가로 걸어갔어.
강 근처에 사는 물쥐를 만났어.

Day 2
물쥐는 친절하고 강을 좋아했어.
물쥐가 두더지에게 자기 배를 보여줬어.
두더지는 배가 마음에 들었어. 둘이 함께 타기로 했어.

Day 3
두더지와 물쥐는 배를 탔어.
나무, 꽃, 동물들이 보였어.
두더지는 행복하고 신났어.
강을 더 탐험하고 싶었어.
