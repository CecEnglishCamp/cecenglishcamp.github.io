이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Mole was cleaning his house.
He was very tired.
He ran outside into the field.

He walked and walked.
Then he saw a river!
He had never seen a river before.
A water rat lived by the river.
Mole was doing his spring cleaning when he suddenly felt tired of it all.
He dropped his brush and ran out into the sunny meadow.
He walked until he reached the river bank.

He had never seen a river before in his life.
The water sparkled and danced in the sunlight.
A brown little face appeared on the other side — it was the Water Rat!
"Hello, Mole!" he said. "Would you like to come in my boat?"
The Mole had been working very hard all morning on his spring cleaning.
Suddenly, something called to him — the smell of warm earth and fresh air.
He flung down his brush and bolted out of the house, racing across the meadow.

He came to the river bank and stopped, breathless with wonder.
He had never seen a river before — it was brand new to him.
The Water Rat appeared from his hole on the opposite bank.
"Ratty," said the Mole, "please, I want to know more about rivers."
두더지(Mole)는 봄맞이 대청소를 하다가 갑자기 지쳐버렸어요. 솔을 내팽개치고 밖으로 뛰어나가 들판을 달렸어요.

달리다 보니 강가에 도착했어요. 두더지는 태어나서 처음으로 강을 보았어요! 반짝이는 물결에 눈을 뗄 수 없었어요. 맞은편 둑에서 갈색 얼굴이 불쑥 나타났어요. 물쥐(Water Rat)였어요. "안녕, 두더지! 내 배에 타볼래?"
