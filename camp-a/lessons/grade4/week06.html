이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Rat found Mole in the Wild Wood.
They found a small door in the snow.
It said: Mr. Badger.
Badger let them in.
It was warm and safe inside.
Rat searched the Wild Wood and found Mole hiding under a tree root, cold and shaking.
Together they looked for shelter. Then Rat spotted a small door in a snowy bank — the home of Mr. Badger.
Badger opened the door. He looked a little grumpy at first, but when he saw his cold, wet friends, his face softened right away.
'Come in, come in!' he said warmly.
Badger's home was warm, dry, and full of good things. Mole felt safe at last.
Rat searched the Wild Wood and found Mole huddled under a tree root, shaking with cold and fear. Together they stumbled through the snow until Rat's foot struck something — a small door set into the bank.
Badger opened the door, looking grumpy at being disturbed. But one glance at his cold, frightened friends was enough — the door opened wide and they were brought inside.
The warmth hit Mole like a wave. Badger's home was warm, dry, and full of good things — long passages, cosy rooms, warm fires. Mole sat down in a chair by the fire and felt, at last, completely safe.
Rat found Mole shaking in the snow. Together they found a small door — Mr. Badger's home.
Badger opened the door and brought them inside. Warm fires, cosy rooms, and good things to eat.
Mole sat by the fire and felt completely safe at last.
