READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Rat had a small boat.
He took Mole on the river.
They had a big picnic.
Mole loved it.
It was a great day!
Rat invited Mole to come on his little boat for a picnic on the river.
Rat brought a big basket full of good things — sandwiches, chicken, and lemonade.
They floated slowly down the river. The sun was warm and everything was quiet.
Mole had never been on a boat before. He loved every moment of it.
It was the best day of Mole's life.
Rat invited Mole into his little boat for a picnic on the river. He had packed a big basket with sandwiches, cold chicken, lemonade, and many other good things.
They drifted slowly downstream in the afternoon sun while Rat talked happily about river life — the seasons, the animals, the quiet pleasures of living beside the water. Mole just listened and smiled.
By the end of the day, Mole felt that he had found the most wonderful place in the world — and the most wonderful friend.
쥐는 두더지를 강에서 소풍을 위해 작은 배에 태웠어요. 샌드위치, 차가운 닭고기, 레모네이드, 그리고 다른 좋은 것들이 가득 찬 큰 바구니를 챙겼어요.
그들은 오후 햇살 속에서 강을 따라 천천히 흘러갔어요. 쥐는 강 생활에 대해 즐겁게 이야기했어요 — 계절, 동물들, 물가에서 사는 조용한 즐거움에 대해. 두더지는 그냥 듣고 웃었어요.
하루가 끝날 때, 두더지는 세상에서 가장 멋진 곳 — 그리고 가장 멋진 친구를 찾은 것 같았어요.
