이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Rat invited Mole into his boat.
He had a big picnic basket.
There was lots of good food inside.

They floated down the river.
The sun was warm and bright.
Mole loved being on the river.
It was the best day of his life!
Rat invited Mole to join him in his boat for a picnic on the river.
He brought a large basket full of sandwiches, cold chicken, and lemonade.
As they drifted lazily downstream, Mole felt perfectly happy.

Rat explained his great love for the river.
"Believe me, there is nothing half so much worth doing as simply messing about in boats," he said.
Mole understood completely — the river was full of life, beauty, and endless surprises.
Rat fetched his little boat and invited Mole aboard for a day on the river.
A generous picnic basket was stowed beneath the seat, packed with all manner of good things.
They drifted gently downstream under the warm spring sun, the water whispering past the hull.

Rat spoke with quiet passion about his life on the river.
"There is nothing — absolutely nothing — half so much worth doing as simply messing about in boats," he declared.
Mole, who had never known such freedom, felt as though he had discovered a whole new world.
물쥐가 두더지를 배에 태워 강에서 피크닉을 하자고 초대했어요. 샌드위치, 닭고기, 레모네이드가 가득 든 커다란 바구니를 가져왔어요. 따뜻한 봄 햇살 아래 강물을 따라 천천히 내려가며 두더지는 완벽한 행복을 느꼈어요.

물쥐는 강에 대한 사랑을 이야기했어요. "배에서 노는 것만큼 가치 있는 일은 없어." 태어나서 이런 자유를 처음 느껴본 두더지는 새로운 세계를 발견한 것 같았어요.
