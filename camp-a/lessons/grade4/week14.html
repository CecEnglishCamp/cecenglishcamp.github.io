READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Dorothy brought the broomstick to the Wizard.
But the Wizard was not real magic.
Toto pulled back a curtain.
A small man was hiding there.
The Wizard was just an ordinary man.
Dorothy and her friends returned to the Emerald City with the witch's broomstick. They demanded to see the Wizard. He appeared again as a great booming voice and refused to help them straight away.
Then Toto the dog ran to a curtain in the corner and pulled it aside. Behind it stood a small, ordinary-looking man pulling levers and speaking into a machine.
"Pay no attention to that man behind the curtain!" the great voice boomed -- but it was too late.
The Wizard had been a fake all along. Dorothy was furious. But the Wizard turned out to be kind and wise in his own way, and he found a way to give each friend what they needed.
They had earned the broomstick at great cost. Dorothy placed it before the projected image of the Wizard and waited. The Wizard blustered and delayed, promising help while clearly intending to give none.
Then Toto, oblivious to the drama, trotted over to the curtain in the corner and nosed it aside.
A small bald man with a kind face was working frantically at a panel of switches and levers. The great booming voice was a machine. The giant floating head was a projection.
"Pay no attention to that man behind the curtain!" the machine thundered -- but everyone was already looking.
The Wizard of Oz was a fraud. But as Dorothy's fury subsided, she realised he was also the most human character she had met in the whole strange land: someone who had built a kingdom out of nothing but cleverness and nerve.
그들은 큰 대가를 치르고 빗자루를 얻었어요. 도로시는 마법사의 투영된 이미지 앞에 빗자루를 놓고 기다렸어요. 마법사는 도움을 약속하면서도 분명히 아무것도 줄 생각이 없었어요.
그때 토토가 구석의 커튼으로 다가가 코로 밀어냈어요.
친절한 얼굴을 가진 작은 대머리 남자가 스위치와 레버 패널 앞에서 열심히 작동하고 있었어요. 큰 울리는 목소리는 기계였어요. 거대한 떠다니는 머리는 투영이었어요.
"커튼 뒤의 그 남자는 신경 쓰지 마세요!" 기계가 울렸어요 -- 하지만 모두가 이미 보고 있었어요.
오즈의 마법사는 사기꾼이었어요. 하지만 도로시의 분노가 가라앉으면서, 그가 이상한 나라에서 만난 가장 인간적인 인물이라는 것을 깨달았어요: 영리함과 배짱만으로 왕국을 만든 사람.
