이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Toad drove his car too fast.
He crashed into a wall!
The car was broken.

Toad was not hurt.
But he was not sorry.
He just wanted a new car.
Rat and Mole were very angry with him!
Rat's prediction came true — Toad crashed his motor car into a ditch.
He had been speeding wildly when he lost control and smashed into a wall.
The car was completely wrecked.

Toad climbed out, shaken but unhurt.
Instead of feeling sorry, he was already dreaming about his next car.
"I'm done with cars forever!" he declared — but nobody believed him.
Rat and Mole tried to talk sense into him, but it was no use.
Just as Rat had feared, Toad's motor car adventure ended in disaster.
Speeding along a country lane, Toad lost control and ploughed into a ditch.
The car was a total wreck — twisted metal and shattered glass scattered across the road.

Toad crawled out, dishevelled but miraculously uninjured.
With breathtaking shamelessness, he declared: "I'm done with cars forever!"
Rat exchanged a weary glance with Mole. They both knew it was only a matter of time before Toad was behind the wheel of another motor car.
No lesson had been learned.
물쥐의 예상대로 결국 두꺼비가 자동차 사고를 냈어요. 시골 길을 과속으로 달리다가 통제를 잃고 도랑에 처박혔어요. 차는 완전히 박살났어요.

두꺼비는 기어 나왔어요. 다행히 다치지는 않았어요. 그런데 반성하기는커녕 "자동차랑 영원히 끝이야!"라고 선언했어요. 물쥐와 두더지는 서로 피곤한 눈빛을 교환했어요. 둘 다 알고 있었어요 — 곧 또 새 차를 살 거라는 것을.
