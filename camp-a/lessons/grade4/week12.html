이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Dorothy and friends reached the city.
Everything was green and bright.
They wore green glasses.
They met the great Wizard.
He agreed to help them.
After a long journey, Dorothy and her friends finally arrived at the Emerald City. Everything was green -- the buildings, the streets, the clothes of the people. Everyone wore green glasses so that the city looked even brighter.
The great Wizard of Oz agreed to see them. He appeared to each person differently -- a giant head, a beautiful woman, a ball of fire.
"I will help you," he said. "But first you must do something for me. Bring me the broomstick of the Wicked Witch of the West."
Dorothy and her friends looked at each other. Their journey was not over yet.
The Emerald City rose before them like something from a dream -- towers of green glass catching the sunlight, streets that gleamed underfoot, citizens in green robes and green-tinted spectacles. Dorothy felt her heart lift.
The Wizard received each visitor separately, appearing in a different form each time: a monstrous floating head for Dorothy, a beautiful fairy for the Scarecrow, a terrible beast for the Tin Man, and a ball of living fire for the Lion.
"The Emerald City was dazzling and green," Dorothy wrote in her head, trying to memorise everything.
"Help will come," said the Wizard to each of them, "when you have brought me the broomstick of the Wicked Witch of the West."
Four hearts sank -- but four friends turned toward the west together.
에메랄드 시티가 꿈속의 장면처럼 그들 앞에 솟아올랐어요 -- 햇빛을 받아 반짝이는 초록 유리 탑들, 발아래 빛나는 거리, 초록 옷과 초록색 안경을 쓴 시민들. 도로시는 가슴이 두근거렸어요.
마법사는 각 방문객을 따로따로 만났어요. 매번 다른 모습으로 나타났어요: 도로시에게는 거대한 떠다니는 머리, 허수아비에게는 아름다운 요정, 양철 나무꾼에게는 무서운 짐승, 사자에게는 살아있는 불덩이.
"에메랄드 시티는 눈부시도록 초록색이었어요," 도로시는 속으로 중얼거리며 모든 것을 기억하려 했어요.
"도움은 올 거예요," 마법사가 각자에게 말했어요. "서쪽의 나쁜 마녀의 빗자루를 가져오면."
네 개의 마음이 가라앉았어요 -- 하지만 네 친구는 함께 서쪽을 향해 돌아섰어요.
