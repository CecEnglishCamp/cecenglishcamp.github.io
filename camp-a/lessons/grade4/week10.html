READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Dorothy followed the yellow brick road.
She met a Scarecrow.
He had no brain.
He wanted to visit the Wizard.
They walked together.
Dorothy set off down the yellow brick road toward the Emerald City. The road was bright and easy to follow.
Soon she met a Scarecrow standing in a field. He could talk and move, but he felt sad.
"I have no brain," he said. "I cannot think properly."
Dorothy told him about the Wizard of Oz, who could grant any wish. The Scarecrow decided to come along.
"Follow the yellow brick road!" Dorothy said cheerfully, and they walked on together.
Dorothy walked along the yellow brick road, Toto at her heels, taking in the strange and colourful land around her. The road was easy to follow -- it gleamed in the sunlight like something out of a dream.
In a cornfield she spotted a Scarecrow, arms outstretched on his post. To her surprise, he blinked at her.
"I have no brain," he told her, sounding quite sorry for himself. "I cannot think at all."
Dorothy explained that the Wizard of Oz might be able to help. The Scarecrow's painted eyes lit up. He climbed down from his post without hesitation.
"Follow the yellow brick road!" Dorothy called, and they set off side by side.
