이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
The children wished for a real castle.
A castle appeared in their garden.
But soldiers came to attack it.
The children were trapped inside.
They had to wait for sunset.
Their fifth wish was for a real castle, right there in their garden. The Psammead granted it with a groan. An enormous stone castle appeared, complete with towers, a moat, and a drawbridge.
It was magnificent -- for about ten minutes. Then the local villagers noticed a castle had appeared where a house had been. They assumed it was an enemy fortification and sent for soldiers.
The children spent the afternoon barricaded inside, listening to soldiers trying to break down the gates. They could not explain the situation without being thought mad.
"A real castle came with real problems," Cyril said grimly. When sunset dissolved the castle around them, they found themselves sitting in their ordinary garden, surrounded by very confused soldiers.
A castle. An actual, full-scale medieval castle, complete with battlements, a moat, a portcullis, and a proper dungeon. It appeared between breakfast and lunch in the middle of their English country garden.
For thirty minutes, it was the most wonderful thing any of them had ever seen. Then the village constable arrived. Then a sergeant. Then a detachment of soldiers who had been told an enemy fortification had appeared from nowhere.
The children barricaded themselves in and tried to think. There was no way to explain a Psammead to soldiers. There was no way to explain a castle that had not existed an hour ago. Everything true sounded insane.
"A real castle came with real problems," Cyril observed, as arrows began to bounce off the outer walls.
Sunset arrived like a rescue. The castle dissolved. The children sat in the grass, blinking, while soldiers who had been trying to storm a fortress found themselves standing in a garden with five children and no castle at all.
성. 진짜, 완전한 규모의 중세 성. 흉벽, 해자, 내리닫이 격자문, 제대로 된 지하 감옥까지 갖춘. 아침과 점심 사이에 영국 시골 정원 한가운데 나타났어요.
30분 동안은 그들이 본 것 중 가장 멋진 것이었어요. 그런데 마을 경찰관이 왔어요. 그 다음에 하사관이. 그 다음에 아무데서도 나타난 적 방어 시설이 있다는 말을 들은 군인 부대가.
아이들은 자신들을 안에 가두고 생각하려 했어요. Psammead를 군인들에게 설명할 방법이 없었어요. 한 시간 전에 존재하지 않았던 성을 설명할 방법도 없었어요. 진실인 것은 모두 미친 소리처럼 들렸어요.
"진짜 성에는 진짜 문제가 따라왔어요," 사이릴이 화살이 외벽에 튕기기 시작하자 관찰했어요.
해질 녘이 구조처럼 찾아왔어요. 성이 사라졌어요. 아이들은 풀밭에 앉아 눈을 깜빡였어요. 요새를 공격하려 했던 군인들은 성 없이 정원에 다섯 아이와 함께 서 있는 자신들을 발견했어요.
