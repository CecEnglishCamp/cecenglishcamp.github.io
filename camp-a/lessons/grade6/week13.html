이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
The children made a fourth wish.
They wished the baby was grown up.
The baby became a tall young man.
He did not know his family at all.
It was very sad.
The children's fourth wish was for the baby -- the Lamb -- to be grown up. They were tired of looking after him and thought it would be easier if he could take care of himself.
The Psammead granted the wish. The baby became a tall, serious young man who did not know any of them. He looked at his brothers and sisters as strangers. He spoke politely but with no warmth.
The children had not thought about what it meant to grow up. Growing up without a childhood, without memories -- the Lamb was an adult with no history. The wish had changed everything. They spent the afternoon desperately wanting their real baby brother back.
When sunset came and the wish wore off, they hugged the baby so tightly he cried.
Their wish had seemed so reasonable at the time: make the Lamb a grown-up so he could look after himself. They were tired. He was heavy. He cried at inconvenient moments.
The Psammead granted it with particular reluctance. The baby became a tall, quiet young man who stood in the garden looking at them with courteous incomprehension. He knew his name because they told him. He did not know them.
The wish changed everything. Not just his size -- his entire identity had been constructed in a single moment, without experience, without love, without the slow accumulation of being known. He was an adult built from nothing.
The children spent the afternoon in silent misery, watching a stranger wear their brother's face. When sunset undid the wish and the Lamb tumbled back into their arms -- fat, furious, himself -- they held him as if they would never let go again.
"Some things," Anthea said, "should never be wished away."
소원은 그때 너무 합리적으로 보였어요: 램을 어른으로 만들어서 스스로 돌볼 수 있게 하자. 그들은 지쳤어요. 그는 무거웠어요. 불편한 순간에 울었어요.
모래요정은 특별한 마지못함으로 들어줬어요. 아기는 정원에 서서 그들을 정중한 이해 불능으로 바라보는 키가 크고 조용한 청년이 됐어요. 그들이 알려줘서 자신의 이름을 알았어요. 그들을 몰랐어요.
소원이 모든 것을 바꿔버렸어요. 키뿐만 아니라 -- 전체 정체성이 단 한 순간에 구축됐어요. 경험도, 사랑도, 알려지는 느린 축적도 없이. 아무것도 없는 상태에서 만들어진 어른이었어요.
아이들은 낯선 사람이 동생의 얼굴을 하고 있는 것을 바라보며 오후를 조용한 비참함 속에 보냈어요. 해질 녘이 소원을 취소하고 램이 팔 안으로 다시 돌아왔을 때 -- 통통하고, 화나고, 그 자신 -- 다시는 놓지 않겠다는 듯이 꼭 안았어요.
"어떤 것들은," 안시아가 말했어요, "절대 소원으로 없애면 안 돼요."
