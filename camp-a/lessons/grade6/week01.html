이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Sara Crewe was seven years old.
She came from India with her father.
Her father took her to a school in London.

The school was called Miss Minchin's.
Sara was very smart and kind.
Her father was very rich.
Sara had beautiful clothes and toys.
Sara Crewe was a seven-year-old girl who had lived in India all her life.
Her father, Captain Crewe, was a wealthy English officer.
He brought Sara to London to attend Miss Minchin's Select Seminary for Young Ladies.

Sara was unlike other children — she was thoughtful, imaginative, and very kind.
Miss Minchin welcomed Sara warmly because Captain Crewe paid well.
Sara received the best room and the finest clothes.
But Sara never forgot to be kind to everyone, rich or poor.
Sara Crewe had spent her entire childhood in India, in the warm company of her beloved father.
When Captain Crewe decided it was time for a proper English education, he brought Sara to London.
Miss Minchin's Select Seminary for Young Ladies stood cold and formal on a grey London street.

Miss Minchin herself was all smiles when she saw the wealthy captain and his well-dressed daughter.
Sara observed everything with calm, intelligent eyes.
She did not boast about her fine clothes or her father's wealth.
Instead, she quietly resolved to be kind to everyone — "Whatever comes, I can bear it."
사라 크루(Sara Crewe)는 일곱 살 소녀로 평생을 인도에서 살았어요. 아버지 크루 대위는 부유한 영국 장교였어요. 아버지는 사라에게 제대로 된 영국식 교육을 받게 하려고 런던으로 데려왔어요.

미스 민친의 기숙학교는 런던의 쌀쌀한 거리에 있었어요. 미스 민친은 부자 아버지를 보자 환하게 웃으며 반겼어요. 사라는 훌륭한 방과 아름다운 옷을 받았지만 절대 자랑하지 않았어요. "무슨 일이 닥쳐도, 나는 견딜 수 있어."
