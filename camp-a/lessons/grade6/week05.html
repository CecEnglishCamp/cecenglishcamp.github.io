이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Miss Minchin took everything from Sara.
Sara had to work at the school.
She got a small cold room at the top.
But Sara did not complain.
Everything changed for Sara in one day. Miss Minchin took away her nice room, her clothes, and her maid Mariette.
Now Sara had to work at the school to pay for her food and her place to sleep.
She was moved to a small, cold room at the very top of the house. The room had a hard bed and one small window.
She no longer slept in a beautiful room.
But Sara did not complain. She decided to be brave and keep her dignity, like a princess would.
Everything changed for Sara in a single day. Miss Minchin took away the room, the clothes, the maid — everything — and Sara had to start working at the school to pay for her place there.
Her new room was at the top of the house: small, cold, bare, with a hard bed and one tiny window that looked out over grey rooftops.
She no longer slept in a beautiful room, and everyone at the school knew it.
But Sara made a quiet decision. Miss Minchin could take away her things. She could not take away how Sara chose to behave. Sara would keep her dignity — not because it was easy, but because it was the one thing that still belonged entirely to her.
변화는 거의 비현실적으로 느껴질 만큼 빠르게 일어났어요. 하루는 사라가 학교에서 가장 특권 있는 소녀였어요. 다음 날, 미스 민친은 모든 것을 빼앗아버렸어요 — 방, 옷, 하녀, 수업, 지위를요.

사라는 다락방을 받았어요. 슬레이트 지붕 아래에 있었고, 겨울에는 몹시 추웠어요. 그녀는 더 이상 아름다운 방에서 자지 않았어요 — 기울어진 천장 아래 얇은 매트리스에서, 벽 속의 쥐 소리를 들으며 잤어요. 하지만 그녀는 미스 민친이 가져갈 수 없는 한 가지를 가지고 왔어요: 자신의 마음의 습관. 그녀는 텅 빈 작은 방을 바라보며 스스로에게 말했어요 — 자신이 상상하기로 선택하는 무엇이든 될 수 있다고.
