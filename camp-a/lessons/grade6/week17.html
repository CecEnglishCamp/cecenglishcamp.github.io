Mowgli grew up in the jungle. 🔊

On a dark night in the Indian jungle, the fearsome tiger Shere Khan descended upon a human village, scattering its inhabitants in terror. 🔊

In the chaos, a tiny human infant crawled away and stumbled deep into the jungle. 🔊

There, Father Wolf and Mother Wolf discovered the man-cub, as helpless as he was fearless. 🔊

Despite Shere Khan's furious protests, the wolf pack voted to accept Mowgli. 🔊

Baloo the bear and Bagheera the panther vouched for the boy, and so began one of literature's most extraordinary childhoods. 🔊
