이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Sara woke up one morning.
Something in the room was different.
There was warm food on the table.
The bed had a warm blanket.
Sara could not believe it.
One cold morning Sara woke up in her attic room. She stopped and looked around. Something had changed.
There was a small fire in the fireplace. There was food on the table — hot soup and fresh bread. The bed had a warm blanket on it.
Sara looked at everything carefully. Nobody had told her this would happen.
It was as if some kind magic had been at work. She sat down and ate slowly, and for the first time in a long time, she felt warm.
Sara woke to find her attic room transformed. A small fire burned in the fireplace. On the table was a hot meal — soup, bread, and fruit. The hard bed had a thick blanket. There were even flowers on the windowsill.
Sara stood in the middle of the room and turned slowly, taking it all in.
It was as if some kind magic had been at work, she thought quietly.
She did not yet know that Ram Dass had crept through the skylight in the night. But she knew one thing: somewhere nearby, someone was being kind.
사라는 다락방이 변한 것을 보며 깨어났어요. 난로에 불이 타고 있었어요. 식탁에는 따뜻한 음식이 있었어요. 두꺼운 담요가 침대에 놓여 있었어요.
사라는 방 한가운데 서서 천천히 돌아보며 모든 것을 받아들였어요.
마치 어떤 친절한 마법이 일어난 것 같았어요, 라고 조용히 생각했어요.
람 다스가 밤에 채광창을 통해 들어온 것을 아직 몰랐어요. 하지만 한 가지는 알았어요: 근처 어딘가에서 누군가가 친절을 베풀고 있다는 것을.
