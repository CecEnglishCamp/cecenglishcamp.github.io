Sara was popular at school.
She had nice things and a maid.
But Sara was kind to everyone.
She helped poor girls too.
Sara believed a real princess is kind.
Sara Crewe was well-liked at Miss Minchin's school. Her father was wealthy, so she had a nice room, pretty clothes, and her own maid named Mariette.
But Sara did not act like she was better than anyone else. She was kind to everyone — even poor girls with nothing.
Ermengarde had trouble with her lessons. Sara sat with her and helped her patiently. She also gave food to Becky, the school servant, and talked to her like a real friend.
Sara believed that a real princess is kind — not just rich.
Sara Crewe was the most admired girl at Miss Minchin's school. Her father's wealth gave her the finest room, beautiful clothes, and a French maid called Mariette.
But Sara did not think of these things as important. She was kind to everyone — even those others ignored.
She helped Ermengarde, who struggled with her lessons, sitting with her patiently when the other girls laughed. She befriended Becky, the school servant, slipping her food and treating her with genuine warmth.
Sara had a private belief: a real princess is not made of wealth or pretty clothes. A real princess is kind and keeps her dignity — especially when life is difficult.
사라 크루는 미스 민친의 학교에서 가장 존경받는 학생이었어요. 아버지의 재산 덕분에 가장 좋은 방, 아름다운 옷, 그리고 마리에트라는 프랑스 메이드가 있었어요.
하지만 사라는 이것들이 중요하다고 생각하지 않았어요. 다른 사람들이 무시하는 사람들에게도 친절했어요.
그녀는 수업이 너무 힘든 에르멩가르드를 도왔어요. 다른 학생들이 비웃을 때도 인내심 있게 옆에 앉아줬어요. 학교 하인인 베키와 친구가 됐고, 음식을 몰래 주며 진심으로 따뜻하게 대해줬어요.
사라는 조용히 믿는 것이 있었어요: 진짜 공주는 재산이나 예쁜 옷으로 만들어지지 않는다. 진짜 공주는 친절하고 품위를 지킨다 — 특히 삶이 힘들 때도.
