READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Sara came to school in London.
Her father was very rich.
Miss Minchin was very nice to them.
Sara was kind and quiet.
Sara Crewe came to Miss Minchin's school in London. Her father, Captain Crewe, brought her all the way from India.
Sara was only seven years old. She was quiet and kind, and she watched everything with her big grey eyes.
Miss Minchin smiled and was very warm to them.
She was not usually so nice to new students. But Sara's father looked very rich — and Miss Minchin liked rich parents very much.
Sara Crewe arrived at Miss Minchin's school in London on a cold morning. Her father, Captain Crewe, had brought her all the way from India.
Sara was seven years old and small. But there was something about her big grey eyes that made people look at her twice. She was quiet and watched everything carefully.
Miss Minchin greeted Captain Crewe with her warmest smile. She was kind to Sara too — but it was the heavy trunks and boxes being carried up the steps that made her smile the most. This father was clearly very, very rich — and that was always welcome at Miss Minchin's school.
사라 크루는 추운 아침 런던의 미스 민친 학교에 도착했어요. 아버지 크루 선장이 인도에서 멀리까지 데려다줬어요.
사라는 일곱 살이고 작았어요. 하지만 그녀의 크고 회색인 눈에는 사람들이 두 번 쳐다보게 만드는 무언가가 있었어요. 그녀는 조용하고 모든 것을 주의 깊게 바라봤어요.
미스 민친은 가장 따뜻한 미소로 크루 선장을 맞이했어요. 그녀는 사라에게도 친절했어요 — 하지만 미소를 짓게 한 것은 계단 위로 올라가는 무거운 트렁크와 상자들이었어요. 이 아버지는 분명히 매우, 매우 부유했어요 — 그리고 그것은 미스 민친의 학교에서 항상 환영받는 것이었어요.
