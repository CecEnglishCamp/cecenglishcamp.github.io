이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Five children found a magic creature.
It was called a Psammead.
It lived under the sand.
It could grant one wish a day.
The children were very excited.
Five children moved to a house in the country for the summer. One day they were digging in a sand pit when they found something unusual buried in the sand.
It was a small, round, furry creature with eyes on stalks. It said its name was a Psammead -- a sand fairy.
It can grant one wish a day, the oldest child said.
The children could not believe their luck. Each wish would last until sunset. They had so many plans.
Five children had moved to a house in the country while their parents were away. In the old sand pit behind the house, they dug up something extraordinary: a small, bad-tempered creature with eyes like a snail and fur like a very old cat. It was a Psammead, or sand fairy.
The Psammead could grant one wish per day. Each wish lasted until sunset. It hated granting wishes, and it made this perfectly clear. But it had no choice.
The children had no idea yet how much trouble a wish could cause.
다섯 아이들은 부모님이 없는 동안 시골 집으로 이사했어요. 집 뒤편 모래지에서 그들은 놀라운 생물을 파냈어요: 달팽이의 눈과 매우 늙은 고양이 털 같은 작고 성미 굳은 생물이었어요. 이것은 Psammead, 즉 모래요정이에요.
모래요정은 하루에 소원 하나를 들어줄 수 있었어요. 각 소원은 해질 녘까지 지속됩니다. 소원 이루어주는 것을 싫어했고 이를 아주 명확히 표현했어요.
아이들은 소원 하나가 얼마나 많은 마시를 무에 불러올 수 있는지 아직 엔리지 못했어요.
