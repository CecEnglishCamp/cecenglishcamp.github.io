이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
The children visited the Psammead one last time.
They asked what it really wanted.
The Psammead wanted to go to Egypt.
The children used their last wish wisely.
They said a sad goodbye.
Summer was ending and the children knew they would soon leave the house. They went to the sand pit to say goodbye to the Psammead.
For the first time, they asked it what it wanted, instead of asking for something themselves. The Psammead seemed surprised. It said it was lonely and would like to go to Egypt, where its kind had once lived.
The children used their final wish to send the Psammead to Egypt, where it would be happy among ancient sand and the pyramids.
"Goodbye," the Psammead said, sinking into the sand for the last time. "And be careful what you wish for."
The children stood at the edge of the pit for a long time. Then they went home, changed by everything the summer had given them.
The last day. The Psammead was smaller than they remembered, or perhaps they were simply larger -- summer has a way of doing that to children.
They had come to say goodbye. For once, they did not come with a wish. They came with a question: what did the Psammead want?
The question seemed to genuinely surprise it. In all its centuries of granting wishes, no one had ever asked. It wanted, it said after a pause, to go home -- to Egypt, to desert sand, to the ancient world where its kind had flourished and been understood.
The children granted this without hesitation. They used their last wish not for themselves but for the small bad-tempered creature that had changed their lives.
"Goodbye," the Psammead said, already sinking. "And be careful what you wish for."
It said the words without irony for the first time. It had meant them all along.
마지막 날. 모래요정은 기억했던 것보다 작았어요, 아니면 단순히 그들이 더 커진 것일 수도 있어요 -- 여름은 아이들에게 그런 일을 하는 경향이 있어요.
작별 인사를 하러 왔어요. 모처럼 소원을 들고 오지 않았어요. 질문을 가지고 왔어요: 모래요정은 무엇을 원하는가?
그 질문이 모래요정을 정말로 놀라게 한 것 같았어요. 수 세기 동안 소원을 들어주면서, 아무도 물어본 적이 없었어요. 잠시 후 말했어요, 집에 가고 싶다고 -- 이집트로, 사막 모래로, 동족이 번성하고 이해받던 고대 세계로.
아이들은 망설임 없이 이것을 들어줬어요. 마지막 소원을 자신들이 아니라 자신들의 삶을 바꿔놓은 작고 성미 굳은 생물을 위해 썼어요.
"안녕히," 모래요정이 이미 가라앉으며 말했어요. "그리고 무엇을 소원하는지 조심하세요."
처음으로 아이러니 없이 그 말을 했어요. 처음부터 진심이었어요.
