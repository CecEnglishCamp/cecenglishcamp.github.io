이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Sara lived in the attic now.
The room was cold and small.
But Sara had a big idea.

She used her imagination.
She pretended the room was beautiful.
She pretended she was a princess.
"If you pretend hard enough, things can seem real."
Sara smiled and felt better.
Sara's new room was cold, dark, and small. The bed was hard. The ceiling was low. There was one tiny window.

But Sara had a special power — her imagination. She looked around the bare room and began to pretend.
"This is not a cold attic," she told herself. "It is a room in a palace. The walls are covered in beautiful cloth. There is a warm fire."
"If you pretend hard enough, things can seem real," Sara said quietly.
She closed her eyes and smiled. The room did not change. But Sara felt a little warmer inside.
The attic was everything Sara had expected and nothing she had hoped for. Cold in winter, airless in summer, furnished with only a hard bed and a cracked mirror. The mice had been there before her and saw no reason to leave.

Yet Sara discovered something important on her very first night. The room could be changed — not in fact, but in her mind. She sat on the hard bed and looked at the bare walls, and she thought: what if this were a palace?
She began to fill it in, detail by detail. Soft rugs. Warm light. A fire that never went out.
"If you pretend hard enough, things can seem real," she told herself.
It was not happiness. But it was something close enough to survive on, and for now, that was what mattered.
다락방은 사라가 예상했던 모든 것이었고, 희망했던 것은 아무것도 없었어요. 겨울에는 춥고, 여름에는 답답하고, 딱딱한 침대와 금이 간 거울만 있었어요. 쥐들이 그녀보다 먼저 거기 있었고 떠날 이유를 못 느꼈어요.

하지만 사라는 첫날 밤에 중요한 것을 발견했어요. 방은 바뀔 수 있었어요 — 실제로가 아니라, 마음속에서요. 그녀는 딱딱한 침대에 앉아 텅 빈 벽을 바라보며 생각했어요: 이곳이 궁전이라면 어떨까? 그녀는 세부적으로 채우기 시작했어요. 부드러운 양탄자. 따뜻한 빛. 절대 꺼지지 않는 불. "충분히 상상하면, 것들이 진짜처럼 느껴질 수 있어," 그녀는 스스로에게 말했어요. 행복은 아니었어요. 하지만 살아남을 만큼 충분히 가까운 무언가였고, 지금으로선 그것이 중요했어요.
