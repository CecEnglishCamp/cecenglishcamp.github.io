이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Sara was popular at school.
She had nice clothes and her own maid.
But Sara was kind to everyone.
She helped girls who had nothing.
Sara was one of the most liked girls at Miss Minchin's school. Her father was rich, so she had a nice room, pretty clothes, and her own maid named Mariette.
But Sara did not act like she was better than anyone else. She was kind to everyone.
She helped a girl called Ermengarde who found lessons very hard. She was also friends with Becky, the school servant, and gave her food when she was hungry.
Sara believed that a real princess is kind — not just rich.
Sara was well-liked at Miss Minchin's school. Because her father was wealthy, she had the best room, beautiful clothes, and her own French maid called Mariette.
But Sara did not use these things to make herself feel important. She was genuinely kind to everyone — even the students others ignored.
She spent time with Ermengarde, who found her lessons very hard and felt ashamed about it. She also befriended Becky, the school servant, and brought her food when she could.
Sara had a quiet belief: a true princess is not made of money or fine clothes. A true princess is kind — and she stays kind even when things are hard.
사라 크루는 미스 민친의 기숙학교에서 특별한 위치를 차지하고 있었어요. 아버지의 상당한 재산 덕분에 가장 좋은 방, 예쁜 옷, 그리고 매일 아침 옷을 입혀주는 프랑스인 하녀 마리에트가 있었어요.

하지만 사라를 진정으로 특별하게 만든 것은 소유물이 아니라 그녀의 성품이었어요. 미스 민친에게 보여주는 것과 같은 예의를 주방 하녀들에게도 베풀었고, 어멀라이드가 프랑스어를 어려워해도 절대 바보같다고 느끼게 만들지 않았어요. 사라는 자신만의 철학이 있었어요 — 어떤 소녀라도, 어떤 상황에 처하든, 공주의 품격을 선택할 수 있다고 믿었어요: 품위, 우아함, 그리고 연민. "나는 공주야, 누더기를 입어도." 그녀는 어느 날 오후 어멀라이드에게 조용한 확신으로 말했어요. 자랑이 아니었어요. 그것은 맹세였어요 — 그녀가 알고 있는 것보다 훨씬 더 중요해질.
