이야기 읽기
READING · 수준을 골라 읽어보세요
Sara's father had a secret plan.
He invested his money in diamond mines in India.
He thought the mines would make him very rich.

He told Sara about the mines.
Sara was excited and happy.
Her father said, "We will have more money than ever!"
Sara believed him completely.
The future looked bright and wonderful.
Sara's father, Captain Crewe, had invested nearly all of his money in diamond mines in India. He had partnered with an old friend named Mr. Carrisford. Both men believed the mines would make them enormously wealthy.

When Captain Crewe visited Sara at the school, he spoke about the mines with great excitement. His eyes shone as he described the diamonds waiting to be discovered deep underground.
"Captain Crewe believed it would make him rich," Sara later told Ermengarde with a smile.
Sara was too young to worry. She trusted her father completely, and the future seemed full of glittering promise.
The foundation of the Crewe fortune — and of Sara's comfortable life at Miss Minchin's — rested upon a single, enormous gamble. Captain Crewe had invested nearly everything he owned in a set of diamond mines in India, entering the venture with his closest friend, Mr. Carrisford.

The captain spoke of the mines with barely contained excitement whenever he visited Sara. His letters were full of optimism. He described the tunnels, the shafts, the labourers, and the extraordinary richness of the seams they had found. It was only a matter of time, he assured her, before the diamonds would pour forth in abundance.
"Captain Crewe believed it would make him rich," Sara recalled later — and she had believed it too, because she had believed in him.
Neither father nor daughter could have imagined how completely that faith would be tested.
크루 대위는 사라의 편안한 생활의 기반을 인도의 다이아몬드 광산에 걸었어요. 그는 가장 친한 친구인 카리소파티 씨와 함께 가진 재산의 거의 전부를 그 사업에 투자했어요.

사라를 방문할 때마다 아버지는 억제하기 어려운 흥분으로 광산 이야기를 했어요. 편지는 낙관적인 말들로 가득했어요. 그는 터널, 갱도, 광부들, 그리고 발견한 광맥의 놀라운 풍부함을 설명했어요. 조금만 기다리면 다이아몬드가 풍부하게 쏟아져 나올 거라고 확신했어요. "크루 대위는 그것이 자신을 부자로 만들어줄 것이라 믿었다"라고 사라는 나중에 회상했어요 — 그녀도 그렇게 믿었어요, 왜냐하면 아버지를 믿었으니까요. 아버지도 딸도 그 믿음이 얼마나 철저히 시험받게 될지는 상상도 못했어요.
