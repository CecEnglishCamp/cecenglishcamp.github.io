1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

ELLIPSISEllipsis and Substitution

Ellipsis: 반복되는 단어나 구를 생략하여 문장을 간결하게 만듦
Substitution: 반복되는 표현을 다른 단어나 구조로 대체함
문장 응집력 향상, 회화 간결화, 시험 응답 간소화에 효과적

Ellipsis: I can play the guitar, and she can ___.

Substitution: I like apples. **So does he.** / I’ll take the **red one.**

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

  • Ellipsis (생략):
    • 조동사/동사 생략: I can swim, and she can **___.**
    • to부정사 생략: He wants to go, and I want **to ___.**
    • 명사구 생략: I’ll take the red shirt, and you take the blue **___.**
  • Substitution (대용):
    • do/does/did: She likes tea. So **do** I.
    • so/neither/too: I’m tired. **So is he**. / I’m not hungry. **Neither am I**.
    • one/ones (명사 대용): I’ll take the red **one**. / These shoes are better than those **ones**.
    • that/those (지시 대용): I prefer **that**. / **Those** are mine.
  • Substitution은 문맥에 따라 정확한 대명사/조동사 선택이 필요
  • Ellipsis는 반복 제거를 통해 문장을 간결하고 유기적으로 만듦

3. 긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

Ellipsis

  • I can play the guitar, and she **can** ___.
  • He wants to go, and I **want to** ___.
  • I’ll take the red shirt, and you take the **blue** ___.
  • She’s reading a book, and he **is** ___.
  • They were laughing, and we **were** ___ too.

Substitution

  • I like apples. **So do I**. (나도 그래)
  • She is tired. **So is he**. (그도 그래)
  • I’m not hungry. **Neither am I**. (나도 안 그래)
  • He plays soccer. **I do too**. (나도 해)
  • I’ll take the **red one**. (빨간 것)
  • These are better than **those**. (저것들)
  • She wants **that**. I want **this**. (저것/이것)
  • He likes tea. **So does she**. (그녀도 그래)
  • I didn’t go. **Neither did they**. (그들도 안 갔어)
  • We chose the big **ones**. (큰 것들)

5. 상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황
A: I like math.
B: **So do I.**
A: Let’s study together.

여행 상황
A: I want to visit Paris.
B: I want **to** ___.
A: Maybe next summer.

직장 상황
A: She finished the report.
B: He **did** ___ as well.
A: Great teamwork.

온라인 상황
A: I bought the red phone.
B: I’ll take the **blue one**.
A: Good choice.

가족 상황
A: I’m not hungry.
B: **Neither am I.**
A: Let’s eat later.

병원 상황
A: She’s feeling better.
B: He **is** ___ too.
A: That’s good news.

6. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

  • I like apples. He likes apples too. → I like apples, and **so does he**.
  • I’m not hungry. You’re not hungry. → I’m not hungry, and **neither are you**.
  • She can swim. He can swim. → She can swim, and **he can too**.
  • I want to go. You want to go. → I want to go, and **so do you**.
  • I’ll take the red shirt. You’ll take the blue shirt. → I’ll take the red shirt, and **you’ll take the blue one**.
  • He bought the small phone. I bought the big phone. → He bought the small phone, and **I bought the big one**.
  • She is tired. He is tired. → She is tired, and **so is he**.
  • I didn’t go. They didn’t go. → I didn’t go, and **neither did they**.
  • We chose the red shoes. They chose the blue shoes. → We chose the red shoes, and **they chose the blue ones**.
  • I prefer this. You prefer that. → I prefer this, and **you prefer that**.

7. 실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

  • 학교 상황: I finished my homework. So did she.
  • 직장 상황: He submitted the report. I did too.
  • 가족 상황: I’m not hungry. Neither is Dad.
  • 온라인 상황: I’ll buy the red phone. You take the blue one.
  • 병원 상황: She feels better. So does he.
  • 여행 상황: I want to visit Paris. So do they.
  • 회의 상황: We agree with the plan. So does the manager.

8. 자주 쓰이는 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • So + 조동사 + 주어 → 긍정 동의 (So do I)
  • Neither + 조동사 + 주어 → 부정 동의 (Neither am I)
  • 주어 + 조동사 + too → 긍정 동의 (I do too)
  • Ellipsis with modal: She can swim, and he can ___.
  • Ellipsis with to: He wants to go, and I want to ___.
  • Substitution with one/ones: I’ll take the red one / the blue ones
  • Substitution with that/those: I prefer that / Those are mine
  • Negative agreement: I didn’t go. Neither did he.
  • Positive agreement: I like tea. So does she.
  • Ellipsis with be: She is tired, and he is ___ too.

9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ I like apples. So he does. → ✅ I like apples. So does he.
  • ❌ I’m not hungry. Neither I am. → ✅ Neither am I.
  • ❌ She can swim, and he can swim too. → ✅ She can swim, and he can too.
  • ❌ I’ll take the red shirt, and you take the blue shirt. → ✅ I’ll take the red shirt, and you take the blue one.
  • ❌ He didn’t go. Neither he did. → ✅ Neither did he.

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • Ellipsis는 반복 제거, Substitution은 대체 표현
  • 조동사(do/does/did/can/will 등)를 정확히 선택해야 함
  • 긍정 동의: So do I / I do too
  • 부정 동의: Neither do I / I don’t either
  • one/ones는 명사 대체, that/those는 지시 대명사로 사용
  • 문맥에 따라 생략 위치와 대체 표현이 달라짐
  • 문장 결합 연습을 통해 자연스러운 응집력 향상

11. 연습문제 (Practice Exercises)

    A. So he does.
    B. So does he.
  2. Choose the correct form:
    I’m not hungry. ___ am I.
    A. Neither
    B. So
  3. Fill in: I’ll take the red shirt. You take the blue ___.
    A. one
    B. it

1. B. 'So'를 이용한 긍정 동의 구문은 'So + 조동사 + 주어'의 도치 형태를 사용합니다. (So does he)

2. A. 'Neither'는 부정문 동의에 사용되며 'Neither + 조동사 + 주어' 형태를 따릅니다. ('So'는 긍정 동의에 사용)

3. A. 명사 'shirt'의 반복을 피하기 위해 대용어 'one'을 사용합니다. ('it'은 특정 사물을 지칭)
