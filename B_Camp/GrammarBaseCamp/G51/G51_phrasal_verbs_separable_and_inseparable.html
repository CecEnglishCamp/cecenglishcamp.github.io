1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

PVPHRASAL VERBS

동사 + 전치사/부사 조합으로 새로운 의미를 만드는 표현
예: turn off, look after, give up, run into
원래 동사의 의미와 다른 독특한 의미를 가짐

turn off (끄다) - I turned off the light.

look after (돌보다) - She looks after her baby.

give up (포기하다) - I gave up smoking.

run into (우연히 만나다) - I ran into my friend.

Separable vs Inseparable

Separable (분리 가능)

turn off the light
turn the light off
turn it off (대명사는 반드시 중간)

목적어가 중간 또는 뒤에 올 수 있음

Inseparable (분리 불가)

look after the baby
look after him
❌ look the baby after

목적어가 항상 뒤에 와야 함

중요한 규칙

  • 대명사 목적어일 경우: Separable은 반드시 중간에 넣음
  • turn it off (✅) vs turn off it (❌)
  • pick it up (✅) vs pick up it (❌)
  • Inseparable은 대명사도 항상 뒤에: look after him

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

Separable 구동사

  • turn off, pick up, give back, take out, put on
  • 동사 + 목적어 + 부사 → turn the light off
  • 동사 + 부사 + 목적어 → turn off the light
  • 대명사 목적어 → 반드시 중간 → turn it off

Inseparable 구동사

  • look after, run into, get over, come across, rely on
  • 동사 + 전치사 + 목적어 → look after the baby
  • 대명사도 뒤에 → look after him
  • 절대로 분리할 수 없음

사용 원칙

  • 구동사는 의미 유추가 어려우므로 암기와 문맥 연습 필요
  • 일상 대화에서 매우 자주 사용됨
  • 대명사 목적어 위치 규칙을 반드시 지켜야 함
  • 예문을 통해 자연스러운 사용법 익히기

3. 예시 문장 (Example Sentences)

Separable 구동사

  • I turned off the light. / I turned the light off. / I turned it off.
  • She picked up the phone. / She picked the phone up. / She picked it up.
  • We gave back the book. / We gave the book back. / We gave it back.
  • He took out the trash. / He took the trash out. / He took it out.
  • They put on their jackets. / They put their jackets on. / They put them on.
  • I threw away the paper. / I threw the paper away. / I threw it away.
  • She filled out the form. / She filled the form out. / She filled it out.
  • We set up the equipment. / We set the equipment up. / We set it up.
  • He cleaned up the room. / He cleaned the room up. / He cleaned it up.
  • They brought back the car. / They brought the car back. / They brought it back.

Inseparable 구동사

  • I looked after the baby. / I looked after him.
  • She ran into her friend. / She ran into him.
  • We got over the problem. / We got over it.
  • He came across a great idea. / He came across it.
  • They relied on their teacher. / They relied on her.
  • I looked for my keys. / I looked for them.
  • She listened to the music. / She listened to it.
  • We went through the documents. / We went through them.
  • He looked forward to the trip. / He looked forward to it.
  • They came up with a solution. / They came up with it.

5. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황
A: Did you turn off the projector?
B: Yes, I turned it off.
A: Good. Did you give back the textbook?
B: I gave it back to the teacher.
여행 상황
A: I ran into an old friend in Paris.
B: Really? Did you talk to him?
A: Yes, we got over our past issues.
B: That's great.
직장 상황
A: Did you take out the trash?
B: Yes, I took it out before lunch.
A: Did you pick up the package?
B: I picked it up from the lobby.
온라인 상황
A: Did you turn off your camera?
B: Yes, I turned it off during the break.
A: Did you log out?
B: Not yet.
가족 상황
A: Can you look after the baby?
B: Sure, I'll look after him while you cook.
A: Thanks. Did you put on his shoes?
B: Yes, I put them on.
병원 상황
A: Did the nurse take out the needle?
B: Yes, she took it out gently.
A: Did she look after the patient?
B: She looked after him very well.

7. 실생활 사용 (Real-Life Usage)

이메일 안내

I gave it back to the manager. She looked over the report and sent it out.

자기소개

I often pick up ideas from books. I also run into interesting people at events.

업무 회의

We turned off the system at 5 PM. The technician looked after the server overnight.

여행 후기

I gave back the rental car on time. We came across a hidden beach on the way.

건강 상담

The nurse looked after me well. She took out the needle gently and put on a bandage.

11. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

구동사 마스터 팁

  • Separable 구동사: 목적어가 대명사일 경우 반드시 중간에 넣기
  • Inseparable 구동사: 목적어는 항상 뒤에 위치
  • 구동사 의미는 문맥 속에서 유추해야 함
  • 자주 쓰이는 구동사부터 패턴으로 암기하기
  • 구동사는 일상 대화, 이메일, 업무 보고에서 자주 등장
  • 구동사 사전적 의미보다 예문 중심 학습이 효과적
  • 구동사 + 대명사 위치 오류는 시험에서 자주 출제
  • 구동사 리스트를 Separable / Inseparable로 나눠 정리해두기
  • 실제 대화에서 자연스럽게 쓰는 연습이 중요
