1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

RSREPORTED SPEECH

누군가의 말을 간접적으로 전달하는 표현
직접인용문의 따옴표를 제거하고 시제, 인칭, 어순을 변경
뉴스, 회의, 대화 요약에서 자주 사용됨

Direct: "I'm tired," she said.

Reported: She said she was tired.

Direct: "Where are you?" he asked.

Reported: He asked where I was.

문장 유형별 구조

  • 평서문: say/tell + (that) + 주어 + 동사
  • 의문사 질문: ask/wonder + 의문사 + 주어 + 동사
  • Yes/No 질문: ask + if/whether + 주어 + 동사
  • 명령문: tell/ask + 목적어 + to 부정사

Say vs Tell 차이

  • say: 목적어 없이 사용 가능 → She said she was tired.
  • tell: 반드시 목적어 필요 → She told me she was tired.
  • ask: 질문에 사용 → He asked if I was ready.

3. 예시 문장 (Example Sentences)

평서문 (Statements)

  • "I'm tired." → She said she was tired.
  • "We are happy." → They said they were happy.
  • "He works hard." → She said he worked hard.
  • "I have finished." → He said he had finished.
  • "We will go." → They said they would go.
  • "I can help." → She said she could help.
  • "I like pizza." → He said he liked pizza.
  • "She is studying." → He said she was studying.
  • "I saw the movie." → She said she had seen the movie.
  • "We may join." → They said they might join.
  • "I'm working late." → He said he was working late.
  • "The meeting starts at 3." → She said the meeting started at 3.

의문문 (Questions)

  • "Where are you?" → He asked where I was.
  • "What do you want?" → She asked what I wanted.
  • "Did you see it?" → He asked if I had seen it.
  • "Can you help me?" → She asked if I could help her.
  • "Why is he late?" → They asked why he was late.
  • "Do you like it?" → He asked if I liked it.
  • "When will you come?" → She asked when I would come.
  • "Are you ready?" → He asked if I was ready.
  • "Have you finished?" → She asked if I had finished.
  • "What is your name?" → He asked what my name was.
  • "How old are you?" → She asked how old I was.
  • "Which one do you prefer?" → He asked which one I preferred.

5. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황
A: What did the teacher say?
B: She said we had to finish the homework.
A: Did she ask when we would submit it?
B: Yes, she asked if we could send it by Friday.
여행 상황
A: Did he say where the hotel was?
B: He said it was near the station.
A: Did he ask if we had booked it?
B: Yes, and he asked when we would arrive.
직장 상황
A: What did the manager say?
B: He said the deadline was Monday.
A: Did he ask why the report was late?
B: Yes, and he asked if we needed help.
온라인 상황
A: Did she say anything in the chat?
B: She said she was busy.
A: Did she ask if we could wait?
B: Yes, she asked when we would be ready.
가족 상황
A: What did Mom say?
B: She said dinner was ready.
A: Did she ask if we were hungry?
B: Yes, and she asked what we wanted to eat.
병원 상황
A: What did the doctor say?
B: He said I needed rest.
A: Did he ask if you had taken the medicine?
B: Yes, and he asked how I felt.

7. 실생활 사용 (Real-Life Usage)

이메일 보고

The manager said the deadline was Friday. He asked if we had finished the draft.

자기소개

I told them I was from California. They asked why I moved to Korea.

업무 회의

She said the client wanted changes. We asked what the new requirements were.

여행 후기

The guide said the museum opened at 10. I asked if photos were allowed.

건강 상담

The doctor said I needed more rest. He asked how I was sleeping.

8. 자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴 (Common Patterns)

패턴별 정리

11. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

간접화법 마스터 팁

  • 시제 일치표를 외워두면 변환이 쉬움
  • 의문문은 do/does/did 제거 + 어순 변경
  • "say"는 목적어 없이 사용 가능 / "tell"은 목적어 필요
  • Yes/No 질문은 if / whether로 시작
  • 인칭 변화 주의: I → he/she, my → his/her
  • 간접화법은 보고, 회의, 대화 요약에 자주 사용됨
  • 직접화법 → 간접화법 변환 시 시제, 인칭, 어순 모두 고려
  • 의문사 질문은 what / where / why / when / how로 시작
  • 간접화법은 정중하고 간결한 표현으로 유용함
