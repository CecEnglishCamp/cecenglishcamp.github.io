1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

ARTARTICLES

명사 앞에 오는 a / an / the / 무관사 표현
명사의 특정성과 수량을 나타내는 중요한 문법 요소
처음 언급, 반복 언급, 일반 개념을 구분하는 핵심 도구

First mention: I saw a dog.

Repeated: The dog was cute.

Profession: She is a teacher.

General concept: I like music. (no article)

관사의 3가지 유형

a / an

처음 언급 / 하나의

a + 자음 시작

an + 모음 시작

I saw a dog.

She has an idea.

the

특정 / 반복 언급 / 유일한

이미 언급된 것

세상에 하나뿐인 것

The dog was cute.

The sun is bright.

무관사 (—)

일반 개념 / 추상 명사

식사, 장소, 언어

복수 명사의 일반적 개념

I like music.

We had lunch.

관사 선택 기준

a / an
처음 언급
하나의 개체
a book, an apple
the
특정 대상
반복 언급
the book, the sun
—
일반 개념
추상 명사
music, lunch

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

a / an 사용법

  • 처음 언급하는 가산명사 단수 → I saw a dog.
  • 직업 표현 → She is a teacher. / He is an engineer.
  • a + 자음 시작 명사 → a book, a car, a university
  • an + 모음 시작 명사 → an apple, an idea, an hour
  • 수량 표현 → a few, a little, a lot of

the 사용법

  • 반복 언급 → I saw a cat. The cat was black.
  • 특정 대상 → The book on the table is mine.
  • 세상에 하나뿐인 것 → the sun, the moon, the Earth
  • 최상급 → the best, the most important
  • 서수 → the first, the second

무관사 사용법

  • 식사 → We had breakfast / lunch / dinner.
  • 장소 (일반적 목적) → He is at school / work / home.
  • 언어 → She speaks English / Korean.
  • 과목 → I study math / history.
  • 추상 명사 → I love music / art.
  • 고유명사 → Mount Everest, Lake Tahoe

5. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황
A: I met a new student today.
B: Really? What's the student's name?
A: His name is Leo. He speaks English very well.
B: Is he a freshman?
A: Yes, he is.
여행 상황
A: We saw a castle in France.
B: Was the castle big?
A: Yes, and we had lunch nearby.
B: Did you take photos?
A: Of course!
직장 상황
A: I met an engineer from Germany.
B: What does the engineer do?
A: He designs software.
B: That's cool.
온라인 상황
A: I joined a webinar yesterday.
B: What was the topic?
A: It was about education and technology.
B: Sounds interesting.
가족 상황
A: We had dinner at home.
B: Did you cook the meal?
A: Yes, I made pasta.
B: I love Italian food.
병원 상황
A: I saw a doctor this morning.
B: Was the doctor helpful?
A: Yes, and I got medicine.
B: Hope you feel better soon.

6. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

처음 언급 → 반복 언급 연습

I saw a dog. The dog was cute.
→ I saw a dog, and the dog was cute.
She bought a book. The book is on the table.
→ She bought a book, and the book is on the table.
He met a student. The student is from Korea.
→ He met a student who is from Korea.
We visited a museum. The museum was amazing.
→ We visited a museum that was amazing.
I had lunch. Lunch was delicious. (무관사)
→ I had lunch, and it was delicious.
She speaks English. English is her first language. (무관사)
→ She speaks English, which is her first language.
He is a teacher. The teacher works at the school.
→ He is a teacher who works at the school.
I need a plan. The plan must be simple.
→ I need a plan that is simple.
They saw a movie. The movie was exciting.
→ They saw a movie that was exciting.
She has an idea. The idea is brilliant.
→ She has an idea that is brilliant.

7. 실생활 사용 (Real-Life Usage)

이메일 보고

I met a client yesterday. The client wants to revise the proposal.

자기소개

I'm a software engineer. I work at the company near San Jose. I love technology.

업무 회의

We discussed a new strategy. The strategy focuses on customer feedback.

여행 후기

We visited a temple in Kyoto. The temple was peaceful. We had lunch nearby.

건강 상담

I saw a doctor last week. The doctor recommended exercise and healthy meals.

8. 자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴 (Common Patterns)

패턴별 정리

a / an - 처음 언급 a dog, a book, an apple, an idea
a / an - 직업 She is a doctor. He is an engineer.
the - 반복 언급 I saw a cat. The cat was black.
the - 유일한 것 the sun, the moon, the Earth
the - 특정 대상 the book on the table
무관사 - 식사 breakfast, lunch, dinner
무관사 - 장소 at school, at work, at home
무관사 - 언어 speak English, learn Korean
무관사 - 과목 study math, teach history
무관사 - 추상명사 love music, enjoy art
수량 표현 a few, a little, a lot of
고유명사 Mount Everest, Lake Tahoe (무관사)

11. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

관사 마스터 팁

  • a / an: 처음 언급, 하나의 개체
  • the: 반복 언급, 특정 대상, 유일한 것
  • 무관사: 일반 개념, 식사, 장소, 언어, 과목
  • 직업 표현에는 a / an 사용
  • 고유명사에는 무관사 사용
  • 문맥에 따라 관사 선택이 달라짐 → 항상 전체 문장을 고려
  • 관사 생략은 패턴 중심으로 익히기
  • 반복 언급 시 반드시 the로 바꾸기
  • 관사는 정확한 의미 전달에 매우 중요
