1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

LINKLINKING WORDS

문장과 문장을 연결해 의미를 확장하는 표현
however: 대조 (그러나, 반면에)
therefore: 결과 (그러므로, 그래서)
although: 양보 (비록 ~이지만)

대조: It was raining. However, we went out.

결과: He studied hard. Therefore, he passed.

양보: Although it was raining, we went out.

although
양보 • 인정

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

however (대조)

  • 문장 앞: However, we continued. → 쉼표 필요
  • 문장 중간: We continued, however, it was difficult. → 쉼표 양쪽
  • 문장 사이: It was hard. However, we tried. → 쉼표 뒤에

therefore (결과)

  • 문장 앞: Therefore, we succeeded. → 쉼표 필요
  • 문장 중간: We worked hard, therefore, we succeeded. → 쉼표 양쪽
  • 문장 사이: It was hard. Therefore, we tried harder. → 쉼표 뒤에

although (양보)

  • 문장 앞: Although it was late, we stayed. → 쉼표 중간
  • 문장 중간: We stayed although it was late. → 쉼표 없음

쉼표 사용법 가이드

✓ However, we went out.
✓ Therefore, he passed.
✓ Although it rained, we stayed.
✗ However we went out.
✗ Although it rained. We stayed.

3. 예시 문장 (Example Sentences)

however (대조)

  • It was cold. However, we went hiking.
  • She is shy. However, she spoke clearly.
  • He failed once. However, he didn't give up.
  • We were tired. However, we finished the task.
  • It was expensive. However, we bought it.
  • The test was hard. However, I tried my best.
  • Traffic was heavy. However, we arrived on time.
  • The weather was bad. However, we enjoyed the trip.

therefore (결과)

  • He studied hard. Therefore, he passed the test.
  • It rained. Therefore, the game was canceled.
  • She practiced daily. Therefore, she improved.
  • We arrived early. Therefore, we got good seats.
  • They worked together. Therefore, they succeeded.
  • The roads were icy. Therefore, we drove slowly.
  • She forgot her keys. Therefore, she couldn't get in.
  • The restaurant was full. Therefore, we waited outside.

although (양보)

  • Although it was raining, we went out.
  • Although he was tired, he kept working.
  • Although she was nervous, she performed well.
  • Although it was late, we stayed longer.
  • Although they disagreed, they cooperated.
  • Although the movie was long, it was interesting.
  • Although he was young, he was very wise.
  • Although the task was difficult, we completed it.

7. 실생활 사용 (Real Life Usage)

📧 이메일 보고

The project was delayed. However, the team remained focused. Therefore, we met the final deadline.

Although we faced challenges, we delivered quality results.

👋 자기소개

I'm shy. However, I enjoy public speaking. Although I get nervous, I prepare well. Therefore, I often receive positive feedback.

💼 업무 회의

We lacked resources. However, we used creative solutions. Therefore, the client was satisfied. Although the timeline was short, we delivered.

✈️ 여행 후기

It rained every day. However, we explored the city. Although the weather was bad, the food was amazing. Therefore, we had a great time.

🏥 건강 상담

The patient was weak. However, he followed instructions. Therefore, he recovered quickly. Although the medicine tasted bad, he took it daily.

🎓 학습 경험담

English was difficult for me. However, I studied every day. Although I made many mistakes, I kept practicing. Therefore, I improved significantly.

🏃 운동 일기

I was out of shape. However, I started exercising regularly. Although it was hard at first, I didn't give up. Therefore, I'm much healthier now.

8. 표현 패턴 (Expression Patterns)

자주 사용되는 패턴

however (대조)

It was difficult. However, we tried.

She is shy. However, she spoke clearly.

therefore (결과)

He studied hard. Therefore, he passed.

We arrived early. Therefore, we got good seats.

although (양보)

Although it was raining, we went out.

Although she was tired, she kept working.

쉼표 위치

However, we continued. (앞)

Although it was late, we stayed. (중간)

문장 중간 사용

We continued, however, it was hard.

We stayed although it was late.

복합 사용

Although it rained, we went out. However, we got wet. Therefore, we went home early.

기능별 구분

대조 → however

결과 → therefore

양보 → although

강조 표현

It was very difficult. However, we succeeded.

Although it was extremely late, we stayed.

9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

It was raining, however we went out.
It was raining. However, we went out.
He studied hard therefore he passed.
He studied hard. Therefore, he passed.
Although it was raining. We went out.
Although it was raining, we went out.
We stayed, although it was late.
We stayed although it was late.
She was shy however she spoke.
She was shy. However, she spoke.
It was expensive therefore we didn't buy.
It was expensive. Therefore, we didn't buy it.
Although he was tired. He worked.
Although he was tired, he worked.
However we tried our best.
However, we tried our best.

10. 연습문제 (Practice Questions)

문제 1: 알맞은 연결어를 선택하세요

It was raining. _____, we went out. (However/Therefore)

문제 2: 문장을 올바르게 연결하세요

He studied hard. _____, he passed the test. (However/Therefore)

문제 3: 양보 표현을 완성하세요

_____ it was late, we stayed at the party. (Although/However)

문제 4: 올바른 문장을 선택하세요

다음 중 맞는 문장은? (A/B)

A: It was expensive, however we bought it.

B: It was expensive. However, we bought it.

문제 5: 문장을 결합하세요

"She was nervous" + "She performed well" → Although를 사용해서 결합하세요

문제 6: 결과 표현 완성하기

The weather was bad. _____, the flight was canceled. (However/Therefore)

문제 7: 대조 표현 완성하기

She was tired. _____, she finished her work. (Although/However)

문제 8: 오류 수정하기

다음 문장을 올바르게 고치세요: "Although it was cold. We went swimming."

  • 이메일, 보고서 작성 시 문장 간 관계를 명확히 하세요

  • 대화에서도 자연스럽게 사용해서 논리적으로 말하세요
  • 복합적인 상황에서 여러 연결어를 조합해서 사용해보세요
  • 매일 간단한 일기를 쓸 때 연결어를 의식적으로 사용해보세요
