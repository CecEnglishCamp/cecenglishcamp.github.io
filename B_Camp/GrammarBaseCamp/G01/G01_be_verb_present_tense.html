1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

BEBe Verb – Present Tense

Be 동사: **am** / **is** / **are** → **존재, 상태, 신분** 등을 나타냄
주어에 따라 형태가 달라짐
영어 문장의 기본 구조로, 자기소개, 감정, 위치 등 표현에 사용됨

I am a student.

She is happy.

They are friends.

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

  • I → am
  • He / She / It → is
  • You / We / They → are
  • 긍정문: 주어 + be동사 + 보어 (예: She is a teacher.)
  • 부정문: 주어 + be동사 + **not** + 보어 (예: He is not tired.)
  • 의문문: Be동사 + 주어 + 보어? (예: Are you ready?)
  • 축약형: **I’m** / **You’re** / **He’s** / **She’s** / **We’re** / **They’re** / **It’s**

3. 긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

  • I am a student.
  • You are my friend.
  • He is a doctor.
  • She is happy.
  • It is a cat.
  • We are ready.
  • They are teachers.

5. 상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황
A: Are you a student?
B: Yes, I am.
A: Cool!

여행 상황
A: Are we in Paris?
B: No, we are in Rome.
A: Oh!

직장 상황
A: Is he the manager?
B: Yes, he is.
A: I see.

온라인 상황
A: Are you online?
B: Yes, I am.
A: Let’s chat.

가족 상황
A: Is she your sister?
B: Yes, she is.
A: She’s nice.

병원 상황
A: Is he sick?
B: No, he is tired.
A: Okay.

6. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

  • I student. → I am a student.
  • She happy. → She is happy.
  • They friends. → They are friends.
  • We ready. → We are ready.
  • He tired. → He is tired.
  • You my teacher. → You are my teacher.
  • It a dog. → It is a dog.
  • I from Korea. → I am from Korea.
  • She my sister. → She is my sister.
  • We in class. → We are in class.

7. 실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

  • 학교 상황: I am in English class. (위치)
  • 직장 상황: He is the team leader. (신분/직업)
  • 가족 상황: She is my cousin. (관계)
  • 온라인 상황: We are online now. (상태)
  • 병원 상황: He is sick today. (상태/감정)
  • 여행 상황: They are in Paris. (위치)
  • 회의 상황: I am ready to start. (상태/준비)

8. 자주 쓰이는 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • I am + 명사/형용사: I am happy.
  • You are + 명사/형용사: You are my friend.
  • He/She/It is + 명사/형용사: She is a doctor.
  • We/They are + 명사/형용사: They are students.
  • Be + from + 장소: I am from Korea. (출신)
  • Be + in/on/at + 장소: We are in class. (위치)
  • Be + ready/happy/sick: He is ready. (상태/감정)
  • Be + not + 보어: She is not tired. (부정문)
  • Be + question (도치): Are you okay? (의문문)
  • Be + 축약형: I’m / You’re / He’s / We’re / They’re (회화)

9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ I student. → ✅ I am a student. (주어-동사 생략 불가)
  • ❌ She happy. → ✅ She is happy. (동사 생략 불가)
  • ❌ They friend. → ✅ They are friends. (복수 주어는 복수 명사 사용 및 be동사 일치)
  • ❌ You teacher. → ✅ You are my teacher. (동사 생략 불가)
  • ❌ He not tired. → ✅ He is not tired. (Be동사 없이는 부정형 **not** 사용 불가)

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • Be동사는 **감정, 상태, 위치, 신분**을 표현할 때 사용합니다. (예: I'm sad. They're here. He's an engineer.)
  • 부정문은 **be + not**으로 만듭니다. (예: She isn't tired.) 축약형을 사용하면 더 자연스럽습니다.
  • 의문문은 **Be동사 + 주어** 순서로 만듭니다. (예: **Is** it raining?)
  • **단수 주어** (He, She, It, A cat)는 **is**를, **복수 주어** (We, They, Cats)는 **are**를 사용해야 합니다. 주어-동사 일치(Subject-Verb Agreement)가 가장 중요합니다.
  • 문장 결합 연습을 통해 **be동사 구조를 자동화**하고, 실생활에서 빠르게 반응할 수 있도록 훈련하세요.

11. 연습문제 (Practice Exercises)

객관식

  1. Choose the correct verb:
    The book ___ interesting.
    A. are
    B. is
    C. am
  2. Which is the correct negative form?
    We ___ ready.
    A. aren't
    B. not are
    C. not is
  3. Fill in the blank for a question:
    ___ they your parents?
    A. Are
    B. Is
    C. Am

1. B. The book (단수)는 He/She/It에 해당하므로 **is**가 정답입니다.

2. A. We (복수)의 be동사는 are이며, 부정문은 **aren't** (are not)입니다.

3. A. They (복수)의 be동사는 **Are**이며, 의문문은 Be동사가 먼저 옵니다.

12. 자가진단 체크리스트 (Self-Check)

학습 완료 체크

0%
  • I can use “am,” “is,” and “are” correctly based on the subject.
  • I can form negative sentences by adding “not” after the be verb.
  • I can form questions by inverting the be verb and the subject.
  • I can use common contractions (I’m, You’re, He’s, etc.) correctly.
  • I can identify and correct the most common be verb mistakes.

자가진단 문항

  1. Can you describe your current mood or state using an 'am' sentence?
  2. Can you tell me where you are right now using an 'are' sentence?
  3. Can you ask a question about a friend using 'Is he/she...'?
  4. Can you write a short paragraph correctly using 'am,' 'is,' and 'are'?
  5. Can you explain why "He are here" is wrong?

다음 단계 학습 가이드

  • Review the **subject + be verb** combinations until they are automatic.
  • Practice creating conversations focusing solely on asking and answering **location and identity** questions using be verbs.
  • Start making the jump to **Be Verb + -ing** (현재 진행형) to express continuous actions.
  • Use flashcards to memorize the **contractions** (I'm, You're, etc.) for fluency.
