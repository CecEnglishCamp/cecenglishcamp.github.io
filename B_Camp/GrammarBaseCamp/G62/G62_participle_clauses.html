1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

PARTParticiple Clauses

분사구문: 문장을 간결하게 만드는 고급 표현
접속사 + 주어 + 동사를 분사(-ing, -ed, having+p.p.)로 축약
시간, 이유, 조건, 대조 등의 의미를 간략하게 표현
주절과 종속절의 주어가 같을 때 주로 사용

시간: Walking to school, I met my friend.

이유: Being tired, he went to bed early.

완료: Having finished homework, she watched TV.

핵심 포인트

  • Participle Clauses: 분사를 사용한 종속절
  • 문장을 간결하고 세련되게 만드는 고급 표현
  • 접속사와 주어를 생략하고 분사로 시작
  • 주절과 종속절의 주어가 동일해야 함
  • 세 가지 형태: -ing형, -ed형, having+과거분사

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

Present Participle (-ing)
동사원형 + ing

• 능동: Walking to school, I saw him.

• 시간/이유: Being tired, he slept.

• 조건: Working hard, you'll succeed.

Past Participle (-ed)
과거분사 (-ed/-en/-t 등)

• 수동: Surprised by the news, she cried.

• 감정/상태: Confused by the question, he asked again.

• 결과: Broken by the storm, the tree fell.

Perfect Participle
Having + 과거분사

• 완료: Having finished work, she left.

• 순서: Having eaten dinner, we watched TV.

• 시간순: Having studied hard, he passed.

형성 규칙

  • 능동/진행 → 동사원형 + ing (walking, studying, working)
  • 수동/완료 → 과거분사 (surprised, confused, broken)
  • 완료/순서 → having + 과거분사 (having finished, having studied)
  • 주절과 종속절의 주어가 같아야 함
  • 시간, 이유, 조건, 대조 등의 의미 관계 표현

변환 과정

  • Because he was tired → Being tired (이유)
  • When I walked to school → Walking to school (시간)
  • After she had finished → Having finished (완료)
  • Since he was surprised → Surprised (수동/감정)

5. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

A: You should sleep more at night.
직장 상황
A: How did you finish so quickly?
B: Having prepared well, I completed it efficiently.
A: Good planning always helps.
여행 상황

A: It's going to be amazing!

A: It's okay to feel emotional.

B: Walking to the store, I bumped into him accidentally.
A: What a coincidence!

8. 자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴

시간 표현

-ing (동시행동) / Having + p.p. (순서)

• Walking to school, I met my friend.

• Having finished work, she went home.

이유 표현

Being + adj/p.p. (상태/감정)

• Being tired, he went to bed early.

• Being confused, she asked for help.

감정/반응 표현

과거분사 + by/about/with

• Surprised by the news, she gasped.

• Excited about the trip, they packed early.

객관식 (5문항)

1. Which is correct?
A. Having finish the work, he left.
B. Having finished the work, he left.
정답: B. Having finished the work, he left.
2. Choose the correct form: ___ by the news, she cried.
A. Surprising
B. Surprised
정답: B. Surprised (수동/감정 표현)
3. Fill in: ___ tired, he went to bed early.
A. Being
B. Been
정답: A. Being (상태 표현)

문장 결합 (5문항)

4. Because he was tired, he slept early. →
정답: Being tired, he slept early.
5. After she had finished, she left. →
정답: Having finished, she left.
