1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

FUTUREFuture in the Past

과거 시점에서 미래에 일어날 예정이었던 일
기본 구조: was/were going to + 동사원형
계획, 의도, 기대를 표현하며
실제로 일어났는지 여부는 문맥에 따라 달라짐

계획: I was going to call you.

의도: We were going to leave early.

기대: She was going to help, but she got sick.

핵심 포인트

  • Future in the Past: 과거 시점에서 미래에 일어날 예정이었던 일
  • 기본 구조: was/were going to + 동사원형
  • 계획, 의도, 기대를 표현함
  • 실제로 일어났는지 여부는 문맥에 따라 달라짐
  • 주어에 따라 was / were 선택

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

기본 구조

  • was/were going to + 동사원형 → 과거 시점의 미래 계획
  • was → I / he / she / it
  • were → you / we / they
  • 부정형: was/were not going to + 동사원형
  • 의문형: Was/Were + 주어 + going to + 동사원형?
  • 문맥에 따라 실제 행동이 일어났는지 설명 가능

주어에 따른 be동사 선택

  • I was going to call you
  • You were going to join us
  • He/She/It was going to help
  • We were going to leave early
  • They were going to travel abroad

시제의 의미

  • 과거의 어떤 시점에서 미래를 바라본 표현
  • 계획이나 의도가 있었음을 나타냄
  • 실제로 그 행동을 했는지는 별개의 문제
  • often used with "but" to show contrast

8. 자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴 (Common Patterns)

기본 구조
was/were going to + 동사원형

• I was going to call you.

• We were going to leave early.

대조 표현
was/were going to + but + 실제 결과

• I was going to study, but I fell asleep.

• She was going to visit, but she changed her mind.

의도 표현
동사 → was/were going to

• planned to → was going to

• intended to → was going to

• expected to → were going to

시제 흐름
과거 시점 → 미래 행동

• 과거 시점에서 미래 행동 → was/were going to

• 실제 결과는 과거 완료 또는 단순 과거

감정 표현
계획 + 감정적 맥락

• I was going to tell you the truth (후회)

• We were going to surprise her (아쉬움)

이유 설명
was/were going to + but + 이유

• I was going to come, but I got stuck in traffic.

• They were going to help, but they were too busy.

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • was/were going to는 과거 시점에서의 미래 계획을 표현
  • 실제 행동이 일어났는지 여부는 문맥으로 판단
  • 계획된 행동이 취소되었을 경우 → but + 과거 시제
  • 의도/계획/기대 표현을 자연스럽게 연결할 수 있음
  • 실생활 대화, 이메일, 회의 등에서 계획 변경 설명에 자주 사용됨
  • 문장 결합 연습을 통해 시제 흐름과 의미 전달력 향상
  • "was going to"는 감정과 맥락을 담아내는 표현으로 매우 유용
  • 스토리텔링에서 과거의 계획과 실제 결과를 대비하여 흥미를 더할 수 있음
  • 비즈니스 영어에서 변경된 계획을 정중하게 설명할 때 필수적
  • 일상 대화에서 후회나 아쉬움을 자연스럽게 표현하는 방법

11. 연습문제 (Practice Exercises)

25문항 연습문제

객관식 (5문항)

1. Which is correct?
A. I was go to call you.
B. I was going to call you.
정답: B. I was going to call you.
2. Choose the correct form: She ___ going to help us.
A. was
B. were
정답: A. was (She는 3인칭 단수이므로 was 사용)
3. Fill in: We ___ going to leave early, but we changed our minds.
A. were
B. was
정답: A. were (We는 복수이므로 were 사용)
4. What is the question form of "He was going to join us"?
A. Was he going to join us?
B. Were he going to join us?
정답: A. Was he going to join us?
A. They were going to travel to Paris.
B. I was going to call you, but I forgot.
정답: B. I was going to call you, but I forgot. (but절이 취소된 계획을 보여줌)

정답: I was going to visit.

계획과 실제 결과 비교 (5문항)

16. Write 3 sentences using "was/were going to" + but + past result.
예시답안: I was going to study, but I fell asleep. / She was going to visit, but she got sick. / We were going to leave early, but we missed the bus.
17. Write 3 sentences showing canceled plans.
예시답안: The meeting was going to start at 9, but it was postponed. / I was going to buy a car, but I changed my mind. / They were going to move to Seoul, but they decided to stay.
18. Write 3 sentences showing completed plans.
예시답안: I was going to call you, and I did. / She was going to finish the project, and she completed it on time. / We were going to travel together, and we had a great trip.
19. Write 3 sentences using "was going to" with emotion (regret, relief).
예시답안: I was going to tell him the truth, but I lost courage. (regret) / I was going to take that flight, but luckily it was delayed. (relief) / She was going to quit her job, but she's glad she didn't. (relief)
20. Write 2 questions using "What were you going to do?"
예시답안: What were you going to do before I called? / What were you going to say when he interrupted you?

혼합 연습 (5문항)

정답: I was going to call you.
23. Translate: 우리는 일찍 떠나려고 했지만 못 했어. →

24. Write a question using "Were you going to join us?"
예시답안: Were you going to join us for dinner tonight?

예시답안: She was going to cook dinner for us, but the stove wasn't working.
