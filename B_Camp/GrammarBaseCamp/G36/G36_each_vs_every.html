G36: Each vs Every

A2 Grammar Lesson – 개별성과 전체성 표현

기본 개념 (Concept)

Each와 Every는 모두 "모든"을 의미하지만 강조하는 방식이 다릅니다. 이 차이를 이해하면 더 정확하고 자연스러운 영어를 구사할 수 있습니다.

EACH

개별성 강조

하나하나 따로따로

개인적/개별적 관점

2개 이상일 때 사용

EVERY

전체성 강조

모든 것을 일반화

집단적/전체적 관점

3개 이상일 때 사용

시각적 이해:

Each: 🔴 🔴 🔴 (개별적으로 하나씩)

Every: 🔵🔵🔵 (전체를 하나로 보기)

예시 비교:

Each student has a locker. (학생마다 개별적으로 사물함이 있음)
Every student studies hard. (전체 학생이 일반적으로 열심히 공부함)
Each person chose a different gift. (각자 다른 선물을 선택 - 개별성)
Every person needs love. (모든 사람이 사랑이 필요 - 보편성)

문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

공통 규칙

  • 둘 다 단수 명사와 함께 사용: each book / every person
  • 둘 다 단수 동사와 함께 사용: Each student is / Every student studies
  • 둘 다 명사 앞에 위치하는 한정사 역할

Each의 특별한 용법

  • Each of + 복수 명사 가능: Each of the students is ready
  • 문장 끝에도 위치 가능: The students each received a book
  • 2개 이상일 때 사용: Each hand (양손), Each eye (양 눈)
  • 선택적 의미 포함: Each person chose differently
  • 개별 배분 강조: Give each child a toy

Every의 특별한 용법

  • 시간 표현과 결합: every day, every week, every month
  • 3개 이상일 때 사용: every student (3명 이상의 학생)
  • 일반적 진술: Every child needs love
  • 강조 표현: every single day (매일매일)
  • 주기적 의미: every two hours (2시간마다)

동사 일치 규칙

  • Each student is ready. (단수)
  • Every student studies hard. (단수)
  • Each of the students is ready. (단수)
  • Every one of them knows the answer. (단수)

긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

Each - 개별성 강조

  • Each student has a different book. (학생마다 다른 책을 가지고 있어요)
  • Each person received a personal gift. (각자 개인적인 선물을 받았어요)
  • Each child wore a unique costume. (아이마다 독특한 의상을 입었어요)
  • Each of the apples is fresh. (사과가 각각 신선해요)
  • Each team played a different strategy. (팀마다 다른 전략으로 경기했어요)
  • Each member has individual responsibilities. (회원마다 개별 책임이 있어요)
  • Each question requires a separate answer. (질문마다 별도의 답이 필요해요)
  • Each of the chairs is handmade. (의자가 각각 수제품이에요)
  • Each dog has its own personality. (개마다 고유한 성격이 있어요)
  • Each of the answers is worth 10 points. (답마다 10점씩이에요)

Every - 전체성 강조

  • Every student studies hard. (모든 학생이 열심히 공부해요)
  • Every person needs food and water. (모든 사람이 음식과 물이 필요해요)
  • Every child loves to play. (모든 아이가 놀기를 좋아해요)
  • Every apple in this box is sweet. (이 상자의 모든 사과가 달아요)
  • Every team wants to win. (모든 팀이 이기고 싶어해요)
  • Every member is important to us. (모든 회원이 우리에게 중요해요)
  • Every question matters in the exam. (시험에서 모든 문제가 중요해요)
  • Every chair in this room is comfortable. (이 방의 모든 의자가 편해요)
  • Every dog barks when strangers come. (낯선 사람이 오면 모든 개가 짖어요)
  • Every answer helps us understand better. (모든 답이 이해하는 데 도움이 돼요)

시간 표현 (Time Expressions)

  • I exercise every day. (매일 운동해요)
  • We have meetings every week. (매주 회의가 있어요)
  • She visits her parents every month. (매달 부모님을 찾아봬요)
  • The bus comes every 15 minutes. (버스가 15분마다 와요)
  • Every morning, I drink coffee. (매일 아침 커피를 마셔요)

의문문 예시 (Question Examples)

  • A: Does each student have a different book? (학생마다 다른 책이 있나요?)
    B: Yes, they chose their own books. (네, 각자 책을 선택했어요)
  • A: Did each person get a personal gift? (각자 개인 선물을 받았나요?)
    B: Yes, customized for each one. (네, 각자에게 맞춰서요)
  • A: Is each child ready for the trip? (아이들이 각자 여행 준비가 됐나요?)
    B: Yes, all packed individually. (네, 각자 짐을 쌌어요)
  • A: Does every student study math? (모든 학생이 수학을 공부하나요?)
    B: Yes, it's a required subject. (네, 필수 과목이에요)
  • A: Does every person in your family agree? (가족 모두가 동의하나요?)
    B: Yes, we discussed it together. (네, 함께 논의했어요)
  • A: Is every child here today? (오늘 모든 아이가 왔나요?)
    B: Yes, perfect attendance. (네, 모두 출석했어요)
  • A: Did every team participate in the tournament? (모든 팀이 토너먼트에 참가했나요?)
    B: Yes, all 12 teams. (네, 12팀 모두요)
  • A: Is every member present at the meeting? (모든 회원이 회의에 참석했나요?)
    B: Almost everyone, just two missing. (거의 모두, 두 명만 빠졌어요)
  • A: Does every question have the same difficulty? (모든 문제가 같은 난이도인가요?)
    B: No, they vary in difficulty. (아니요, 난이도가 다양해요)
  • A: Is every answer on the sheet correct? (시트의 모든 답이 맞나요?)
    B: Let me check them all. (모두 확인해볼게요)

상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (School Situation)
A: Does each student have their own locker?
B: Yes, one locker per person.
A: And every student studies math, right?
B: Yes, it's a required subject for everyone.
A: What about science? Does each student choose different experiments?
B: Yes, they pick their own research topics.

직장 상황 (Work Situation)
A: Did each team submit their individual reports?
B: Yes, all teams submitted by Friday.
A: Does every report include the budget section?
B: That's correct, it's a standard requirement.
A: Are we reviewing each report separately?
B: Yes, each team will present individually.

여행 준비 (Travel Preparation)
A: Did each person bring their own passport?
B: I checked everyone individually.
A: Perfect. Every traveler needs proper documentation.
B: Exactly. Each passport was verified.
A: Do we meet every day during the trip?
B: Yes, every morning at 8 AM.

이벤트 준비 (Event Planning)
A: Did each guest receive a personal invitation?
B: Yes, customized invitations for each person.
A: Great! Every detail matters for this event.
B: Absolutely. Each guest will feel special.
A: Are we checking every arrangement?
B: Yes, every single detail.

가족 모임 (Family Gathering)
A: Did each child get a different toy?
B: Yes, we picked individual gifts.
A: That's nice. Every child seems happy.
B: They are! Each one loves their gift.
A: Do you do this every year?
B: Yes, every holiday season.

의료 상황 (Medical Situation)
A: Did each patient get an individual room?
B: Yes, we assigned private rooms.
A: Perfect. Every room has been sanitized?
B: Of course, every single room.
A: Are you checking each patient separately?
B: Yes, individualized care for each person.

문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

전체를 나타내는 표현을 Each 또는 Every를 사용해서 바꾸는 연습입니다:

All students have books. → Each student has a book.
All children like playing. → Every child likes playing.
  • All students received different assignments. → Each student received a different assignment.
  • All children need proper nutrition. → Every child needs proper nutrition.
  • All apples in the basket are sweet. → Every apple in the basket is sweet.
  • All teams developed unique strategies. → Each team developed a unique strategy.
  • All members contribute to our success. → Every member contributes to our success.
  • All dogs in the park are friendly. → Every dog in the park is friendly.
  • All chairs were arranged individually. → Each of the chairs was arranged individually.
  • All answers require careful thought. → Every answer requires careful thought.
  • All students chose personal projects. → Each student chose a personal project.
  • All questions are equally important. → Every question is equally important.

실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

회사 이메일

Please ensure that each participant receives a personalized welcome package. Every employee should attend the mandatory training session.

교육 안내

Each student will work on an individual project. Every assignment must be submitted by the deadline.

이벤트 기획

Each guest will receive a customized gift bag. Every detail has been carefully planned for your enjoyment.

의료 서비스

Each patient receives individualized treatment. Every room is sanitized according to health protocols.

일상 루틴

자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • Each + 단수 명사: Each student has a book.
  • Each of + the + 복수 명사: Each of the students is ready.
  • Every + 단수 명사: Every child likes candy.
  • Every + 시간 표현: Every day / Every week / Every month
  • Each + 개별 동작: Each person chose a different path.
  • Every + 일반적 특성: Every student needs encouragement.
  • Each + of + 소유격: Each of our products is tested.
  • Every single + 명사: Every single detail matters. (강조)
  • Each time: Each time I visit, I learn something new.
  • Every other + 명사: Every other day (하루 걸러 하루)

흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ Each students has a book → ✅ Each student has a book
  • ❌ Every children like candy → ✅ Every child likes candy
  • ❌ Each of the apples are fresh → ✅ Each of the apples is fresh
  • ❌ Every students studies hard → ✅ Every student studies hard
  • ❌ Each team play well → ✅ Each team plays well
  • ❌ Every dogs bark loudly → ✅ Every dog barks loudly
  • ❌ Each of the chairs are broken → ✅ Each of the chairs is broken
  • ❌ Every of the students → ✅ Each of the students
  • ❌ Each person have → ✅ Each person has
  • ❌ Every day I goes → ✅ Every day I go

레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • Each는 개별 항목을 강조, Every는 전체 집단을 일반화
  • 둘 다 단수 명사 + 단수 동사와 함께 사용됨
  • Each of + 복수 명사는 단수 동사와 함께 사용
  • Every는 시간 표현과 자주 결합: every day, every week
  • Each는 선택적 의미나 개별적 차이를 강조할 때 유용
  • "Every single"은 강조 표현으로 사용됨
  • 문맥에 따라 개별성 vs 전체성을 구분하는 것이 중요
  • Each는 2개 이상, Every는 3개 이상일 때 일반적으로 사용
