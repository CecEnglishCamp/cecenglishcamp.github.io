1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

FPFUTURE PERFECT

미래의 특정 시점 이전에 완료될 일을 표현
주로 by / before / by the time / until 등의 시간 표현과 함께 사용

마감 전 완료: I will have finished the report by 5 PM.

계획 표현: She will have arrived before the meeting starts.

예측 표현: We will have completed the project by Friday.

기본 구조

will have + 과거분사 (p.p)

예시: will have finished / will have gone / will have seen

Future Simple vs Future Perfect

Future Simple (단순 미래):
I will finish the report. (언젠가 끝낼 것)
Future Perfect (미래완료):
I will have finished the report by 5 PM. (5시 이전에 끝낼 것)

시간 흐름 이해하기

지금 (Now)

Working on report

→
완료 (Completion)

Will have finished

→
마감 (Deadline)

5 PM

"I will have finished the report by 5 PM."

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

문법 구조

  • will have + p.p → will have finished / will have gone / will have seen
  • 시간 표현: by 5 PM / before you arrive / by next week / by the time we start
  • 미래완료는 결과 강조: 어떤 일이 미래 시점 이전에 끝남
  • 현재완료와 과거완료는 현재 또는 과거 기준, 미래완료는 미래 기준
  • 미래완료는 계획, 예측, 마감에 자주 사용됨

시간 표현

BY + 시간

  • by 5 PM
  • by Friday
  • by next week
  • by tomorrow

BEFORE + 현재시제

  • before the meeting starts
  • before she arrives
  • before class begins
  • before lunch

BY THE TIME + 현재시제

  • by the time you get here
  • by the time he arrives
  • by the time we start
  • by the time class ends

기타 표현

  • until then
  • by the end of
  • before long
  • within a week

사용 목적

  • 마감 계획: I will have submitted the report by Monday.
  • 예측 표현: She will have graduated by next year.
  • 순서 설명: He will have left before you arrive.
  • 성과 예상: We will have completed 100 projects by December.

3. 예시 문장 (Example Sentences)

긍정문 (Positive Examples)

  • I will have finished my homework by 8 PM.
  • She will have arrived before the meeting starts.
  • We will have completed the project by Friday.
  • He will have left by the time you get here.
  • They will have eaten dinner before 7.
  • I will have read the book by next week.
  • She will have cleaned the room before the guests arrive.
  • We will have packed everything by tomorrow morning.
  • He will have written the report before lunch.
  • I will have learned all the grammar points by the end of the course.

의문문 (Question Examples)

  • Will you have finished your homework by 8?
  • Will she have arrived before the meeting?
  • Will they have completed the project by Friday?
  • Will he have left by the time I get there?
  • Will you have eaten dinner before 7?
  • Will you have read the book by next week?
  • Will she have cleaned the room before the guests arrive?
  • Will we have packed everything by tomorrow?
  • Will he have written the report before lunch?
  • Will you have learned all the grammar points by the end?

4. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (School Situation)

A: Great. Will you have studied the grammar part too?
