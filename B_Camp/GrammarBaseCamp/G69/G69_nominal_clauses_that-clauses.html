1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

NOMNominal Clauses (That-Clauses)

명사절(Nominal Clause): 문장에서 명사처럼 쓰이는 절
that + 주어 + 동사 형태로 구성됨
문장의 주어, 목적어, 보어 역할을 함
생각, 감정, 사실, 주장 등을 표현할 때 사용

주어: That he lied surprised me.

목적어: I know that he lied.

보어: The truth is that he lied.

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

  • 주어 역할: That + 절이 문장의 주어가 됨
    예: That she won surprised us.
  • 목적어 역할: 동사의 목적어로 사용됨
    예: I believe that she won.
  • 보어 역할: be동사 뒤에서 주어를 설명함
    예: The fact is that she won.
  • that은 생략 가능 (구어체에서 자주 생략됨)
  • 시제는 절 안의 동사에 따라 결정됨
  • 명사절은 문장 전체를 하나의 정보 단위로 표현함

3. 긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

주어 역할

  • That she passed the test is amazing.
  • That he apologized was unexpected.
  • That they arrived early helped a lot.
  • That you tried means a lot.
  • That we finished on time was lucky.

목적어 역할

  • I know that she passed the test.
  • We believe that he is honest.
  • She said that they arrived early.
  • They think that you tried hard.
  • He heard that we finished on time.

보어 역할

  • The truth is that she passed the test.
  • The problem is that he lied.
  • The idea is that they arrived early.
  • The fact is that you tried.
  • The reason is that we finished on time.

5. 상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황
A: She got full marks.
B: I heard that she studied all night.
A: That she passed is amazing.

여행 상황
A: They arrived early.
B: The fact is that they took a taxi.
A: That makes sense.

직장 상황
A: He apologized.
B: That he apologized was unexpected.
A: I agree.

온라인 상황
A: She posted a long message.
B: I think that she was upset.
A: Possibly.

가족 상황
A: Dad fixed the sink.
B: That he fixed it surprised me.
A: He’s full of surprises.

병원 상황
A: She recovered quickly.
B: The truth is that she followed every instruction.
A: That’s great.

6. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

  • He lied. + I know → I know that he lied.
  • She passed the test. + That surprised me → That she passed the test surprised me.
  • They arrived early. + We heard → We heard that they arrived early.
  • You tried hard. + That means a lot → That you tried hard means a lot.
  • He is honest. + We believe → We believe that he is honest.
  • She was upset. + I think → I think that she was upset.
  • We finished on time. + The fact is → The fact is that we finished on time.
  • He apologized. + That was unexpected → That he apologized was unexpected.
  • She studied all night. + I heard → I heard that she studied all night.
  • He fixed the sink. + That surprised me → That he fixed the sink surprised me.

7. 실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

  • 학교 상황: I know that she studied hard for the exam.
  • 직장 상황: We believe that the report is accurate.
  • 가족 상황: That he cooked dinner was a nice surprise.
  • 온라인 상황: I think that she posted it late.
  • 병원 상황: The truth is that she followed every instruction.
  • 여행 상황: I heard that they arrived early.
  • 회의 상황: That he apologized was unexpected.

8. 자주 쓰이는 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • I think that + 주어 + 동사
  • I know that + 주어 + 동사
  • We believe that + 주어 + 동사
  • She said that + 주어 + 동사
  • The truth is that + 주어 + 동사
  • The fact is that + 주어 + 동사
  • That + 주어 + 동사 + 동사 (주어 역할)
  • That + 주어 + 동사 + be동사 + 명사/형용사 (보어 역할)
  • 동사 + that절 (목적어 역할)
  • 명사 + is + that절 (보어 역할)

9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ I know that he lie → ✅ I know that he lied
  • ❌ That she passed the test is surprise → ✅ That she passed the test is a surprise
  • ❌ The truth is she passed → ✅ The truth is that she passed
  • ❌ I think that she upset → ✅ I think that she was upset
  • ❌ He said that she go → ✅ He said that she went

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • 명사절은 문장 전체를 하나의 정보 단위로 표현함
  • that은 생략 가능하지만 글쓰기에서는 유지하는 것이 명확함
  • 주어 역할일 때는 that절이 문장 맨 앞에 위치함
  • 보어 역할일 때는 be동사 뒤에 위치함
  • 목적어 역할일 때는 동사 뒤에 위치함
  • 시제는 that절 안의 동사에 따라 결정됨
  • 문장 결합 연습을 통해 정보 흐름과 문장 구조 향상

11. 연습문제 (Practice Exercises)

객관식

  1. Which is correct?
    A. I know that he lie.
    B. I know that he lied.
  2. Choose the correct form:
    ___ she passed the test surprised me.
    A. That
    B. What
  3. Fill in: The truth ___ that he apologized.
    A. is
    B. are

1. B. 'that' 절 안의 동사는 주절의 시제에 맞게 과거형 'lied'가 되어야 합니다.

2. A. 문장의 주어 역할을 하는 명사절을 이끌 때는 'That'을 사용합니다.

3. A. 'The truth'가 단수 주어이므로, 단수 동사 'is'가 와야 합니다.
