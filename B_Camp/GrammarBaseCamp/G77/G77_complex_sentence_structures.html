1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

COMPLEXComplex Sentence Structures (복합문 구조)

Complex Sentence: **주절(main clause)**과 **종속절(subordinate clause)**로 구성된 문장
종속절은 접속사로 시작하며, 주절에 정보를 추가하거나 **이유·조건·시간 등**을 설명함
문장 간 **논리 연결**과 글쓰기 **응집력 향상**에 효과적

단순문: I was tired. I kept working.

복합문: **Although I was tired**, I kept working.

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

  • 시간절 접속사: **when, while, before, after, since**
  • 이유절 접속사: **because, since, as**
  • 조건절 접속사: **if, unless, provided that**
  • 양보절 접속사: **although, even though, though** (대조)
  • 목적/결과절 접속사: **so that, in order that** (목적)
  • 종속절이 문장 **앞**에 올 경우 **쉼표(,)** 사용 (예: **Although it was late**, we kept working.)
  • 종속절은 완전한 문장이 아니며, 주절과 함께 의미가 완성됨 (단독 사용 불가)
  • 조건절에서 미래를 나타낼 때도 **현재 시제** 사용 (예: If you **study**, you'll pass.)

3. 긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

시간절 (When, After, Before, While, Since)

  • We started **when the bell rang**.
  • **After she arrived**, we began dinner.
  • **Before you leave**, check your bag.
  • **While I was studying**, he watched TV.
  • **Since I moved here**, I’ve made new friends.

이유절 (Because, Since, As)

  • I stayed home **because I was sick**.
  • **Since it’s raining**, we’ll stay inside.
  • **As you’re busy**, I’ll go alone.

조건절 (If, Unless, Provided that)

  • **If you study**, you’ll pass.
  • **Unless it stops raining**, we can’t play.
  • **Provided that you finish**, you may leave early.

양보절 (Although, Even though, Though)

  • **Although I was tired**, I kept working.
  • **Even though it was hard**, they succeeded.
  • **Though he’s young**, he’s very wise.

목적/결과절 (So that, In order that)

  • I explained it **so that everyone could understand**.
  • She practiced **in order that she might improve**.
  • He worked hard **so that he could earn money**.

5. 상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (이유)
A: I didn’t study much.
B: **Because you were tired?**
A: Yes, I slept early.

여행 상황 (시간/이유)
A: We missed the train.
B: **Since we arrived late**, it was gone.
A: Let’s check the next one.

직장 상황 (목적)
A: He finished early.
B: **So that he could leave before traffic**.
A: Smart move.

온라인 상황 (조건)
A: I’ll update the file.
B: **If you do**, let me know.
A: Will do.

가족 상황 (조건)
A: I’m going out.
B: **Unless you clean your room**, you’re not going anywhere.
A: Okay, okay.

병원 상황 (양보)
A: She’s still resting.
B: **Although she looks better**, she needs more time.
A: I agree.

6. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

  • I was tired. I kept working. → **Although I was tired**, I kept working. (양보)
  • She arrived. We began dinner. → **After she arrived**, we began dinner. (시간)
  • You study. You’ll pass. → **If you study**, you’ll pass. (조건)
  • It’s raining. We’ll stay inside. → **Since it’s raining**, we’ll stay inside. (이유)
  • You’re busy. I’ll go alone. → **As you’re busy**, I’ll go alone. (이유)
  • You finish. You may leave early. → **Provided that you finish**, you may leave early. (조건)
  • He worked hard. He earned money. → He worked hard **so that he could earn money**. (목적)
  • She practiced. She improved. → She practiced **in order that she might improve**. (목적)
  • He’s young. He’s wise. → **Though he’s young**, he’s wise. (양보)
  • I moved here. I’ve made friends. → **Since I moved here**, I’ve made new friends. (시간)

7. 실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

  • 학교 상황 (조건): **If you study**, you’ll pass the exam.
  • 직장 상황 (양보): **Although the deadline was tight**, we finished on time.
  • 가족 상황 (시간): **Before you go out**, clean your room.
  • 온라인 상황 (이유): **Since the server is down**, we can’t upload files.
  • 병원 상황 (이유): **Because she was tired**, she needed rest.
  • 여행 상황 (조건): **Unless you bring your ID**, you can’t board the train.
  • 회의 상황 (목적): **So that everyone understands**, let’s explain it again.

8. 자주 쓰이는 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • **Although / Even though / Though + 주어 + 동사** (양보)
  • **Because / Since / As + 주어 + 동사** (이유)
  • **If / Unless / Provided that + 주어 + 동사** (조건)
  • **When / While / Before / After / Since + 주어 + 동사** (시간)
  • **So that / In order that + 주어 + 조동사 + 동사원형** (목적)
  • **종속절 + , + 주절** (쉼표 사용)
  • **주절 + 종속절** (쉼표 사용 안 함)
  • **조건절은 미래 표현에도 현재 시제 사용** (예: If you **study**, you’ll pass.)
  • **양보절은 대조 강조에 효과적**
  • **목적절은 시험/계획/지시 표현에 자주 사용**

9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ Although he is tired. He kept working. → ✅ **Although he is tired, he kept working.** (종속절은 단독 문장으로 쓸 수 없음)
  • ❌ Because I was sick. I stayed home. → ✅ **Because I was sick, I stayed home.** (종속절은 단독 문장으로 쓸 수 없음)
  • ❌ If you will study, you will pass. → ✅ **If you study, you will pass.** (조건절에는 현재 시제 사용)
  • ❌ She practiced. So that she might improve. → ✅ **She practiced so that she might improve.** (종속절은 주절에 연결되어야 함)
  • ❌ Since it’s raining. We’ll stay inside. → ✅ **Since it’s raining, we’ll stay inside.** (종속절은 단독 문장으로 쓸 수 없음)

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • 복합문은 **논리 연결**과 **정보 확장**에 효과적
  • 접속사 선택에 따라 **의미와 분위기**가 달라짐 (예: 'Because' vs 'Since')
  • 종속절이 문장 앞에 오면 **쉼표**를 사용하여 구분
  • **조건절은 현재 시제**로 미래를 표현하는 규칙을 명심
  • **양보절**은 대조나 예외를 강조할 때 유용 (Although, Even though)
  • **목적절**은 계획이나 의도를 명확히 설명할 때 사용 (So that, In order that)
  • 문장 결합 연습을 통해 **자연스러운 복합문 표현 향상**

11. 연습문제 (Practice Exercises)

객관식

  1. Which is correct?
    A. Although he is tired. He kept working.
    B. Although he is tired, he kept working.
  2. Choose the correct tense:
    If you ___ (study), you will pass.
    A. study
    B. will study
  3. Fill in the blank: She finished the report early ___ she could leave before traffic.
    A. because
    B. so that

1. B. 종속절(Although he is tired)은 주절(he kept working)과 쉼표로 연결되어야 하나의 문장이 됩니다. A처럼 마침표로 구분하면 오류입니다.

2. A. 시간이나 조건을 나타내는 종속절(If-clause)에서는 미래 시제 대신 **현재 시제(study)**를 사용합니다.

3. B. 뒤 문장이 앞 문장의 **목적**을 설명하므로, 목적을 나타내는 접속사 **so that**이 적절합니다.
