1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

CVCAUSATIVE VERBS

다른 사람으로 하여금 무엇을 하게 하는 동사
지시, 허락, 강요, 설득의 의미를 표현
주어가 직접 행동하지 않고 다른 사람이 행동하게 만드는 상황

Have: I had him fix my computer. (지시)

Let: I let her use my car. (허락)

Make: I made him apologize. (강요)

Get: I got her to help me. (설득)

기본 구조

Causative Verb + 사람 + 동사

예시: I had him fix it. / I got him to fix it.

사역동사별 의미 차이

Have (지시)

I had him clean the room.
(그에게 방을 청소하도록 시켰다)

권한이나 책임에 의한 지시

Let (허락)

I let her go home early.
(그녀가 일찍 집에 가도록 허락했다)

허용이나 허락의 의미

Make (강요)

I made him apologize.
(그를 사과하게 만들었다)

강제나 억지로 하게 함

Get (설득)

I got him to help me.
(그가 나를 돕게 설득했다)

설득이나 부탁으로 하게 함

사역동사 사용 과정

상황 발생

My computer broke
(컴퓨터가 고장났다)

→
사역 행동

I had him fix it
(그에게 고치게 했다)

사역동사 사용 목적

업무 지시
I had my assistant send the email.
허락/허용
She let me borrow her book.
강제/억지
The teacher made us study.
설득/부탁
I got him to change his mind.

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

기본 구조와 규칙

  • Have/Make/Let + 사람 + 동사원형
  • Get + 사람 + to부정사
  • Have/Get + 사물 + 과거분사 (서비스를 받을 때)
  • 부정문: didn't have/make/let/get + 사람 + 동사(원형/to부정사)
  • 의문문: Did you have/make/let/get + 사람 + 동사?
  • 과거형: had/made/let/got + 사람 + 동사

각 동사별 특징

  • Have: 권한이나 책임에 의한 지시 → I had him clean it.
  • Make: 강제나 억지로 하게 함 → I made him apologize.
  • Let: 허락이나 허용 → I let her go home.
  • Get: 설득이나 부탁 → I got her to help me.

서비스/수동적 사역

  • Have/Get + 사물 + p.p: 서비스를 받을 때
  • I had my hair cut. (머리를 잘랐다 - 미용사에게)
  • I got my car repaired. (차를 수리했다 - 정비사에게)
  • She had her nails done. (네일을 했다 - 네일샵에서)

3. 예시 문장 (Example Sentences)

Have (지시/시키기)

  • I had him fix my computer.
  • She had her assistant send the email.
  • We had the workers paint the house.
  • He had his secretary book the flight.
  • I had my friend pick me up.
  • They had the chef prepare special meal.
  • She had her son clean his room.
  • We had the technician install the software.
  • I had my colleague review the document.
  • He had his driver take him to the airport.

Let (허락/허용)

  • I let her use my car.
  • She let him go home early.
  • We let the children play outside.
  • He let me borrow his book.
  • They let us stay overnight.
  • I let my daughter choose her clothes.
  • She let him watch TV after homework.
  • We let the dog run in the garden.
  • He let me use his phone.
  • They let us leave the meeting early.

Make (강요/강제)

  • I made him apologize.
  • She made me laugh.
  • The teacher made us study harder.
  • He made her cry.
  • They made him pay the bill.
  • I made my son clean his room.
  • She made him tell the truth.
  • We made them wait outside.
  • He made me understand the problem.
  • They made us work overtime.

Get (설득/부탁)

  • I got him to help me.
  • She got her friend to come to the party.
  • We got the manager to change his mind.
  • He got me to believe his story.
  • They got us to join the project.
  • I got my neighbor to water my plants.
  • She got him to apologize.
  • We got the teacher to extend the deadline.
  • He got her to go on a date.
  • They got me to try the new restaurant.

Have/Get + 사물 + p.p (서비스)

  • I had my hair cut.
  • She got her car repaired.
  • We had the house painted.
  • He got his phone fixed.
  • They had their clothes cleaned.
  • I got my eyes tested.
  • She had her nails done.
  • We got the garden designed.
  • He had his suit tailored.
  • They got their photos printed.

5. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

직장 상황
A: Did you have John finish the report?
B: Yes, I had him submit it yesterday.
A: Great! Did you get him to review it too?
B: I tried, but I couldn't get him to do it.
가족 상황
A: Can you let me borrow your car tonight?
B: Sure, but I'll make you fill up the gas tank.
A: That's fair. I'll have it washed too.
B: That would be great!
친구 상황
A: I got my friend to help me move.
B: How did you get him to do that?
A: I made him dinner last week.
B: Smart! I should try that too.
서비스 상황
A: I need to get my hair cut.
B: Where do you usually have it done?
A: I go to that salon downtown.
B: I got my nails done there too!
학교 상황
A: The teacher made us stay after class.
B: Why? What happened?
A: She had us clean the classroom.
B: Did she let you go home early at least?
수리 상황
A: I had my computer fixed today.
B: How much did you have to pay?
A: $200. I got them to replace the hard drive.
B: That's expensive but necessary.

6. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

상황 + 사역 표현 결합

My computer broke. → He fixed it for me.
→ I had him fix my computer.
She wanted to use my car. → I allowed it.
→ I let her use my car.
He didn't want to apologize. → I forced him.
→ I made him apologize.
She didn't want to help. → I convinced her.
→ I got her to help me.
My hair was too long. → The barber cut it.
→ I had my hair cut.
My car was broken. → The mechanic repaired it.
→ I got my car repaired.
The children were noisy. → The teacher made them quiet.
→ The teacher made the children be quiet.
He wanted to go out. → His mother allowed it.
→ His mother let him go out.
The report was incomplete. → I asked him to finish it.
→ I got him to finish the report.
The room was messy. → I ordered him to clean it.
→ I had him clean the room.

7. 실생활 사용 (Real-Life Usage)

직장에서

I had my assistant schedule the meeting. The boss made us work overtime. I got my colleague to help with the presentation.

집에서

I let my son watch TV after homework. We had the plumber fix the sink. I got my daughter to clean her room.

친구와

I got my friend to drive me to the airport. She let me borrow her dress. We made him pay for dinner as a joke.

서비스 이용

I had my car serviced yesterday. She got her nails done at the salon. We had the house cleaned before the party.

학교에서

The teacher made us memorize the poem. She let us use calculators for the test. I got my classmate to explain the lesson.

8. 자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴 (Common Patterns)

패턴별 정리

Have + 사람 + 동사원형 I had him fix it.
Let + 사람 + 동사원형 I let her go home.
Make + 사람 + 동사원형 I made him apologize.
Get + 사람 + to부정사 I got him to help me.
Have + 사물 + p.p I had my hair cut.
Get + 사물 + p.p I got my car repaired.
의문문 패턴 Did you have him do it?
부정문 패턴 I didn't let him go.
과거형 패턴 I had him fix it yesterday.
미래형 패턴 I will have him do it tomorrow.

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

사역동사 마스터 팁

  • Have/Make/Let + 사람 + 동사원형 (to 없음!)
  • Get + 사람 + to부정사 (to 필수!)
  • Have: 권한/책임에 의한 지시
  • Make: 강제/억지로 하게 함
  • Let: 허락/허용
  • Get: 설득/부탁으로 하게 함
  • Have/Get + 사물 + p.p는 서비스 받을 때
  • 의문문: Did you have/make/let/get...?
  • 부정문: didn't have/make/let/get...
