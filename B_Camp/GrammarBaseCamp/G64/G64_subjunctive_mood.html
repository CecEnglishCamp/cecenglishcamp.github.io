1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

SUBJSubjunctive Mood

가정법(Subjunctive Mood): 현실과 다른 상황, 희망, 제안, 요구를 표현하는 문법
실제 사실이 아닌 상상, 바람, 조건 등을 나타냄
동사 원형 유지, were 사용, that절 구조가 핵심
시험, 공식 표현, 글쓰기에서 자주 등장

가정: If I were you, I would rest.

희망: I wish I were taller.

제안: I suggest that he study harder.

요구: It is essential that she be on time.

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

  • 가정법 if절: If I were you / If he were here
  • 희망 표현: I wish I were / He wishes he were
  • 제안/요구/필수 표현: that절 + 동사원형
    • I suggest that he study.
    • It is important that she be ready.
    • They demanded that he leave.
  • 주의: 주어가 3인칭이라도 동사에 s 붙이지 않음 (he study, not he studies)
  • be동사: 모든 주어에 대해 be 사용 (she be, they be)
  • 가정법 과거: 현실과 반대되는 상황 표현 (If I were rich…)

6. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

He is not here. → If he were here, we could begin.
I want to be taller. → I wish I were taller.
She needs to be careful. → I suggest that she be careful.
He should study. → It is important that he study.
They must leave. → We demand that they leave now.
I am not rich. → If I were rich, I'd buy a house.
She is not confident. → She wishes she were confident.
He must apologize. → The teacher insisted that he apologize.
She should attend. → It is essential that she attend the meeting.
We are not ready. → If we were ready, we could start.
It is not easy. → I wish it were easier.

❌ I wish I was taller → ✅ I wish I were taller
❌ He suggests that she studies → ✅ He suggests that she study

❌ If I was rich → ✅ If I were rich

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • 가정법에서는 were를 모든 주어에 사용 (I/he/she 모두 were)
  • 제안/요구/필수 표현에서는 동사 원형 사용 (he study, she be)
  • 현실과 다른 상황을 표현할 때 if절 + 과거형 사용
  • 희망 표현은 wish + 과거형(were)으로 시작
  • that절에서는 동사에 s 붙이지 않음
  • 시험, 글쓰기, 공식 문서에서 자주 등장하는 고급 표현
  • 문장 결합 연습을 통해 논리적 흐름과 강조력 향상
  • suggest, recommend, insist, demand 등의 동사 뒤에는 that절 + 원형동사
  • It is important/vital/essential 등의 표현 뒤에도 that절 + 원형동사
  • 가정법은 정중한 표현을 만들어 비즈니스 영어에서 유용
  • If I were in your position으로 조언할 때 더 예의바르게 표현
  • 일기나 에세이 작성 시 I wish 구문으로 감정 표현력 향상
  • As if/As though와 함께 사용하여 비교 표현 강화
  • 회의나 발표에서 I suggest that we...로 전문적인 제안
  • 영어 면접에서 If I were hired로 가정 상황 설명

11. 연습문제 (Practice Exercises)

객관식 문제

1. Which is correct?
A. I wish I was taller.
B. I wish I were taller.
정답: B. "I wish I were taller." - wish 다음에는 가정법 과거를 사용하므로 were를 써야 합니다.
2. Choose the correct form: It is vital that she ___ on time.
A. is
B. be
정답: B. be - "It is vital that..." 구문에서는 that절에 동사 원형을 사용합니다.
3. Fill in: I suggest that he ___ harder.
A. studies
B. study
정답: B. study - "suggest that" 뒤에는 동사 원형을 사용합니다.
4. Which sentence is grammatically correct?
A. If he was here, we could start.
B. If he were here, we could start.
정답: B. "If he were here..." - 가정법 조건문에서는 were를 사용합니다.
5. Complete the sentence: The doctor recommends that she ___.
A. rests
B. rest
정답: B. rest - "recommend that" 뒤에는 동사 원형을 사용합니다.
