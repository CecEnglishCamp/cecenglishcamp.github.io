1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

SOSO

형용사나 부사를 강조할 때 사용

So + 형용사: She is so tired.

So + 부사: He runs so fast.

So + much/many: I have so much work.

SUSUCH

명사구를 강조할 때 사용

Such + a/an + 명사: It was such a good idea.

Such + 복수명사: They are such nice people.

Such + 추상명사: We had such fun.

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

기본 문법 규칙

  • So + 형용사/부사 → so fast, so beautiful, so well
  • Such + (a/an) + 형용사 + 명사 → such a nice day, such an interesting book
  • So + 형용사 + that → She was so tired that she fell asleep.
  • Such + 명사구 + that → It was such a good idea that everyone agreed.
  • So는 단독 형용사/부사 강조, Such는 명사 포함 강조
  • "Such"는 감탄문에서도 자주 사용됨: What such fun!

결과 표현 (Result Expressions)

  • So + 형용사 + that + 결과 → It was so hot that we stayed inside.
  • Such + 명사구 + that + 결과 → It was such a loud noise that we woke up.
  • So much/many + 명사 + that → I have so much work that I can't go out.
  • Such + 명사 + that → He showed such talent that everyone was amazed.

3. 예시 문장 (Example Sentences)

So Examples

  • She is so kind.
  • This movie is so funny.
  • He runs so fast.
  • I feel so tired today.
  • They speak English so well.
  • The weather is so cold.
  • This cake tastes so good.
  • He works so hard.
  • We are so excited.
  • It's so quiet here.

Such Examples

  • It was such a great party.
  • She is such a good teacher.
  • He has such a big house.
  • They are such nice people.
  • This is such an important meeting.
  • We had such a fun time.
  • It's such a beautiful day.
  • He gave such a long speech.
  • She made such a delicious meal.
  • That was such a surprise.

의문문 예시 (Question Examples)

  • Why is she so tired?
  • Is this movie so funny?
  • Do they speak so well?
  • Was the weather so bad?
  • Is he so busy today?
  • Was it such a good idea?
  • Did she give such a great answer?
  • Is this such an important topic?
  • Was that such a surprise?
  • Did you have such a good time?

4. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (School Situation)
A: The test was so hard!
B: Yes, and it was such a long test too.
A: I studied so much last night.
B: Me too. It was such a stressful day.
여행 상황 (Travel Situation)
A: The beach was so beautiful.
B: Yes, it was such a perfect place to relax.
A: We had such a great time.
B: And the sunset was so amazing.
직장 상황 (Work Situation)
A: He gave such a clear presentation.
B: Yes, and he spoke so confidently.
A: It was such an important meeting.
B: Everyone was so focused.
온라인 상황 (Online Situation)
A: This app is so easy to use.
B: I agree. It's such a helpful tool.
A: The design is so clean.
B: And it saves so much time.
가족 상황 (Family Situation)
A: Dinner was so delicious.
B: Mom made such a tasty meal.
A: I'm so full now.
B: It was such a nice evening.
병원 상황 (Hospital Situation)
A: The doctor was so kind.
B: Yes, and she gave such good advice.
A: I feel so much better now.
B: It was such a helpful visit.

5. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining Practice)

결과 연결 문장 만들기

She was tired. She fell asleep.
→ She was so tired that she fell asleep.
It was a good idea. Everyone agreed.
→ It was such a good idea that everyone agreed.
The weather was cold. We stayed inside.
→ The weather was so cold that we stayed inside.
He gave a long speech. People got bored.
→ He gave such a long speech that people got bored.
The cake was delicious. We ate it all.
→ The cake was so delicious that we ate it all.
She is a good teacher. Students love her.
→ She is such a good teacher that students love her.
They worked hard. They finished early.
→ They worked so hard that they finished early.
It was a beautiful day. We went hiking.
→ It was such a beautiful day that
