1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

DSDIRECT SPEECH

말한 그대로 인용
원래 말을 그대로 전달

특징: 인용부호(" ") 사용

시제: 원래 시제 유지

예시: "I'm tired," she said.

ISINDIRECT SPEECH

말한 내용을 전달
보고하는 형태로 변환

특징: 인용부호 없음

시제: 시제 변화 (현재→과거)

예시: She said she was tired.

주요 차이점

Direct Speech:
인용부호 사용, 원래 시제 유지, 원래 말 그대로
Indirect Speech:
시제/대명사/시간 표현 변화, 보고 형태로 전달

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

기본 구조

  • Direct Speech: "문장," + 주어 + said
  • Indirect Speech: 주어 + said + that + 문장
  • 의문문 (Yes/No): 주어 + asked + if/whether + 문장
  • 의문문 (Wh-): 주어 + asked + wh-word + 문장
시제 변화
현재 → 과거
현재진행 → 과거진행
현재완료 → 과거완료
조동사 변화
will → would
can → could
may → might
대명사 변화
I → he/she
my → his/her
we → they
시간 표현 변화
now → then
today → that day
tomorrow → the next day

상세 변화 규칙

  • 시제 변화: 현재 → 과거 / 현재진행 → 과거진행 / 현재완료 → 과거완료
  • 대명사 변화: I → he/she / my → his/her / we → they
  • 시간 표현 변화: today → that day / now → then / tomorrow → the next day
  • 의문문: "Where are you?" → He asked where I was

3. 예시 문장 (Example Sentences)

Direct Speech

  • "I'm happy," she said.
  • "We love music," they said.
  • "He is tired," John said.
  • "I have finished," I said.
  • "She can swim," he said.
  • "We are ready," they said.
  • "I will go," she said.
  • "They play soccer," he said.
  • "I like this book," she said.
  • "We are studying," they said.

Indirect Speech

  • She said that she was happy.
  • They said that they loved music.
  • John said that he was tired.
  • I said that I had finished.
  • He said that she could swim.
  • They said that they were ready.
  • She said that she would go.
  • He said that they played soccer.
  • She said that she liked that book.
  • They said that they were studying.

의문문 예시 (Question Examples)

Direct Questions

  • "Where are you?" he asked.
  • "Do you like pizza?" she asked.
  • "Can you help me?" I asked.
  • "Are they coming?" we asked.
  • "What is your name?" he asked.

Indirect Questions

  • He asked where I was.
  • She asked if I liked pizza.
  • I asked if he could help me.
  • We asked if they were coming.
  • He asked what my name was.

4. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (School Situation)
A: "I'm ready for the test," she said.
B: She said that she was ready for the test.
A: "Do you understand the question?" the teacher asked.
B: The teacher asked if I understood the question.
여행 상황 (Travel Situation)
A: "We love this city," they said.
B: They said that they loved the city.
A: "Where is the hotel?" I asked.
B: You asked where the hotel was.
직장 상황 (Work Situation)
A: "I finished the report," he said.
B: He said that he had finished the report.
A: "Can you join the meeting?" she asked.
B: She asked if I could join the meeting.
온라인 상황 (Online Situation)
A: "I'm logging in now," she said.
B: She said that she was logging in then.
A: "Did you get the link?" I asked.
B: You asked if I had gotten the link.
가족 상황 (Family Situation)
A: "We are cooking dinner," they said.
B: They said that they were cooking dinner.
