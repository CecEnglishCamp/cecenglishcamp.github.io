1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

NOUNIFYNominalization

Nominalization (명사화): **동사**나 **형용사**를 **명사**로 바꾸어 문장을 더 공식적이고 **응집력** 있게 만드는 표현
명사화된 표현은 주어, 목적어, 전치사구 등 다양한 위치에서 사용 가능
**학술적 글쓰기, 보고서, 시험 응답** 등 공식적인 글쓰기에서 자주 사용됨

동사 → 명사: They decided. → Their **decision** was final.

형용사 → 명사: She is strong. → Her **strength** is impressive.

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

  • **동사 → 명사 (Verb to Noun):**
    • decide → **decision** (결정)
    • fail → **failure** (실패)
    • grow → **growth** (성장)
    • move → **movement** (움직임)
    • approve → **approval** (승인)
  • **형용사 → 명사 (Adjective to Noun):**
    • happy → **happiness** (행복)
    • strong → **strength** (강점)
    • kind → **kindness** (친절)
    • active → **activity** (활동)
    • curious → **curiosity** (호기심)
  • 명사화된 표현은 다음 위치에서 사용 가능:
    • **주어:** **Her decision** surprised us.
    • **목적어:** We support **his growth**.
    • **전치사구:** In **conclusion**, the results were clear.
  • 명사화는 문장을 더 **공식적**이고 **응집력** 있게 만듦
  • **불필요한 동사** 사용을 줄이고 **요약 효과**를 줌

3. 긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

동사 → 명사

  • They decided. → Their **decision** was final.
  • He failed. → His **failure** taught him a lesson.
  • We grew quickly. → Our **growth** was impressive.
  • She moved. → Her **movement** was graceful.
  • They approved the plan. → Their **approval** was necessary.

형용사 → 명사

  • She is happy. → Her **happiness** is contagious.
  • He is strong. → His **strength** is admirable.
  • They are kind. → Their **kindness** is appreciated.
  • She is active. → Her **activity** increased.
  • He is curious. → His **curiosity** led to discovery.

5. 상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (Failure)
A: She failed the test.
B: Her **failure** was unexpected.
A: She’ll try again.

여행 상황 (Movement)
A: We moved quickly.
B: Our **movement** impressed the guide.
A: We were prepared.

직장 상황 (Approval)
A: They approved the budget.
B: Their **approval** came just in time.
A: Let’s begin.

온라인 상황 (Curiosity)
A: He is curious about AI.
B: His **curiosity** is growing.
A: That’s great.

가족 상황 (Happiness)
A: She is happy today.

A: Me too.

병원 상황 (Strength)
A: He is strong.
B: His **strength** helps him recover.
A: I admire that.

7. 실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

  • 학교 상황: Her **failure** motivated her to study harder. (동사 'fail'의 명사화)
  • 직장 상황: Their **approval** allowed us to launch the project. (동사 'approve'의 명사화)
  • 가족 상황: His **kindness** made everyone feel welcome. (형용사 'kind'의 명사화)
  • 온라인 상황: Our **growth** in followers was unexpected. (동사 'grow'의 명사화)
  • 병원 상황: Her **strength** helped her recover quickly. (형용사 'strong'의 명사화)
  • 여행 상황: The **movement** of the train was smooth. (동사 'move'의 명사화)
  • 회의 상황: In **conclusion**, the team agreed to proceed. (동사 'conclude'의 명사화)

8. 자주 쓰이는 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • **동사 → 명사:** decide → **decision** / fail → **failure** / grow → **growth**
  • **형용사 → 명사:** happy → **happiness** / strong → **strength** / kind → **kindness**
  • **명사화된 주어:** Her **decision** surprised us. (문장의 주체로 활용)
  • **명사화된 목적어:** We support his **growth**. (문장의 대상으로 활용)
  • **전치사구 명사화:** **In conclusion**, the results were clear. (문장 전환, 요약)
  • **추상 명사 활용:** **curiosity**, **kindness**, **strength**, **approval** (글의 논조를 공식적으로 만듦)
  • **명사화된 표현 + 동사:** Their **movement** increased / His **failure** affected the team

9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ She decided. Her decided was final. → ✅ Her **decision** was final. (동사를 명사로 변환)
  • ❌ He failed. His fail taught him. → ✅ His **failure** taught him. (동사를 올바른 명사 형태로 변환)
  • ❌ She is happy. Her happy is obvious. → ✅ Her **happiness** is obvious. (형용사를 명사 형태로 변환)
  • ❌ Their kind is appreciated. → ✅ Their **kindness** is appreciated. (형용사를 명사 형태로 변환)
  • ❌ His curious led to discovery. → ✅ His **curiosity** led to discovery. (형용사를 명사 형태로 변환)

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • Nominalization은 **공식적이고 응집력 있는 문장**을 만드는 데 효과적
  • 동사/형용사를 명사로 바꾸면 **요약과 강조**가 쉬워짐
  • 명사화된 표현은 **주어, 목적어, 전치사구**로 다양하게 활용 가능
  • 학술적 글쓰기에서는 **명사화된 추상어**가 자주 등장함
  • 명사화는 **문장 구조를 다양화**하고 반복을 줄임
  • 명사화된 표현은 **수식어와 함께 사용**하면 더 풍부한 표현 가능 (e.g., *unexpected failure*, *unwavering kindness*)
