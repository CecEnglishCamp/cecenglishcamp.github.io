1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

PPPRESENT PERFECT

과거의 경험, 결과, 완료를 현재와 연결할 때 사용

경험: I have been to Korea.

결과: I have lost my key. (지금 없음)

완료: I have finished my work.

PSPAST SIMPLE

과거의 특정 시점에 일어난 일을 말할 때 사용

과거 사실: I went to Korea in 2020.

특정 시점: I lost my key yesterday.

완료된 과거: I finished my work at 5 PM.

핵심 차이점

Present Perfect

시점 없음 / 경험 강조 / 현재와 연결

Past Simple

시점 있음 / 과거 사실 / 현재와 단절

시간 표현으로 구분하기

Present Perfect와 함께

  • ever, never
  • just, already, yet
  • recently, lately
  • so far, up to now
  • since, for

Past Simple과 함께

  • yesterday, last week
  • in 2020, ago
  • when I was young
  • at 3 PM, on Monday
  • during, while

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

기본 구조

  • Present Perfect: have/has + 과거분사 (I have seen / She has gone)
  • Past Simple: 동사 과거형 (I saw / She went)
  • Present Perfect는 시점 표현 없이 사용: ever, never, just, already, yet, recently
  • Past Simple은 시점 표현과 함께 사용: yesterday, last week, in 2020, two days ago
  • 현재완료는 결과/경험/지속을 강조, 과거시제는 사건/사실/완료를 강조

사용 목적별 구분

  • 경험 표현: Have you ever been to Japan? (현재완료)
  • 과거 사실: I went to Japan last year. (과거시제)
  • 현재 결과: I have lost my key. (지금 없음)
  • 과거 완료: I lost my key yesterday. (과거 사건)
  • 막 끝난 일: I have just finished. (현재완료)
  • 특정 시점: I finished at 5 PM. (과거시제)

3. 동사별 비교 예시 (Verb-by-Verb Comparisons)

동사: GO
Present Perfect

4. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (School Situation)
A: Have you finished your homework?
B: Yes, I've just finished it.
A: When did you finish?
B: I finished it 10 minutes ago.
여행 상황 (Travel Situation)
A: Have you ever been to Jeju Island?
B: Yes, I've been there twice.
A: When did you go?
B: I went last summer and again in March.
직장 상황 (Work Situation)
A: Have you sent the report?
B: Yes, I've already sent it.
A: When did you send it?
B: I sent it this morning.
온라인 상황 (Online Situation)
A: Have you logged in?
B: Yes, I've just logged in.
A: Did you log in before 9?
B: No, I logged in at 9:15.
가족 상황 (Family Situation)
A: Have you eaten?
B: Yes, I've eaten already.
A: What time did you eat?
B: I ate around 6:30.
병원 상황 (Hospital Situation)
A: Have you taken your medicine?
B: Yes, I've taken it.
A: When did you take it?
B: I took it after lunch.

7. 자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴 (Common Patterns)

표현 패턴

Present Perfect: have/has + p.p I have seen / She has gone
Past Simple: 동사 과거형 I saw / She went
Present Perfect + just/already/yet/ever/never I've just finished / Have you ever been?
Past Simple + yesterday/last week/in 2020/ago I went yesterday / She called two hours ago
Have you ever been to ___? 경험 질문 (Present Perfect)
Did you go to ___ last year? 과거 시점 질문 (Past Simple)
I've never tried ___ 경험 부정 (Present Perfect)
I tried ___ last night 과거 사실 (Past Simple)
She has finished her work 결과 강조 (Present Perfect)
She finished her work at 3 시점 강조 (Past Simple)

10. 자가진단 (Self-Check)

학습 완료 체크

레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • 시점 표현이 있으면 → Past Simple
  • 시점 표현이 없고 경험/결과 강조 → Present Perfect
  • 현재완료는 지금과 연결됨 / 과거시제는 지금과 단절
  • "Have you ever…?"는 경험 질문 / "Did you…?"는 과거 사실 질문
  • "I've just…"는 막 끝난 일 / "I finished…"는 과거 완료
  • "I've never…"는 경험 부정 / "I didn't…"는 과거 행동 부정
  • 동일 동사로 비교 연습하면 구분이 쉬워짐
  • 시점 표현이 핵심! → last night, yesterday, in 2020 등은 과거시제

  • Practice tense choice in writing and conversation.
  • Use both tenses in storytelling and experience sharing.
  • Compare with "just," "recently," and "before" for nuance.
  • Record and review your own sentences for clarity and fluency.
