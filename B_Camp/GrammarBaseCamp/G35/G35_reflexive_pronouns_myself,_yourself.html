G35: Reflexive Pronouns

A2 Grammar Lesson – 재귀대명사

기본 개념 (Concept)

Reflexive Pronouns는 주어가 자기 자신에게 동작을 할 때 사용되는 대명사입니다. 행동의 주체와 대상이 같은 사람일 때 사용합니다.

Subject Pronoun Reflexive Pronoun Example
I myself I hurt myself
You yourself You cut yourself
He himself He taught himself
She herself She introduced herself
It itself The cat cleaned itself
We ourselves We prepared ourselves
You (plural) yourselves You enjoyed yourselves
They themselves They hurt themselves

재귀 동작

I cut myself

자기 자신에게 하는 행동

강조

I myself did it

주어를 강조할 때

혼자서

I did it by myself

도움 없이 혼자서

예시:

I cut myself while cooking. (요리하다가 베었어요)
She taught herself French. (혼자서 프랑스어를 배웠어요)
We prepared ourselves for the exam. (시험을 준비했어요)

문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

기본 사용 규칙

  • 주어와 재귀대명사는 반드시 일치해야 함
  • 재귀대명사는 목적어 자리에서 사용됨
  • 행동의 주체와 대상이 같을 때 사용
  • 타동사와 함께 자주 사용: hurt, teach, introduce, cut, enjoy 등

사용 상황

  • 자기 자신에게 하는 행동: She hurt herself. (자신을 다쳤다)
  • 혼자서 하는 행동: I did it by myself. (혼자서 했다)
  • 강조 표현: He himself said it. (그 자신이 말했다)
  • 감정이나 상태: I'm proud of myself. (자랑스러워한다)

주의사항

  • 재귀동작이 아닌 경우 사용하지 않음: He saw him. (다른 사람을 봄)
  • 전치사 뒤에도 사용 가능: I bought it for myself.
  • Itself는 사물이나 동물에 사용: The door opened by itself.
  • 복수 주어에는 복수 재귀대명사: They taught themselves.

자주 사용되는 동사들

  • hurt: I hurt myself. (다쳤다)
  • teach: She taught herself. (가르쳤다)
  • introduce: He introduced himself. (소개했다)
  • cut: You cut yourself. (베었다)
  • enjoy: We enjoyed ourselves. (즐겼다)
  • prepare: They prepared themselves. (준비했다)

긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

Myself (나 자신)

  • I introduced myself to the group. (그룹에 자기소개를 했어요)
  • I taught myself how to code. (혼자서 코딩을 배웠어요)
  • I hurt myself while cooking. (요리하다가 다쳤어요)
  • I made this cake myself. (이 케이크를 직접 만들었어요)
  • I cleaned the room myself. (방을 혼자서 청소했어요)
  • I'm proud of myself. (자랑스러워요)
  • I bought this for myself. (이걸 제가 사용하려고 샀어요)
  • I talk to myself when I'm nervous. (긴장하면 혼잣말을 해요)

Yourself (당신 자신)

  • You should take care of yourself. (자신을 돌보세요)
  • You taught yourself Spanish. (혼자서 스페인어를 배웠어요)
  • You hurt yourself playing soccer. (축구하다가 다쳤어요)
  • You can do it yourself. (혼자서 할 수 있어요)
  • You introduced yourself well. (자기소개를 잘 했어요)
  • You should be proud of yourself. (자랑스러워해야 해요)
  • You cut yourself shaving. (면도하다가 베었어요)
  • You prepared yourself for the test. (시험을 준비했어요)

Himself / Herself / Itself

  • He cut himself shaving. (면도하다가 베었어요)
  • She taught herself piano. (혼자서 피아노를 배웠어요)
  • The cat cleaned itself. (고양이가 스스로 그루밍했어요)
  • He made dinner himself. (혼자서 저녁을 만들었어요)
  • She introduced herself confidently. (자신 있게 자기소개를 했어요)
  • The door closed by itself. (문이 저절로 닫혔어요)
  • He talks to himself while working. (일하면서 혼잣말을 해요)
  • She prepared herself for the interview. (면접을 준비했어요)

Ourselves / Yourselves / Themselves

  • We prepared ourselves for the test. (시험을 준비했어요)
  • You all should enjoy yourselves. (모두 즐기세요)
  • They hurt themselves during practice. (연습 중에 다쳤어요)
  • We taught ourselves the rules. (규칙을 스스로 배웠어요)
  • They introduced themselves politely. (정중하게 자기소개를 했어요)
  • We bought this for ourselves. (우리가 사용하려고 샀어요)
  • You can be proud of yourselves. (자랑스러워해도 돼요)
  • They prepared themselves well. (잘 준비했어요)

의문문 예시 (Question Examples)

  • A: Did you hurt yourself? (다쳤어요?)
    B: Yes, but it's just a small cut. (네, 하지만 작은 상처예요)
  • A: Did she teach herself French? (혼자서 프랑스어를 배웠나요?)
    B: Yes, using online courses. (네, 온라인 강의로요)
  • A: Did he introduce himself? (자기소개를 했나요?)
    B: Yes, very politely. (네, 아주 정중하게요)
  • A: Did they enjoy themselves at the party? (파티에서 즐겁게 보냈나요?)
    B: Yes, they had a great time. (네, 정말 즐거워했어요)
  • A: Did you clean the room yourself? (방을 혼자서 청소했어요?)
    B: Yes, it took two hours. (네, 두 시간 걸렸어요)
  • A: Did we prepare ourselves well? (우리가 잘 준비했나요?)
    B: I think so, yes. (그런 것 같아요)
  • A: Did the cat wash itself? (고양이가 스스로 그루밍했나요?)
    B: Yes, cats are very clean. (네, 고양이는 아주 깨끗해요)
  • A: Did you do this project yourself? (이 프로젝트를 혼자서 했어요?)
    B: Mostly, with some help. (대부분은 혼자, 약간의 도움을 받았어요)
  • A: Did she make this dress herself? (이 드레스를 직접 만들었나요?)
    B: Yes, she's very talented. (네, 정말 재능이 있어요)
  • A: Did they fix the problem themselves? (문제를 스스로 해결했나요?)
    B: Yes, without any help. (네, 도움 없이요)

상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (School Situation)
A: Did you write the essay yourself?
B: Yes, I did it all by myself.
A: That's impressive. You taught yourself well.
B: Thanks. I'm proud of myself.
A: You should be. Did you prepare yourself for the presentation?
B: Yes, I practiced by myself for hours.

요리 상황 (Cooking Situation)
A: Did you make this delicious meal yourself?
B: Yes, I taught myself using YouTube videos.
A: Be careful with that knife, you might cut yourself.
B: Don't worry, I always take care of myself in the kitchen.
A: Did you enjoy yourself while cooking?
B: Absolutely! I find it very relaxing.

직장 상황 (Work Situation)
A: He fixed the printer himself, didn't he?
B: Yes, he taught himself how to do it.
A: That saved us time and money.
B: He always handles problems himself.
A: Did he hurt himself while fixing it?
B: No, he was very careful.

여행 상황 (Travel Situation)
A: Did you book the tickets yourself?
B: Yes, I used the airline app.
A: You organized everything yourself?
B: I wanted to challenge myself with trip planning.
A: Are you traveling by yourself?
B: No, my friends will join themselves to the trip later.

운동 상황 (Exercise Situation)
A: Did you hurt yourself during the workout?
B: Just a little, but I'll be fine.
A: You should take care of yourself better.
B: You're right. I push myself too hard sometimes.
A: Did you train yourself or have a coach?
B: I trained myself using fitness apps.

기술 학습 (Technology Learning)
A: Did you design this website yourself?
B: Yes, I learned it online by myself.
A: You taught yourself coding?
B: I practiced every day by myself for months.
A: Are you proud of yourself?
B: Very much! It was challenging but rewarding.

문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

두 문장을 재귀대명사를 사용해서 하나로 결합하는 연습입니다:

I made this cake. + I did it alone.
→ I made this cake myself.
You cleaned your room. + You didn't get help.
→ You cleaned your room yourself.
  • He fixed the bike. + No one helped him. → He fixed the bike himself.
  • She taught French. + She learned alone. → She taught herself French.
  • The cat washed. + No one helped. → The cat washed itself.
  • We prepared for the test. + No tutor helped. → We prepared ourselves for the test.
  • You all enjoyed the party. + No one forced you. → You enjoyed yourselves.
  • They introduced. + No one spoke for them. → They introduced themselves.
  • I hurt my hand. + I did it while cooking. → I hurt myself while cooking.
  • She made dinner. + She did it alone. → She made dinner herself.
  • We organized the event. + We did it without help. → We organized the event ourselves.
  • He cut his finger. + He was shaving. → He cut himself while shaving.

실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

자기소개서

Hello, I'm Alex. I taught myself web design and built this portfolio website myself. I'm someone who challenges myself every day and enjoys learning new skills by myself.

업무 보고

The new employee has been performing excellently. He fixed the technical issue himself and didn't need IT support. He taught himself the new software in just one week.

여행 후기

We planned the whole trip ourselves and booked everything ourselves. We really enjoyed ourselves and are proud of ourselves for organizing such a successful vacation.

건강 상담

The patient injured himself while exercising at home. He treated himself with basic first aid before coming to the clinic. He takes good care of himself generally.

학습 경험 공유

I taught myself three languages using online resources. I prepared myself for the exams by studying every day. I'm proud of myself for achieving fluency without formal classes.

자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • subject + verb + reflexive pronoun: I hurt myself.
  • verb + reflexive pronoun + object: She taught herself French.
  • by + reflexive pronoun: He did it by himself. (혼자서)
  • reflexive pronoun for emphasis: I myself don't agree. (강조)
  • enjoy + reflexive: They enjoyed themselves. (즐겼다)
  • introduce + reflexive: Please introduce yourself. (자기소개하다)
  • prepare + reflexive: We prepared ourselves. (준비하다)
  • help + reflexive: She helped herself to some food. (스스로 가져다 먹다)
  • talk to + reflexive: He talks to himself when nervous. (혼잣말하다)
  • reflexive after preposition: I did it for myself. (나를 위해)

흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ I hurt me → ✅ I hurt myself
  • ❌ She taught her → ✅ She taught herself
  • ❌ They introduced them → ✅ They introduced themselves
  • ❌ You cleaned you → ✅ You cleaned yourself
  • ❌ We prepared us → ✅ We prepared ourselves
  • ❌ He made dinner by him → ✅ He made dinner by himself
  • ❌ The cat washed him → ✅ The cat washed itself
  • ❌ I'm proud of me → ✅ I'm proud of myself
  • ❌ She talks to her → ✅ She talks to herself
  • ❌ We enjoyed us → ✅ We enjoyed ourselves

레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • 재귀대명사는 주어와 반드시 일치해야 함
  • "by myself"는 혼자서라는 의미로 강조됨
  • "myself"는 나 자신에게라는 의미로 동작의 대상
  • 재귀대명사는 감정/행동/강조 표현에 자주 사용됨
  • "enjoy
