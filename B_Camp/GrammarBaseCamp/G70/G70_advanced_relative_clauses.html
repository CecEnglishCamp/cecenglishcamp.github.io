1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

RELAdvanced Relative Clauses

관계절(Relative Clause): 명사 뒤에서 설명을 덧붙이는 절
관계대명사(who, which, that 등)를 사용하여 명사를 수식함
문장의 흐름을 자연스럽게 연결하고 정보를 추가함
제한적(필수 정보)과 비제한적(추가 정보)으로 나뉨

제한적: The man who helped me is my teacher.

비제한적: My brother, who lives in Seoul, is visiting.

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

  • who: 사람을 주어로 설명 (The girl who called me)
  • whom: 사람을 목적어로 설명 (The man whom I met)
  • whose: 소유 표현 (The student whose book is missing)
  • which: 사물/동물 설명 (The car which broke down)
  • that: 사람/사물 모두 가능, 제한적 관계절에 사용
  • where: 장소 설명 (The café where we met)
  • when: 시간 설명 (The day when we arrived)
  • 제한적 관계절: 필수 정보, 쉼표 없음
  • 비제한적 관계절: 추가 정보, 쉼표 있음
  • 관계절은 명사 바로 뒤에 위치함

3. 긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

who / whom / whose

  • The woman who called is my aunt.
  • The man whom I met was kind.
  • The boy whose bike was stolen is crying.

which / that

  • The book which helped me is on the shelf.
  • The movie that we watched was exciting.
  • The phone that broke is expensive.

where / when

  • The café where we met is closed now.
  • The day when we arrived was rainy.

비제한적 관계절

  • My sister, who lives in Busan, is a doctor.
  • This book, which I bought yesterday, is amazing.
  • Our teacher, whose lessons are fun, is very popular.

5. 상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황
A: Who’s that student?
B: The one who won the contest.
A: Impressive!

여행 상황
A: Where did you stay?
B: At the hotel where we met last year.
A: Oh, I remember!

직장 상황
A: Who’s leading the project?
B: The manager whose team succeeded last quarter.
A: Good choice.

온라인 상황
A: What’s that app?
B: The one which helps you focus.
A: I’ll try it.

가족 상황
A: Who’s that man?
B: My uncle, who lives in Jeju.
A: He looks kind.

병원 상황
A: Who treated her?
B: The doctor who specializes in recovery.
A: She’s lucky.

6. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

  • The man is my teacher. He helped me. → The man who helped me is my teacher.
  • I met a woman. She called yesterday. → I met the woman who called yesterday.
  • The student lost his book. His book is red. → The student whose book is red lost it.
  • We visited a café. It was closed. → The café which was closed was the one we visited.
  • He bought a phone. It broke. → The phone that he bought broke.
  • I remember the day. We arrived then. → I remember the day when we arrived.
  • She lives in a house. The house is old. → She lives in a house which is old.
  • We met a man. He lives in Jeju. → We met a man, who lives in Jeju.
  • This book is amazing. I bought it yesterday. → This book, which I bought yesterday, is amazing.
  • Our teacher is popular. Her lessons are fun. → Our teacher, whose lessons are fun, is popular.

7. 실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

  • 학교 상황: The student who asked the question was curious.
  • 직장 상황: The report which you submitted was excellent.
  • 가족 상황: My uncle, who lives in Busan, is visiting.
  • 온라인 상황: The app that helps you focus is trending.
  • 병원 상황: The doctor whose patients recover quickly is respected.
  • 여행 상황: The hotel where we stayed was beautiful.
  • 회의 상황: The manager who led the project gave a speech.

8. 자주 쓰이는 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • The person who + 동사
  • The thing which/that + 동사
  • The place where + 주어 + 동사
  • The time when + 주어 + 동사
  • The person whose + 명사 + 동사
  • 명사, who/which/whose + 동사 (비제한적)
  • 명사 that + 동사 (제한적)
  • 명사 which + 동사 (사물)
  • 명사 who + 동사 (사람)
  • 명사 where/when + 절 (장소/시간)

9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ The man which helped me → ✅ The man who helped me
  • ❌ The book who is new → ✅ The book which is new
  • ❌ The student whose is smart → ✅ The student who is smart
  • ❌ The café when we met → ✅ The café where we met
  • ❌ My brother who lives in Seoul is visiting → ✅ My brother, who lives in Seoul, is visiting

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • who은 사람, which은 사물, that은 둘 다 가능
  • whose는 소유를 표현할 때 사용
  • where은 장소, when은 시간을 설명
  • 제한적 관계절은 쉼표 없이, 비제한적 관계절은 쉼표와 함께 사용
  • 비제한적 관계절은 문장의 핵심 정보가 아님 (추가 설명)
  • 관계절은 명사 바로 뒤에 위치해야 자연스러움
  • 문장 결합 연습을 통해 정보 흐름과 문장 구조 향상

11. 연습문제 (Practice Exercises)

객관식

  1. Which is correct?
    A. The man which helped me.
    B. The man who helped me.
  2. Choose the correct form:
    The café ___ we met is closed.
    A. where
    B. when
  3. Fill in: The student ___ book is missing is crying.
    A. whose
    B. who

1. B. 'man'은 사람이므로 관계대명사 'who'가 적절합니다.

2. A. 'café'는 장소이므로 관계부사 'where'가 적절합니다.

3. A. 'student'의 소유인 'book'을 설명하므로 소유격 관계대명사 'whose'가 적절합니다.
