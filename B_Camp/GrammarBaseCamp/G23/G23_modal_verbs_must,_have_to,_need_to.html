G23: Modal Verbs – Must, Have to, Need to

A1 Grammar Lesson – 의무 표현

기본 개념 (Concept)

Must, Have to, Need to는 모두 "해야 한다"는 의미를 가진 조동사입니다. 하지만 뉘앙스와 사용 상황이 조금씩 다릅니다.

Modal Verb 의미 사용 상황 예시
Must 강한 의무 규칙, 법, 개인적 판단 You must wear a seatbelt.
Have to 외부 의무 일반적 의무, 외부 요구 I have to finish homework.
Need to 필요성 실용적 이유, 개인적 필요 We need to buy groceries.

예시로 비교해보면:

You must wear a seatbelt. (법적 규칙 - 반드시 해야 함)
I have to finish my homework. (학교 과제 - 외부에서 요구)
We need to buy groceries. (생활 필요 - 실용적 이유)

문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

긍정문 (Affirmative)

  • I must go now. (지금 가야 해요)
  • You have to study tonight. (오늘 밤 공부해야 해요)
  • She needs to sleep early. (일찍 자야 해요)
  • We must be quiet. (조용히 해야 해요)
  • They have to work tomorrow. (내일 일해야 해요)
  • He needs to call his mom. (엄마에게 전화해야 해요)
  • You must listen carefully. (주의 깊게 들어야 해요)
  • I have to leave at 5. (5시에 떠나야 해요)
  • We need to hurry. (서둘러야 해요)
  • She must finish the report. (보고서를 끝내야 해요)

부정문 (Negative)

  • You must not smoke here. (여기서 담배 피우면 안 됩니다)
  • I don't have to go today. (오늘 갈 필요 없어요)
  • She doesn't need to come. (올 필요 없어요)
  • We must not be late. (늦으면 안 됩니다)
  • He doesn't have to pay now. (지금 돈 낼 필요 없어요)
  • They don't need to bring anything. (아무것도 가져올 필요 없어요)
  • You mustn't touch that. (그거 만지면 안 됩니다)
  • I don't have to work weekends. (주말에 일할 필요 없어요)
  • We don't need to rush. (서두를 필요 없어요)

의문문 (Interrogative)

  • Must I finish this now? (지금 이걸 끝내야 하나요?)
  • Do you have to leave? (떠나야 하나요?)
  • Does she need to study? (공부해야 하나요?)
  • Must we wear uniforms? (교복을 입어야 하나요?)
  • Do they have to pay? (돈을 내야 하나요?)
  • Does he need to come early? (일찍 와야 하나요?)
  • Must you go now? (지금 가야 하나요?)
  • Do I have to sign this? (이것에 서명해야 하나요?)

긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

Must - 강한 의무/규칙

  • Students must attend all classes. (학생들은 모든 수업에 참석해야 합니다)
  • You must show your ID. (신분증을 보여줘야 합니다)
  • We must respect the rules. (규칙을 지켜야 합니다)
  • Drivers must stop at red lights. (운전자는 빨간불에 멈춰야 합니다)
  • Everyone must be on time. (모든 사람이 시간을 지켜야 합니다)

Have to - 외부 의무

  • I have to submit the report by Friday. (금요일까지 보고서를 제출해야 해요)
  • She has to take medicine twice a day. (하루에 두 번 약을 먹어야 해요)
  • We have to clean our rooms. (방을 청소해야 해요)
  • He has to go to the dentist. (치과에 가야 해요)
  • They have to pass the exam. (시험에 통과해야 해요)

Need to - 필요성

  • I need to buy new shoes. (새 신발을 사야 해요)
  • You need to get some rest. (휴식을 취해야 해요)
  • We need to save money. (돈을 절약해야 해요)
  • She needs to practice more. (더 연습해야 해요)
  • They need to fix the computer. (컴퓨터를 고쳐야 해요)

부정문 예시 (Negative Examples)

Must not (Mustn't) - 금지

  • You must not park here. (여기 주차하면 안 됩니다)
  • Students mustn't cheat on exams. (학생들은 시험에서 부정행위하면 안 됩니다)
  • We must not forget the meeting. (회의를 잊으면 안 됩니다)
  • Children mustn't play with fire. (아이들은 불장난하면 안 됩니다)
  • You mustn't tell anyone. (아무에게도 말하면 안 됩니다)

Don't have to - 의무 없음

  • You don't have to come early. (일찍 올 필요 없어요)
  • I don't have to work tomorrow. (내일 일할 필요 없어요)
  • She doesn't have to cook dinner. (저녁을 요리할 필요 없어요)
  • We don't have to wear suits. (정장을 입을 필요 없어요)
  • He doesn't have to pay today. (오늘 돈 낼 필요 없어요)

Don't need to - 필요 없음

  • You don't need to worry. (걱정할 필요 없어요)
  • I don't need to study tonight. (오늘 밤 공부할 필요 없어요)
  • We don't need to hurry. (서두를 필요 없어요)
  • She doesn't need to bring lunch. (점심을 가져올 필요 없어요)
  • They don't need to wait. (기다릴 필요 없어요)

의문문 예시 (Question Examples)

  • A: Must I wear a tie? (넥타이를 매야 하나요?)
    B: Yes, you must. It's formal. (네, 정식 행사라서요)
  • A: Do you have to work late? (늦게까지 일해야 하나요?)
    B: Yes, I have to finish this project. (네, 이 프로젝트를 끝내야 해요)
  • A: Does she need to bring anything? (그녀가 뭔가 가져와야 하나요?)
    B: No, she doesn't need to. (아니요, 필요 없어요)
  • A: Must we arrive on time? (정시에 도착해야 하나요?)
    B: Yes, you must. The show starts at 8. (네, 8시에 쇼가 시작해요)
  • A: Do they have to take the test? (시험을 봐야 하나요?)
    B: No, they don't have to. It's optional. (아니요, 선택사항이에요)
  • A: Does he need to call back? (다시 전화해야 하나요?)
    B: Yes, he needs to confirm. (네, 확인해야 해요)
  • A: Must I pay now? (지금 돈을 내야 하나요?)
    B: No, you can pay later. (아니요, 나중에 내도 돼요)
  • A: Do we have to attend the meeting? (회의에 참석해야 하나요?)
    B: Yes, it's mandatory. (네, 필수예요)

상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (School Situation)
A: Do I have to submit the essay today?
B: Yes, you must. The deadline is today.
A: Do I need to print it?
B: No, you don't need to. Email is fine.
A: Must I include references?
B: Yes, you have to cite all sources.

직장 상황 (Work Situation)
A: Do I have to work overtime?
B: No, you don't have to stay late.
A: Must I attend the training?
B: Yes, it's required for all employees.
A: Do I need to bring my laptop?
B: Yes, you need to for the presentation.

여행 상황 (Travel Situation)
A: Must I show my passport?
B: Yes, you must at immigration.
A: Do I have to declare anything?
B: Only if you have items over the limit.
A: Do I need to take off my shoes?
B: Yes, you need to at security.

병원 상황 (Hospital Situation)
A: Must I fast before the test?
B: Yes, you mustn't eat for 12 hours.
A: Do I have to bring insurance?
B: Yes, you have to show your card.
A: Do I need to make an appointment?
B: No, you don't need to for emergencies.

가족 상황 (Family Situation)
A: Must I clean my room?
B: Yes, you must before going out.
A: Do I have to do the dishes?
B: No, your sister has to do them.
A: Do I need to call grandma?
B: Yes, you need to. It's her birthday.

쇼핑 상황 (Shopping Situation)
A: Must I pay in cash?
B: No, you don't have to. Cards are fine.
A: Do I need to bring a bag?
B: You don't need to. We have bags.
A: Must I show ID for returns?
B: Yes, you must have a receipt and ID.

흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ I must to go. → ✅ I must go.
  • ❌ Do you must come? → ✅ Must you come?
  • ❌ She don't have to work. → ✅ She doesn't have to work.
  • ❌ You don't must smoke. → ✅ You must not smoke.
  • ❌ I need study. → ✅ I need to study.
  • ❌ Must I to pay? → ✅ Must I pay?
  • ❌ He has not to come. → ✅ He doesn't have to come.
  • ❌ We need not to worry. → ✅ We don't need to worry.
  • ❌ Does she must go? → ✅ Must she go?
  • ❌ You haven't to do it. → ✅ You don't have to do it.

레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • Must는 강한 의무와 금지를 표현할 때 사용하세요.
  • Have to는 외부에서 요구하는 의무를 나타냅니다.
  • Need to는 개인적 필요나 실용적 이유를 강조합니다.
  • 부정문에서 must not = 금지, don't have to = 의무 없음입니다.
  • 의문문에서 Must는 앞으로, Have to와 Need to는 do/does를 사용합니다.
  • 격식적 상황에서는 Must, 일상적 상황에서는 Have to/Need to를 더 많이 씁니다.
  • Must는 과거형이 없어서 과거에는 had to를 사용합니다.

비교 표현 (Comparison)

  • Must vs Have to:
    You must be quiet. (강한 명령)
    You have to be quiet. (규칙이나 요구)
  • Must not vs Don't have to:
    You must not smoke. (금지)
    You don't have to smoke. (의무 없음, 이상한 문장)
  • Have to vs Need to:
    I have to go to work. (의무)
    I need to go to the store. (필요)
  • 강도 순서:
    Must (가장 강함) > Have to (외부 의무) > Need to (개인적 필요)
  • 과거형 비교:
    Had to work yesterday. (과거의 의무)
    Needed to buy food. (과거의 필요)
