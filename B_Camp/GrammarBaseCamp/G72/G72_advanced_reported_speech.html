1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

REPORTEDAdvanced Reported Speech

간접화법(Reported Speech): 다른 사람의 말을 간접적으로 전달하는 표현
직접화법: “I’m tired,” she said.
간접화법: She said (that) she was tired.
시제, 인칭, 시간/장소 표현이 바뀜

직접: “I am busy,” he said.

간접: He said (that) he was busy.

직접: “Can you help me?” she asked.

간접: She asked if I could help her.

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

  • 시제 전환:
    • 현재 → 과거 (am → was)
    • 현재완료 → 과거완료 (have seen → had seen)
    • will → would / can → could / may → might
  • 인칭 변화: I → he/she, you → I/he/she 등
  • 시간/장소 변화: today → that day, now → then, here → there
  • 진술문: say/tell + (that) + 주어 + 동사
  • 의문문: ask + if/whether/wh- + 주어 + 동사 (어순 변화)
  • 명령문: tell/ask + 목적어 + to 동사
  • 제안문: suggest + that + 주어 + 동사 / suggest + ing

3. 긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

진술문

  • “I’m tired,” she said. → She said (that) she was tired.
  • “We are ready,” they said. → They said (that) they were ready.
  • “He has finished,” she said. → She said (that) he had finished.

의문문

  • “Are you okay?” he asked. → He asked if I was okay.
  • “Where do you live?” she asked. → She asked where I lived.
  • “Can you help me?” he asked. → He asked if I could help him.

명령문

  • “Close the door,” she said. → She told me to close the door.
  • “Don’t be late,” he said. → He told me not to be late.
  • “Please help me,” she said. → She asked me to help her.

제안문

  • “Let’s go,” he said. → He suggested that we go.
  • “Why don’t we wait?” she said. → She suggested waiting.
  • “Let’s take a break,” they said. → They suggested that we take a break.

5. 상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황
A: “I forgot my homework,” he said.
B: He said he had forgotten his homework.
A: That’s okay.

여행 상황
A: “Can we leave now?” she asked.
B: She asked if we could leave then.
A: Let’s go.

직장 상황
A: “Finish the report today,” the manager said.
B: He told us to finish the report that day.
A: Got it.

온라인 상황
A: “Where is the file?” he asked.
B: He asked where the file was.
A: I’ll send it.

가족 상황
A: “I’m cooking dinner,” Mom said.
B: She said she was cooking dinner.
A: Smells great!

병원 상황
A: “Don’t worry,” the nurse said.
B: She told me not to worry.
A: That helped.

7. 실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

  • 학교 상황: He said (that) he had forgotten his homework.
  • 직장 상황: She told us to finish the report that day.
  • 가족 상황: Mom said she was cooking dinner.
  • 온라인 상황: He asked where the file was.
  • 병원 상황: The nurse told me not to worry.
  • 여행 상황: She asked if we could leave then.
  • 회의 상황: He suggested that we take a break.

8. 자주 쓰이는 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • say/tell + (that) + 주어 + 과거동사
  • ask + if/whether + 주어 + 과거동사
  • ask + wh- + 주어 + 과거동사
  • tell/ask + 목적어 + to 동사
  • tell + 목적어 + not to 동사
  • suggest + that + 주어 + 동사원형
  • suggest + 동명사
  • 시제 전환: am → was / have seen → had seen / will → would
  • 인칭 변화: I → he/she / you → I/he/she
  • 시간/장소 변화: now → then / today → that day / here → there

9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ He said he is busy → ✅ He said he was busy
  • ❌ She asked where do I live → ✅ She asked where I lived
  • ❌ He told me close the door → ✅ He told me to close the door
  • ❌ She said I am late → ✅ She said I was late
  • ❌ He asked can I help → ✅ He asked if I could help

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • 간접화법에서는 시제 전환이 핵심 (현재 → 과거)
  • 의문문은 어순 변화가 필요 (do/does/did 제거)
  • 명령문은 to 동사로 바뀜 (tell/ask + to)
  • 부정 명령문은 not to 동사
  • 제안문은 suggest + that + 동사원형 또는 suggest + ing
  • 시간/장소 표현도 함께 바뀜 (now → then, here → there)
  • 문장 결합 연습을 통해 자연스러운 전달력 향상

11. 연습문제 (Practice Exercises)

객관식

  1. Which is correct?
    A. He said he is busy.
    B. He said he was busy.
  2. Choose the correct form:
    She asked ___ I could help.
    A. if
    B. that
  3. Fill in: He told me ___ the door.
    A. to close
    B. close

1. B. 간접화법에서는 주절의 시제에 맞춰 종속절의 시제가 바뀌므로 'is'가 'was'가 됩니다.

2. A. yes/no 의문문을 간접화법으로 바꿀 때 'if' 또는 'whether'를 사용합니다.

3. A. 명령문을 간접화법으로 바꿀 때 'tell/ask + 목적어 + to 동사' 형태를 사용합니다.
