G33: Used to vs Be Used to

A2 Grammar Lesson – 과거 습관과 익숙함 표현

기본 개념 (Concept)

Used to는 과거의 습관이나 상태를 표현합니다. 지금은 더 이상 그렇지 않다는 의미를 포함합니다. Be used to는 어떤 것에 익숙해졌다는 의미입니다. 현재에도 그 익숙함이 유지됩니다.

USED TO

과거의 습관/상태

지금은 더 이상 아님

used to + 동사원형

BE USED TO

현재의 익숙함

지금도 계속됨

be used to + 명사/동명사

시간선에서 이해하기:

Used to: ───●●●───────X─── (과거 습관, 현재는 안 함)

Be used to: ───────────●●● (현재 익숙한 상태)

예시 비교:

I used to live in Seoul. (과거에 살았지만 지금은 아님)
I am used to living in Seoul. (서울에서 사는 것이 익숙함)
She used to eat meat. (과거엔 고기를 먹었지만 지금은 채식주의자)
She is used to spicy food. (매운 음식에 익숙함)

문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

Used to 구조

  • 긍정문: subject + used to + base verb
  • 부정문: subject + didn't use to + base verb
  • 의문문: Did + subject + use to + base verb?
  • 예시: I used to play / I didn't use to play / Did I use to play?
  • 의미: 과거 습관이나 상태 (현재는 아님)

Be used to 구조

  • 긍정문: subject + be (am/is/are/was/were) + used to + noun/verb-ing
  • 부정문: subject + be + not + used to + noun/verb-ing
  • 의문문: Be + subject + used to + noun/verb-ing?
  • 예시: I am used to cold weather / He is used to working late
  • 의미: 현재의 익숙함 (어떤 것이 자연스럽고 편함)

주요 차이점

  • Used to: 과거 → 현재 변화를 나타냄
  • Be used to: 현재 상태를 나타냄
  • Used to: 동사원형과 함께
  • Be used to: 명사나 동명사(-ing)와 함께

긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

Used to - 과거 습관/상태

  • I used to play soccer every weekend. (매 주말 축구를 했었어요)
  • She used to eat meat, but now she's vegetarian. (고기를 먹었지만 지금은 채식주의자예요)
  • We used to live in Canada. (캐나다에 살았었어요)
  • They used to go to the beach often. (자주 해변에 갔었어요)
  • He used to work at a bank. (은행에서 일했었어요)
  • I used to watch cartoons every morning. (매일 아침 만화를 봤었어요)
  • My brother used to drive a taxi. (형/동생이 택시를 운전했었어요)
  • We used to study together. (함께 공부했었어요)
  • She used to speak French fluently. (불어를 유창하게 했었어요)
  • I used to be shy. (수줍음이 많았었어요)

Be Used to - 현재 익숙함

  • I am used to waking up early. (일찍 일어나는 것에 익숙해요)
  • She is used to spicy food. (매운 음식에 익숙해요)
  • We are used to working long hours. (장시간 일하는 것에 익숙해요)
  • They are used to city life. (도시 생활에 익숙해요)
  • He is used to driving in traffic. (교통체증 속 운전에 익숙해요)
  • I am used to speaking English at work. (직장에서 영어 말하기에 익숙해요)
  • She is used to cold weather. (추운 날씨에 익숙해요)
  • We are used to online meetings. (온라인 회의에 익숙해요)
  • They are used to traveling often. (자주 여행하는 것에 익숙해요)
  • He is used to eating late dinner. (늦은 저녁 식사에 익숙해요)

시제 변화 예시

  • I was used to hot weather in my hometown. (고향의 더운 날씨에 익숙했었어요)
  • They will be used to the new system soon. (곧 새 시스템에 익숙해질 거예요)
  • She has been used to working nights. (밤 근무에 익숙해져 있어요)
  • We were used to walking everywhere. (어디든 걸어다니는 것에 익숙했어요)
  • He will be used to living alone. (혼자 사는 것에 익숙해질 거예요)

의문문 예시 (Question Examples)

Used to 의문문

  • A: Did you use to play piano? (피아노를 쳤었나요?)
    B: Yes, when I was young. (네, 어렸을 때요)
  • A: Did she use to live in Japan? (일본에 살았었나요?)
    B: Yes, for three years. (네, 3년 동안요)
  • A: Did they use to eat meat? (고기를 먹었었나요?)
    B: No, they were always vegetarian. (아니요, 항상 채식주의자였어요)
  • A: Did he use to work here? (여기서 일했었나요?)
    B: Yes, in the marketing department. (네, 마케팅 부서에서요)
  • A: Did you use to watch this show? (이 프로그램을 봤었나요?)
    B: All the time! It was my favorite. (항상요! 제일 좋아했어요)
  • A: Did your family use to travel together? (가족이 함께 여행했었나요?)
    B: Every summer vacation. (매 여름휴가마다요)

Be Used to 의문문

  • A: Are you used to waking up early? (일찍 일어나는 것에 익숙해요?)
    B: Yes, it's no problem now. (네, 이제 문제없어요)
  • A: Is she used to spicy food? (매운 음식에 익숙한가요?)
    B: Not yet, but she's trying. (아직은 아니지만 노력하고 있어요)
  • A: Are they used to city life? (도시 생활에 익숙한가요?)
    B: They're still adjusting. (아직 적응 중이에요)
  • A: Is he used to driving in traffic? (교통체증 운전에 익숙한가요?)
    B: Yes, he does it daily. (네, 매일 해요)
  • A: Are you used to speaking English? (영어 말하기에 익숙해요?)
    B: More and more each day. (매일 점점 더요)
  • A: Were you used to the cold weather? (추운 날씨에 익숙했었나요?)
    B: Eventually, yes. (결국에는 그랬어요)

상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (School Situation)
A: Did you use to study here?
B: Yes, I used to take math classes.
A: Are you used to online learning now?
B: Yes, it's comfortable for me.
A: Did you use to prefer in-person classes?
B: I did, but now I'm used to this format.

직장 상황 (Work Situation)
A: Did he use to work in sales?
B: Yes, for five years.
A: Is he used to remote work now?
B: Yes, he enjoys the flexibility.
A: Did he use to travel for work?
B: All the time. Now he's used to video calls.

이주 상황 (Moving Situation)
A: Did you use to live in a small town?
B: Yes, my whole life.
A: Are you used to the big city yet?
B: I'm getting used to it slowly.
A: What are you still not used to?
B: The noise and the fast pace.

식단 변화 (Diet Change)
A: Did you use to eat a lot of meat?
B: Yes, every meal.
A: Are you used to being vegetarian now?
B: Yes, I don't miss meat anymore.
A: Did you use to cook meat dishes?
B: I did, but now I'm used to plant-based cooking.

기술 적응 (Technology Adaptation)
A: Did your grandpa use to write letters?
B: Yes, he never used email.
A: Is he used to texting now?
B: He's getting used to it.
A: What is he still not used to?
B: Video calls make him nervous.

문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

상황을 설명하는 문장들을 적절한 표현으로 결합하는 연습입니다:

I lived in Seoul. + I now live in Busan.
→ I used to live in Seoul.
I eat spicy food often. + It's normal for me.
→ I am used to eating spicy food.
  • She worked at a bank. + She now teaches. → She used to work at a bank.
  • He drives in traffic every day. + It doesn't bother him. → He is used to driving in traffic.
  • We watched cartoons every morning. + Now we don't. → We used to watch cartoons every morning.
  • I speak English at work. + It feels natural. → I am used to speaking English at work.
  • They traveled often. + Now they stay home. → They used to travel often.
  • She eats late dinner. + It's her habit. → She is used to eating late dinner.
  • We studied together. + Now we study alone. → We used to study together.
  • He works long hours. + It's normal for him. → He is used to working long hours.
  • I was shy. + Now I'm confident. → I used to be shy.
  • She lives in a noisy area. + The noise doesn't bother her. → She is used to the noise.

실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

자기소개서

Hi, I'm Alex. I used to work in finance for 10 years, but now I'm in education. I'm used to managing teams remotely and adapting to new technologies.

면접 중 답변

I used to be shy in presentations, but now I'm confident. I'm used to speaking in front of large groups and handling difficult questions.

이주 경험 공유

When I first moved to Korea, everything was different. I used to eat only Western food, but now I'm used to Korean cuisine. I used to speak only English, but I'm used to switching between languages daily.

업무 보고

Our new employee used to handle customer service at his previous job. He's quickly getting used to leading training sessions and is already used to our company culture.

건강 상담

The patient used to take pills three times daily. Now he's used to injections once a week and is much more comfortable with the new treatment.

자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • used to + base verb: I used to play soccer every day.
  • didn't use to + base verb: I didn't use to like coffee.
  • Did + subject + use to + base verb? Did you use to live here?
  • be used to + noun: I'm used to the noise.
  • be used to + verb-ing: She's used to waking up early.
  • not used to + noun/verb-ing: He's not used to spicy food.
  • Are you used to...? Are you used to driving in the city?
  • Was/Were used to... We were used to working late hours.
  • Used to + be/have: I used to be a student. / She used to have long hair.
  • Getting used to: I'm getting used to the new schedule.

흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ I use to play soccer → ✅ I used to play soccer
  • ❌ Did you used to live here? → ✅ Did you use to live here?
  • ❌ I am used to eat spicy food → ✅ I am used to eating spicy food
  • ❌ She used to working late → ✅ She is used to working late
  • ❌ He didn't used to drive → ✅ He didn't use to drive
  • ❌ Are you used to drive? → ✅ Are you used to driving?
  • ❌ I used to be used to it → ✅ I am used to it (현재 익숙함)
  • ❌ I'm used to wake up early → ✅ I'm used to waking up early
  • ❌ She use to be a teacher → ✅ She used to be a teacher
  • ❌ We didn't use to didn't like it → ✅ We didn't use to like it

레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • Used to: 과거의 습관/상태 → 동사원형과 함께 사용
  • Be used to: 익숙함 → 명사 또는 동사-ing와 함께 사용
  • "Did you use to…"에서 used가 아닌 use를 사용해야 함
  • "Be used to"는 현재, 과거, 미래 시제 모두 가능: I'm / I was / I'll be used to
  • "Used to"는 현재에는 해당되지 않는 과거 상태를 강조
  • "Be used to"는 현재에도 익숙함이 유지됨
  • "Getting used to"로 적응 과정을 표현할 수 있음
  • 둘 다 자주 혼동되므로 문맥과 형태를 정확히 구분해야 함
