1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

CLEFTCLEFT SENTENCES

문장을 나누어 특정 정보를 강조하는 구조
기본 구조: It is/was + 강조 대상 + who/that + 나머지 문장
who: 사람 강조 / that: 사물, 행동, 시간, 장소 강조

일반: John helped me. → 강조: It was John who helped me.

일반: She called at 5. → 강조: It was at 5 that she called.

일반: They fixed the computer. → 강조: It was the computer that they fixed.

사람 강조
It was + 사람 + who
It was John who helped me.
사물 강조
It was + 사물 + that
It was the book that he read.
시간 강조
It was + 시간 + that
It was yesterday that we met.
장소 강조
It was + 장소 + that
It was in Paris that they met.

구조 비교 가이드

Normal: John helped me yesterday at school.
Person: It was John who helped me yesterday at school.
Time: It was yesterday that John helped me at school.
Place: It was at school that John helped me yesterday.

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

기본 구조

  • It is/was + 강조 대상 + who/that + 나머지 문장
  • 강조 대상이 문장 맨 앞으로 이동 → 정보 분리 효과
  • 의미는 같지만 강조 효과와 문장 흐름이 달라짐
  • 강조 대상은 주어, 목적어, 시간, 장소 모두 가능

시제 규칙

  • 현재 강조: It is John who helps me.
  • 과거 강조: It was John who helped me.
  • 원래 문장의 시제와 강조 시제가 일치해야 함
  • 강조 뒤의 동사는 원래 시제 유지

who vs that 규칙

  • 사람 강조: who 사용 → It was Sarah who called.
  • 사물/행동/시간/장소 강조: that 사용 → It was the book that he read.
  • 사람에게만 who, 나머지는 모두 that
  • 의문문: Who was it that…? / What was it that…?

3. 예시 문장 (Example Sentences)

사람 강조 (who)

  • It was John who helped me.
  • It was Sarah who called you.
  • It is my brother who drives us.
  • It was the teacher who explained it.
  • It is Lisa who organizes the event.
  • It was the doctor who treated the patient.
  • It is my mother who cooks dinner.
  • It was the manager who approved the plan.

사물/행동 강조 (that)

  • It was the book that he read.
  • It is the report that she finished.
  • It was the email that confused me.
  • It is the idea that matters most.
  • It was the project that took time.
  • It is the music that relaxes me.
  • It was the medicine that helped him.
  • It is the exercise that keeps me healthy.

시간/장소 강조 (that)

  • It was yesterday that we met.
  • It is at 5 that she arrives.
  • It was in Paris that they got married.
  • It is on Monday that we start.
  • It was at the station that I saw him.
  • It is in the morning that I work best.
  • It was during lunch that we discussed it.
  • It is at home that I feel comfortable.

5. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황
A: Who explained the grammar?
B: It was the teacher who explained it.
A: What helped you understand?
B: It was the example that helped me.
여행 상황
A: Where did you meet him?
B: It was in Paris that I met him.
A: When was that?
B: It was last summer that we traveled.
직장 상황
A: Who finished the report?
B: It was Sarah who finished it.
A: What took the most time?
B: It was the data that delayed us.
온라인 상황
A: What confused you?
B: It was the message that confused me.
A: Who sent it?
B: It was John who sent it.
가족 상황
A: Who cooked dinner?
B: It was Mom who cooked it.
A: What did she make?
B: It was pasta that she made.
병원 상황
A: Who helped the patient?
B: It was the nurse who helped him.
A: What medicine worked?
B: It was the new pill that helped.

10. 연습문제 (Practice Questions)

문제 1: 사람 강조에 알맞은 관계사를 선택하세요

It was John _____ helped me. (who/that)

문제 2: 사물 강조에 알맞은 관계사를 선택하세요

It was the book _____ he read. (who/that)

문제 3: 시제에 알맞은 be동사를 선택하세요

It _____ yesterday that we met. (is/was)

문제 4: 올바른 문장을 선택하세요

다음 중 맞는 문장은? (A/B)

A: It was the teacher that explained.

B: It was the teacher who explained.

문제 5: 문장을 강조 구조로 변환하세요 (사람 강조)

"Sarah called me" → It was _____ who called me.

문제 6: 문장을 강조 구조로 변환하세요 (시간 강조)

"We met yesterday" → It was _____ that we met.

문제 7: 장소 강조 문장 완성하기

It was _____ Paris that they got married. (in/at)

문제 8: 오류 수정하기

다음 문장을 올바르게 고치세요: "It was the book who he read."

11. 자가진단 체크리스트 (Self-Check)

학습 완료 체크

학습 팁 (Study Tips)

  • who → 사람 강조 / that → 사물, 시간, 장소 강조
  • It is → 현재 강조 / It was → 과거 강조
  • 강조 대상은 문장 맨 앞 → 정보 분리 효과로 의미 강조
  • 의문형: Who was it that… / What was it that… → 강조된 질문
  • 실생활 대화, 발표, 글쓰기에서 강조와 명확한 전달에 효과적
  • 문장 변환 연습을 통해 강조 구조에 익숙해지기
  • 강조 대상이 명확할수록 문장의 힘과 설득력이 높아짐
  • 발표나 프레젠테이션에서 핵심 정보 강조에 매우 유용
