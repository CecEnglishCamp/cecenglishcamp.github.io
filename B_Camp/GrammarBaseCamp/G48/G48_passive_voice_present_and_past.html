1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

PVPASSIVE VOICE

행위의 대상을 강조하는 문장 구조
주어가 행동을 받는 입장일 때 사용
주로 행위자보다 결과나 대상이 중요할 때 사용

능동: They clean the room.

수동: The room is cleaned (by them).

강조: 방이 청소된다는 사실이 중요

기본 구조

be동사 + 과거분사(p.p)

예시: The room is cleaned every day.

능동태 vs 수동태

능동태 (Active Voice)

They clean the room.
(그들이 방을 청소한다)

She teaches English.
(그녀가 영어를 가르친다)

누가 하는지 강조

수동태 (Passive Voice)

The room is cleaned.
(방이 청소된다)

English is taught.
(영어가 가르쳐진다)

무엇이 되는지 강조

수동태 변환 과정

능동태

They clean the room
(그들이 방을 청소한다)

→
수동태

The room is cleaned
(방이 청소된다)

수동태 사용 목적

결과 강조
The project is finished.
상태 표현
The door is locked.
행위자 모름
My wallet was stolen.
공식적 표현
Letters are delivered.

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

기본 구조와 규칙

  • 현재형 수동태: am / is / are + p.p → The book is read.
  • 과거형 수동태: was / were + p.p → The book was read.
  • 주어에 따라 be동사 형태가 달라짐
  • 행위자를 말하고 싶을 때: by + 사람 → The cake was made by my mom.
  • 능동태 → 수동태 변환 시 목적어가 주어로 이동
  • 수동태는 결과, 상태, 책임을 강조할 때 유용

시제별 수동태 형태

  • 현재: am/is/are + p.p → The room is cleaned daily.
  • 과거: was/were + p.p → The room was cleaned yesterday.
  • 현재완료: have/has been + p.p → The room has been cleaned.
  • 미래: will be + p.p → The room will be cleaned tomorrow.

의문문과 부정문

  • 의문문: Is the room cleaned? / Was it cleaned?
  • 부정문: The room is not cleaned. / It wasn't cleaned.
  • Wh-의문문: When is the room cleaned? / Where was it done?
  • 행위자 의문: Who was this done by?

3. 예시 문장 (Example Sentences)

현재형 수동태 (Present Passive)

  • The room is cleaned every day.
  • English is taught at this school.
  • The homework is checked by the teacher.
  • Lunch is served at noon.
  • Letters are delivered in the morning.
  • The windows are washed weekly.
  • The project is finished on time.
  • Books are borrowed from the library.
  • The door is locked at night.
  • Classes are held online.

과거형 수동태 (Past Passive)

  • The room was cleaned yesterday.
  • English was taught last year.
  • The homework was checked this morning.
  • Lunch was served at 12.
  • Letters were delivered late.
  • The windows were washed last week.
  • The project was finished yesterday.
  • Books were borrowed yesterday.
  • The door was locked last night.
  • Classes were held offline before.

의문문 (Questions)

  • Is the room cleaned every day?
  • Was the homework checked this morning?
  • Are the windows washed weekly?
  • Were the letters delivered on time?
  • Is lunch served at noon?
  • Was the door locked last night?
  • Are books borrowed from the library?
  • Were classes held offline before?
  • Is English taught here?
  • Was the project finished yesterday?

4. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황
A: Is the homework checked today?
B: Yes, it's checked every morning.
A: Was it checked yesterday?
B: No, it wasn't.
여행 상황
A: Are the rooms cleaned daily?
B: Yes, they are cleaned by staff.
A: Was our room cleaned yesterday?
B: I think so.
직장 상황
A: Is the report sent to the manager?
B: Yes, it's sent every Friday.
A: Was it sent last week?
B: No, it was delayed.
온라인 상황
A: Are the files uploaded?
B: Yes, they are uploaded automatically.
A: Were they uploaded yesterday?
B: No, there was an error.
가족 상황
A: Is dinner cooked?
B: Yes, it's cooked by Dad.
A: Was it cooked yesterday?
B: No, we ordered food.
병원 상황
A: Is the medicine given now?
B: Yes, it's given every 6 hours.
A: Was it given this morning?
B: Yes, it was.

5. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

→ The room is cleaned.
She teaches English.
→ English is taught.
The teacher checks homework.
→ Homework is checked.
We serve lunch at noon.
→ Lunch is served at noon.
They deliver letters.
→ Letters are delivered.

→ The room was cleaned.
She taught English.
→ English was taught.
The teacher checked homework.
→ Homework was checked.
We served lunch at noon.
→ Lunch was served at noon.
They delivered letters.
→ Letters were delivered.

6. 실생활 사용 (Real-Life Usage)

이메일 보고

The report was sent yesterday. It was reviewed by the manager.

자기소개

English is taught at my school. Homework is checked every morning.

업무 회의

The project was completed last week. Feedback was given by the client.

여행 후기

The rooms were cleaned daily. Breakfast was served at 8 AM.

건강 상담

The medicine is given every 6 hours. Blood tests were done yesterday.

7. 자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴 (Common Patterns)

패턴별 정리

am/is/are + p.p The room is cleaned.
was/were + p.p The room was cleaned.
by + 사람 The cake was made by my mom.
be동사 + p.p + 시간 표현 Lunch is served at noon.
복수 주어 + are/were + p.p Letters are delivered / were delivered
현재 수동태 The homework is checked every day.
과거 수동태 The homework was checked yesterday.
행위자 생략 The room was cleaned. (by someone)
상태 강조 The door is locked.
결과 강조 The report was sent.

  • 현재형: am / is / are + p.p → The book is read
  • 과거형: was / were + p.p → The book was read
  • 행위자가 중요하지 않으면 by + 사람 생략 가능
