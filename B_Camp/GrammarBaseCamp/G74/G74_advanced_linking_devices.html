1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

LINKAdvanced Linking Devices

Linking Devices: 문장과 문장을 연결하여 논리적 흐름을 만드는 표현
글쓰기와 말하기에서 논리, 대조, 예시, 결과, 강조를 표현할 때 사용
문장 응집력 향상, 시험 응답 구조화, 발표/보고서에 필수

대조: She was tired. **However**, she kept working.

결과: He didn’t study. **Therefore**, he failed.

예시: I like fruits, **such as** apples and bananas.

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

  • 순서/추가: first, then, next, **also, moreover, in addition**
  • 예시/설명: **for example, such as, that is, namely**
  • 대조/양보: but, **however, although, even though, whereas**
  • 결과/조건: so, **therefore, thus, as a result, if, unless**
  • 강조/요약: **indeed, in fact, above all, in short, to sum up**
  • 문장 앞/중간/끝에 위치 가능 (예: **However**, she kept working. / She kept working, **however**.)
  • 쉼표 사용 주의: 대부분 문장 앞에 올 경우 **쉼표(comma)**로 구분
  • 문맥에 따라 적절한 연결어 선택 필요

3. 긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

순서/추가

  • **First**, we need to plan.
  • **Then**, we can start working.
  • **Next**, we’ll review the results.
  • **Also**, remember to check grammar.
  • **Moreover**, the design must be clear.
  • **In addition**, we’ll add examples.

예시/설명

  • **For example**, apples are healthy.
  • **Such as** bananas and oranges.
  • **That is**, we need more time.
  • **Namely**, the writing section.

대조/양보

  • She was tired, **but** she kept going.
  • **However**, she didn’t give up.
  • **Although** it was late, he kept working.
  • **Even though** it was hard, they succeeded.
  • He likes tea, **whereas** she prefers coffee.

결과/조건

  • He didn’t study, **so** he failed.
  • She worked hard. **Therefore**, she passed.
  • It rained. **Thus**, the game was canceled.
  • **As a result**, we changed the plan.
  • **If** you study, you’ll improve.
  • **Unless** you try, you won’t succeed.

강조/요약

  • **Indeed**, the results were surprising.
  • **In fact**, no one expected it.
  • **Above all**, stay focused.
  • **In short**, we need more time.
  • **To sum up**, the project was a success.

5. 상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황
A: I finished my homework.
B: Good. **In addition**, you should review grammar.
A: Okay.

여행 상황
A: It started raining.
B: **Therefore**, we canceled the hike.
A: That makes sense.

직장 상황
A: She submitted the report.
B: **However**, it was incomplete.
A: Let’s fix it.

온라인 상황
A: I like digital art.
B: **For example**, anime posters?
A: Exactly.

가족 상황
A: I want to go out.
B: **Unless** you finish your chores, you can’t.
A: I’ll do them now.

병원 상황
A: She’s recovering well.
B: **In fact**, she might go home tomorrow.
A: That’s great!

6. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

  • She was tired. She kept working. → She was tired. **However**, she kept working.
  • He didn’t study. He failed. → He didn’t study. **Therefore**, he failed.
  • It rained. The game was canceled. → It rained. **As a result**, the game was canceled.
  • He likes tea. She prefers coffee. → He likes tea, **whereas** she prefers coffee.
  • We need more time. We need more examples. → We need more time. **In addition**, we need more examples.
  • Apples are healthy. Bananas are healthy. → Apples are healthy. **Moreover**, bananas are healthy.
  • It was hard. They succeeded. → **Even though** it was hard, they succeeded.
  • We planned well. The project succeeded. → We planned well. **Thus**, the project succeeded.
  • We need to focus. We need to finish. → **Above all**, we need to finish.
  • We finished the work. The report was submitted. → **To sum up**, the report was submitted.

7. 실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

  • 학교 상황: I studied hard. As a result, I passed the test.
  • 직장 상황: She missed the deadline. However, the team helped her.
  • 가족 상황: We were tired. Even though we were tired, we cooked dinner.
  • 온라인 상황: I like design tools, such as Canva and Figma.
  • 병원 상황: He followed instructions. Therefore, he recovered quickly.
  • 여행 상황: It was raining. Nevertheless, we went hiking.
  • 회의 상황: We discussed the issue. In short, we need a new plan.

8. 자주 쓰이는 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • 순서: First, Then, Next
  • 추가: Also, Moreover, In addition
  • 예시: For example, Such as, That is
  • 대조: But, **However**, Although, Even though, Whereas
  • 결과: So, **Therefore**, Thus, As a result
  • 조건: If, Unless
  • 강조: Indeed, In fact, Above all
  • 요약: In short, To sum up
  • 문장 앞: However, she continued.
  • 문장 중간: She continued, however, without help.

9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ She was tired. But she kept working. → ✅ She was tired. However, she kept working.
  • ❌ He didn’t study. So he failed. → ✅ He didn’t study. Therefore, he failed.
  • ❌ Apples are healthy. Bananas are healthy. → ✅ Apples are healthy. Moreover, bananas are healthy.
  • ❌ He likes tea. She likes coffee. → ✅ He likes tea, whereas she prefers coffee.
  • ❌ We need more time. We need more examples. → ✅ We need more time. In addition, we need more examples.

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • 연결 표현은 논리 흐름을 명확히 해줌
  • 대조 표현은 but → however / although / whereas로 다양화
  • 결과 표현은 so → therefore / thus / as a result로 강화
  • 예시 표현은 for example / such as / namely로 구체화
  • 강조 표현은 indeed / in fact / above all로 감정과 논리 강조
  • 요약 표현은 in short / to sum up으로 마무리 효과
  • 문장 결합 연습을 통해 자연스러운 연결력 향상

11. 연습문제 (Practice Exercises)

객관식

  1. Which is correct?
    A. She was tired. But she kept working.
    B. She was tired. However, she kept working.
  2. Choose the correct connector:
    It rained. ___, the game was canceled.
    A. Therefore
    B. For example
  3. Fill in: He likes tea, ___ she prefers coffee.
    A. whereas
    B. although

1. B. 'But'은 두 개의 독립된 문장을 연결할 때 단독으로 문장 시작에 사용하기보다 'However'를 사용해 문장의 흐름을 명확히 합니다.

2. A. 비가 온 것이 게임 취소의 **결과**이므로 'Therefore'가 적절합니다. ('For example'은 예시를 들 때 사용합니다.)

3. A. 두 개의 대조되는 사실을 동시에 언급할 때 **'whereas'**를 사용합니다. ('Although'는 양보의 의미로 사용됩니다.)
