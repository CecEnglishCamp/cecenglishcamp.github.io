1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

PHRASALComplex Phrasal Verbs

구동사(Phrasal Verb): 동사 + 전치사/부사 조합으로 의미가 달라지는 표현
직역이 불가능하거나 문맥에 따라 의미가 달라지는 경우가 많음
예: “take off” = 이륙하다 / 옷을 벗다
일상 대화, 이메일, 뉴스, 시험 등에서 자주 사용됨

예시:
- He took off his jacket. (벗다)
- The plane took off at 3 p.m. (이륙하다)
- She brought up an important topic. (제기하다)

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

  • 분리 가능: 목적어가 중간에 들어갈 수 있음 (She put it off / She put off the meeting)
  • 분리 불가능: 목적어가 항상 뒤에 와야 함 (We ran into a friend)
  • 수동태 가능: The meeting was put off
  • 명사화 가능: The breakdown was serious (from “break down”)
  • 구동사는 문맥에 따라 의미가 달라지므로 전체 문장을 이해해야 함
  • 구동사 뒤에 오는 목적어가 사람/사물인지에 따라 어순이 달라질 수 있음

3. 긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

take off

  • The plane took off on time. (이륙하다)
  • He took off his shoes. (벗다)
  • Her career took off after the award. (급성장하다)

bring up

  • She brought up an interesting idea. (제기하다)
  • Don’t bring up that topic again. (꺼내다)
  • He was brought up in Canada. (자라다)

put off

  • They put off the meeting. (연기하다)
  • She put it off until next week. (미루다)
  • The event was put off due to rain. (수동태)

run into

  • I ran into an old friend. (우연히 만나다)
  • We ran into traffic. (마주치다)
  • They ran into problems during testing. (겪다)

5. 상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황
A: Did you finish the project?
B: No, we put it off until Friday.
A: Got it.

여행 상황
A: How was the trip?
B: We ran into some delays.
A: Hope it wasn’t too bad.

직장 상황
A: What did she say?
B: She brought up the budget issue.
A: That’s important.

온라인 상황
A: What happened to the app?
B: It broke down during the update.
A: Let’s report it.

가족 상황
A: Where’s Dad?
B: He took off his jacket and went outside.
A: Okay.

병원 상황
A: How’s the patient?
B: She gave up too soon, but now she’s trying again.
A: That’s good to hear.

6. 문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

  • He delayed the meeting. → He put off the meeting.
  • She mentioned the problem. → She brought up the problem.
  • The plane departed. → The plane took off.
  • I met an old friend by chance. → I ran into an old friend.
  • He quit smoking. → He gave up smoking.
  • They investigated the issue. → They looked into the issue.
  • We completed the task. → We carried out the task.
  • The machine stopped working. → The machine broke down.
  • She rejected the offer. → She turned down the offer.
  • He found the book by accident. → He came across the book.

7. 실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

  • 학교 상황: We put off the group meeting until Friday.
  • 직장 상황: She brought up the budget issue during the meeting.
  • 가족 상황: Dad gave up coffee last month.
  • 온라인 상황: The app broke down after the update.
  • 병원 상황: The team carried out the procedure successfully.
  • 여행 상황: We ran into heavy traffic on the way.
  • 회의 상황: They looked into the complaint carefully.

8. 자주 쓰이는 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • 동사 + 부사/전치사 (take off, give up)
  • 동사 + 목적어 + 부사 (put the meeting off)
  • 동사 + 부사 + 목적어 (put off the meeting)
  • 수동태: The meeting was put off
  • 명사화: The breakdown was unexpected
  • 의미 변화: “bring up” = 제기하다 / “carry out” = 수행하다
  • 우연한 상황: “run into” / “come across”
  • 거절 표현: “turn down” the offer
  • 조사 표현: “look into” the issue
  • 포기 표현: “give up” smoking

9. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ He put off the. → ✅ He put off the meeting.
  • ❌ She brought the topic. → ✅ She brought up the topic.
  • ❌ The plane take offed. → ✅ The plane took off.
  • ❌ I ran to a friend. → ✅ I ran into a friend.
  • ❌ He gave smoking. → ✅ He gave up smoking.

10. 레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • 구동사는 전체 문맥을 보고 의미를 판단해야 함
  • 분리 가능한 구동사는 목적어 위치에 따라 어순이 달라짐
  • 수동태로 바꿀 수 있는 구동사는 시험/글쓰기에서 유용
  • 명사화된 형태는 보고서/뉴스 표현에 자주 등장
  • 구동사마다 다의적 의미가 있으므로 예문을 많이 접하는 것이 중요
  • 문장 결합 연습을 통해 자연스러운 표현력 향상
  • 동사 자체보다 전체 표현을 기억하는 것이 효과적

11. 연습문제 (Practice Exercises)

객관식

  1. Which is correct?
    A. He put off the.
    B. He put off the meeting.
  2. Choose the correct form:
    She ___ the topic during the meeting.
    A. brought up
    B. brought
  3. Fill in: I ___ an old friend at the airport.
    A. ran into
    B. ran to

1. B. 'put off'는 뒤에 목적어가 필요합니다.

2. A. '화제를 꺼내다'는 의미는 'bring up'으로 표현합니다.

3. A. '우연히 만나다'는 'run into'로 표현합니다.
