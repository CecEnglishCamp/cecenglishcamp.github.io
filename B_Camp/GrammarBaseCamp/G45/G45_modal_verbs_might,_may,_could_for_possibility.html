1. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

MPMODAL VERBS

Might / May / Could: 어떤 일이 가능할 수 있음을 표현
확신은 낮지만 가능성 있음을 말할 때 사용

불확실한 추측: It might rain.

정중한 예측: She may come later.

열린 가능성: He could be tired.

세 가지 조동사 비교

MIGHT
불확실한 추측
말하는 사람의 의견
"It might rain today."
MAY
정중한 예측
공식적인 표현
"She may join us later."
COULD
열린 가능성
능력과 가능성
"He could be at home."

가능성 정도 (일반적 경향)

30-50%
MIGHT
40-60%
MAY
30-70%
COULD

* 상황에 따라 확률은 달라질 수 있습니다

2. 문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

기본 구조

  • 조동사 + 동사원형 → might go / may come / could be
  • 의문문: Might / May / Could + 주어 + 동사원형?
  • 부정형: Might not / May not / Could not
  • 확률 순서 (일반적): may > might ≈ could
  • "May"는 공식적/조심스러운 표현에 자주 사용됨
  • "Might"는 말하는 사람의 불확실한 추측에 자주 사용됨
  • "Could"는 가능성 + 능력을 함께 암시할 수 있음

특징별 사용법

  • Might: 개인적 추측, 불확실성 강조
  • May: 정중한 표현, 공식적 상황
  • Could: 넓은 가능성, 제안이나 추천
  • 부정형: might not (가능성 낮음) / may not (허가 거부) / could not (불가능)
  • 의문문: Could it be...? (가장 자연스러움)

4. 상황별 대화 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (School Situation)
B: It might rain in the afternoon.
A: Should we bring umbrellas?
B: That could be a good idea.
직장 상황 (Work Situation)
A: Is John joining the meeting?
B: He may join later.
A: He might be stuck in traffic.
B: Or he could be working from home.
온라인 상황 (Online Situation)
A: The app isn't working.
B: It might be a server issue.
A: Could it be my internet?
B: That's possible too.
가족 상황 (Family Situation)
A: Where's Mom?
B: She may be shopping.
A: Or she might be at the café.
B: She could be with Aunt Mina.
병원 상황 (Hospital Situation)
A: I feel dizzy.
B: You might need rest.
A: Could it be stress?
B: It may be. Let's ask the doctor.

6. 실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

이메일 안내

We might need to reschedule the meeting. The manager may be unavailable.

자기소개

I might join the workshop next week. It could be helpful for my project.

업무 보고

The results may change after more testing. We could see improvements soon.

여행 후기

It might rain tomorrow, so we may stay indoors.

건강 상담

You might be dehydrated. It could be stress too. Let's check further.

7. 자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴 (Common Patterns)

표현 패턴

Might / May / Could + 동사원형 might go / may come / could be
Might not / May not / Could not might not work / may not agree / could not help
It might / may / could be + 명사/형용사 It might be true / It could be difficult
He/She might / may / could be + ~ing She might be working / He could be sleeping
We might / may / could need We might need help / We could need more time
There might / may / could be There might be a delay / There could be a problem
조심스러운 제안 That could be a good idea / You might want to check
추측 표현 He might be tired / It may be a mistake
가능성 강조 It could happen / She might join
불확실한 예측 We may see results / It might change

10. 자가진단 (Self-Check)

학습 완료 체크

레벨업 팁 (Level-Up Tips)

  • Might / May / Could + 동사원형 → to 없이 사용
  • "May"는 공식적이고 조심스러운 표현에 적합
  • "Might"는 말하는 사람의 불확실한 추측에 자주 사용
  • "Could"는 가능성과 능력 모두 암시할 수 있음
  • 부정형은 "might not / may not / could not"
  • "It might be…" / "She could be…" / "We may need…" 등 패턴 익히기
  • 가능성 표현은 확신이 낮을 때 사용
  • 조심스러운 제안에도 활용 가능: "That could be helpful"
  • 현재/미래 가능성 중심으로 사용 (과거 가능성은 G46에서 다룸)

  • Practice modal verbs in writing and conversation.
  • Use modal verbs to express possibility, advice, and polite suggestions.
  • Compare with "will," "can," and "should" for clarity.
  • Record and review your own sentences for fluency and accuracy.
