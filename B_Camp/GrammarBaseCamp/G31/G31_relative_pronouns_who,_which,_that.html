G31: Relative Pronouns

A2 Grammar Lesson – 관계대명사 (who, which, that)

기본 개념 (Concept)

관계대명사(Relative Pronouns)는 두 문장을 연결하여 하나의 문장으로 만드는 데 사용됩니다. 대표적인 관계대명사는 who, which, that입니다.

WHO

사람을 설명할 때

주어 또는 목적어 역할

WHICH

사물이나 동물을 설명할 때

주어 또는 목적어 역할

THAT

사람, 사물 모두 가능

who/which 대신 사용

예시:

This is the teacher who helped me. (나를 도와준 선생님이에요)
I have a book which explains grammar. (문법을 설명하는 책이 있어요)
She likes the dog that barks loudly. (크게 짖는 개를 좋아해요)

문법 규칙 (Grammar Rules)

관계대명사 선택 규칙

  • who: 주로 사람 → The man who lives next door is friendly.
  • which: 사물/동물 → The book which you gave me is interesting.
  • that: 사람/사물 모두 → The movie that we watched was fun.

제한적 관계절 vs 비제한적 관계절

  • 제한적 관계절 (Defining): 필수 정보, 쉼표 없음
    → The student who studies hard will pass. (열심히 공부하는 학생이 합격할 거야)
  • 비제한적 관계절 (Non-defining): 추가 정보, 쉼표 사용
    → My brother, who lives in Seoul, is a doctor. (서울에 사는 내 형/동생은 의사야)

관계대명사 생략 규칙

  • 목적어 역할일 때 생략 가능
    → The book (that) I read was great. (내가 읽은 책이 훌륭했어)
  • 주어 역할일 때 생략 불가
    → The girl who helped me is kind. (나를 도와준 소녀는 친절해)
  • 비제한적 관계절에서는 생략 불가
    → My car, which is red, is new. (빨간색인 내 차는 새 거야)

관계대명사의 역할

  • 주어 역할: The man who called you is my uncle.
  • 목적어 역할: The book (that) I bought is useful.
  • 소유격: The student whose book is missing should see me.

긍정문 예시 (Positive Examples)

WHO - 사람

  • The teacher who teaches math is strict. (수학을 가르치는 선생님은 엄격해요)
  • I met a woman who speaks five languages. (다섯 개 언어를 하는 여성을 만났어요)
  • He is the boy who won the race. (경주에서 이긴 소년이에요)
  • She knows a doctor who works at that hospital. (그 병원에서 일하는 의사를 알아요)
  • We saw a singer who performed beautifully. (아름답게 공연한 가수를 봤어요)
  • My friend who lives in Canada is visiting. (캐나다에 사는 친구가 방문해요)
  • The student who studies hard gets good grades. (열심히 공부하는 학생이 좋은 성적을 받아요)
  • There's a man who can fix anything. (뭐든 고칠 수 있는 남자가 있어요)
  • I have a cousin who plays the violin. (바이올린을 연주하는 사촌이 있어요)
  • The person who called you is my uncle. (당신에게 전화한 사람은 내 삼촌이에요)

WHICH - 사물/동물

  • This is the book which explains everything. (모든 것을 설명하는 책이에요)
  • I bought a phone which has a great camera. (좋은 카메라가 있는 폰을 샀어요)
  • She likes the movie which won the award. (상을 받은 영화를 좋아해요)
  • We visited a museum which was very old. (아주 오래된 박물관을 방문했어요)
  • He showed me a picture which he painted. (그가 그린 그림을 보여줬어요)
  • I found a website which helps with grammar. (문법에 도움이 되는 웹사이트를 찾았어요)
  • The cake which she made was delicious. (그녀가 만든 케이크가 맛있었어요)
  • They have a dog which understands commands. (명령을 이해하는 개가 있어요)
  • This is the pen which writes smoothly. (부드럽게 써지는 펜이에요)
  • I saw a bird which sings loudly. (크게 노래하는 새를 봤어요)

THAT - 사람/사물 모두

  • This is the song that I love. (내가 좋아하는 노래예요)
  • I know a place that serves good food. (좋은 음식을 파는 곳을 알아요)
  • She has a bag that matches her shoes. (신발과 어울리는 가방이 있어요)
  • We watched a movie that made us laugh. (우리를 웃게 한 영화를 봤어요)
  • He bought a car that is very fast. (아주 빠른 차를 샀어요)
  • I met a boy that knows my brother. (내 형/동생을 아는 소년을 만났어요)
  • They live in a house that has a garden. (정원이 있는 집에 살아요)
  • This is the book that I was talking about. (내가 말하던 책이에요)
  • She wears a necklace that her mom gave her. (엄마가 준 목걸이를 해요)
  • I saw a dog that chased a cat. (고양이를 쫓는 개를 봤어요)

의문문 예시 (Question Examples)

  • A: Who is the person who called you? (전화한 사람이 누구예요?)
    B: It's my cousin from Australia. (호주에 있는 사촌이에요)
  • A: Which book do you like that explains grammar? (문법을 설명하는 책 중 어느 걸 좋아해요?)
    B: The blue one on the shelf. (선반에 있는 파란 책이요)
  • A: Is this the teacher who teaches science? (과학을 가르치는 선생님이에요?)
    B: Yes, that's Mrs. Kim. (네, 김 선생님이에요)
  • A: Do you know anyone who speaks Korean? (한국어 하는 사람 아세요?)
    B: Yes, my neighbor does. (네, 이웃이 해요)
  • A: What's the name of the movie that you watched? (본 영화 제목이 뭐예요?)
    B: It's called "Parasite." (기생충이라고 해요)
  • A: Is this the dog that barks at night? (밤에 짖는 개예요?)
    B: No, that's a different one. (아니요, 다른 개예요)
  • A: Do you have a friend who lives abroad? (해외에 사는 친구 있어요?)
    B: Yes, several in different countries. (네, 여러 나라에 몇 명 있어요)
  • A: Which website do you use that helps with English? (영어에 도움이 되는 웹사이트 뭐 써요?)
    B: I use BBC Learning English. (BBC 러닝 잉글리시 써요)
  • A: Who is the student who won the prize? (상을 받은 학생이 누구예요?)
    B: It's Sarah from Class 3A. (3A반 사라예요)
  • A: Is this the pen that you gave me? (당신이 준 펜이에요?)
    B: Yes, I hope it works well. (네, 잘 써지길 바라요)

상황별 대화 흐름 (Situational Dialogues)

학교 상황 (School Situation)
A: Who is the teacher who teaches English?
B: That's Mr. Kim. He's very kind.
A: Is he the one who gave us homework?
B: Yes, and the homework that he gave is interesting.
A: I like teachers who explain things clearly.
B: Me too. He's definitely one of those.

여행 상황 (Travel Situation)
A: Do you know a place that has good food?
B: Yes, there's a restaurant which serves Korean dishes.
A: Is it the one that's near the station?
B: Exactly. The chef who works there is famous.
A: Perfect! I love restaurants that have authentic food.
B: You'll definitely enjoy it.

직장 상황 (Work Situation)
A: Who is the colleague who speaks French?
B: That's Marie. She's the one who helped with translation.
A: Is she the person that joined last month?
B: Yes, and the project which she started is going well.
A: We need more people who have language skills.
B: I agree. It's very helpful for international clients.

온라인 상황 (Online Situation)
A: What's the app that helps with vocabulary?
B: I use one which gives daily quizzes.
A: Is it the one that has pictures?
B: Yes, and the teacher who made it is from Canada.
A: I prefer apps that are interactive.
B: This one definitely is. You'll like it.

쇼핑 상황 (Shopping Situation)
A: Where's the store that sells electronics?
B: It's the one which is on the second floor.
A: Is that the shop that has the big sale?
B: Yes, the owner who runs it is very friendly.
A: I like stores that have good customer service.
B: You'll be happy with this place then.

문장 결합 연습 (Sentence Combining)

두 개의 간단한 문장을 관계대명사를 사용해서 하나로 결합하는 연습입니다:

This is the man. + He helped me.
→ This is the man who helped me.
I have a book. + It explains grammar.
→ I have a book which explains grammar.
She likes the dog. + It barks loudly.
→ She likes the dog that barks loudly.
  • We visited a museum. + It was very old. → We visited a museum which was very old.
  • He met a woman. + She speaks French. → He met a woman who speaks French.
  • This is the movie. + You recommended it. → This is the movie that you recommended.
  • I saw a bird. + It sings beautifully. → I saw a bird which sings beautifully.
  • She knows a teacher. + He teaches math. → She knows a teacher who teaches math.
  • They live in a house. + It has a garden. → They live in a house that has a garden.
  • I have a friend. + He plays guitar. → I have a friend who plays guitar.
  • We bought a car. + It is very economical. → We bought a car which is very economical.
  • She met a student. + The student studies Korean. → She met a student who studies Korean.
  • I found a website. + It teaches pronunciation. → I found a website that teaches pronunciation.

실생활 사용 예시 (Real-Life Usage)

이메일 소개

This is Jane, who works in our marketing team. She will help you with the campaign that we discussed yesterday.

자기소개

I'm a student who loves learning languages. I use an app which gives daily practice and follow teachers who post helpful videos.

제품 설명

This is a phone that has a long battery life. It's perfect for people who travel frequently and need a device which works reliably.

관광 안내

We'll visit a temple which is over 500 years old. The guide who speaks English will explain the history that makes this place special.

소셜 미디어 프로필

I'm a developer who builds educational tools. I created a website that helps students learn grammar and an app which tracks progress.

자주 쓰이는 표현 패턴 (Common Patterns)

  • The person who + 동사 → The person who called is my friend.
  • The thing which + 동사 → The thing which broke was expensive.
  • The noun that + 주어 + 동사 → The book that I read was great.
  • Someone who + 동사 → She met someone who speaks French.
  • Something that + 동사 → I need something that works better.
  • People who + 동사 → People who exercise regularly are healthier.
  • 관계대명사 생략 패턴 → The book I bought is useful.
  • 비제한적 관계절 → My brother, who lives in Seoul, is a doctor.
  • 장소 + that/which → The place that we visited was beautiful.
  • 시간 + when → The day when we met was special.

흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

  • ❌ The man which helped me → ✅ The man who helped me
  • ❌ I saw a dog who barked → ✅ I saw a dog that/which barked
  • ❌ The book who explains grammar → ✅ The book which/that explains grammar
  • ❌ I have a friend he plays guitar → ✅ I have a friend who plays guitar
  • ❌ This is the movie which I watched it → ✅ This is the movie which I watched
  • ❌ My brother who lives in Seoul is a doctor → ✅ My brother, who lives in Seoul, is a doctor
  • ❌ The girl that I spoke to her → ✅ The girl that I spoke to
  • ❌ I like teachers which explain clearly → ✅ I like teachers who explain clearly
  • ❌ The car who is red → ✅ The car which/that is red
  • ❌ She is the woman which I told you about her → ✅ She is the woman who I told you about
