🔤 영어 스토리

Young-ja uses an ATM alone. 🔊

She inserts her card and enters her PIN. 🔊

She selects "Withdrawal" and "Checking." 🔊

She enters one hundred dollars. 🔊

The money comes out. 🔊

She takes her card and cash. 🔊

"ATM English is only ten words!" 🔊

Young-ja decided to use the ATM alone for the first time. 🔊

She followed each step carefully. 🔊

"Insert your card." She inserted it. 🔊

"Enter your PIN." She typed her number. 🔊

"Select transaction type." She pressed "Withdrawal." 🔊

"Checking or Savings?" She chose "Checking." 🔊

"Enter amount." She typed one hundred. 🔊

Cash slid out of the machine. 🔊

"Please take your cash." "Don\u0027t forget your card!" 🔊

Young-ja stepped back triumphantly. "ATM English is really just ten words." 🔊
