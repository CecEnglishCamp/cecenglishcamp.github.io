Young-ja visits the library. "Do you have a library card?" "No, I want one." "It's free!" She can borrow ten books for three weeks. WiFi is also free. "American libraries are great!"

✅ B1 Level

Young-ja decided to visit her local library. The librarian greeted her, "Welcome! Do you have a library card?" "No," said Young-ja. "I want to make one, please." "Of course! It's completely free. I just need your address." Young-ja gave her address, and the librarian made a card. "You can borrow up to ten books for three weeks. WiFi and computers are all free too!" Young-ja's eyes went wide. Walking home with her new card and three books, she smiled. "American libraries are really wonderful."
