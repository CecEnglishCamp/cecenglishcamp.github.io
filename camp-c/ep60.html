Episode 60

영자의 영어 일기 쓰기 시작

Young-ja Starts Writing an English Diary

🟡 한글 이야기
딸이 말했습니다. "엄마, 매일 영어 일기 써봐. 짧아도 돼."
영자는 처음으로 영어 일기를 썼습니다.
"Today I go to library. I borrow two book.
I am happy. Weather is good."
딸이 읽어보더니 말했습니다.
"\u0027I go\u0027는 \u0027I went\u0027로, \u0027two book\u0027은 \u0027two books\u0027로 고쳐봐."
영자가 고쳐 썼습니다.
"완벽해! 계속 써!"
그날부터 영자는 매일 일기를 썼습니다.
🔤 영어 스토리

Young-ja writes her first English diary. 🔊

She writes about going to the library. 🔊

She borrows two books. 🔊

Her daughter helps. "Change \u0027I go\u0027 to \u0027I went.\u0027" 🔊

"Change \u0027two book\u0027 to \u0027two books.\u0027" 🔊

Young-ja fixes it. "Perfect!" 🔊

She writes a diary every day now. 🔊

Young-ja\u0027s daughter encouraged her to write a short diary every day. 🔊

"Even three sentences is enough." 🔊

That evening, Young-ja wrote carefully. 🔊

"Today I go to library. I borrow two book. I am happy. Weather is good." 🔊

Her daughter read it and smiled. 🔊

"Just two things to fix: \u0027I go\u0027 should be \u0027I went\u0027 \u2014 past tense." 🔊

"And \u0027two book\u0027 should be \u0027two books\u0027 \u2014 add S for plural." 🔊

Young-ja corrected both and read it back. 🔊

"Now it\u0027s perfect. Keep writing every day." 🔊

From that day on, Young-ja never missed a day. 🔊

과거 시제
""Went" is past tense of "go.""
plural 🔊
복수형
"Add S to make a noun plural."

고치다
"Her daughter helped her correct mistakes."

격려하다
"Her daughter encouraged her to write."
📋 문법 포인트
1. "I went to the library." — 과거형
I went to the store this morning. 🔊
2. "I borrowed two books." — 복수형 + 과거형
I bought three apples yesterday. 🔊
3. 짧은 일기 문장 작성
Today was a good day. I am happy. 🔊
🗣️ Speaking
  • Today I went to... 🔊
  • I ate... for lunch. 🔊
  • It was a good day. 🔊
  • 🎭 3문장 영어 일기 말하기 연습
