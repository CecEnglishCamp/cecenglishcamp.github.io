미국 마트에서 영어로 도움 요청

Asking for Help at the Supermarket

🟡 한글 이야기
영자는 마트에서 두부를 찾고 있었습니다.
직원에게 물었습니다. "Excuse me. Do you have tofu?"
"It\u0027s in aisle seven, near the produce section."
영자가 또 길을 잃었습니다.
다른 직원이 직접 데리고 가주었습니다.
"Thank you so much!"
"모르면 물어보면 되는구나."
🔤 영어 스토리

Young-ja looks for tofu. 🔊

"Do you have tofu?" she asks. 🔊

"Aisle seven, near produce." 🔊

She gets lost. 🔊

Another worker takes her there. 🔊

"Thank you so much!" 🔊

Young-ja spent fifteen minutes looking for tofu. 🔊

"Excuse me, do you have tofu?" 🔊

"Sure! Aisle seven, near produce." 🔊

"Which way?" 🔊

"Go straight and turn left." 🔊

She got confused again. 🔊

Another worker walked her there directly. 🔊

"Thank you so much!" 🔊

"If you don\u0027t know, just ask." 🔊
