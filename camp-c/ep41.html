Episode 41

미국 식당에서 팁 계산 대참사

The Tip Calculation Disaster

🟡 한글 이야기
영자는 딸과 식당에서 밥을 먹었습니다. 계산서가 나왔습니다. 총 42달러였습니다.
딸이 화장실에 간 사이 영자가 카드를 내밀었습니다.
카드 단말기에 이런 화면이 나왔습니다. "Add tip? 18% / 20% / 25% / Custom"
영자는 당황했습니다. 20%가 무난할 것 같아서 눌렀습니다.
총 50달러 40센트였습니다.
딸이 돌아와서 말했습니다. "엄마 잘 했어. 20%가 딱 적당해."
"그래? 나 그냥 눌렀는데!"
"실수로 잘 한 거야, 엄마."
🔤 영어 스토리

Young-ja and her daughter eat at a restaurant. 🔊

The bill is forty-two dollars. 🔊

The screen asks, "Add tip? 18%, 20%, or 25%?" 🔊

Young-ja does not know. She presses 20%. 🔊

The total is fifty dollars and forty cents. 🔊

Her daughter says, "Good choice! 20% is right." 🔊

"I just guessed!" Young-ja says. 🔊

Young-ja and her daughter enjoyed dinner at a restaurant. 🔊

The check came to forty-two dollars. 🔊

Her daughter stepped away, and Young-ja decided to pay. 🔊

The screen popped up: "Add a tip? 18% / 20% / 25% / Custom" 🔊

She thought, "Twenty percent sounds fair," and pressed it. 🔊

The total came to fifty dollars and forty cents. 🔊

Her daughter returned and smiled. "Mom, twenty percent is perfect!" 🔊

"Honestly? I just guessed!" 🔊

"You accidentally did the right thing, Mom." 🔊

📋 문법 포인트
1. "Add a tip?" — 선택 요청
Would you like to add a tip? 🔊
2. "The total is fifty forty." — 금액 읽기
The total is twenty-three fifty. 🔊
3. "I just guessed." — just + 과거동사
I just picked the middle option. 🔊
