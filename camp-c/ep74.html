오늘의 이야기 — 한글 스토리
KOREAN STORY · 미국 편의점 셀프 계산대 처음 쓰기

영자는 편의점에서 물건을 두 개 샀습니다.

직원이 있는 계산대는 줄이 길었습니다.

셀프 계산대는 비어 있었습니다.

"한번 해볼까?"

기계 앞에 섰습니다.

화면에 나왔습니다.

"Scan your item."

영자가 바코드를 찾아서 스캔했습니다.

삐 — 소리가 났습니다.

"Place item in bagging area."

봉투에 물건을 넣었습니다.

그런데!

"Unexpected item in bagging area. Please wait for assistance."

경보음이 울렸습니다.

영자가 얼어붙었습니다.

"내가 기계를 고장낸 거야?"

직원이 달려왔습니다.

"It's okay! No worries!"

직원이 설명했습니다.

"You put the bag in the wrong spot. The bagging area is here — right next to the scanner."
"Oh! Sorry, sorry!"

직원이 버튼을 눌러 기계를 초기화했습니다.

"Try again! You've got this."

영자가 다시 했습니다.

이번엔 올바른 자리에 놓았습니다.

기계가 멈추지 않았습니다.

두 번째 물건도 스캔했습니다.

"Please select payment method. Insert card or tap to pay."

영자가 카드를 넣었습니다.

"Transaction complete. Thank you! Have a nice day!"

영자가 숨을 내쉬었습니다.

직원이 엄지를 들며 말했습니다.

"See? You got it! You'll get the hang of it — next time will be easy."

영자가 웃으며 말했습니다.

"Next time I will be expert!"

직원이 웃으며 말했습니다.

"I believe it!"

밖으로 나오며 영자가 생각했습니다.

"셀프 계산대. 정복."
🔤
영어 스토리 — 3단계 레벨별
A1 · A2 · B1 — 내 수준에 맞게 읽어보세요
★ A1 Level — 입문 · 가장 쉬운 버전

Young-ja uses a self-checkout machine.

She scans her item. Beep!

She puts it in the bag area.

The machine says, "Unexpected item!"

An alarm sounds.

A worker comes. "No worries! Wrong spot."

Young-ja tries again. This time it works!

She pays by card.

"Transaction complete. Have a nice day!"

The worker says, "You'll get the hang of it!"

Young-ja smiles. "Next time I will be expert!"

✅ A2 Level — 초급 · 기본 회화 수준

Young-ja decides to try the self-checkout machine.

The regular checkout line is long. The self-checkout is empty.

She reads the screen: "Scan your item."

She scans it. Beep!

"Place item in bagging area."

She puts it in — wrong spot.

"Unexpected item in bagging area!"

An alarm goes off. Young-ja freezes. "Did I break the machine?"

A worker runs over. "No worries! Wrong spot — bagging area is right here."

Young-ja tries again. Correct spot this time. No alarm!

She scans the second item and pays by card.

"Transaction complete. Have a nice day!"

The worker gives a thumbs up. "You'll get the hang of it. Next time will be easy!"

Young-ja laughs. "Next time I will be expert!"

She walks out, thinking: "Self-checkout — conquered."

✅ B1 Level — 중급 · 자연스러운 영어

The regular checkout line had seven people in it.

The self-checkout had nobody.

Young-ja stood at the boundary between them, weighing her options.

She had watched people use these machines many times.

It looked simple enough.

"One time," she told herself. "Try once."

She stepped up to the machine and read the screen.

"Scan your item."

She found the barcode, positioned it under the red beam. Beep.

Good start.

"Place item in bagging area."

She put her water bottle into the bag on the left side of the machine.

The machine went silent for one moment.

Then: "UNEXPECTED ITEM IN BAGGING AREA. PLEASE WAIT FOR ASSISTANCE."

An alarm beeped. A light flashed.

Young-ja went completely still.

Around her, other shoppers glanced over.

"Did I break it?" she thought.

A young worker appeared within seconds, already smiling.

"Hey, no worries at all — happens constantly. You just put it in the wrong spot.

See this area right here, next to the scanner? That's the bagging area. "

He tapped a button. The screen reset.

"Go ahead and try again. You've totally got this."

Young-ja moved the bottle. Placed it in the correct area.

The machine hummed contentedly and moved to the next screen.

She scanned her second item. It beeped happily.

"Please select payment method."

She inserted her card.

"Transaction complete. Thank you! Have a nice day!"

Young-ja exhaled for what felt like the first time in several minutes.

The worker was still nearby, giving her a genuine thumbs up.

"See? Perfect. You'll get the hang of it — by the third or fourth time, it'll feel totally natural."

Young-ja straightened up and smiled.

"Next time — I will be the expert."

"I one hundred percent believe that," the worker said.

Outside, she walked to the bus stop with a small but definite spring in her step.

셀프 계산대. 정복.

One more thing America could no longer surprise her with.
