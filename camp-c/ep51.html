미국 세탁소에서 드라이클리닝 맡기기

Dropping Off Dry Cleaning

🟡 한글 이야기
영자는 딸의 정장을 드라이클리닝 맡기러 갔습니다.
"Dry clean this, please."
"Any stains we should know about?"
영자가 재킷을 살펴봤습니다. 작은 얼룩이 있었습니다.
"Yes. Here. Small stain."
"Ready by Thursday. Twelve fifty for the jacket."
목요일에 찾았습니다. 깨끗했습니다.
"Perfect! Thank you!"
🔤 영어 스토리

Young-ja takes a jacket to the dry cleaner. 🔊

The worker asks, "Any stains?" 🔊

Young-ja finds a small stain and points to it. 🔊

The jacket is ready on Thursday. 🔊

It costs twelve fifty. 🔊

The jacket is clean and perfect. 🔊

Young-ja brought her daughter\u0027s blazer to the dry cleaner. 🔊

The worker asked, "Any stains we should know about?" 🔊

Young-ja inspected the jacket and found a small dark spot near the cuff. 🔊

"Yes. Here. One small stain." 🔊

"Got it. We\u0027ll do our best. Ready by Thursday. Twelve fifty." 🔊

Thursday, she returned. The jacket was spotless. 🔊

"Perfect! Thank you so much!" 🔊

"We got the stain out completely." 🔊

Young-ja thought, "Dry cleaning English: easier than expected." 🔊
