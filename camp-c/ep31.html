Episode 31

동네 헬스장 등록 도전

Joining the Local Gym

🟡 한글 이야기
영자는 딸의 권유로 동네 헬스장에 등록하러 갔습니다.
직원이 말했습니다. "Welcome! Are you interested in a membership?"
영자가 대답했습니다. "Yes. How much one month?"
직원이 설명했습니다. "We have three plans. Basic is twenty-nine ninety-nine a month."
영자가 고개를 끄덕였습니다.
직원이 계속 말했습니다. "There's also an enrollment fee of fifty dollars."
영자가 물었습니다. "Enrollment fee? What is that?"
직원이 설명했습니다. "It's a one-time sign-up fee."
영자는 속으로 생각했습니다. '처음 낼 때만 내는 돈이 따로 있다고?'
결국 영자는 등록했습니다.
헬스장을 나오며 말했습니다. "미국엔 숨어있는 돈이 너무 많아."
🔤 영어 스토리

Young-ja goes to the gym. 🔊

She asks, "How much is one month?" 🔊

The worker says, "Twenty-nine ninety-nine a month." 🔊

There is also an enrollment fee of fifty dollars. 🔊

Young-ja does not understand "enrollment fee." 🔊

The worker explains it is a one-time fee. 🔊

Young-ja joins the gym. 🔊

"There are many hidden fees in America." 🔊

Young-ja\u0027s daughter encouraged her to join the local gym. 🔊

At the front desk, a friendly staff member greeted her. 🔊

"Welcome! Are you interested in a membership?" 🔊

"Yes. How much is it per month?" Young-ja asked. 🔊

"Our basic plan is twenty-nine ninety-nine a month." 🔊

Young-ja nodded, thinking that was reasonable. 🔊

But then the worker added, "There\u0027s also a fifty-dollar enrollment fee." 🔊

Young-ja frowned. "Enrollment fee? What is that?" 🔊

"It\u0027s a one-time sign-up fee, only when you first join." 🔊

Young-ja sighed and paid it anyway. 🔊

"In America, there are so many hidden fees." 🔊
