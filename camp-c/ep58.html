미국 이웃의 개가 짖을 때

When the Neighbor\u0027s Dog Barks at Night

🟡 한글 이야기
이웃집 개가 밤마다 크게 짖었습니다.
영자는 용기를 냈습니다.
"Your dog barks a lot at night. Hard to sleep."
"I\u0027m so sorry! He gets anxious. I\u0027ll keep him quiet."
그날 밤 개가 조용했습니다.
다음 날 이웃이 쿠키를 들고 왔습니다.
"불평도 친절하게 하면 쿠키가 온다."
🔤 영어 스토리

The neighbor\u0027s dog barks at night. 🔊

Young-ja tells the neighbor politely. 🔊

"Your dog barks a lot. Hard to sleep." 🔊

The neighbor is sorry. 🔊

That night, the dog is quiet. 🔊

The next day, the neighbor brings cookies. 🔊

"Kind complaints bring cookies!" 🔊

Young-ja had lost sleep for three nights because of the barking dog. 🔊

She gathered her courage and spoke calmly to the neighbor. 🔊

"Your dog has been barking quite a lot at night. It\u0027s been hard to sleep." 🔊

The neighbor looked embarrassed. "I\u0027m so sorry! He gets anxious after dark. I\u0027ll keep him inside." 🔊

"Thank you. I appreciate it." 🔊

That night \u2014 silence. 🔊

The next morning, a knock. The neighbor brought cookies. 🔊

"These are for you. Sorry about the noise." 🔊

Young-ja smiled. "In America, polite complaints come with cookies." 🔊
