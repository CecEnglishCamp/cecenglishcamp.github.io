🔤 영어 스토리

Young-ja tries a drive-through alone. 🔊

"Can I take your order?" 🔊

"One hamburger and one coffee, please." 🔊

She chooses medium size. 🔊

"That\u0027ll be seven fifty-three." 🔊

Young-ja pays ten dollars. 🔊

She gets her food. 🔊

"I did it alone!" she is proud. 🔊

Young-ja\u0027s daughter taught her how to order at a drive-through. 🔊

The next day, she tried alone. 🔊

"Welcome to McDonald\u0027s! Can I take your order?" 🔊

"Yes! One hamburger and one coffee, please!" 🔊

"What size coffee? Small, medium, or large?" 🔊

"Medium, please!" 🔊

"Anything else?" 🔊

"No, thank you. That is all!" 🔊

"That\u0027ll be seven fifty-three." 🔊

Young-ja handed over ten dollars. 🔊

"I ordered at the drive-through all by myself!" 🔊
