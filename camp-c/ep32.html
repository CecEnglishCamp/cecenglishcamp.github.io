미국 우체국에서 소포 보내기

Sending a Package at the Post Office

🟡 한글 이야기
영자는 한국에 있는 친구에게 선물을 보내려고 우체국에 갔습니다.
직원이 물었습니다. "Where is this going?"
영자가 대답했습니다. "Korea. South Korea."
직원이 물었습니다. "First class or priority?"
영자가 당황했습니다. '퍼스트 클래스? 비행기처럼?'
직원이 설명했습니다. "Priority is faster but costs more."
영자가 물었습니다. "How many days?"
직원이 대답했습니다. "About six to ten business days."
영자가 말했습니다. "Priority, please."
"우체국 영어도 따로 공부해야 하는구나."
🔤 영어 스토리

Young-ja goes to the post office. 🔊

She wants to send a package to Korea. 🔊

"First class or priority?" the worker asks. 🔊

Young-ja does not understand. 🔊

Priority is faster. 🔊

Young-ja chooses priority. 🔊

It takes six to ten days. 🔊

"Post office English is also hard." 🔊

Young-ja visited the post office to send a gift to her friend in Korea. 🔊

"Where is this going?" the postal worker asked. 🔊

"South Korea," Young-ja replied. 🔊

"Would you like first class or priority mail?" 🔊

Young-ja was confused. "Priority?" 🔊

The worker smiled. "Priority mail is faster, but costs more." 🔊

"How many days will it take?" 🔊

"About six to ten business days." 🔊

"Priority, please," she decided. 🔊

"There are special English words for everything here." 🔊

📋 문법 포인트
1. "Where is this going?" — 목적지 묻기

3. "Would you like A or B?" — 선택 질문
Would you like hot or cold? 🔊
