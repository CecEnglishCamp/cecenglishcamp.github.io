오늘의 이야기 — 한글 스토리
KOREAN STORY · 미국 보험회사에 전화하기

딸이 말했습니다.

"엄마, 내일 치과 가기 전에 보험회사에 전화해서 치과 치료가 적용되는지 확인해봐."

영자가 눈이 커졌습니다.

"내가 직접? 영어로?"

딸이 말했습니다.

"응. 엄마 할 수 있어. 번호 여기 있어."

영자는 전화기를 손에 쥐고 5분을 기다렸습니다.

심호흡.

전화를 걸었습니다.

자동응답이 나왔습니다.

"Thank you for calling Blue Shield Insurance.

For English, press 1. Para Español, press 2."

영자가 1을 눌렀습니다.

"Please say or enter your member ID number."

영자가 번호를 눌렀습니다.

"Please hold while we connect you to a representative."

음악이 흘렀습니다. 3분.

드디어 사람 목소리가 들렸습니다.

"Thank you for holding! This is Sarah. How can I help you today?"

영자가 준비한 문장을 말했습니다.

"Hello. I'd like to check my coverage, please.

I have a dental appointment tomorrow."

"Of course! Can I have your member ID and date of birth?"

영자가 또박또박 말했습니다.

"Member ID is... B-7-7-4-2-1. Date of birth... January 15, 1958."
"Thank you! Let me pull up your account."

잠시 후.

"Okay! You have dental coverage. Routine checkups are covered at 100%.

Basic procedures like fillings — covered at 80%."

영자가 말했습니다.

"So I pay... 20% for filling?"
"That's correct! Your out-of-pocket maximum is $500 for the year."

영자가 메모를 하면서 말했습니다.

"Okay. Thank you very much. Very helpful."

전화를 끊고 영자가 혼잣말했습니다.

"나 방금 영어로 보험회사랑 통화했어."

딸에게 달려가서 말했습니다.

"엄마 해냈어! 치과 보험 80% 적용된대!"

딸이 환하게 웃었습니다.

"엄마, 이제 못할 게 없다."
🔤
영어 스토리 — 3단계 레벨별
A1 · A2 · B1 — 내 수준에 맞게 읽어보세요
★ A1 Level — 입문 · 가장 쉬운 버전

Young-ja calls the insurance company.

She says, "quote">"I want to check my coverage."

The worker asks, "quote">"Member ID and birthday?"

Young-ja gives the information.

The worker says, "quote">"You have dental coverage."

Checkups are 100% covered.

Fillings are 80% covered.

Young-ja says, "quote">"Thank you! Very helpful."

She tells her daughter, "quote">"I did it! In English!"

✅ A2 Level — 초급 · 기본 회화 수준

Young-ja's daughter tells her to call the insurance company before her dental appointment.

Young-ja is nervous, but she tries.

She presses 1 for English.

She waits on hold for three minutes.

Finally, Sarah answers. "quote">"How can I help you?"

Young-ja says, "quote">"I'd like to check my coverage. I have a dental appointment tomorrow."

Sarah asks for her member ID and date of birth.

Young-ja gives both clearly.

Sarah says, "You have dental coverage.

Checkups are 100% covered. Fillings are 80% covered."

Young-ja asks, "quote">"So I pay 20% for a filling?"

"quote">"That's correct!"

Young-ja thanks her and hangs up.

She runs to her daughter. "quote">"I did it! Dental insurance is 80%!"

✅ B1 Level — 중급 · 자연스러운 영어

"Before your dental appointment tomorrow, call the insurance company

and confirm your dental coverage," her daughter said.

Young-ja stared at the phone number on the insurance card.

"quote">"Call them? In English? By myself?"

"quote">"You can do it, Mom. I'll be right here."

Young-ja held the phone for five full minutes before pressing call.

The automated system greeted her.

"Thank you for calling Blue Shield Insurance.

For English, press 1."

She pressed 1. She entered her member ID.

She was put on hold.

The hold music played for three minutes.

Young-ja used the time to rehearse:

"I'd like to check my coverage. I have a dental appointment tomorrow.

Coverage. Dental. Tomorrow. I can do this."

"quote">"Thank you for holding! This is Sarah. How can I help you today?"

Young-ja sat up straight.

"Hello. I'd like to check my dental coverage, please.

I have an appointment tomorrow and I want to know what is covered."

"quote">"Absolutely! May I have your member ID and date of birth?"

Young-ja gave both slowly and clearly.

"quote">"Member ID: B-7-7-4-2-1. Date of birth: January 15th, 1958."

"quote">"Perfect, thank you! Let me pull up your account."

A brief pause.

"Okay, great news! You do have dental coverage.

Routine checkups and cleanings are covered at one hundred percent.

Basic procedures such as fillings are covered at eighty percent."

Young-ja gripped her pen. "quote">"So for a filling, I pay twenty percent?"

"quote">"That's correct. And your annual out-of-pocket maximum is five hundred dollars."

"quote">"I understand. Thank you so much. This was very helpful."

"quote">"Of course! Have a great appointment tomorrow!"

Young-ja set the phone down and sat quietly for a moment.

Then she stood up and walked to the kitchen where her daughter was waiting.

"quote">"80% covered. Checkups are free. And I understood everything."

Her daughter hugged her. "quote">"Mom, I told you."

Young-ja laughed. "quote">"Next time, I will call without waiting five minutes first."
