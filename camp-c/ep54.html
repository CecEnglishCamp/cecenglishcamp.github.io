미국 약국에서 처방전 없이 약 사기

Buying Medicine at the Pharmacy

🟡 한글 이야기
영자는 두통이 심해서 약국에 갔습니다.
"I have headache. Bad headache."
"How long have you had it?" "Since morning. Three hours."
"Are you taking any other medications?"
영자가 당황했습니다. 'Medications?'
"No other medicine."
"Try Tylenol. Two tablets with water."
영자가 메모했습니다. "Medication = 약. 이 단어는 꼭 알아야 해."
🔤 영어 스토리

Young-ja has a bad headache. 🔊

She goes to the pharmacy. 🔊

"How long?" "Three hours." 🔊

The pharmacist asks about other medicines. 🔊

Young-ja takes no other medicine. 🔊

"Try Tylenol," the pharmacist says. 🔊

Young-ja buys it and feels better. 🔊

Young-ja had a headache since early morning. 🔊

She went to the pharmacy. "I have a bad headache." 🔊

"How long have you had it?" "About three hours." 🔊

"Are you currently taking any other medications?" 🔊

Young-ja blinked. "Medications?" 🔊

The pharmacist clarified. "Other medicine you take regularly." 🔊

"No, no other medicine." 🔊

"I\u0027d recommend Tylenol \u2014 two tablets with water." 🔊

An hour later, the headache was gone. 🔊

She wrote: "Medications = medicine. Must remember this word." 🔊
