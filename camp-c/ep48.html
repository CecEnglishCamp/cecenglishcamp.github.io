Explaining Your Hairstyle at the Salon

🟡 한글 이야기
영자는 미용실에 갔습니다.
"What are we doing today?"
"Cut and color, please."
"How much are we taking off?"
영자가 손으로 보여줬습니다. "Maybe this much?"
"About two inches?"
"Yes, two inches."
"Lighter or darker?"
영자가 사진을 보여줬습니다. "Like this, please."
"I love it! Let\u0027s do it!"
결과를 보고 영자는 만족했습니다. "사진 보여주는 게 최고의 영어다!"
🔤 영어 스토리

Young-ja goes to the hair salon. 🔊

She wants a cut and color. 🔊

The hairdresser asks how much to cut. 🔊

"Two inches," Young-ja says. 🔊

She shows a photo for the color. 🔊

"I love it! Let\u0027s do it!" 🔊

Young-ja is very happy with the result. 🔊

Young-ja walked into the salon feeling nervous. 🔊

"What are we doing today?" 🔊

"Cut and color, please." 🔊

"How much are we taking off?" 🔊

She pinched her fingers. "Maybe this much?" 🔊

"About two inches?" "Yes!" 🔊

"Lighter or darker?" 🔊

Young-ja showed a photo. "Like this, please." 🔊

"I love it! Let\u0027s make it happen!" 🔊

An hour later, she looked in the mirror and felt wonderful. 🔊

"Showing a photo is the best English of all." 🔊
