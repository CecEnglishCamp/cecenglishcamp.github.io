미국 도서관에서 책 추천받기

Getting a Book Recommendation at the Library

🟡 한글 이야기
영자는 사서에게 물었습니다. "Can you recommend easy English book?"
"What kind of stories do you enjoy?"
"I like funny stories. And cooking."
사서가 책 두 권을 가져왔습니다.
"이 책은 짧은 재미있는 이야기이고, 이 책은 한국-미국 요리책입니다."
영자는 두 권을 빌렸습니다.
요리책을 읽으며 생각했습니다. "영어 공부가 재미있어졌다."
🔤 영어 스토리

Young-ja asks the librarian for a book. 🔊

She likes funny stories and cooking. 🔊

The librarian brings two books. 🔊

One is funny stories. One is a Korean cookbook in English. 🔊

Young-ja borrows both. 🔊

"English is fun now." 🔊

Young-ja approached the librarian\u0027s desk. 🔊

"Could you recommend an easy English book for me?" 🔊

"What kind of stories do you enjoy?" 🔊

"I like funny stories. And I love cooking." 🔊

The librarian returned with two books. 🔊

"Short funny stories \u2014 perfect for your level. And a Korean-American cookbook with English recipes." 🔊

"A Korean cookbook in English? Perfect!" 🔊

"Reading about something you love makes the language easier." 🔊

That evening, she read the cookbook, recognizing ingredient after ingredient. 🔊

"English is actually starting to become fun." 🔊
