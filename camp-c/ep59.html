Selling Korean Food at a Church Bazaar

딸의 교회에서 바자회가 열렸습니다.
영자는 김치전과 잡채를 준비했습니다.
"What is this?" "Kimchi pancake. Spicy. Very delicious!"
"And this?" "Japchae. Glass noodles with vegetables."
여자가 맛을 보았습니다. "Oh my goodness! Amazing!"

🔤 영어 스토리

Young-ja sells Korean food at a church bazaar. 🔊

She makes kimchi pancakes and japchae. 🔊

People love the food. 🔊

"This is amazing!" 🔊

All food sells in one hour. 🔊

"Your food was the most popular!" 🔊

"Everyone loves Korean food!" 🔊

Young-ja spent Friday preparing for the church bazaar. 🔊

She made kimchi jeon and japchae. 🔊

Visitors gathered at her table. "What is this?" 🔊

"Kimchi pancake. A little spicy, very crispy, very delicious!" 🔊

"And this?" "Japchae \u2014 glass noodles with vegetables and sesame oil." 🔊

The woman tasted each and her eyes widened. "Oh my goodness, incredible!" 🔊

Within an hour, everything was sold. 🔊

The pastor stopped by. "Your table was the most popular booth today!" 🔊

"Korean food speaks for itself in any language!" 🔊
