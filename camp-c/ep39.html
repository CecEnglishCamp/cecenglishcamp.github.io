Reporting Noise to the Front Desk

🟡 한글 이야기
위층에서 밤새 쿵쿵 소리가 났습니다.
영자가 관리실에 전화했습니다.
"I am in apartment 305. Upstairs, very noisy. All night."
"Which unit is making the noise?"
"Maybe 405?"
"I\u0027ll send someone to check."
다음 날 조용해졌습니다.
"나 관리실에 영어로 전화했어!"
🔤 영어 스토리

Upstairs is very noisy at night. 🔊

Young-ja calls the front desk. 🔊

"I am in 305. Upstairs is noisy." 🔊

The worker asks which unit. 🔊

"Maybe 405." 🔊

"I\u0027ll send someone." 🔊

Next day it is quiet. 🔊

Her daughter says, "You are brave!" 🔊

Young-ja couldn\u0027t sleep due to loud banging upstairs. 🔊

She called the front desk. 🔊

"I am in apartment 305. The upstairs unit is very noisy all night." 🔊

"I\u0027m sorry to hear that. Which unit?" 🔊

"Maybe 405? Directly above me." 🔊

"I\u0027ll send someone up to check." 🔊

The next morning was completely silent. 🔊

"I called the front desk in English!" 🔊

Her daughter said, "Mom, you are brave!" 🔊

📋 문법 포인트
1. "It has been going on all night." — 현재완료진행
It has been raining all day. 🔊
2. "I\u0027ll send someone." — will + 동사
I\u0027ll call you back. 🔊
3. "Do you know which unit?" — 간접의문
Do you know which floor? 🔊
