손녀딸에게 영어 배우기

Learning English from My Granddaughter

"재채기하면 Bless you라고 해."
영자가 따라 했습니다. 소연이가 재채기를 했습니다.
"Bless you!"
"Perfect, Grandma! You\u0027re a fast learner!"
소연이가 또 가르쳤습니다. "My bad는 내 실수라는 뜻이야."
영자가 고개를 갸우뜻했습니다. "My bad? 내 나쁜?"

🔤 영어 스토리

Soyeon teaches her grandmother English. 🔊

"Bless you" \u2014 when someone sneezes. 🔊

Soyeon sneezes. Young-ja says, "Bless you!" 🔊

"Perfect!" Soyeon claps. 🔊

"My bad" means "my fault." It is slang. 🔊

Young-ja thinks "my bad" is funny English. 🔊

One afternoon, Soyeon announced, "Grandma, I\u0027ll be your English teacher today!" 🔊

Young-ja sat up straight. "Okay, Teacher Soyeon!" 🔊

"When someone sneezes, say Bless you." 🔊

Young-ja repeated carefully. Then Soyeon sneezed. 🔊

Young-ja jumped in. "Bless you!" 🔊

"Perfect! You\u0027re a fast learner!" 🔊

Next lesson: "When you bump into someone, say My bad!" 🔊

Young-ja frowned. "My bad? That sounds like my evil?" 🔊

Soyeon burst out laughing. "No! It means my fault. It\u0027s slang." 🔊

"My bad... my bad..." Young-ja repeated. 🔊

"Grandma, you\u0027re going to sound so cool." 🔊
