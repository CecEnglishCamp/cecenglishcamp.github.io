Episode 44

전화 자동응답 시스템과의 전쟁

Fighting the Automated Phone System

🟡 한글 이야기
영자는 전화요금을 문의하려고 전화를 걸었습니다.
"Press one for English."
영자가 1을 눌렀습니다.
"For billing, press one. For technical support, press two..."
영자가 1을 눌렀습니다.
"Please enter your account number followed by the pound sign."
영자가 당황했습니다. '파운드 사인이 뭐야?'
아무것도 누르지 않았습니다.
"Let me transfer you to an agent."
사람이 나왔습니다.
"Hello! Real person! Thank you!"
"하하! Yes, I\u0027m real. How can I help you?"
🔤 영어 스토리

Young-ja calls the phone company. 🔊

A machine answers first. 🔊

She presses many numbers. 🔊

The machine asks for her "pound sign." 🔊

She does not know what that means. 🔊

She does not press anything. 🔊

The machine transfers her to a person. 🔊

"Real person! Thank you!" 🔊

Young-ja needed to ask about her phone bill. 🔊

An automated system answered. "Press one for English." 🔊

She pressed one. Then another menu appeared. 🔊

She pressed one for billing. 🔊

"Please enter your account number followed by the pound sign." 🔊

Young-ja froze. "Pound sign? What is that?" 🔊

She pressed nothing. 🔊

"I\u0027m sorry, I didn\u0027t catch that. Let me connect you to an agent." 🔊

A real human answered. 🔊

Young-ja nearly shouted. "Hello! A real person! Thank you so much!" 🔊

📋 문법 포인트
1. "Press one for English." — 전화 안내
For customer service, press one. 🔊
2. "I didn\u0027t catch that." — 못 들었을 때
Sorry, I didn\u0027t catch your name. 🔊
3. "Let me connect you." — Let me + 동사
Let me check that for you. 🔊
