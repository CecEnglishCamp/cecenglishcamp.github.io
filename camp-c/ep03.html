오늘의 이야기 — 한글 스토리
KOREAN STORY · 동네 산책에서 생긴 일

딸이 출근하고 혼자 남은 아침, 영자는 집 안을 한 바퀴 둘러보다가 결심했습니다.

"오늘은 좀 나가봐야겠다."

운동화를 신고 문을 나선 영자. 동네 골목길을 따라 천천히 걸었습니다.

공원 잔디밭 앞에서 발걸음을 멈췄습니다. 주인 아저씨가 목줄을 잡고 있는데, 강아지는 땅바닥에 배를 깔고 드러누워 꼼짝도 하지 않고 있었습니다.

아저씨가 목줄을 당겼습니다. 강아지는 고개만 살짝 들더니 다시 눈을 감아버렸습니다.

영자는 입을 막고 웃음을 참았습니다.

"저 강아지, 나보다 훨씬 솔직하네."

조금 더 걷다 보니, 아이들이 자전거를 타고 신나게 달려왔습니다. 웃음소리와 바람 소리가 뒤섞이며 영자 곁을 스쳐갔습니다. 괜히 기분이 좋아졌습니다.

공원 한켠 벤치를 발견하고 앉았습니다. 어제 딸에게 부탁해 받아온 테이크아웃 커피를 꺼내 한 모금 마셨습니다.

나무 그늘, 자전거 타는 아이들, 아직도 잔디 위에 누워 있을 그 강아지...

영자는 조용히 미소 지으며 속으로 중얼거렸습니다.

"이 동네, 생각보다 꽤 재미있네요." 🌳

영어 스토리 — 3단계 레벨별
A1 · A2 · B1 — 내 수준에 맞게 읽어보세요
★ A1 Level — 입문 · 가장 쉬운 버전

Young-ja goes for a walk.

She goes to the park.

She sees a dog. The dog lies on the grass.

The dog does not move. Young-ja laughs.

She sees kids on bikes. They ride fast.

Young-ja sits on a bench. She drinks coffee.

She smiles and says, "This place is more fun than I expected." 🌳

✅ A2 Level — 초급 · 기본 회화 수준

It is a sunny morning. Young-ja's daughter is at work.

Young-ja decides to go for a walk in the neighborhood.

At the park, she sees a dog on the grass.

The owner pulls the leash, but the dog just lies there and does not move.

Young-ja covers her mouth and laughs.

Further down the street, she sees kids riding bikes. They are laughing and having fun.

Young-ja finds a bench and sits down with her coffee.

She looks around at the trees and the kids and the lazy dog.

She smiles. "This place is more fun than I expected." 🌳

✅ B1 Level — 중급 · 자연스러운 영어

On a quiet Tuesday morning, with her daughter at work and the house to herself, Young-ja puts on her sneakers and steps outside.

The neighborhood is calm and unfamiliar, but the fresh air feels welcoming.

Near the park entrance, she notices a man struggling with a dog on a leash. The dog has made a decision — it drops flat onto the grass and refuses to budge, no matter how politely its owner pulls.

Young-ja presses a hand over her mouth to hide her laugh.

"I know that feeling," she thinks. "Some mornings are just like that."

A little further on, a group of kids zoom past on bikes, calling out to each other and laughing. The energy is contagious, and Young-ja feels her own mood lift.

She finds an empty bench near a small fountain, settles in with her takeout coffee, and watches the world go by.

The trees, the noisy kids, the stubborn dog still planted on the grass — she takes it all in and smiles.

"This neighborhood is far more entertaining than I expected." 🌳
