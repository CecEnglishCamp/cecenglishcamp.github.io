미국 슈퍼마켓 쿠폰 사용하기

Using Coupons at the Supermarket

🟡 한글 이야기
영자는 신문에서 쿠폰을 오려두었습니다.
마트에서 계산할 때 쿠폰을 내밀었습니다.
"Sorry, this coupon expired last week."
"Expired? What means?"
"기한이 지났다는 뜻이에요."
쿠폰을 보니 "Exp: 03/05"라고 쓰여 있었습니다. 오늘은 03/12.
"Oh! I miss the date. Sorry!"
"No worries! Check the expiration date next time."
영자가 메모했습니다. "Exp = 만료일. 항상 확인하자."
🔤 영어 스토리

Young-ja uses a coupon at the store. 🔊

"This coupon is expired." 🔊

She does not know "expired." 🔊

It means the coupon is too old. 🔊

She checks the date. It expired one week ago. 🔊

She learns to check the expiration date. 🔊

Young-ja had clipped coupons from the Sunday newspaper. 🔊

At checkout, she handed them to the cashier. 🔊

"I\u0027m sorry, this coupon expired last week." 🔊

Young-ja stiffened. "Expired?" 🔊

"The use-by date has passed." 🔊

She looked at the tiny print. "Exp: 03/05." Today was March 12th. 🔊

"Oh! I missed the date. Sorry!" 🔊

"No worries! Check the expiration date next time." 🔊

Back home, she wrote in bold: "EXP = Expiration Date. ALWAYS CHECK." 🔊

1. "This coupon is expired." — be + 형용사

3. "Check the expiration date." — 명령문
Always check the date before you buy. 🔊
