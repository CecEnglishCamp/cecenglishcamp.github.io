이웃집 BBQ 파티 초대

Invited to a Neighbor\u0027s BBQ Party

🟡 한글 이야기
이웃 Mike가 초대했습니다.
"We\u0027re having a BBQ on Saturday. Can you make it?"
영자는 "make it"이 '뭔가 만들어 오라고?' 생각했습니다.
"Make what?" 영자가 물었습니다.
Mike가 웃었습니다. "I mean, can you come?"
파티에서 Mike가 물었습니다. "Do you want to try my ribs?"
영자가 먹어보고 말했습니다. "Delicious!"
Mike가 말했습니다. "Glad you could make it!"
영자도 웃었습니다. "Me too! I make it!"
🔤 영어 스토리

Neighbor Mike invites Young-ja to a BBQ. 🔊

"Can you make it?" he asks. 🔊

Young-ja thinks she must make something. 🔊

Mike explains it means "Can you come?" 🔊

Young-ja goes to the party. 🔊

She eats ribs. "Delicious!" 🔊

"Glad you could make it!" says Mike. 🔊

"Me too! I make it!" Young-ja smiles. 🔊

Mike knocked on her door with an invitation. 🔊

"We\u0027re having a BBQ Saturday. Can you make it?" 🔊

Young-ja frowned. "Make what?" 🔊

Mike laughed. "I mean, can you come?" 🔊

Young-ja\u0027s face turned red. "Yes, I can come!" 🔊

At the party, Mike offered ribs. "Want to try these?" 🔊

Young-ja\u0027s eyes went wide. "Delicious!" 🔊

"Glad you could make it!" Mike called. 🔊

"Me too! I make it!" Young-ja smiled. 🔊
