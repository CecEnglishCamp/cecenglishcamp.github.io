2025년 겨울, 미소는 태백의 폐교 앞에 서 있습니다. 황지천이 부드럽게 흐르고, 미소는 아버지의 녹슨 상자를 들고 있습니다. 상자를 열자 오래된 종이들이 나왔어요. 종이에는 “T.O.B.I. 프로젝트”라는 탄광 계획이 적혀 있었죠.
미소는 궁금해져서 탄광으로 걸어갑니다. 문에는 “출입금지” 팻말이 있었지만, 미소는 문을 밀고 들어갔어요. 차가운 공기가 나왔습니다. 그때 파란 눈을 가진 로봇, 점프가 나타났어요.
점프는 미소를 광산 깊은 곳, 400미터 아래로 데려갑니다. 공기는 차고 축축했어요. 낡은 기계들과 파이프가 있었고, 또 다른 로봇 로보가 깨어났습니다.
로보는 “이 광산은 데이터센터가 될 수 있어요. 기계를 식히고 마을을 따뜻하게 할 수 있어요.”라고 말했어요. 미소는 신이 났어요. “이건 우리 마을을 구할 수 있어!”라고 외쳤어요.
갑자기 땅이 흔들립니다. 로보는 “위험 신호가 있습니다!”라고 했어요. 점프는 어둠을 바라보고, 미소는 무서웠지만, “우리가 마을을 깨울 거예요!”라고 말했어요. 밖에서는 강물 소리가 크게 들렸습니다.
In Taebaek, winter 2025, Miso stands at an old school. The Hwangjeecheon River makes a soft sound. She holds her dad’s rusty box. She opens it and finds old papers. The papers are a plan called “T.O.B.I. Project” for a mine.
Miso is curious and walks to the mine. The gate is old with a “No Entry” sign. She pushes it open. Cold air comes out. A robot, Jump, appears. Jump is fast with bright blue eyes.
It takes Miso deep into the mine, 400 meters down. The air is cold and wet. Old machines and pipes are around. Another robot, Robo, wakes up.
Robo says, “This mine can be a data center. It can cool machines and heat the village.” Miso is excited. “This can save our village!” she says.
Suddenly, the ground shakes. Robo says, “There is a danger sign!” Jump looks into the dark. Miso is scared but says, “We will wake the village!” The river’s sound is loud outside.
📚 Target Vocabulary
  • winter: cold season
  • soft: quiet or gentle
  • push: to move something forward
  • curious: wanting to know more
  • cold: not warm
  • wake: to start or open
  • heat: to make warm
  • excited: very happy
  • shake: to move quickly
  • danger: not safe
Practice Using the Words
  • It is winter in Taebaek.
  • Miso is curious to know more.
  • Robo can heat the village.
  • The ground starts to shake.
📘 Target Grammar Points
  • Simple Past: "Miso opened." (past actions)
    Examples: Miso opened the box. Robo woke up.
  • Reported Speech: Robo said, “This can be…” → Robo said that it could be a data center.
    Examples: Robo said that it could be a data center. Miso said that they would wake the village.
  • Can for Ability: "This can save."
    Examples: This can save the village. The mine can heat houses.
💬 Speaking Practice
Practice Simple Past and Reported Speech
  1. Talk about what happened and what was said.
    Ex) Miso opened the box. Robo said that it could be a data center.
  2. Now you try: Jump ________ (take) Miso. Miso ________ (say) they would wake the village.
  3. Pair Activity: Share a past experience and report what someone said.
    Ex) I visited a park. My friend said it was fun.
Practice Can for Ability
  1. Talk about what things can do.
    Ex) This can save the village. The mine can heat houses.
  2. Now you try: Robo ________ (make) a data center. Miso ________ (help) the village.
