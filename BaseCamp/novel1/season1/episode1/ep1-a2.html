Season 1, Episode 1 – A2 Level

태백의 겨울. 미소는 옛날 학교 앞에 서 있었습니다.
Winter in Taebaek. Miso stood in front of an old school.
그녀의 손에는 아버지의 녹슨 상자가 들려 있었습니다.
She held her father’s rusty metal box.
상자를 열자, 종이 뭉치가 나왔습니다. "T.O.B.I. 프로젝트"
When she opened the box, she found a pile of papers. “T.O.B.I. Project.”
그녀는 폐광으로 들어갔습니다.
She walked into the abandoned mine.
그곳에서 로봇 ‘점프’를 만났습니다. 파란 눈을 가진 로봇이었습니다.
There she met a robot named Jump. It had blue eyes.
점프는 지하로 내려가는 리프트를 탔습니다. 미소도 함께 탔습니다.
Jump took a lift underground. Miso followed.
400미터 아래, 또 다른 로봇 ‘로보’가 깨어났습니다.
400 meters below, another robot, Robo, woke up.
로보는 말했습니다. “이곳은 데이터센터가 될 수 있어요.”
Robo said, “This place can be a data center.”
그 순간, 땅이 흔들렸습니다. “위험이 감지되었습니다.”
Suddenly, the ground shook. “Danger detected,” said Robo.

📚 Vocabulary

  • rusty: covered with rust, old and brown
  • abandoned: not used anymore
  • project: a planned work
  • detect: to find or notice something
  • lift: a machine to move people up or down

🧠 Reading Comprehension

  1. Where was Miso standing? → In front of an old school ✅
  2. What was inside the metal box? → A pile of papers ✅
  3. Who was the robot with blue eyes? → Jump ✅
  4. What did Robo say about the place? → It can be a data center ✅

💬 Speaking Practice

  • “She held her father’s rusty metal box.”

  • “Danger detected,” said Robo.
  • Practice with a friend: “Where are you going?” “I’m going into the mine.”

🎭 Mini Roleplay

Partner A: You are Miso. Ask Jump questions.

Partner B: You are Jump. Answer Miso’s questions.

  • A: What is this place?
  • B: This is an old mine. Follow me.
  • A: Why are we going down?
  • B: To find the data center.
