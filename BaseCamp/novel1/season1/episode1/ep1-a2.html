Season 1, Episode 1 – A2 Level

2025년 겨울, 미소는 태백의 폐교 앞에서 아버지의 녹슨 상자를 들고 있었습니다. 황지천이 부드럽게 흐르고, 미소는 상자를 열어 오래된 종이들을 발견합니다. 종이에는 “T.O.B.I. 프로젝트”라는 탄광 계획이 적혀 있었죠.
미소는 궁금해져서 탄광으로 걸어갑니다. 문에는 “출입금지” 팻말이 있었지만, 미소는 문을 밀고 들어갔어요. 차가운 공기가 나왔습니다. 그때 파란 눈을 가진 로봇, 점프가 나타났어요.
점프는 미소를 광산 깊은 곳, 400미터 아래로 데려갑니다. 공기는 차고 축축했어요. 낡은 기계들과 파이프가 있었고, 또 다른 로봇 로보가 깨어났습니다.
로보는 “이 광산은 데이터센터가 될 수 있고, 마을을 따뜻하게 할 수 있어요.”라고 말했어요. 미소는 “이것이 우리 마을을 구할 수 있어!”라고 외쳤습니다.
갑자기 땅이 흔들렸어요. 로보는 “위험 신호가 감지됐어요!”라고 말했습니다. 점프는 어둠을 바라보고, 미소는 무서웠지만 “우리가 마을을 깨울 거야!”라고 말했어요. 밖에서 강물 소리가 크게 들렸습니다.
In Taebaek, winter 2025, Miso stands at an old school. The Hwangjeecheon River makes a soft sound. She holds her dad’s rusty box. She opens it and finds old papers. The papers are a plan called “T.O.B.I. Project” for a mine.
Miso is curious and walks to the mine. The gate is old with a “No Entry” sign. She pushes it open. Cold air comes out. A robot, Jump, appears. Jump is fast with bright blue eyes.
It takes Miso deep into the mine, 400 meters down. The air is cold and wet. Old machines and pipes are around. Another robot, Robo, wakes up.
Robo says, “This mine can be a data center. It can cool machines and heat the village.” Miso is excited. “This can save our village!”
Suddenly, the ground shakes. Robo says, “There is a danger sign!” Jump looks into the dark. Miso is scared but says, “We will wake the village!” The river’s sound is loud outside.
📚 Target Vocabulary
  • winter: cold season
  • soft: quiet or gentle
  • push: to move something forward
  • curious: wanting to know more
  • cold: not warm
  • wake: to start or open
  • heat: to make warm
  • excited: very happy
  • shake: to move quickly
  • danger: not safe
✏️ Practice Using the Words
Fill in the blanks:
1. It is ________ in Taebaek. (winter)
2. Miso is ________ to know more. (curious)
3. Robo can ________ the village. (heat)
4. The ground starts to ________. (shake)
📝 Target Grammar
  • Simple Past: "Miso opened the box." (과거 동사)
  • Reported Speech: Robo said that it could be a data center.
  • Can for Ability: "This can save the village."
💬 Speaking Practice
Practice Simple Past and Reported Speech
- Miso opened the box. Robo said that it could be a data center.
- Now you try: Jump ______ (take) Miso. Miso ______ (say) they would wake the village.

Practice Can for Ability
- This can save the village. The mine can heat houses.
- Now you try: Robo ______ (make) a data center. Miso ______ (help) the village.
