Season 1, Episode 1 – B2 Upper-Intermediate

2025년 겨울, 미소는 태백의 폐교 앞에 서 있었다. 그녀의 손에는 아버지의 철제 상자가 들려 있었다.
In the winter of 2025, Miso stood in front of an abandoned elementary school in Taebaek, clutching her father’s metal box.
상자 안에는 세월에 바랜 설계 도면과 낯선 기술 문서들이 정갈히 정리되어 있었다.
Inside the box were neatly organized blueprints and unfamiliar technical documents, faded by time.
그녀는 녹슨 폐광문을 열고, 얼어붙은 호기심을 안고 어둠 속으로 발을 들였다.
She opened the rusted gate of the mine and stepped into the darkness, driven by a frozen curiosity.
그곳에서 만난 건, 네 개의 다리를 가진 인공지능 로봇, 점프였다.
There, she encountered a quadrupedal AI robot named Jump.
점프는 미소를 지하 400미터까지 안내했고, 그곳은 기계와 습기로 가득한 폐광의 심장이었다.
Jump guided her 400 meters underground, to the heart of the mine, filled with machines and humidity.
또 다른 로봇, 로보가 작동을 시작했고, “T.O.B.I. 프로젝트”의 핵심 정보를 제공했다.
Another robot, Robo, powered on and began delivering key data about the “T.O.B.I. Project.”
로보는 이 탄광이 데이터센터로 전환될 수 있고, 마을에 지속가능한 난방을 제공할 수 있다고 설명했다.
Robo explained that the mine could be converted into a data center and provide sustainable heating to the town.
지하의 구조, 수온, 유출량까지 분석된 데이터는 설득력 있는 미래 가능성을 암시했다.
The analyzed data—underground structure, water temperature, outflow—implied a convincing future potential.
그러나 그 순간, 이상한 진동과 함께 외부 침입 경고가 울렸다.
But suddenly, a strange vibration triggered an external intrusion alert.
점프는 어둠 속을 경계하며 말했다. “무언가 있어. 인간은 아니야.”
Jump scanned the darkness warily. “Something’s there. It’s not human.”
미소는 로보와 점프를 바라보며, 조용히 결심을 되뇌었다.
Miso looked at Robo and Jump and silently reaffirmed her resolve.
“이 프로젝트가, 이 마을을 다시 숨 쉬게 할 수 있을 거야.”
“This project might just breathe life back into this village,” she whispered.

📘 Vocabulary

  • clutch: to hold something tightly
  • blueprint: a technical drawing or design plan
  • encounter: to meet unexpectedly
  • humid: having a lot of moisture in the air
  • sustainable: able to continue without harming the environment
  • analyze: to study something in detail
  • resolve: a firm decision or determination
  • vibration: a small, rapid movement

🧠 Reading Comprehension

  1. What was inside Miso’s father’s box? → Blueprints and technical documents
  2. How far did Miso travel underground? → 400 meters
  3. What did Robo explain about the mine? → It could become a sustainable data center
  4. What happened at the end of the episode? → A threat was detected, and Miso made a firm decision

💬 Speaking Practice

  • Repeat 3x: “This project might just breathe life back into this village.”
  • Q&A Pair Work:
    • Q: What is the T.O.B.I. Project?
    • A: It’s a plan to use the old mine for heating and data storage.
  • Mini Roleplay

    • Miso: Are you... a robot?
    Jump: Yes. I am T.O.B.I. UNIT-2.
    Miso: What’s down here?
    Robo: A forgotten future. And it’s ready.

🎧 Listening Script

  1. Miso finds technical papers about her father’s project.
  2. She enters the abandoned mine and meets Jump.
  3. They go underground and meet Robo, who gives data analysis.
  4. A sudden alert signals danger, but Miso remains determined.
