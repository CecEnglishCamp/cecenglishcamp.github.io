Season 1, Episode 1 – A1 Beginner

미소는 광산으로 간다. 점프라는 로봇이 나온다.
Miso goes to the mine. A robot named Jump appears.
점프는 미소를 지하 400m로 데려간다.
Jump takes Miso 400 meters down underground.
로보라는 또 다른 로봇이 깨어난다.
Another robot, Robo, wakes up.
로보는 “이 광산은 마을을 따뜻하게 할 수 있어요”라고 말한다.
Robo says, “This mine can make the village warm.”
미소는 놀라고 말한다. “우리는 마을을 살릴 수 있어요!”
Miso is surprised and says, “We can save the village!”

📘 Vocabulary

  • mine: a place under the ground
  • robot: a smart machine
  • wake: to stop sleeping
  • warm: not cold
  • village: a small town

🧠 Comprehension

  1. Who goes to the mine? → Miso ✅
  2. What is Jump? → A robot ✅
  3. How deep is the mine? → 400 meters ✅
  4. What does Robo say? → The mine can be warm ✅

🗣️ Speaking Practice

  • Repeat: “This mine can be warm.” (3 times)
  • Ask a partner: “Where is Miso?”
  • Mini-dialogue:

Miso: Where are we?
Jump: We are in the mine.
Miso: Can we help the village?
Robo: Yes, we can!

🎧 Listening Script

  1. Miso goes to the mine under the village.
  2. A robot named Jump says, “Follow me.”
  3. They go underground. It is dark and quiet.
  4. Robo wakes up and says, “We can help the village.”
  5. Miso says, “Let’s do it together!”
