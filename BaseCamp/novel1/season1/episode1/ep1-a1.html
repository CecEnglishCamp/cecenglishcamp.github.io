📘 Episode 1: 마을 아래, 빛이 살아 있다 - A1 Level (확장판)

미소는 추운 겨울날, 마을 아래에 있는 광산으로 갑니다.
On a cold winter day, Miso goes to the mine under the village.
그녀는 오래된 문을 열고, 안으로 들어갑니다.
She opens an old door and walks inside.
갑자기, 파란 눈을 가진 로봇이 나옵니다. 이름은 점프입니다.
Suddenly, a robot with blue eyes comes out. Its name is Jump.
점프는 말합니다. “나를 따라오세요.”
Jump says, “Follow me.”
그들은 지하로 내려갑니다. 아래는 어둡고 조용합니다.
They go down underground. It is dark and quiet.
밝은 빛이 켜지고, 또 다른 로봇이 깨어납니다. 이름은 로보입니다.
A bright light turns on, and another robot wakes up. Its name is Robo.
로보는 말합니다. “이 광산은 따뜻합니다. 마을을 도울 수 있어요.”
Robo says, “This mine is warm. It can help the village.”
미소는 놀랍니다. “정말이야? 마을을 다시 살릴 수 있어요?”
Miso is surprised. “Really? Can we help the village again?”
로보와 점프는 고개를 끄덕입니다. “같이 해요.”
Robo and Jump nod. “Let’s do it together.”

📚 Target Vocabulary

  • mine: a place under the ground
  • robot: a smart machine
  • cold: not warm
  • light: something that helps you see
  • help: to give support
  • village: a small town
  • door: you open it to go in
  • together: not alone

🧠 Reading Comprehension

  • Where does Miso go?
  • What is the name of the robot?
  • What does Robo say about the mine?
  • How does Miso feel?

🔈 Listening Script

1. Miso goes to the mine under the village.

2. A robot named Jump says, “Follow me.”

3. They go underground. It is dark and quiet.

4. Robo wakes up and says, “We can help the village.”

5. Miso says, “Let’s do it together!”

🗣️ Speaking Practice

  • Repeat 3 times: “This mine can help the village.”
  • Repeat 3 times: “Let’s do it together!”
  • Ask a friend: “Where do you want to go?”
  • Answer: “I want to go to the mine.”
  • Practice mini-dialogue:

Miso: Where are we?

Jump: We are in the mine.

Miso: Can we help the village?

Robo: Yes, we can!
