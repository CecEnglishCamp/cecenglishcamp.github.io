Season 1, Episode 1 – B1 Intermediate

2025년 겨울, 미소는 태백의 폐교 앞에서 아버지의 철제 상자를 들고 서 있었습니다.
In the winter of 2025, Miso stood in front of a closed elementary school in Taebaek, holding her father’s metal box.
상자 안에는 낡고 바랜 종이들이 들어 있었고, 그 위에는 ‘T.O.B.I. 프로젝트’라는 제목이 적혀 있었죠.
Inside were worn, faded papers titled “T.O.B.I. Project.”
미소는 상자의 내용을 이해할 수는 없었지만, 무언가 중요한 것임을 직감했어요.
Miso didn’t fully understand the contents, but she sensed it was something important.
호기심에 이끌린 그녀는 폐광의 녹슨 문을 밀고 안으로 들어갔습니다.
Driven by curiosity, she pushed open the rusty gate of the abandoned mine and stepped inside.
갑자기, 파란 눈을 가진 네 발의 로봇이 나타났습니다. 그 로봇의 이름은 점프였습니다.
Suddenly, a four-legged robot with glowing blue eyes appeared. Its name was Jump.
점프는 미소를 깊은 지하로 이끌었고, 오래된 리프트는 400미터 아래까지 그녀를 데려갔습니다.
Jump led Miso deep underground, and the old lift descended to 400 meters below the surface.
지하 공간은 어둡고 습했으며, 오래된 기계와 철 파이프들이 놓여 있었죠.
The underground chamber was dark and humid, filled with rusted machinery and steel pipes.
그곳에서 또 다른 로봇이 깨어났습니다. 이름은 로보였습니다.
There, another robot awakened. Its name was Robo.
로보는 이 폐광이 데이터센터로 활용될 수 있으며, 마을 전체에 난방을 제공할 수 있다고 설명했습니다.
Robo explained that the abandoned mine could be used as a data center and provide heating to the entire village.
미소는 놀라움과 희망이 섞인 감정으로 말했어요. “우리가 이 마을을 다시 살릴 수 있어요.”
Miso, filled with both surprise and hope, said, “We can bring this village back to life.”

📘 Vocabulary

  • elementary school: a school for young children
  • abandoned: left without care or attention
  • sense: to feel or understand something without proof
  • rusty: covered with rust
  • descend: to go down
  • chamber: an enclosed space
  • humid: with a lot of moisture in the air
  • provide: to give or supply
  • awaken: to wake up or cause to wake

🧠 Reading Comprehension

  1. What is inside the metal box? → Faded papers about the T.O.B.I. Project ✅
  2. Where does Jump take Miso? → 400 meters underground ✅
  3. What does Robo say about the mine? → It can be a data center and provide heating ✅
  4. How does Miso feel in the end? → Hopeful and inspired ✅

💬 Speaking Practice

  • Repeat: “We can bring this village back to life.” (x3)
  • Ask your partner: “Why was the mine important?” → “Because it could help the town.”
  • Mini Dialogue:

Jump: Follow me, Miso.
Miso: Where are we going?
Jump: Deep into the mine.
Miso: What will we find?
Robo: A new future for the village.

🎧 Listening Script

  1. Miso discovers an old metal box in front of a closed school.
  2. She reads the title: “T.O.B.I. Project.”
  3. A robot named Jump appears and leads her underground.
  4. Robo explains the potential of the mine to help the village.
  5. Miso realizes they can rebuild the town.
