지문 읽기 READING

Many young people have big dreams for their future. They imagine becoming successful in their chosen careers. These dreams give them hope and motivation every day.

Sometimes, society does not encourage certain dreams. People might say some goals are too difficult to reach. This can make young dreamers feel sad or uncertain.

However, strong passion can overcome these challenges. Believing in yourself is very important for success. Your own determination is the key to achieving your goals.

Young individuals often possess mysterious and curious ambitions about their future paths. They secretly imagine discovering their true talents and observing the world with fresh eyes. These unusual dreams describe a hopeful vision that motivates their daily actions.

Occasionally, the surrounding society fails to realize the potential of such aspirations. Curious minds might face unexpected obstacles that challenge their confidence. This situation can create a puzzling conflict between personal desire and external expectation.

Nevertheless, determined people can overcome these mysterious barriers through persistent effort. They gradually discover that their own belief is the most powerful force. Observing successful examples helps them realize that ambitious goals are achievable.

문법 GRAMMAR

G32이번 주 문법 포인트
Past Perfect Continuous
과거완료진행형 — had been + -ing
과거의 특정 시점보다 더 일찍 시작되어 그 시점까지 계속 진행되었던 동작을 나타냅니다. '~해오고 있었다'는 의미로, 과거의 배경 상황을 설명할 때 사용합니다.
📝 예문 3개 (클릭해서 듣기)
She had been writing stories for three years before she published her first book. 🔊
그녀는 첫 책을 출판하기 전까지 3년 동안 이야기를 써오고 있었습니다.
They had been dreaming about success since they were young teenagers. 🔊
그들은 젊은 십대 시절부터 성공에 대해 꿈꾸어오고 있었습니다.
He had been practicing every day until he finally achieved his goal. 🔊
그는 마침내 목표를 달성할 때까지 매일 연습해오고 있었습니다.
