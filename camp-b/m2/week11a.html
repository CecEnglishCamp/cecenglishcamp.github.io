오늘의 본문 READING

The quiet town of Millfield has faced a significant challenge recently. A series of mysterious thefts has troubled the local community for months. The police have investigated the case, but they could not determine the criminal. Everyone wondered who could be responsible for these strange events. The situation turned out to be more complex than anyone had imagined. The person who committed the crimes was someone everyone trusted completely. This discovery has genuinely surprised all the residents of the small town. The real criminal was the town librarian, Mr. Evans, who always seemed so kind. He had established a perfect alibi for every single theft that occurred. People are now questioning how well they truly know their neighbors. The consequence of this betrayal has shaken the community's sense of security. It makes you wonder about the secrets people might hide behind a friendly smile. The case has established a new sense of caution among the townspeople. They have learned that appearances can be very misleading in daily life. The entire experience, which nobody expected, has changed Millfield forever.

In the coastal village of Seagate, a perplexing mystery has unfolded over the past year. Several valuable artifacts have disappeared from the local history museum's secure collection. Authorities have conducted a thorough investigation, yet they failed to establish any solid leads. The community, which valued its historical heritage deeply, felt a growing sense of violation. Having exhausted all conventional methods, the detectives decided to review the security footage again. This careful re-examination revealed a shocking truth that nobody had anticipated. The real criminal turned out to be the head curator, Dr. Anna Bell, whom the entire town council had deeply trusted. She had exploited her exclusive access and impeccable reputation to execute the thefts. This revelation, which contradicted all initial assumptions, created a profound sense of betrayal. The consequence of her actions was a complete erosion of public trust in the institution. Determining her motive became the next significant challenge for the investigation team. It appears she intended to sell the items to fund a personal project overseas. The entire affair has demonstrated how effectively people can conceal their true intentions. The village is now struggling to recover from this breach of faith by a respected figure.

에세이 기초 배우기 ESSAY BASICS

✍️
에세이 잘 쓰는 5가지 요령
Good Essay Writing Tips
1
명확한 주제문 (Topic Sentence)
첫 문장에 내 주장을 한 번에! 모호하면 읽는 사람이 혼란스러워요.
❌ "Beauty is important." ✅ "Beauty without morality leads to corruption."
2
구체적 근거 + 예시
"Because" 다음엔 반드시 구체적인 예시가 따라와야 해요.
❌ "Because it is bad." ✅ "For example, the story shows how..."
3
반대 의견 인정하기
반대 의견을 먼저 인정하면 내 주장이 더 설득력 있어 보여요.
✅ "Although some argue that..., I believe..."
4
연결어 (Transition Words)
단락과 단락을 자연스럽게 이어주는 다리 역할을 해요.
First · Furthermore · However · Therefore · In conclusion
5
강한 마무리 (Concluding Sentence)
마지막 문장이 가장 오래 기억돼요. 핵심 메시지를 새로운 표현으로!
✅ "Therefore, it is clear that..." / "This is why..."
서론
In my opinion...
I strongly believe...
It is clear that...
본론
For instance...
This shows that...
Furthermore...
결론

ESSAY란?
에세이는 내 생각을 논리적으로 쓰는 글이에요. 처음에는 3문장부터 시작해요!
📌 3단락 구조
① 주제문 (Topic Sentence) — 나의 의견을 한 문장으로
예: "I think a good detective needs patience."

② 근거 · 예시 (Supporting) — 왜 그렇게 생각하는지
예: "For example, Holmes always observes carefully."

③ 마무리 (Concluding) — 결론 한 문장
예: "That is why patience is important."
📖 이번 주 샘플 에세이 (읽기용)
Topic: If I could travel in time, I would go to ___ because ___
① I think I would go to the future to see amazing new inventions.
② For example, I want to see flying cars and robots that help people with their work.
③ That is why the future seems more exciting to me than the past.
