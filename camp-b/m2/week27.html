지문 읽기 READING

A young explorer named Leo had always dreamed of finding something special. He studied old maps and read many adventure books for years. Finally, he decided to search for a legendary treasure on a distant island. Leo prepared his boat and supplies for a long journey across the ocean.

After two weeks of sailing, he arrived at the mysterious island described in the stories. He walked through thick forests and climbed steep hills for several days. Suddenly, he noticed something unusual behind a large waterfall. His heart beat fast as he moved closer to investigate the strange shape.

Behind the falling water, Leo discovered an ancient wooden chest covered in dust. He carefully opened the heavy lid and saw gold coins shining in the dark cave. The treasure was real, and his life would change completely because of this amazing find. He felt both excited and nervous about his future.

Throughout history, people have been fascinated by stories of hidden riches waiting to be discovered. These tales often describe courageous individuals who follow mysterious clues to unusual locations. They face numerous challenges and dangers during their difficult journeys. However, the possibility of finding something valuable keeps them curious and determined to continue searching.

Modern treasure hunters use advanced technology to observe areas that might contain lost artifacts. They analyze old documents and study geological formations very carefully. Sometimes they discover objects that help us understand past civilizations better. These findings can be historically significant, even if they are not made of gold or jewels.

Finding real treasure requires patience, research, and sometimes luck. When explorers finally locate something valuable, they often realize it is just the beginning. They must decide what to do with their discovery and how it will affect their lives. The true treasure might be the adventure itself, not just the physical objects they find.

문법 GRAMMAR

G27이번 주 문법 포인트
Past Perfect
과거완료 — had + p.p.
과거완료는 과거의 특정 시점보다 더 일찍 일어난 일을 나타낼 때 사용합니다. 'had + 과거분사' 형태로, 과거 두 사건의 시간 순서를 명확히 할 때 중요해요.
