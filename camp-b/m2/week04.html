지문 읽기 READING

The survivor moved through streets filled with smoke and broken glass. He saw buildings that had collapsed into piles of rubble. People were running in every direction, trying to find safety from the danger. Their faces showed pure fear and confusion as they searched for escape routes.

He remembered his own home, now just a memory in the flames. The heat from the fires made the air difficult to breathe. He kept moving forward, one step at a time, not knowing his final destination. Survival was his only clear thought during those terrible moments.

Finally, he reached the edge of the city where the destruction lessened. He looked back at the dark smoke rising into the sky. A deep feeling of loss washed over him, but also a small hope for the future. He knew the journey ahead would be long and very challenging.

Navigating the devastated urban landscape required constant vigilance and quick decisions. The narrator observed collapsed structures blocking familiar pathways, forcing detours through hazardous zones filled with unusual debris. Each corner presented potential threats, making progress slow and mentally exhausting as he analyzed every shadow for movement.

Witnessing the scale of destruction provoked profound questions about civilization's fragility. He realized how quickly order could dissolve into chaos, transforming peaceful communities into scenes of desperate survival. The mysterious absence of organized resistance deepened his anxiety about what powerful force could cause such systematic ruin.

Despite the overwhelming despair, curious details offered glimpses of persistent humanity. A single untouched flower growing through cracked concrete, distant voices calling names with determined hope—these small observations suggested resilience. He began to describe his experience not just as escape, but as a painful discovery of both vulnerability and unexpected strength within survivors.
