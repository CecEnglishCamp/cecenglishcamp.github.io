📌 초급(B1) = 이 학년의 평균 수준. 입문(A2)은 더 쉽게, 중급(B2)은 더 어렵게 조정된 버전이에요.

The Martians used a new weapon.
It was called the black smoke.
The smoke was very dangerous.
Anyone who breathed it died.
Streets became empty and silent.
The narrator hid in an old well.
He covered his face with wet cloth.
He stayed there for two days.

After the Heat Ray, the Martians used a new and terrible weapon. It was a black smoke that rolled slowly along the ground.

Any person who breathed the smoke was killed immediately. Streets that had been crowded with people were suddenly empty and silent.

The narrator had found an old well near an abandoned house. He climbed inside and covered his face with a wet cloth.

For two days, he hid there while the smoke passed above him. When the air had cleared, he climbed out carefully. The village was completely silent.

The black smoke was worse than the Heat Ray in one way: it was patient.

The Heat Ray was quick — a flash, and then silence. The black smoke moved at walking pace, curling around corners, filling low places first, then rising. It had time. It was in no hurry.

The narrator had found the well by chance. He had been looking for water when his foot found the edge, and he had lowered himself in before he fully understood why.

For two days he waited. He could hear the smoke above him — not a sound exactly, but an absence of sound. No birds. No wind. No human voices.

On the third morning the birds came back. He took that as a sign and climbed out.

※ 번역은 초급 B1 기준
열 광선 이후, 화성인들은 새로운 무기를 사용했어요. 그것은 땅을 따라 천천히 굴러다니는 검은 연기였어요.

연기를 마신 사람은 즉시 죽었어요. 사람들로 붐비던 거리가 갑자기 텅 비고 조용해졌어요.

화자는 버려진 집 근처의 오래된 우물을 발견했어요. 안에 들어가 젖은 천으로 얼굴을 덮었어요. 이틀 동안 연기가 지나가는 동안 숨어 있었어요.
