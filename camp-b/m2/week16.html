지문 읽기 READING

Robinson had been alone on the island for many years before he saw the footprints. He felt both excited and scared about meeting another person. The footprints meant he was not completely alone anymore.

One day, Robinson saw a group of men attacking a single man on the beach. He decided to help the man who was in serious danger. Robinson used his gun to scare the attackers away and saved the man.

Robinson named the man Friday because he met him on that day. They slowly learned to communicate with simple words and gestures. A real friendship grew between them despite their different languages and cultures.

For over two decades, Robinson Crusoe's existence was defined by profound solitude and self-reliance. His discovery of a mysterious footprint on the shore ignited a curious mixture of hope and anxiety within him. This unusual sign suggested his long isolation might finally be coming to an end.

He soon observed a violent scene where cannibals were preparing to execute a prisoner. Realizing the man's desperate situation, Robinson intervened courageously with his firearms. He successfully rescued the brave captive from almost certain death on that fateful Friday.

Robinson decided to call his new companion Friday to commemorate their first meeting. They began a slow process to discover how to communicate across their language barrier. Their growing friendship demonstrated how people can build trust and understanding despite significant differences.

문법 GRAMMAR

G16이번 주 문법 포인트
Past Perfect
과거완료 — had + p.p.
과거완료는 과거의 특정 시점보다 더 이전에 일어난 일을 나타낼 때 사용합니다. 'had + 과거분사' 형태로, 과거 두 사건 중 먼저 일어난 사건을 설명할 때 중요합니다.
📝 예문 3개 (클릭해서 듣기)
Robinson had lived alone for years before he met Friday. 🔊
로빈슨은 프라이데이를 만나기 전에 여러 해 동안 혼자 살았었다.
Friday had been in serious danger before Robinson rescued him. 🔊
프라이데이는 로빈슨이 그를 구하기 전에 심각한 위험에 처해 있었다.
They had never seen each other before that fateful Friday. 🔊
그들은 그 운명적인 금요일 전에는 서로를 본 적이 없었다.
