지문 읽기 READING

Robinson had been alone on the island for many years before he saw the footprints. He felt both fear and excitement when he discovered them on the beach. The footprints meant that he was not completely alone on that island.

He watched from a hidden place and saw a group of people arrive by canoe. They were doing something terrible to a prisoner on the sand. Robinson knew he had to help the man who was in serious danger right then.

He scared the attackers away with his gun and rescued the frightened man. They could not speak the same language at first. Robinson decided to call his new friend Friday, and they slowly learned to trust each other.

For years, Robinson Crusoe observed only his own footprints on the lonely shore, a silent reminder of his isolation. One morning, he discovered a mysterious and unusual mark in the sand that was clearly not his own. This curious discovery filled him with a strange mix of fear and a desperate hope for human contact after so much time alone.

Later, from his hidden position, he realized a terrifying scene was unfolding on the beach. He described the event later as a moment of serious danger for one brave man surrounded by others. Without hesitation, Robinson decided to act, using his weapon to scare the attackers and rescue the stranger from immediate harm.

The two men, from completely different worlds, slowly began to communicate through gestures and simple words. They built a real friendship by observing each other's habits and working together daily. This unusual partnership across a huge cultural difference became the most valuable discovery of Robinson's long island survival.

문법 GRAMMAR

G16이번 주 문법 포인트
Past Perfect
과거완료 — had + p.p.
과거의 특정 시점보다 더 이전에 일어난 일을 나타낼 때 써요. '~했었다'는 의미로, 두 가지 과거 사건의 순서를 명확히 할 수 있어요.
📝 예문 3개 (클릭해서 듣기)
Robinson had lived alone for years before he met Friday. 🔊
로빈슨은 프라이데이를 만나기 전에 여러 해 동안 혼자 살았었다.
He had never seen another person on the island until that day. 🔊
그는 그날까지 섬에서 다른 사람을 본 적이 없었다.
The attackers had already run away when Friday looked up. 🔊
프라이데이가 고개를 들었을 때, 공격자들은 이미 도망갔었다.
