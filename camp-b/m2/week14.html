지문 읽기 READING

The old house stood alone on the hill. Its windows were dark and covered in dust. No one had lived there for many years. The garden was wild with tall grass and weeds. A strange feeling filled the air around the place.

A curious boy decided to explore the house one afternoon. He pushed open the heavy wooden door slowly. Inside, everything was covered in thick gray dust. The furniture looked very old and forgotten. Time seemed to have stopped completely in every room.

In the dining room, he saw something very unusual. A table was set for a meal that never happened. The plates still held food that had turned black long ago. It was as if the people had just disappeared during dinner. The boy left quickly, feeling both excited and afraid.

The mysterious mansion appeared frozen in a forgotten moment. Curious visitors would occasionally approach its iron gates, observing the silent building from a distance. They discovered that all visible clocks inside had stopped at the same unusual time. This strange detail made people realize something tragic had occurred there years before.

Upon entering, one could describe the atmosphere as heavy with suspended time. Dust particles floated in sunlight streaming through broken curtains. Furniture remained perfectly arranged, yet everything showed signs of long abandonment. An observer might notice place settings on the dining table, complete with decayed meals.

The most haunting discovery was in the grand hall. There, faded photographs showed a family celebration that never concluded. Personal belongings lay exactly where owners had left them decades earlier. This house became a perfect example of time captured unwillingly, preserving both objects and memories in perpetual stillness.

문법 GRAMMAR

G14이번 주 문법 포인트
Past Perfect
과거완료 — had + 과거분사
과거완료는 과거의 특정 시점보다 더 이전에 일어난 일을 나타낼 때 사용합니다. 'had + 동사의 과거분사(p.p.)' 형태로, 과거에 이미 완료된 행동을 설명합니다.
📝 예문 3개 (클릭해서 듣기)
The family had left the house before the storm began. 🔊
가족들은 폭풍이 시작되기 전에 집을 떠났었다.
She realized someone had visited the old mansion recently. 🔊
그녀는 누군가가 최근에 그 오래된 저택을 방문했었다는 것을 깨달았다.
All the clocks had stopped working years before we found them. 🔊
모든 시계는 우리가 그것들을 발견하기 몇 년 전에 작동을 멈춘 상태였다.

에세이 단락쓰기 PARAGRAPH WRITING

✍️ Describe what you imagine happened in a mysterious abandoned house.
신비한 버려진 집에서 무슨 일이 일어났을지 상상하여 묘사해 보세요.
① 주제문 (Topic Sentence)
'I imagine that...'로 시작하여 집에서 일어났을 일에 대한 너의 생각을 한 문장으로 써 보세요.
② 근거 · 예시 (Supporting)
'For example,'이나 'This is because...'를 사용하여 너의 생각을 뒷받침하는 구체적인 이유나 예를 들어 보세요.
③ 마무리 (Concluding)
'Therefore,'나 'That is why...'로 시작하여 너의 설명을 마무리하는 문장을 써 보세요.
