지문 읽기 READING

Revenge often seems sweet when you first plan it. You imagine justice and satisfaction after long suffering. However, the reality can be completely different from your dreams.

When someone seeks revenge, they focus only on their target. They do not consider other people around that person. Friends and family members can get hurt in the process.

In the end, the person seeking revenge might feel empty. They realize they have become like their enemy. The cost of revenge is sometimes too high to pay.

The desire for revenge can consume a person completely, transforming their original pain into a burning obsession. They carefully observe their enemy's life, planning each step to cause maximum suffering. However, this mysterious journey often leads to unexpected discoveries about themselves.

As they execute their plan, they might realize that innocent people connected to their target are also affected. These collateral consequences are frequently terrible and difficult to describe. The avenger begins to question their own actions and motives.

Ultimately, they may find the victory hollow and unsatisfying. The unusual cost of their revenge becomes clear only after everything is finished. What they lost in the process often outweighs what they gained.

문법 GRAMMAR

G29이번 주 문법 포인트
Past Perfect
과거완료 — had + 과거분사
과거의 특정 시점보다 더 이전에 일어난 일을 나타낼 때 사용합니다. '~했었다'라는 의미로, 과거의 두 사건 중 먼저 일어난 사건을 설명할 때 중요합니다.
📝 예문 3개 (클릭해서 듣기)
He had planned his revenge for years before he finally acted. 🔊
그는 결국 행동하기 전에 몇 년 동안 복수를 계획했었다.
She realized she had hurt innocent people in her quest for justice. 🔊
그녀는 정의를 찾는 과정에서 무고한 사람들을 해쳤었다는 것을 깨달았다.
They had not considered the consequences before starting their plan. 🔊
그들은 계획을 시작하기 전에 결과를 고려하지 않았었다.
