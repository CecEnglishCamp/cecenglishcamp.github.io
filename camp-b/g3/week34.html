2

지문 읽기 READING

Samuel Beckett fought in the French Resistance during World War II

He hid Jewish friends

He went on the run from the Gestapo

After the war, he wrote in French

Waiting for Godot was written in 1948

Writing in French was liberating — it forced him to write only what was essential.

The post-war context is inseparable from the play's meaning

The bare landscape is also postwar Europe: exhausted, stripped of certainty, unable to move forward

The play is saturated with the war's aftermath: the numbness, the waiting for some authority to give meaning, and the recognition that no such authority will come.

토론 주제 DEBATE

"Art from extreme historical trauma has a moral authority peacetime art cannot achieve."
극단적인 역사적 트라우마에서 나온 예술은 평화로운 시기의 예술이 달성할 수 없는 도덕적 권위를 가진다.
✅ 찬성
  • George and Lennie's dream is destroyed by forces beyond their control
  • The Depression proves the Dream is inaccessible to the poor
  • Candy, Crooks, and Curley's wife all have crushed dreams
❌ 반대
  • The dream gives George and Lennie purpose
  • Without aspiration human life loses meaning
  • Some people do achieve the Dream through perseverance
