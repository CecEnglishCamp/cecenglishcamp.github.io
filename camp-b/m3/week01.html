이야기 읽기
🔊 듣기는 초급 B1 기준으로 읽어드려요.
📌 초급(B1) = 이 학년의 평균 영어 수준. 입문(A2)은 더 쉽게, 중급(B2)은 더 어렵게 조정된 버전이에요.

Sherlock Holmes was a very smart detective.
He lived in London at 221B Baker Street.
His friend was Dr. Watson.
Holmes could solve any mystery.
One day a woman came to visit.
She had a strange problem.
"Please help me," she said.
Holmes smiled. "Tell me everything."

Sherlock Holmes sat in his armchair at 221B Baker Street, his long fingers pressed together. Opposite him, Dr. Watson was reading the morning paper.

A young woman entered. She looked frightened, her hands trembling. Holmes had already observed three things about her before she spoke a word.

"You have come from the country," he said. "You travelled by train this morning. And you are in some kind of danger."

The woman stared. "How could you possibly know that?"

"Elementary," Holmes replied. "The mud on your boots, the return ticket in your pocket, and the fear in your eyes."

"Elementary, my dear Watson." Holmes delivered the words with the calm certainty of a man who has already solved the puzzle while others are still reading the question.

The young woman, Irene Adler, sat across from him in the Baker Street flat, her composure admirable given the circumstances. She had come on a matter she could trust to no one else — which was, Holmes reflected, precisely the kind of case worth taking.

Watson watched his friend with familiar amazement. Holmes had deduced her profession, her recent travel, and her current financial difficulty within thirty seconds of her entering the room — and all without asking a single question.

"How do you do it?" Watson asked later.

"I observe," said Holmes. "You see, Watson, but you do not observe. The difference is everything."

※ 번역은 초급 B1 기준
"기초적인 일이죠, 왓슨." 홈즈는 다른 사람들이 아직 문제를 읽고 있는 동안 이미 퍼즐을 풀어버린 사람의 침착한 확신으로 그 말을 했어요.

젊은 여성 아이린 애들러가 그의 맞은편 베이커 스트리트 아파트에 앉아 있었어요. 그녀는 상황을 고려하면 놀라울 정도로 침착했어요. 다른 누구에게도 믿길 수 없는 문제로 왔어요.

왓슨은 익숙한 놀라움으로 친구를 지켜봤어요. 홈즈는 그녀가 방에 들어온 지 30초 만에 직업, 최근 여행, 현재 재정적 어려움을 추론했어요 — 그것도 단 하나의 질문도 없이.
"어떻게 하시는 거죠?" 왓슨이 나중에 물었어요.
"관찰하는 거요," 홈즈가 말했어요. "왓슨, 당신은 보지만 관찰하지 않아요. 그 차이가 전부예요.
