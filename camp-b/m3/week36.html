지문 읽기 READING

The young soldier stood on the hill overlooking his hometown, but he no longer recognized the person who had left months before. His hands, once soft and unmarked, now bore the permanent scars of conflict and survival. The boy who had dreamed of heroic charges had vanished completely during the long nights in the trenches.

He understood now that courage was not the absence of fear but the determination to act despite it. Maturity had arrived not through age but through witnessing the complex reality of human struggle. The simplistic stories of glory he once believed had been replaced by a deeper, more painful truth.

Returning home felt like entering a different world where ordinary concerns seemed strangely distant. His family welcomed him warmly, yet they could not comprehend the transformation within him. He carried a quiet wisdom that separated him from his former self, a wisdom earned through loss and reflection.

Upon his return from the harrowing campaign, the veteran found himself fundamentally estranged from the youthful ideals he had once cherished with such fervor. The illusions of pristine valor and unambiguous heroism, which had propelled him toward enlistment, had been irrevocably shattered by the chaotic brutality he had witnessed firsthand. What remained was a more complex, albeit somber, understanding of human nature under extreme duress.

This hard-won maturity manifested not as cynicism but as a nuanced appreciation for quiet resilience and small acts of compassion amidst devastation. He had learned that true character is often revealed not in moments of celebrated triumph but during prolonged periods of fear and exhaustion. The boy who sought external validation through medals had been replaced by a man who valued internal integrity above all else.

Consequently, his interactions with his community were now filtered through this altered perspective, making casual conversations about mundane topics feel almost surreal. He possessed a gravity that his peers could sense but not fully decipher, a silent testament to experiences that had forged a different person. This transformation, while isolating, represented the most authentic education he had ever received.

5
강한 마무리

마지막 문장이 가장 중요! 핵심 메시지를 다시 강조하세요

💬 유용한 표현
서론: "In my opinion... / I strongly believe..."
본론: "For instance... / This shows that..."
결론: "In conclusion... / Therefore..."
✍️ True maturity is gained more through enduring hardship and failure than through success and comfort.
진정한 성숙은 성공과 안락함보다 고난과 실패를 견디는 것을 통해 더 많이 얻어진다.
📐 3단락 구조: 서론 → 본론 → 결론

서론: 흥미로운 문장으로 시작하세요 (예: 'Many people believe that success is the ultimate teacher.'). 주제(성숙)를 소개하고, 마지막으로 자신의 논지(Thesis Statement)를 명확히 제시하세요 (예: 'However, I firmly argue that...').

본론: 첫 번째 단락에서는 고난이 자기인식을 어떻게 깊게 하는지 논거를 펴고 구체적 예시를 들어보세요 (예: 'For instance, when a person fails...'). 두 번째 단락에서는 안락함이 가져올 수 있는 성장의 정체에 대해 설명하세요. 각 논거 뒤에는 'This shows that...'와 같이 근거를 설명하는 문장을 추가하세요.

결론: 주요 논지를 다른 표현으로 재진술하세요 (예: 'In conclusion, while success is pleasant...'). 글의 주요 논점을 요약하고, 독자에게 생각할 거리를 주는 강력한 문장으로 마무리하세요 (예: 'Therefore, we should not fear challenges...').
