이야기 읽기
🔊 듣기는 초급 B1 기준으로 읽어드려요.
📌 초급(B1) = 이 학년의 평균 수준. 입문(A2)은 더 쉽게, 중급(B2)은 더 어렵게 조정된 버전이에요.

A man with red hair came to Holmes.

Then the office was suddenly empty.
Wilson did not understand why.

A large, red-faced man came to Baker Street, holding a newspaper advertisement in his shaking hand. His name was Jabez Wilson, whose bright red hair was hard to miss.

He told Holmes about a strange organization called the Red-Headed League, which paid well for simple work. The advertisement, which had appeared suddenly, promised four pounds a week.

The only condition was that the applicant had to have red hair, which seemed completely ridiculous. Wilson had got the job, spending his days copying out an encyclopedia.

Then one morning, he arrived to find the office empty and the League dissolved. Holmes, whose face showed increasing interest, asked Wilson dozens of careful questions.

Holmes had heard stranger things. But what interested him was not the Red-Headed League itself — that was clearly nonsense. What interested him was the assistant.

Wilson had a young assistant named Spaulding who had encouraged him to apply. Spaulding, Wilson said, was the one who had first spotted the advertisement. Spaulding had even offered to manage the shop alone while Wilson was away.

Holmes asked one question that Wilson thought was strange: what did Spaulding's knees look like?

Very dirty, Wilson said. Always. As though he spent a great deal of time on them.

Holmes said nothing for a moment. Then: "I think, Watson, that we shall need to visit a bank tonight."

그는 붉은 머리 연맹이라는 이상한 조직에 대해 이야기했어요. 광고는 주 4파운드를 약속했어요. 유일한 조건은 빨간 머리카락이 있어야 한다는 것이었어요.

윌슨은 매일 백과사전을 필사했어요. 어느 날 아침, 사무실이 텅 비어 있었어요. 홈즈는 점점 흥미를 보이며 질문했어요.
