이야기 읽기
🔊 듣기는 초급 B1 기준으로 읽어드려요.
📌 초급(B1) = 이 학년의 평균 수준. 입문(A2)은 더 쉽게, 중급(B2)은 더 어렵게 조정된 버전이에요.

A man came to Holmes with a problem.
He was the King of Bohemia.
He had a secret photograph.
A woman named Irene Adler had it.
If people saw it, he would be in trouble.
He needed Holmes to get it back.
"She is clever," said the King.
Holmes accepted the case.

The client arrived in disguise — a tall man in a dark cloak. But Holmes had already guessed who he was before he spoke a word.

"You are the King of Bohemia," Holmes said. The man was shocked but confirmed it.

The problem was a photograph. Irene Adler, an opera singer, possessed a photograph of herself with the King. If it was shown to the wrong people, his planned royal marriage would be ruined.

"She is clever," said the King. "More clever than any woman I have known."

Holmes took the case. He had already noticed something — the King spoke of Irene Adler with admiration, not hatred.

Holmes did not take cases for money. He took them for interest, and this one had plenty. A reigning monarch, a compromising photograph, a woman described as the most clever in Europe.

He listened to the King with careful neutrality. The photograph existed. Irene Adler had it. She had refused all offers. She would use it if the marriage proceeded.

"To Sherlock Holmes, she is always the woman," Watson later wrote — meaning not that Holmes loved her, but that she was the only person who had ever beaten him. That distinction was worth more than admiration.

Holmes stood. "I will have a solution within three days," he said. And for the first time, he was genuinely uncertain.

※ 번역은 초급 B1 기준
홈즈는 돈을 위해 사건을 맡지 않았어요. 흥미를 위해 맡았고, 이 사건에는 충분했어요. 재위 중인 군주, 위험한 사진, 유럽에서 가장 영리한 여인.

사진은 존재했어요. 아이린 애들러가 가지고 있었어요. 모든 제안을 거절했어요.

"홈즈에게 그녀는 언제나 그 여인이에요," 왓슨이 나중에 썼어요 — 홈즈가 그녀를 사랑했다는 뜻이 아니라, 그녀가 그를 이긴 유일한 사람이었다는 뜻이에요.
홈즈는 일어섰어요. "3일 안에 해결책을 찾겠습니다." 처음으로 진심으로 불확실했어요.
