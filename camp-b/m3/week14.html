The Picture of Dorian Gray

로드 헨리의 영향 — Oscar Wilde · B2

1

핵심 표현 KEY EXPRESSION

Nowadays people know the price of everything and the value of nothing. 🔊
요즘 사람들은 모든 것의 가격은 알지만 가치는 모른다.
2

지문 읽기 READING

Lord Henry is clever and witty. He tells Dorian that youth and beauty are the only things worth having. Pleasure is the highest goal. Dorian listens and changes. He begins to chase pleasure at any cost.

Lord Henry functions as the novel's most dangerous character precisely because he never acts on his own philosophy. He corrupts Dorian through words alone, remaining aesthetically detached while his protege suffers real consequences.

5

토론 주제 DEBATE TOPIC

💬 "Ideas are more dangerous than actions."
아이디어는 행동보다 더 위험하다.
✅ 찬성 (Agree)
  • Lord Henry's words destroy Dorian without a crime
  • Revolutionary ideas have killed more than individual acts
  • Once an idea takes root it cannot be recalled
❌ 반대 (Disagree)
  • Ideas require human agency to become destructive
  • Dorian bears responsibility for his choices
  • The same ideas can inspire both good and evil
