📌 초급(B1) = 이 학년의 평균 수준. 입문(A2)은 더 쉽게, 중급(B2)은 더 어렵게 조정된 버전이에요.

One night a big storm came.
The river moved very fast.
The raft shook from side to side.
Huck and Jim held on tight.
Jim pulled Huck back to safety.
After the storm, they did not know where they were.
They saw a small town on the shore.

One night, a strong storm came without warning. The rain fell hard and the wind shook the trees on the shore.

The river moved fast and pushed the raft from side to side. Huck held on tight and shouted to Jim over the noise.

Jim grabbed a rope and pulled Huck back onto the raft. They were both very scared, but they did not give up.

When morning came, they did not know where they were. They saw a small town on the bank of the river. "Should we stop?" Huck asked. "No," said Jim. "It is too dangerous. We must keep moving."

The storm arrived without warning, as storms on the Mississippi often did.

One moment the river was smooth and still. The next, the sky cracked open and the rain came down in sheets. The raft, which had felt solid an hour before, suddenly seemed very small.

Huck grabbed the side and held on. A wave hit the raft and swept him toward the edge. He felt himself slipping.

"Hold on!" Jim shouted, throwing a rope.

Huck caught it. His hands were shaking.

For an hour, they fought the storm together — silent, frightened, determined. And then, as suddenly as it had begun, it stopped.

In the grey morning light, they looked around at an unfamiliar river. A small town sat on the western bank.

"Should we ask for help?" Huck said.

Jim was quiet for a moment. "No," he said at last. "We must keep moving. A town means people. People means questions."

헉은 옆을 잡고 버텼어요. 파도가 뗏목을 치며 그를 가장자리로 밀었어요. 미끄러지는 걸 느꼈어요.
"잡아!" 짐이 로프를 던지며 소리쳤어요. 헉이 잡았어요. 손이 떨렸어요.

한 시간 동안 그들은 함께 폭풍과 싸웠어요 — 조용히, 두려워하며, 결연하게. 그리고 시작할 때처럼 갑자기 멈췄어요.

"도움을 요청할까요?" 헉이 말했어요. "아니," 짐이 마침내 말했어요. "계속 가야 해. 마을은 사람들을 의미하고, 사람들은 질문을 의미해."
