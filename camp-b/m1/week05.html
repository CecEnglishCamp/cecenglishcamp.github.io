이야기 읽기

📌 초급(B1) = 이 학년의 평균 수준. 입문(A2)은 더 쉽게, 중급(B2)은 더 어렵게 조정된 버전이에요.

Huck had a big problem in his heart. He knew that helping Jim escape was against the law. People said that he should report Jim to the police.

He picked up a pen and started to write a letter. The letter would tell people where Jim was hiding.

But then Huck stopped and put the pen down. He thought about all the good things Jim had done for him. Jim was always kind, always honest, and always there.

"I cannot do this," Huck said to himself. He tore the letter into small pieces. "I will go to the bad place if I must," he said quietly. Jim was his friend, and friends must help each other.

It was the hardest thing Huck had ever had to decide.

He sat alone with a blank piece of paper and a pen. He was going to write the letter. He was going to do the right thing — the thing his society had taught him was right.

He started to write. The pen moved across the paper slowly.

But then he stopped.

He sat there for a long time, thinking about Jim. He thought about the nights on the raft, the stories, the stars, the time Jim threw the rope in the storm. He thought about Jim's wife and children waiting somewhere, hoping.

Huck looked at the letter. Then he tore it in two.

"All right, then, I'll go to hell," he said quietly.

It sounds dramatic. But really, it was a boy choosing one thing over another: what he had been told was right — and what he actually knew was right. They were not the same.

※ 번역은 초급 B1 기준
그것은 헉이 결정해야 했던 것 중 가장 어려운 일이었어요.

그는 혼자 빈 종이와 펜을 앞에 두고 앉았어요. 편지를 쓰려고 했어요. 올바른 일을 하려고 — 그의 사회가 옳다고 가르쳐준 일을.

쓰기 시작했어요. 펜이 천천히 종이 위를 움직였어요. 그런데 멈췄어요.

짐에 대해 생각하면서 오랫동안 앉아 있었어요. 뗏목 위의 밤들, 이야기들, 별들, 폭풍 속에서 짐이 로프를 던졌을 때. 어딘가에서 기다리며 바라고 있는 짐의 아내와 아이들에 대해 생각했어요.

헉은 편지를 바라봤어요. 그리고 둘로 찢었어요.
"좋아, 그럼 나는 나쁜 곳에 가겠어," 그는 조용히 말했어요.

극적으로 들려요. 하지만 사실 그것은 한 소년이 하나를 다른 것보다 선택하는 것이었어요: 옳다고 들어왔던 것 — 그리고 실제로 옳다고 알고 있는 것.
