2

지문 읽기 READING

Beth gets very sick. The whole family is worried. Jo stays home to take care of her. Meg and Jo give up their Christmas money to help the family. Marmee comes home from helping the poor. The sisters learn about sacrifice and love.

Beth's illness becomes the crucible through which the March sisters' characters are tested. Jo's fierce devotion to her gentle sister reveals the depth of her capacity for selfless love, temporarily suppressing her ambitious nature in service of family duty.

토론 주제 DEBATE TOPIC

💬 "Family duty should come before personal ambition."
가족에 대한 의무는 개인적인 야망보다 우선시되어야 한다.
✅ 찬성 (Agree)
  • Jo gives up her writing career to care for Beth
  • The March family's strength comes from their devotion to each other
  • Without family bonds, individual success means nothing
❌ 반대 (Disagree)
  • Sacrificing personal dreams leads to long-term resentment
  • Jo's ambition ultimately benefits her family financially
  • True love means supporting each other's individual goals
