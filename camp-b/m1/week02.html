이야기 읽기
🔊 듣기는 초급 B1 기준으로 읽어드려요. 📌 초급(B1) = 이 학년의 평균 수준. 입문(A2)은 더 쉽게, 중급(B2)은 더 어렵게 조정된 버전이에요.

Huck and Jim had to run away.
Huck's father Pap was a bad man.
He drank too much and hit Huck.
Huck found a canoe by the river.
He put food and tools inside it.
He made the cabin look dangerous.
People thought Huck was dead.
Then he met Jim on Jackson's Island.

Huck had been living with his father Pap in a small cabin by the river. Pap drank every night and became violent. Huck knew he had to escape.

One afternoon, while Pap was sleeping, Huck found a canoe near the bank. He quietly loaded it with food, tools, and his gun. Then he made the cabin look like there had been a murder — so people would stop searching for him.

"They're after us. We've got to move," Huck told Jim later, when they met on Jackson's Island. Jim had run away from Miss Watson too.

Together they pushed the raft into the dark water. The river carried them away from danger.

Pap had arrived like a bad dream — drunk, demanding, and certain that Huck's money belonged to him. The judge had given him custody, and Huck had tried to endure it. But endurance has limits, and Huck had reached his.

The escape was carefully staged. He killed a pig, dragged its blood across the cabin floor, and left his own belongings scattered as evidence of violence. To anyone who searched, Huckleberry Finn was dead.

On Jackson's Island he found Jim, who had his own reasons for running. They did not need to explain much. Two people hiding from the same world understand each other quickly.

"They're after us. We've got to move," Huck said — and the words felt right in a way that surprised him. He was, for the first time in his life, choosing his own direction.

팝은 나쁜 꿈처럼 나타났어요. 술에 취해, 요구하며, 헉의 돈이 자기 것이라고 확신하면서. 판사가 양육권을 줬고, 헉은 견디려 했어요. 하지만 인내에는 한계가 있었어요.

탈출은 세심하게 준비됐어요. 돼지를 죽이고 오두막 바닥에 피를 끌었어요. 마치 폭력 사건이 있었던 것처럼 물건들을 흩어놨어요. 헉은 죽은 것으로 보였어요.

잭슨 섬에서 짐을 만났어요. "그들이 쫓아오고 있어. 우리는 움직여야 해," 헉이 말했어요. 강이 그들을 위험에서 멀리 데려갔어요.
