지문 읽기 READING

The boy walked near the water. It was dark and quiet. He saw a big ship. The ship looked strange in the night.

A man came from the shadows. He was big and strong. He put a hand on the boy's mouth. The boy could not call for help.

They took him onto the ship. The ship moved into the black sea. The boy felt cold and afraid. He did not know this place. He did not know if he would see home again.

Under the cover of darkness, a young boy found himself near the quiet docks. He observed the large ship waiting in the water, its shape mysterious against the night sky. Suddenly, two strong men appeared from behind some boxes.

Before he could shout or run, they grabbed him and carried him onto the waiting ship. The boy felt a deep fear as he realized what was happening. He was being taken away against his will, with no chance to say goodbye.

As the ship sailed into the open sea, he watched the land disappear. He wondered about his family and friends, and if they would ever know his fate. The future seemed completely unknown and frightening.
