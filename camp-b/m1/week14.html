지문 읽기 READING

Tom woke up in a strange place. He did not know where he was. The grass was very tall. It looked like a green forest.

He saw small houses all around him. The houses were very small. They were only as big as his hand. Then he saw tiny people. They were walking and talking.

The tiny people looked at Tom. They were not afraid. They talked in loud voices. They told Tom to go away. Tom felt very big and strange. He did not want to hurt them.

Tom found himself in an unusual village where everything was remarkably small. The houses were no larger than shoeboxes, and the streets were extremely narrow. He observed tiny people moving about their daily lives with great energy and purpose.

These little people appeared completely confident despite their small size. They spoke with loud voices and gave orders to one another. Tom realized they were organizing themselves as if preparing for something important.

He wondered how such small creatures could be so bold. Their behavior was surprising because they acted like they controlled everything around them. Tom decided to watch quietly and learn more about their mysterious world.
