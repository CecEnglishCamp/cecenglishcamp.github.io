📌 초급(B1) = 이 학년의 평균 수준. 입문(A2)은 더 쉽게, 중급(B2)은 더 어렵게 조정된 버전이에요.

The Mississippi River was long.
Every day brought something new.
One morning, two men ran toward the river.
"Help us!" they cried.
Huck and Jim pulled them onto the raft.
Jim did not trust the two men.
"We should be careful," Jim said.

The Mississippi River was long and full of stories. Every bend in the river brought something new — a town, a boat, or a stranger.

Huck and Jim sometimes saw other boats passing in the night. Jim always kept his face down when boats came close.

One morning, they saw two men who were running toward the river. "Help us!" the men cried. "Someone is chasing us!"

Huck pulled them onto the raft. Jim watched them carefully. "I do not trust men who arrive like this," Jim whispered. "We should be careful." Huck nodded. He was learning to listen to Jim's wisdom.

The river was never the same from one night to the next. Sometimes it was wide and silent. Sometimes it was narrow, with dark trees leaning over both sides. Always it was moving, always changing.

Huck had begun to notice things he never used to notice. He sometimes watched the water for an hour without saying a word. He always listened when Jim spoke.

The two strangers who had come aboard were called the Duke and the King. They were liars and tricksters — men who pretended to be someone they were not in order to take money from honest people.

Jim had known immediately. "There are men who tell the truth," he said quietly to Huck, "and men that do not. Those two are the second kind."

Huck wasn't sure at first. But the more time passed, the more he began to see that Jim was right. The river had been teaching him to see clearly. The question was whether he would act on what he saw.

※ 번역은 초급 B1 기준
강은 하룻밤도 똑같지 않았어요. 때로는 넓고 조용했고, 때로는 좁고 어두운 나무들이 양쪽에 기울어 있었어요. 항상 흐르고 있었고, 항상 변하고 있었어요.

헉은 전에는 눈치채지 못했던 것들을 알아채기 시작했어요. 때때로 아무 말도 하지 않고 한 시간 동안 물을 바라봤어요. 짐이 말할 때는 항상 들었어요.

배에 탄 두 낯선 사람은 공작과 왕이라고 불렸어요. 그들은 거짓말쟁이였어요 — 정직한 사람들에게서 돈을 빼앗기 위해 다른 사람인 척하는 사람들.

짐은 즉시 알았어요. "진실을 말하는 사람들이 있어," 그는 조용히 헉에게 말했어요. "그리고 그렇지 않은 사람들도. 저 둘은 두 번째 종류야."
