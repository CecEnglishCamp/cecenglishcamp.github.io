2

지문 읽기 READING

In the future, firemen burn books. Books are illegal. Guy Montag is a fireman. Then he meets Clarisse, a strange girl who asks questions. She makes him think. He starts to wonder if his life has meaning.

Bradbury's dystopia presents a society that has chosen intellectual destruction voluntarily. Unlike Orwell's forced oppression, citizens willingly traded depth for distraction.

토론 주제 DEBATE TOPIC

💬 "Voluntary ignorance is more dangerous than enforced censorship."
자발적 무지는 강제된 검열보다 위험하다.
✅ 찬성 (Agree)
  • F451's society chose its own intellectual death
  • Voluntary ignorance cannot be blamed on authority
  • When people choose entertainment over truth democracy erodes
❌ 반대 (Disagree)
  • 1984's forced oppression is more brutal
  • People cannot be blamed when the system designs comfort
  • Structural incentives create voluntary ignorance
