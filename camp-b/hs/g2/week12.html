핵심 표현 KEY EXPRESSION

The creatures outside looked from pig to man, from man to pig, and from pig to man again; but already it was impossible to say which was which. 🔊
밖의 생물들은 돼지에서 인간으로, 인간에서 돼지로 번갈아 바라보았다. 이미 어느 쪽이 어느 쪽인지 말하기가 불가능했다.
2

지문 읽기 READING

Napoleon invites the human farmers to visit Animal Farm. The pigs walk on two legs now. They carry whips. They drink alcohol and play cards with humans. The other animals look through the window. They cannot tell the difference between the pigs and the humans. The revolution is complete — and completely betrayed.

The novel's circular structure — ending where it began, with oppressed animals staring at their masters through glass — achieves its devastating effect through Orwell's most economical means. The inability to distinguish pig from man indicts not just Stalinism but any system where the powerful inevitably mirror those they replaced.

토론 주제 DEBATE TOPIC

💬 "Power structures inevitably reproduce themselves regardless of ideology."
권력 구조는 이데올로기에 관계없이 불가피하게 스스로를 재생산한다.
✅ 찬성 (Agree)
  • Animal Farm shows that all revolutions eventually recreate what they destroyed
  • Human (and animal) nature seeks hierarchy regardless of stated ideals
  • History repeatedly shows revolutionary leaders becoming new tyrants
❌ 반대 (Disagree)
  • Some revolutions have created genuinely more equal societies
  • The problem is specific corrupted individuals, not power itself
  • Democratic checks and balances can prevent this cycle
