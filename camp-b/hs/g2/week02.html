지문 읽기
🔊 B1 기준으로 읽어드려요. C1은 수능 실전 수준이에요.

Ralph's most important rule is the signal fire. If the fire goes out, they cannot be seen by passing ships. Every boy must take turns keeping it burning.

But Jack and his hunters have a different priority. One afternoon, they abandon the fire to chase a pig. While they are gone, a ship passes the island.

Ralph sees the ship from the top of the mountain. He looks at the signal fire. It has gone out. The ship disappears over the horizon without seeing them.

Ralph confronts Jack furiously. Jack has killed his first pig. He is proud and excited. He cannot understand why Ralph is so upset about a missed ship. The conflict between survival and hunting — between Ralph's reason and Jack's instinct — now becomes personal.

The signal fire episode is Golding's first demonstration of the central conflict. What looks like a trivial disagreement — fire versus hunting — is in fact a philosophical divide: Ralph represents the primacy of rescue and return to civilisation; Jack represents the seductive pull of the island itself.

Jack's joy at the kill is genuine and troubling. He has discovered something about himself that civilisation had suppressed — the pleasure of the hunt, the power over life and death. Not until he holds the pig's head in his hands does he fully understand the satisfaction it brings him.

Golding suggests that the fire — a symbol of connection to the adult world, to reason, to rescue — requires constant, deliberate maintenance. It does not burn on its own. Neither, he implies, does civilisation.

랄프의 가장 중요한 규칙은 신호 불이에요. 불이 꺼지면 지나가는 배들이 볼 수 없어요. 하지만 잭과 사냥꾼들은 한 오후에 불을 버리고 돼지를 쫓아가요. 그동안 배 한 척이 섬을 지나쳐요.

랄프는 산꼭대기에서 배를 봐요. 신호 불이 꺼져 있어요. 배는 그들을 보지 못하고 수평선 너머로 사라져요. 랄프는 잭에게 분노하며 맞서요. 잭은 첫 번째 돼지를 잡아서 자랑스럽고 흥분되어 있어요.

이번 주 KEY EXPRESSION
"We ought to have a chief to decide things."
ought to = ~해야 한다 → 당위, 필요성 (must보다 약한 의무)
a chief to decide things = 결정을 내릴 지도자 → 권위의 필요성
decide things = 사물을 결정하다 → 집단 생존의 조건
💡 이 표현을 에세이 thesis와 디베이트 opening에서 직접 써보세요.
