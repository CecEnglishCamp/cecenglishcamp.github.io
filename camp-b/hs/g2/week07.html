Animal Farm

혁명의 시작 — George Orwell · C1

1

핵심 표현 KEY EXPRESSION

All animals are equal, but some animals are more equal than others. 🔊
모든 동물은 평등하다. 하지만 어떤 동물들은 더 평등하다.
2

지문 읽기 READING

The animals on Manor Farm are tired of their human owner, Mr. Jones. Old Major gives a speech about freedom. After he dies, the animals start a revolution. They take over the farm and rename it Animal Farm.

Orwell's allegory of the Russian Revolution traces how a genuinely idealistic uprising becomes corrupted by the very power it sought to overthrow. The pigs' gradual usurpation of leadership mirrors the Bolshevik betrayal of revolutionary principles.

5

토론 주제 DEBATE

💬 "Revolutions inevitably betray their original ideals."
혁명은 필연적으로 원래의 이상을 배반한다.
✅ 찬성
  • The pigs become what they originally revolted against
  • History shows most revolutions create new tyranny
  • Power corrupts even genuine idealists
❌ 반대
  • Some revolutions have created lasting positive change
  • The betrayal was caused by individuals not revolution itself
  • Democratic systems with checks can prevent abuse
