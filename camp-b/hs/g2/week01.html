지문 읽기
🔊 B1 기준으로 읽어드려요. C1은 수능 실전 수준이에요.

A plane carrying a group of boys has been shot down during a war. The boys land on a deserted island with no adults. Having survived the crash, they must now decide how to organise themselves.

Ralph finds a conch shell on the beach. He blows it, and the sound brings all the boys together. They decide that whoever holds the conch has the right to speak. This is their first rule — a small act of civilisation in a wild place.

Ralph is elected leader. Jack, the head of a choir group, is given control of the hunters. At first, the two boys cooperate. They explore the island and confirm that they are completely alone.

But even on the first day, a tension appears. Jack wants to hunt. Ralph wants to build shelters and maintain a signal fire. Only by keeping the fire lit can they hope to be rescued. Their different priorities will soon tear the group apart.

Golding sets his experiment in motion with deliberate simplicity: remove the adults, place the children on an island, and observe. The conch is introduced almost immediately as the instrument of democratic order — whoever holds it holds, for the moment, the authority of reason over instinct.

Ralph is a natural democratic leader — fair, well-intentioned, and fundamentally optimistic about human nature. Jack is the opposite: driven by hunger, dominance, and the pleasure of the hunt. Not until the first pig eludes him does Jack understand what he truly wants — not food, but power.

Golding is asking a question that civilisation has always tried to avoid: what is the origin of social order? Is it natural to humans, or is it merely a fragile agreement — a conch shell, easily broken — that holds only as long as everyone agrees to respect it?

전쟁 중에 소년들을 태운 비행기가 격추됐어요. 소년들은 무인도에 착륙했고 어른이 없어요. 랄프는 해변에서 소라 껍데기를 발견해요. 그것을 불면 소리가 모든 소년을 모아요. 소라를 든 사람이 말할 권리를 갖는다고 정해요.

수능 유형 — 빈칸 추론
29번 유형
In Lord of the Flies, Golding places a group of boys on an uninhabited island and removes all adult authority. The conch shell becomes the central symbol of democratic order: whoever holds it has the right to speak, and this right is initially respected by all. However, Golding suggests that this arrangement is fundamentally          — it depends not on any natural human tendency toward order, but entirely on the collective decision to respect a shared symbol. The moment individuals prioritise personal desire over collective agreement, the entire system collapses.

* uninhabited: 무인의  ** fundamentally: 근본적으로
정답 ③ — contingent on voluntary collective consent

핵심 구조: "depends not on... but entirely on..."
→ 빈칸 = "depends entirely on collective decision to respect"
→ ③ "contingent on voluntary collective consent" = 자발적 집단 동의에 달려 있는

오답 분석:
① 자연적 지도자 → 지문은 지도자가 아닌 집단 동의를 강조
② 불가피한 → 지문은 취약성을 말하지 불가피성을 말하지 않음
④ 공유 문화 배경 → 지문에 없는 개념
⑤ 경쟁 권위의 부재 → 지문의 논리와 반대
