🔊 B1 기준으로 읽어드려요. C1은 수능 실전 수준이에요.

Nick Carraway came to New York to work in the bond business. He rented a small house in West Egg, next to an enormous mansion that belonged to a man named Gatsby. Every weekend, Gatsby threw lavish parties for hundreds of guests — but he never seemed to enjoy them himself.

One night, Nick saw Gatsby standing alone at the edge of the water, reaching toward a single green light on the opposite shore. His arm was stretched out, as though he was trying to touch something very far away.

Nick did not know what the light meant. But he could see that it meant everything to Gatsby — more than the parties, more than the mansion, more than anything his money could buy.

West Egg was new money — loud, ambitious, and slightly embarrassing to those who had inherited their wealth across the bay. Nick observed this distinction without fully understanding it.

What he understood less was Gatsby himself: a man who filled his house with strangers, played the gracious host to people whose names he barely knew, and yet stood every night at the water's edge, solitary and reaching, as though the party behind him were merely a backdrop for something else entirely.

The green light blinked at the end of Daisy's dock. Nick would later understand what it represented — not just Daisy, but the idea that the past could be recovered, that time moved in a circle rather than a line.

닉 캐러웨이는 채권 사업을 위해 뉴욕에 왔어요. 웨스트 에그에 작은 집을 빌렸는데, 개츠비라는 사람의 거대한 저택 옆이었어요. 매 주말 개츠비는 수백 명의 손님을 위해 화려한 파티를 열었지만, 정작 자신은 즐기지 않는 것 같았어요.

어느 날 밤, 닉은 개츠비가 물가 끝에 혼자 서서 맞은편 해안의 초록빛 불빛을 향해 손을 뻗는 것을 봤어요. 마치 아주 멀리 있는 무언가를 만지려는 것처럼 팔을 뻗고 있었어요.

닉은 그 빛이 무엇을 의미하는지 몰랐어요. 하지만 그것이 개츠비에게 모든 것을 의미한다는 것은 알 수 있었어요.

핵심 표현 분석
이번 주 KEY EXPRESSION
"He reached toward something he could never touch."
이 문장은 단순한 물리적 동작이 아니에요.
reach toward = 손을 뻗다 → 갈망하다, 추구하다
could never touch = 절대 닿을 수 없는 → 불가능한 꿈
something = 구체적이지 않음 → 데이지? 과거? 아메리칸 드림?
💡 이 표현을 에세이 thesis와 디베이트 opening에서 직접 써보세요.
🔍
문장 구조 분석 → 재생산
구조 분석은 해석이 목적이 아니에요.
이해 → 구조 추출 → 내 문장으로 재생산하는 것이 목표예요.
STEP 1 — 분석 (Analyse)
"His arm was stretched out, as though he was trying to touch something very far away."
주어
His arm
신체 부위로 감정을 표현
동사 (수동태)
was stretched out
뻗어진 상태 — 의지가 아닌 욕망
연결어
as though
마치 ~인 것처럼 (가정법)
가정절
he was trying to touch something far away
불가능한 시도 → 희망과 절망
💡 수능 어법 포인트: "as though + 가정법 과거"
격식: "as though he were trying" / 구어: "as though he was trying"
STEP 2 — 구조 추출 (Extract)
이 문장의 구조를 공식으로 추출하면:
[주어] + was + [과거분사],
as though + [주어] + was + [동사-ing] + [목적어/부사]
이 구조의 핵심: 수동태 상태 + 가정법 비교
→ 의도적 행동이 아닌 욕망의 상태를 표현할 때 사용
STEP 3 — 재생산 (Reproduce)
같은 구조로 내 문장을 만들어보세요.
예시: "Her eyes were fixed on the door,
as though she was waiting for someone who would never come."

29번 유형
Nick saw Gatsby standing alone at the water's edge every night, his arm stretched toward a green light across the bay. Gatsby had built his entire life around this moment — the parties, the mansion, the wealth — all of it directed toward one small point of light. Nick eventually understood that the green light was not really about Daisy. It was about the belief that the past could be recovered, that time itself could be reversed. This is why Fitzgerald calls Gatsby "great" despite his flaws: his capacity for hope was          in its intensity, if not in its wisdom.
정답 ③ — extraordinary and almost irrational

핵심 구조: "great despite his flaws" → 결점에도 불구하고 위대함
