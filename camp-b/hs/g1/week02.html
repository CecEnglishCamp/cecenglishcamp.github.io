지문 읽기
🔊 B1 기준으로 읽어드려요. C1은 수능 실전 수준이에요.

Nick visited his cousin Daisy and her husband Tom in East Egg. Their house was enormous and white, surrounded by manicured lawns. Tom was a powerful man — he had been a famous athlete at university — but there was something brutal about the way he spoke.

Tom talked confidently about race and civilisation, as though his opinions were facts. Daisy listened with a faint smile. Jordan Baker, a professional golfer, sat quietly in the corner.

Nick noticed that Daisy's voice was unusually compelling. "Her voice is full of money," he thought — warm and exciting, always promising something it never quite delivered.

Later, Jordan told Nick quietly that Tom had "a woman in New York." Nobody seemed surprised. Tom and Daisy were, Nick realised, careless people who smashed things up and then retreated behind their money.

The distinction between East Egg and West Egg was, on the surface, merely geographic. In reality it was civilisational. Tom and Daisy represented inherited wealth — the kind that had settled into ease and carelessness over generations.

They did not need to perform richness. They simply were it, and felt entitled to treat the world as something arranged for their convenience. Daisy's voice, which Nick famously described as "full of money," was the perfect instrument of this class.

It promised intimacy while delivering nothing. Tom's casual mention of his affair, and Daisy's equally casual reception of it, suggested two people who had long since stopped expecting anything of each other — or of themselves.

닉은 사촌 데이지와 남편 톰을 이스트 에그에서 방문했어요. 집은 거대하고 하얬으며, 잘 다듬어진 잔디로 둘러싸여 있었어요. 톰은 강력한 남자였지만 그가 말하는 방식에는 뭔가 잔인한 것이 있었어요.

닉은 데이지의 목소리가 특별히 매력적이라는 것을 알아챘어요. "그녀의 목소리는 돈으로 가득 차 있어," 그는 생각했어요. 나중에 조던이 닉에게 조용히 톰이 뉴욕에 여자가 있다고 말했어요. 아무도 놀라지 않는 것 같았어요.

핵심 표현 분석
이번 주 KEY EXPRESSION
"They were careless people — they smashed up things and creatures."
이 문장은 단순한 물리적 동작이 아니에요.
careless = 부주의한 → 특권층의 무책임함
smashed up = 망가뜨리다 → 돌이킬 수 없는 피해
things and creatures = 사물과 생명 → 계층적 무관심
💡 이 표현을 에세이 thesis와 디베이트 opening에서 직접 써보세요.
🔍
문장 구조 분석 → 재생산
구조 분석은 해석이 목적이 아니에요.
이해 → 구조 추출 → 내 문장으로 재생산하는 것이 목표예요.

STEP 2 — 구조 추출 (Extract)
이 문장의 구조를 공식으로 추출하면:
[주어] + was + [과거분사],
as though + [주어] + was + [동사-ing] + [목적어/부사]
이 구조의 핵심: 수동태 상태 + 가정법 비교
→ 의도적 행동이 아닌 욕망의 상태를 표현할 때 사용
STEP 3 — 재생산 (Reproduce)
같은 구조로 내 문장을 만들어보세요.
예시: "Her eyes were fixed on the door,
as though she was waiting for someone who would never come."
