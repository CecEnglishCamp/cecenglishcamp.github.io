지문 읽기
📡 텔레스크린이 당신을 감시합니다. B1으로 읽어드려요. C1은 수능 실전 수준이에요.

Winston notices a dark-haired girl at work. He suspects she might be a member of the Thought Police. He is afraid of her.

One day, she slips him a folded piece of paper. Winston is terrified. Not until he is alone does he dare to open it. The note says three words: "I love you."

Winston is astonished. He has lived so long in fear and isolation that he has almost forgotten what private human connection feels like. The note changes everything.

They arrange to meet secretly in the countryside. Julia tells Winston that she has broken the Party's rules many times before. She is not a rebel with a political philosophy — she simply enjoys life and refuses to give it up.

Julia is Winston's opposite in every significant way. He is cerebral, tormented, ideologically driven; she is practical, sensual, and indifferent to abstract principle. What unites them is not politics but the body — the stubborn insistence of physical desire against a system designed to eliminate it.

Orwell makes clear that the Party's anti-sex league is not primarily about morality. It is about energy. Sexual desire, redirected into political hysteria, becomes one of the Party's most reliable fuels. Julia's promiscuity is therefore, in Orwell's schema, a political act — not because she intends it as one, but because it diverts energy the Party needs.

Their affair is doomed — Orwell never allows the reader to doubt this. But its significance lies precisely in its hopelessness: love in Room 101's world is not a solution but a testament, a proof that something in human nature resists even the most total system of control.

윈스턴은 직장에서 검은 머리 소녀를 알아채요. 그는 그녀가 사상경찰일지도 모른다고 의심해요. 어느 날 그녀가 쪽지를 건네줘요. 윈스턴은 혼자가 될 때까지 감히 열지 못해요. 쪽지에는 "사랑해"라고 적혀 있어요.

그들은 시골에서 비밀리에 만나기로 해요. 줄리아는 윈스턴에게 전에도 당의 규칙을 여러 번 어겼다고 말해요. 그녀는 이념적 반란군이 아니에요 — 그저 삶을 즐기고 포기하기를 거부해요.

핵심 표현 분석 — Doublethink
이번 주 KEY EXPRESSION
"I love you."
War is Peace = 전쟁은 평화다 → 외부 전쟁이 내부 통합을 만든다
Freedom is Slavery = 자유는 노예다 → 개인 자유는 오히려 고립과 취약함
Ignorance is Strength = 무지는 힘이다 → 모르면 저항할 수 없다
💡 이 슬로건들이 수능에 나오는 이유:
모순(contradiction) → 역설(paradox) → 역설이 진실이 되는 구조
→ 빈칸 추론, 요약문 완성, 주제 파악 문제 단골 소재

💡 수능 어법 최빈출: "Not until + 절/구 → did/could/would + 주어 + 동사원형"

STEP 2 — 구조 추출 (Extract)
Had + [주어] + [과거분사], [주어] + would/could + never + have + [과거분사]
핵심: Had + 주어 + p.p. = If + 주어 + had + p.p. (If 생략 도치)
→ 과거 사실의 반대를 가정 — 수능 가정법 도치 단골
