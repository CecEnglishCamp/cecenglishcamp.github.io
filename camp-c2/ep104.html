오늘의 이야기
STORY

미국 3년차 영자씨가 Safeway 정육 코너에서 직원 Dan에게 마블링 좋은 부위를 부탁한다. 그릴엔 ribeye(꽃등심), 디너 파티엔 tenderloin(안심)을 추천받고, USDA Choice와 Prime 등급 차이, 두께·레스팅·신선도 확인법까지 우아하게 배운다. 그리고 당당하게 Prime ribeye 두 덩이를 산다. 'Now that's graceful grocery shopping.'

🗣️
대화 전문
FULL DIALOGUE · 직원 & 영자씨
영자씨
Excuse me, could you help me find a well-marbled cut? I'm planning a special dinner tonight.
Dan
Of course! Are you thinking of grilling, pan-searing, or slow cooking?
영자씨
I'd love to grill it. What cut would you recommend for the best flavor?
Dan
For grilling, ribeye is your best bet — the fat marbling keeps it juicy and tender. This one is USDA Choice, beautifully marbled.
영자씨
And what about for a special occasion — something truly elegant, like a dinner party?
Dan
Then you'd want the tenderloin — the most tender cut, very lean. Perfect for beef Wellington.
영자씨
Could you tell me the difference between USDA Choice and Prime?
Dan
Prime is the top grade — more marbling, richer flavor. It runs about 20% more, but for a special dinner, it's worth it.
영자씨
I'll go with the Prime ribeye. How thick would you recommend?
Dan
About an inch and a quarter — a beautiful sear outside while staying juicy inside.
영자씨
How should I prepare it? Should I let it rest at room temperature first?
Dan
Absolutely — take it out 30 minutes before cooking. Season generously, then sear on high heat.
영자씨
One last question — how can I tell if the beef is truly fresh?
Dan
Look for a bright cherry-red color, firm texture, and minimal liquid in the package.
영자씨
That's incredibly helpful. I'll take two of the Prime ribeyes, please.
