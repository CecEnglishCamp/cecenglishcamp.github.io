오늘의 이야기
STORY

미국 생활 3년차, 영자씨가 예약 없이 네일샵 문을 연다. 예전 같으면 긴장했겠지만 오늘은 다르다. 'I was hoping you might be able to fit me in.' 우아한 한마디로 자리를 잡고, 색상은 sheer pink와 muted coral, 모양은 oval로 품격 있게 주문한다. 물 온도까지 정중하게 조절을 부탁하고, 마무리는 'Simple is always elegant.' 계산할 때는 20% 팁까지 우아하게 더하며, 직원에게 '함께 일하기 편한 손님'이라는 최고의 칭찬을 듣는다.

🗣️
대화 전문
FULL DIALOGUE · 직원 & 영자씨
직원
Welcome in! Do you have an appointment?
영자씨
I don't, but I was hoping you might be able to fit me in. I'd love a gel manicure.
직원
Of course! Right this way.
직원
What kind of finish are you looking for today?
영자씨
I'd prefer something soft and elegant — maybe a sheer pink or a muted coral.
직원
What shape would you like?
영자씨
Oval, please — medium length. I find it the most flattering for everyday wear.
영자씨
Would it be possible to add a pedicure as well?
직원
Is the water temperature comfortable?
영자씨
It's just slightly warm — could you add a touch of cold water? Thank you.
직원
Would you like any nail art or keep it simple?
영자씨
I'll keep it classic — simple is always elegant.
직원
Your total is $52. How would you like to pay?
영자씨
I'll put it on my card. I'd like to add a 20% gratuity — you did a wonderful job.

  • 6I'd like to add a 20% gratuity.
    20% 팁을 추가하고 싶어요.
