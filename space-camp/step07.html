📖 Reading Passage — Grade 5

In Step 7, trainees practice seat ingress and harness training.

Ingress is the procedure for entering a spacecraft safely.
It is not simply walking in — trainees must follow specific steps in order.

Step 1: Enter through the hatch carefully.
Step 2: Locate and move to the assigned seat.
Step 3: Fasten the five-point harness.

The five-point harness has two shoulder straps, two waist straps, and one crotch strap.
Each strap must be pulled tight and confirmed as secure.

During launch, a spacecraft can shake very hard.
The force can reach three times normal gravity — called 3G.
Without a harness, an astronaut's body would be pushed out of the seat.

When the harness is fully secured, the trainee reports:
"Seat ingress complete. Harness secure — nominal!"

📖 Reading Passage — Grade 6

Seat ingress is one of the most critical procedures in pre-launch preparation.
It may appear simple, but every step must be executed with precision.

The ingress procedure consists of three stages:
entering the spacecraft through the hatch, locating the assigned seat, and securing the restraint system.

The five-point harness is the primary restraint system used in spacecraft.
It consists of two shoulder straps, two lap straps, and one crotch strap.
Each strap must be individually tightened and verified before launch clearance is granted.

During launch, astronauts experience significant G-forces.
A typical rocket launch can generate up to 3 times the force of gravity.
At 3G, a person weighing 50 kilograms would feel as if they weigh 150 kilograms.
Without proper restraint, the body cannot remain in the seat, and injuries can occur.

The harness also keeps astronauts in position during orbital maneuvers, re-entry, and emergency situations.

Once all five points are confirmed, the trainee formally reports:
"Seat ingress complete. Five-point harness verified — all points nominal. Requesting launch clearance. Over."

5점식 하네스는 우주선에서 사용되는 주요 구속 시스템입니다.
어깨 스트랩 2개, 무릎 스트랩 2개, 가랑이 스트랩 1개로 구성됩니다.
발사 허가가 내려지기 전에 각 스트랩을 개별적으로 조이고 검증해야 합니다.

발사 중 우주비행사들은 상당한 G-포스를 경험합니다.
일반적인 로켓 발사는 중력의 3배까지 발생할 수 있습니다.

적절한 구속 없이는 몸이 좌석에 있을 수 없고 부상이 발생할 수 있습니다.

하네스는 또한 궤도 기동, 재진입, 비상 상황에서도 우주비행사를 제자리에 유지시켜 줍니다.
