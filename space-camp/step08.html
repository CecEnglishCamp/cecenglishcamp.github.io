📖 Reading Passage — Grade 5

In Step 8, trainees practice Mission Control communication.

Mission Control is the team on Earth that monitors and supports every space mission.
They track the spacecraft's position, check system data, and guide astronauts through problems.

NASA has three communication rules:
Speak short. Speak clear. Always confirm.

Astronauts use special words during communication.
"Over" means "I am finished. Please respond."
"Copy" means "I received and understood your message."
"Roger" means "I understand and will follow the instruction."

During training, the signal is suddenly lost.
The screen shows: "COMMUNICATION ERROR — SIGNAL LOST."

The trainees do not panic. They stay calm, check their systems, and try to reconnect.
They report: "Mission Control, do you copy? Signal lost. Attempting to reconnect. Stand by. Over."

The signal is restored. The crew reports back:
"Signal restored. Communication reestablished. All systems nominal. Over."

8단계에서 훈련생들은 관제센터 교신을 연습합니다.

관제센터는 지구에서 모든 우주 미션을 모니터링하고 지원하는 팀입니다.
우주선의 위치를 추적하고, 시스템 데이터를 확인하고, 우주비행사들이 문제를 해결하도록 안내합니다.

NASA에는 세 가지 교신 규칙이 있습니다.
짧게 말하기. 명확하게 말하기. 항상 확인하기.

"Over"는 "내 말 끝. 응답해주세요."를 의미합니다.
"Copy"는 "메시지를 받고 이해했습니다."를 의미합니다.

📖 Reading Passage — Grade 6

Communication is one of the most critical elements of any space mission.
Without reliable contact with Mission Control, astronauts cannot receive guidance, report emergencies, or confirm procedures.

Mission Control monitors every aspect of the spacecraft — from life support data and power levels to orbital trajectory and communication signals.
When a problem occurs on board, Mission Control helps the crew diagnose and resolve it.

NASA uses a structured communication protocol to ensure clarity and efficiency.
The three core rules are: speak short, speak clear, and always confirm.

Key communication terms include:
Over — transmission complete, awaiting response.
Copy — message received and understood.
Roger — understood and will comply.
Wilco — will comply with the instruction.
Out — transmission complete, no response required.

During the training exercise, a communication blackout is simulated.
Trainees must switch to a backup frequency, continue monitoring telemetry data, and prepare a formal reconnection report.

When contact is restored, the crew reports:
"Mission Control, this is Spacecraft Alpha. Communication reestablished on backup frequency. Telemetry nominal. All systems operational. Requesting status confirmation. Over."

This training teaches that communication is not only about words.
It is about protocol, precision, and staying calm under pressure.

교신은 모든 우주 미션에서 가장 중요한 요소 중 하나입니다.
관제센터와의 안정적인 연락 없이 우주비행사는 안내를 받거나, 비상 상황을 보고하거나, 절차를 확인할 수 없습니다.

관제센터는 생명유지 데이터부터 전력 수준, 궤도 경로, 통신 신호까지 우주선의 모든 측면을 모니터링합니다.

NASA는 명확성과 효율성을 보장하기 위해 구조화된 통신 프로토콜을 사용합니다.
핵심 3원칙: 짧게 말하기, 명확하게 말하기, 항상 확인하기.

훈련 중 통신 블랙아웃이 시뮬레이션됩니다.
훈련생들은 백업 주파수로 전환하고, telemetry 데이터를 계속 모니터링하고, 공식 재연결 보고를 준비해야 합니다.

이 훈련이 가르치는 것은 교신이 단순히 말에 관한 것이 아니라는 점입니다.
그것은 프로토콜, 정확성, 그리고 압박 상황에서 침착함에 관한 것입니다.
