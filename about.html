The Philosophy

From English as a Subject to English as a Tool for Thought

Our Approach

We treat English not as a set of rules to memorize, but as a structural framework for understanding literature and the world. We build a solid foundation with grammar and expand it through AI-driven immersion.

The AI Trinity System

CEC Camp utilizes three distinct AI roles to ensure a perfect learning loop. We call this the Coach-Practice-Review cycle.

ChatGPT
(Personal Coach)

"LEARN"

  • Level-specific (A/B/C) grammar & expression guides
  • Real-time strategy: "How should I say this?"
  • Preparation before entering the playground

Open WebUI
(The Playground)

"PRACTICE"

  • Free conversation with classic literature characters
  • Immersive "Real-world" English environment
  • Applying what you learned from your coach

NotebookLM
(The Reviewer)

"REVIEW"

  • Fact-checking based on original literature texts
  • Analyzing conversation logs for improvement
  • Connecting and organizing your learning data

"Coach with ChatGPT, Practice in Open WebUI, and Review with NotebookLM."
