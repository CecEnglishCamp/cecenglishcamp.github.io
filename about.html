Our Story

What if we designed English education backwards?

CEC English Camp was born from a single bold question. Instead of starting with grammar rules and vocabulary lists, we started at the destination — high school students debating complex topics, writing persuasive essays, and expressing themselves with genuine confidence in English.

Then we asked: what must a third-grader learn today to get there?

That reverse-engineering process became the foundation of everything we do.

Our Philosophy

Great English education starts with great stories.

Every child in Camp A journeys through the classics — Peter Rabbit, The Wizard of Oz, Treasure Island, A Little Princess. Not as translations to memorize, but as living worlds to inhabit.

Each week, students encounter one carefully chosen sentence, expanded across three days from its simplest form to its full literary expression.

MondayHe ran away.
WednesdayHe ran away fast.
FridayHe ran away as fast as he could.

This is not drill-and-repeat. This is language growing inside a child, one story at a time.

Four Worlds, One Vision

Every learner has a path.

🏕️
Camp A

Elementary

Grades 3 through 6. Classic literature meets structured language acquisition. From fairy tales to adventure novels, students build vocabulary, sentence patterns, and reading confidence through stories they actually love.

🏕️
Camp B

Middle & High School

The stakes rise. Students engage with sophisticated texts, write academic essays, and practice structured debate. Every skill points toward fluent, confident self-expression in academic English.

Adult Learners

Real English for real life. One hundred episodes of practical, situational English designed for Korean adults — navigating airports, hospitals, restaurants, and everyday conversations with ease and dignity.

Program

Mom Teacher

Because the most powerful English teacher a child can have is the one at home. We equip parents with the tools, confidence, and curriculum to extend English learning beyond the classroom.

What Makes Us Different

We teach English through things, not about things.

Most English programs teach English about things. We teach English through things — through stories, debates, conversations, and real creative expression.

Our AI-integrated platform brings personalized feedback to every student, every session. Robo, our AI companion, guides elementary students through their lessons. Advanced learners engage with ChatGPT-powered tutoring that adapts to their level and goals.

We don't just teach English. We build the foundation for a lifetime of confident communication.

Our Technology
