	Baa, baa, black sheep,
	Have you any wool?
	Yes, sir, yes, sir,
	Three bags full;
	One for my master,
	And one for my dame,
	and one for the little boy
	Who lives down the lane.

	Baa, baa, black sheep,
	Have you any wool?
	Yes, sir, yes, sir,
	Three bags full;

	and one for the little boy
	Who lives down the lane.
